# Sozu

The extra energy (3 -> 4 per turn) is extremely powerful, but the potion lockout is a severe tradeoff. Losing ALL potion access means no safety net for bad draws and no supplemental healing.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: [[relics/Toy Ornithopter]] (completely dead), [[relics/Potion Belt]] (worthless), [[relics/White Beast Statue]] (worthless). Healing potions as sustain plan is unavailable -- healing cards ([[cards/Reaper]], Feed) become even MORE critical.

SILENT-SPECIFIC DANGER (3 deaths confirmed): The [[characters/Silent]] lacks innate healing and relies on potions as emergency saves. Pattern: Sozu removes the safety net that would have saved the run in a single critical turn. For the Silent, **strongly prefer other boss relics** unless the deck has [[cards/Wraith Form]] and multiple block sources independent of potions.

WHEN TO TAKE:
- Skip all potion-related relics and events.
- Conservative play is more important -- no emergency saves.
- The extra energy partially compensates by enabling more block per turn.
- Strong pick if confident in finding healing cards.
