# Centennial Puzzle

Effectively gives an 8-card hand on the turn you first take damage. Extremely strong for setting up big combo turns.

STRATEGY: On turn 1, if enemies are attacking, you can intentionally under-block to take a small hit and trigger the draw. In multi-turn fights, the 3 extra cards often let you play a devastating setup turn (Power + burst damage + block all in one turn). Against enemies that buff turn 1 (no damage), the trigger happens on turn 2 instead.

SYNERGIES: [[cards/Fiend Fire]] (larger hand = more damage), [[cards/Metallicize]] (small chip damage still triggers draw), multi-enemy first attacks.

ANTI-SYNERGY: Full block strategies that prevent all damage delay the trigger. [[relics/Torii]] reduces damage to 1 but still triggers the draw since HP is still lost.
