# Juzu Bracelet

Extremely high value. Unknown rooms become free value (events) instead of potential death traps. Fundamentally changes [[acts/Act 2]] routing -- Unknown rooms become preferable to Monster rooms.

In Act 2, Unknown rooms can spawn [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]], Byrd flocks, or [[enemies/Snake Plant]] — fights whose clocks are brutal for decks that haven't scaled yet. Juzu Bracelet eliminates this risk entirely.

Worth buying even when it costs a large share of your gold. Route through Unknown rooms for guaranteed safe rooms.
