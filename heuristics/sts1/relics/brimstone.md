# Brimstone

**Run-defining relic.** +2 Str/combat to both you and all enemies. Turns every fight into a DPS race. When acquired early (e.g. Floor 5), Brimstone carries damage scaling for the entire run -- by mid-Act 2, Str reaches 10+, making Strikes deal 16+ and [[cards/Heavy Blade]]+ deal 50-100+. It can serve as the primary damage engine for an entire Ironclad run.

WHEN TO TAKE: Almost always correct for Ironclad, especially with [[cards/Reaper]] (more Str = more healing per Reaper hit). Best purchased early for maximum compounding. Pairs well with [[debuffs/Weak]] application ([[cards/Bash]], [[cards/Thunderclap]], [[cards/Shockwave]]) to offset enemy [[buffs/Strength]] gains.

**PURCHASE CHECKLIST:**
1. Is [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] a possible [[acts/Act 2]] elite? If yes, buy ONLY if you will route around it or your deck already kills elites in ~3 turns — a long Brimstone-fueled Book of Stabbing fight is unsurvivable (see CRITICAL DANGER below).
2. Can your deck end fights in 4-5 turns? If no, enemy Str gain will overwhelm you.
3. Do you have Weak application? Weak offsets enemy Str gains.
4. In Act 2 already? Evaluate remaining enemy pool before buying.

SYNERGIES: [[cards/Demon Form]] (+4 Str/turn combined), [[cards/Reaper]] (heals more as Strength grows), [[cards/Heavy Blade]] (3x/5x multiplied), [[cards/Pummel]]/multi-hit (Strength added per hit), [[cards/Limit Break]] (doubles accumulated Strength).

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Slow decks, long fights, enemies with multi-hit attacks (Book of Stabbing, [[enemies/Snake Plant]]).

CRITICAL DANGER -- BOOK OF STABBING: By turn 5, incoming is 102 damage vs 42 without Brimstone (143% increase). If you have Brimstone, AVOID Book of Stabbing entirely or kill it in 3 turns.
