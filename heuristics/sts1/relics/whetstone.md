# Whetstone

Equivalent to two rest site upgrades for Attacks specifically, but with no control over which cards are chosen. Best when the deck has multiple high-priority Attack upgrades ([[cards/Heavy Blade]] 3x->5x, [[cards/Carnage]] 20->28, [[cards/Twin Strike]], [[cards/Pommel Strike]]). Worst when key Attacks are already upgraded or only Strikes remain.

Strong early (more Attacks in deck = higher chance of hitting good targets, plus upgrades compound over more fights). Weaker late. Always acceptable as a free relic with no downside.
