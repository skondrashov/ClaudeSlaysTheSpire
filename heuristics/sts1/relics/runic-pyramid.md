# Runic Pyramid

Retains entire hand between turns. No discard at end of turn. Cards accumulate across turns.

**Strengths:** Guarantees access to key cards every turn. Eliminates draw RNG for critical plays. Allows holding combo pieces ([[cards/Fiend Fire]] + [[buffs/Strength]] buffs, [[cards/Shockwave]] + block cards) until the right turn.

**Hand management rule:** With Runic Pyramid, every end-of-turn hand is a deliberate inventory. Mass exhaust effects (Fiend Fire, [[cards/Second Wind]], [[cards/Corruption]]) become more powerful but also more destructive — the growing hand means more cards exhausted, including everything being held for later turns or phases. Before any mass exhaust, separate the hand into "expendable" and "must keep," and play or protect the "must keep" cards first — see [[layer:heuristics, cards/Fiend Fire]].
