# Runic Pyramid

Retains entire hand between turns. No discard at end of turn. Cards accumulate across turns.

**Strengths:** Guarantees access to key cards every turn. Eliminates draw RNG for critical plays. Allows holding combo pieces ([[cards/Fiend Fire]] + [[buffs/Strength]] buffs, [[cards/Shockwave]] + block cards) until the right turn.

**Danger with exhaust cards:** Mass exhaust effects (Fiend Fire, [[cards/Second Wind]], [[cards/Corruption]]) become more powerful but also more destructive. With a growing hand, Fiend Fire exhausts MORE cards for more damage -- but also exhausts cards being held for later turns. In phased boss fights ([[bosses/The Champ]], [[bosses/Awakened One]]), this can permanently destroy Phase 2 cards.

**The Champ trap:** Runic Pyramid retains debuff cards ([[cards/Disarm]], Shockwave, [[cards/Exhume]]) in hand across turns while waiting for Phase 2. If Fiend Fire fires during Phase 1, ALL retained cards are exhausted -- including the debuffs needed for Phase 2. Before any mass exhaust effect, identify which cards in hand are being saved and play or protect them first.

**Hand management rule:** With Runic Pyramid, every end-of-turn hand is a deliberate inventory. Before playing mass-exhaust cards, mentally separate the hand into "expendable" and "must keep." Play or protect "must keep" cards before triggering mass exhaust.
