# Mutagenic Strength

Provides a powerful turn-1 burst window. With +3 [[buffs/Strength]], the combined effect across multiple cards on turn 1 can add 10-20 extra damage. After turn 1, the Strength disappears and has no further effect.

Front-load damage on turn 1. Play as many Attack cards as possible before the Strength expires. Pairs especially well with AOE attacks ([[cards/Immolate]], [[cards/Thunderclap]]) where the bonus applies to each enemy hit. The free relic is always worth taking from the event.
