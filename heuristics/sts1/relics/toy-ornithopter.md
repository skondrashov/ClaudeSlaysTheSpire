# Toy Ornithopter

Turns potion usage into healing. Each potion heals an extra 5 HP. With [[relics/Potion Belt]] and aggressive potion acquisition, can heal 25-35 HP across a run.

HEALING GAP SOLUTION: When [[cards/Reaper]] and Feed are not offered, Toy Ornithopter + aggressive potion usage becomes the backup healing plan. Buy healing potions at shops, use liberally in tough fights. Three potion uses in a single boss fight = 15 HP healed, equivalent to a medium Reaper hit.

ANTI-SYNERGY: Completely dead with [[relics/Sozu]] (prevents obtaining potions). Do NOT take Toy Ornithopter if you have Sozu.
