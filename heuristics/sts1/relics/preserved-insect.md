# Preserved Insect

Makes every elite fight significantly easier. 25% HP reduction means fewer turns needed to kill, which means fewer turns of incoming damage. Particularly strong against high-HP elites like [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] and [[enemies/Gremlin Leader]].

Passive benefit with no downside. Always take when offered. The 25% reduction compounds with [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (50% more damage taken) -- an elite at 75% HP taking 50% more damage effectively has ~50% of its original effective HP.
