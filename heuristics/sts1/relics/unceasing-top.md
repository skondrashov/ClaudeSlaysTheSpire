# Unceasing Top

Creates a draw engine with any card that empties your hand. One of the strongest relics for exhaust-focused decks. Less useful in decks with large hands that never empty.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **[[cards/Fiend Fire]]** (CRITICAL): Exhausts hand -> empty -> draw -> play -> empty -> draw. Infinite draw engine limited only by energy and draw pile size.
- **0-cost cards**: [[cards/Bloodletting]] (gain 2 energy, hand empties), Rage (play for free, hand empties), [[cards/Inflame]]/powers (play, hand empties) all trigger additional draws.
- **[[cards/Offering]]**: Lose 6 HP but gain 2 energy + draw 3. Combined with Top, massive turns.
