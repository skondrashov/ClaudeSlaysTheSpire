# Meal Ticket

Makes shop visits double as healing stops. At low HP, pathing through shops provides 15 HP recovery in addition to purchasing options. More healing than [[relics/Burning Blood]] (6 HP) and stacks with it. Prioritize shop paths on the map when carrying Meal Ticket and low on HP.

15 HP per shop is significant -- roughly one fight's worth of damage absorption. In [[acts/Act 2]] where HP drain is the primary cause of death, Meal Ticket can extend a run by several floors if the map has shop nodes available.

LIMITATION: Only triggers at shops (1-2 per act). Cannot be relied upon as a primary healing source, but excellent supplemental healing alongside Burning Blood.
