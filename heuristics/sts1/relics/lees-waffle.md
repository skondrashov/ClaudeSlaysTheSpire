# Lee's Waffle

Immediate full heal plus permanent Max HP increase. At any HP percentage, this restores you to maximum. The +7 Max HP also raises all HP thresholds, giving more buffer for future fights.

One of the strongest shop purchases in the game. Combines immediate value of a full heal with permanent Max HP scaling. Buy it whenever affordable and HP is below 70%. At 157g it is expensive, but preventing a death from low HP is worth the entire gold reserve.
