# Incense Burner

The [[buffs/Intangible]] turn is a free turn for maximum offense -- play all attacks, use no block. Track the counter to plan around it — and remember it RESETS when it fires: after an Intangible turn, the next one is a full 6 turns away. Do not price a survival plan on a second Intangible arriving sooner. Against bosses with predictable big-hit turns ([[bosses/Hexaghost]] Inferno, [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] Hyper Beam), Intangible can negate the most dangerous attack entirely.

Best in long fights (bosses, elites) where the counter triggers 2+ times. A 12-turn fight gets 2 Intangible turns. Combined with scaling ([[cards/Rampage]], [[cards/Demon Form]]), Intangible turns enable all-offense plays that accelerate the kill.

MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Hexaghost (Intangible on Inferno = 42 damage reduced to 1). Excellent vs Bronze Automaton (Hyper Beam = 45-57 reduced to 1).
