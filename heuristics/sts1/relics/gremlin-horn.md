# Gremlin Horn

Transforms multi-enemy fights from resource-draining attrition into snowball opportunities. Against 4 Gremlins, killing 3 provides +3E and +3 cards -- enough for an entire extra turn's worth of actions.

SYNERGIES:
- **AOE damage** ([[cards/Thunderclap]], [[cards/Cleave]], [[cards/Whirlwind]]) -- weakens multiple enemies, triggering chain kills.
- **[[bosses/Slime Boss]] split** -- kills during the split phase refund energy for handling new slimes.
- **Any multi-enemy fight** -- 3 Cultists, Gremlins, Slavers, [[enemies/Centurion]]+[[enemies/Mystic]].

Always take. No downside. In single-enemy fights it does nothing harmful. In multi-enemy fights (the most common dangerous [[acts/Act 2]] encounters), it is one of the strongest relics available.
