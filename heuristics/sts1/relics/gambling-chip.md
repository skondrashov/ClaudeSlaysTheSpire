# Gambling Chip

The mulligan effect lets you find key cards ([[cards/Bash]], [[cards/Inflame]], [[cards/Shockwave]], [[cards/Whirlwind]]) on Turn 1. Consistently useful in every combat.

FIGHT-SPECIFIC MULLIGANS:
- **[[enemies/Gremlin Leader]]**: Discard block, keep attacks. Turn economy demands damage from Turn 1.
- **[[enemies/Spheric Guardian]]**: Discard block, keep [[buffs/Artifact]] strippers and attacks. DPS check.
- **Hallway fights**: Discard expensive Powers, keep Bash+ and cheap attacks.
- **[[enemies/Chosen]]/[[debuffs/Hex]]**: Discard Skills (trigger Hex adding [[cards/Dazed]]). Keep Attacks and Powers.
- **Boss fights**: Discard based on boss pattern.

SYNERGIES: Lean decks (15-17 cards, mulligan more likely to find specific answers). Cards with fight-specific value can be safely included because they can be discarded when not needed.

**SNECKO EYE SYNERGY**: When [[enemies/Snecko]] assigns expensive costs (3E) to key cards, Gambling Chip lets you discard and redraw with new cost rolls. Dramatically improves Turn 1 consistency.
