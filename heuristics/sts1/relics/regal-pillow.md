# Regal Pillow

Turns rest sites from 24 HP healing to 39 HP healing. In [[acts/Act 3]] where HP management is critical, can restore nearly half of max HP at each rest site. Makes it safer to choose Rest over Smith when HP is below threshold.

Strong utility relic that patches healing gaps. At a pre-boss rest site it can lift you from around half HP to full, comfortably clearing the 70% boss-entry threshold.
