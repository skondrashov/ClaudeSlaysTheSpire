# Bird-Faced Urn

Each Power played becomes a small heal. In Power-heavy decks (3+ Powers), adds 6+ HP healing per combat, partially offsetting the [[relics/Burning Blood]] gap. Incentivizes taking and playing Power cards.

SYNERGIES: [[relics/Mummified Hand]] (free Power plays = free healing), [[cards/Corruption]] (frees energy for Powers), multiple cheap Powers ([[cards/Inflame]] 1E, [[cards/Metallicize]] 1E) maximize triggers per fight.

Best in long fights (bosses, elites) where multiple Powers are played. Less impactful in short hallway fights where only 1 Power is played. Does not replace real healing cards ([[cards/Reaper]], Feed) but supplements them.
