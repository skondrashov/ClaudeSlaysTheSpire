# Odd Mushroom

DOUBLE-EDGED relic. Defensively, reduces damage taken when you are [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]. Offensively, REDUCES your damage output by ~17% against Vulnerable targets.

WHEN TO TAKE: Good if your deck relies on block/defense and rarely applies Vulnerable offensively. BAD if your deck relies on [[cards/Bash]]/[[cards/Thunderclap]] Vulnerable for burst damage (most [[characters/Ironclad]] decks). Consider taking the 22 HP heal (Eat option) from the Mushroom event instead if your deck is Vulnerable-dependent.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Bash+ (Vuln 3 is your primary damage amp), Thunderclap+ (mass Vuln for burst), any DPS-race fight ([[bosses/Bronze Automaton]], [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]]), [[bosses/Hexaghost]] (the ~17% damage reduction extends the fight by 1-2 turns, adding more Burns -- confirmed lethal).
