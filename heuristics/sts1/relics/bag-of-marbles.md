# Bag of Marbles

Free [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] on Turn 1 without spending energy or a card play. Enables aggressive Turn 1 plays -- every attack card deals significantly more damage before [[cards/Bash]] is even needed.

SYNERGIES: [[relics/Paper Phrog]] (1.75x damage to all enemies Turn 1 for free), [[cards/Immolate]] (28 * 1.75 = 49 AOE Turn 1), [[cards/Heavy Blade]] (Str multiplied + Vuln), any front-loaded damage strategy.

One of the best common relics. Free damage amplification on Turn 1 of every combat.
