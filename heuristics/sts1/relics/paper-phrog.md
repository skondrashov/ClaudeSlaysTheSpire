# Paper Phrog

Massively amplifies all damage against [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] targets. [[cards/Bash]] becomes a much stronger setup card. Over a multi-turn Vulnerable window (Bash+ = 3 turns), adds up to massive extra damage.

MATH (with Paper Phrog):
- Strike (6) vs Vulnerable: 10 (vs 9 without)
- [[cards/Immolate]] (28) vs Vulnerable: 49 (vs 42 without)
- [[cards/Twin Strike]] (5+5) vs Vulnerable: 17 (vs 14 without)

SYNERGIES: Bash+ (3 turns amplified Vulnerable), [[cards/Thunderclap]] (mass Vulnerable), [[relics/Bag of Marbles]] (free Turn 1 Vulnerable), any high-damage attack card.

One of the best common relics for [[characters/Ironclad]]. Since Bash+ applies Vulnerable in every fight, provides permanent damage amplification. Stronger than most uncommon relics.
