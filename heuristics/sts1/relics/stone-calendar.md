# Stone Calendar

Free 52 AOE damage with no energy or card investment in long fights. In fights that end in 4-5 turns, the relic does nothing.

KEY MATCHUPS:
- **[[enemies/Centurion]]+[[enemies/Mystic]]**: Ideal -- fight naturally lasts 7+ turns while killing Mystic through block. 52 damage lands on Centurion.
- **Boss fights**: Most last 10+ turns, so the nuke always fires. 52 free damage is significant.
- **[[enemies/Shelled Parasite]]**: 52 damage overkills through remaining [[buffs/Plated Armor]] block.
- **[[enemies/Spheric Guardian]]**: 52 damage into 65 block only removes block -- does NOT kill. Insufficient against high-block enemies.
- **Short fights**: Useless against enemies that die in 3-5 turns.

Strong in decks that expect long fights (defensive/block-heavy builds). [[debuffs/Weak]] in burst-focused decks that aim to kill quickly.
