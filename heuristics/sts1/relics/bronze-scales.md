# Bronze Scales

Passive damage every fight requiring no energy, no card plays, no setup. **Thorns damage scales with the attacker's HIT COUNT, not its attack size** — a 6-hit multi-attack eats 18 thorns in one turn while a single 30-damage slash eats 3. Against multi-hit-heavy enemies and bosses, 3 Thorns performs like a free damage card played every enemy turn.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **[[bosses/Donu and Deca]]**: Can deliver killing blows without requiring a card play.
- **Multi-hit enemies**: [[enemies/Centurion]] (10x3), [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] (6xN), [[enemies/Gremlin Leader]] minions -- each hit triggers Bronze Scales.
- **[[bosses/Hexaghost]]**: Divider and Inferno are 6-hit attacks — thorns can contribute a large share of the total damage over the fight.

Excellent as a finishing tool when enemies are at low HP. Scales with fight length. No downside.
