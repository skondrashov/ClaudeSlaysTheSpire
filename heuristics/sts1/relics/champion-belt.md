# Champion Belt

Turns [[cards/Bash]] into a dual-debuff card (3 [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] + 1 [[debuffs/Weak]]) for free. Every Bash+ play both increases your damage AND reduces incoming damage simultaneously.

SYNERGIES: Bash+ (main trigger), [[cards/Thunderclap]] (mass Vuln = mass Weak to all enemies), [[relics/Paper Phrog]] (75% Vuln + Weak = massive amplification with damage reduction).

Excellent defensive relic requiring no deck changes. Since Bash+ is played in nearly every combat, provides passive Weak application for free. Reduces the need for dedicated Weak sources ([[cards/Intimidate]], [[cards/Shockwave]], [[cards/Clothesline]]).
