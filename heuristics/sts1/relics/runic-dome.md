# Runic Dome

Almost never take. The +1 Energy is powerful, but losing intent information is devastating. Intent drives every decision: when to block, when to attack, when to play Powers, when to use potions.

THE CRITICAL PROBLEM: Some boss/elite attacks deal enough unblocked damage to kill or nearly kill you in one hit ([[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] Hyper Beam, Guardian 32-damage, [[bosses/Hexaghost]] Inferno 42 damage). Without intent, you cannot prepare your block for those hits — so without solid knowledge of every enemy's pattern, one of them lands unblocked and kills you.

PREREQUISITE: Requires memorized enemy attack patterns to use safely. Until all enemy patterns are known, skip it in favor of other boss relics.

WHEN IT COULD WORK: A deck with heavy passive block ([[cards/Metallicize]], [[cards/Barricade]] + block engine) that doesn't need to react to intents. Or once all enemy patterns are memorized and predictable from cycle position.
