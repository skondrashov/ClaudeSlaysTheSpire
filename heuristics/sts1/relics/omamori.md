# Omamori

Strong defensive relic. Curses are permanent deck pollution that clog draws. Omamori prevents 2 curses from ever entering the deck. Enables safe event choices that would otherwise add curses.

WHEN TO USE:
- Before curse-giving events (open Coffin for free relic at zero cost).
- General insurance against random curse events in Unknown rooms.
- Note: Bites (Vampires event) are NOT Curses -- Omamori does not help there.
