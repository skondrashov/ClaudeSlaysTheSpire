# Bloody Idol

Provides both economy and sustain. Every combat victory that awards gold also heals 5 HP and gives 5 bonus gold. Over a full act (8-12 gold-awarding events): 40-60 bonus gold and 40-60 HP healing.

Makes combat more sustainable (5 HP heal per fight stacks with [[relics/Burning Blood]]'s +6 = 11 HP recovered per fight). Incentivizes taking fights over avoiding them since each fight provides healing.

SYNERGIES: [[relics/Maw Bank]] (gold gain triggers heal), [[relics/Golden Idol]] (+25% gold = more frequent triggers), any relic or event that awards gold.
