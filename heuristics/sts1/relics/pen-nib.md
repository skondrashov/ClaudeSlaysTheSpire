# Pen Nib

Track the counter. Time the 10th Attack to coincide with high-damage cards ([[cards/Fiend Fire]], [[cards/Carnage]], [[cards/Headbutt]]) or [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] targets. Pen Nib + Strike = 12 damage. Pen Nib + Fiend Fire (4 cards) = 56 damage. Plan your Attacks across fights to line up the 10th hit on the right card.
