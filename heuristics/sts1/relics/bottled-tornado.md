# Bottled Tornado

Guarantees a specific Power from Turn 1 of every fight. The value depends entirely on which Power is bottled.

BEST TARGETS ([[characters/Silent]]):
1. **[[cards/After Image]]** (victory-proven) -- 1 block per card played from Turn 1.
2. **[[cards/Noxious Fumes]]** -- Passive poison from Turn 1.
3. **[[cards/Footwork]]** -- +3 Dex from Turn 1.

BEST TARGETS ([[characters/Ironclad]]):
1. **[[cards/Barricade]]** -- Block persists from Turn 1. Excellent with [[cards/Corruption]] engine.
2. **[[cards/Metallicize]]** -- Passive block every turn from Turn 1.
3. **[[cards/Inflame]]** -- +2 Str from Turn 1.

High draft priority when you have a Power that you always want Turn 1. After Image innate is game-defining; a situational Power is less impactful.
