# Emotion Chip

Effectively doubles orb passive output on turns after taking damage.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **Frost orbs + Focus**: With 5 Frost orbs at Focus 3, Emotion Chip adds 25 block at start of turn (5 x 5). Stacks with normal 25 at end of turn for 50 passive block per cycle.
- **Lightning orbs**: Double passive damage. Less impactful but meaningful in multi-enemy fights.
- **Dark orbs**: Triggers passive gain twice, accelerating scaling.

Strongest in Frost-heavy builds with Focus scaling. Rewards taking chip damage (often unavoidable) by converting it into defensive value the following turn.

LIMITATION: Only triggers if HP was lost previous turn. In fights with zero damage taken (full block discipline), provides no value. It is a recovery tool, not a proactive one.
