# Necronomicon

Doubles the first expensive Attack each turn for free. Effectively doubles damage AND secondary effects (orb channels, debuffs, draws).

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **[[cards/Barrage]] (2E base)**: Always triggers. With 5 orbs: 40 damage total (50 upgraded). Highest single-card damage output.
- **[[cards/Cold Snap]] (when 2+ cost)**: Plays twice = 12 damage + channels 2 Frost orbs.
- **Any Attack with secondary effects**: Full replay including draw/channel/debuff.

SNECKO EYE SYNERGY: Cards normally costing 0-1 can roll 2+, triggering Necronomicon. With 7 cards drawn, at least one Attack costing 2+ is very likely. Dramatically increases trigger frequency.

NECRONOMICURSE: Unplayable hand clog, cannot be removed at shops. In [[relics/Snecko Eye]] deck (7 draws), minor nuisance. In standard 5-draw, more impactful. Keep deck lean.

DECISION: Take when deck has multiple 2+ cost Attacks, or [[enemies/Snecko]] Eye is active. Plan turns so highest-value Attack is played first to receive the doubling.
