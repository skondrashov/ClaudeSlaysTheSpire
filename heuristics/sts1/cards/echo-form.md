# Echo Form

The single most powerful card in the [[characters/Defect]]'s pool. Doubles the first card played each turn.

**When to play:** As early as possible in long fights. The doubling compounds -- Turn 1 doubles 9+ cards across 10 turns. Plan for a low-pressure turn (after building Frost wall) to deploy.

**Critical mechanic -- Ethereal:** Unupgraded exhausts at end of turn if not played. If drawn on a turn where 3E cannot be spent, permanently lost.

**Upgrade priority:** Arguably the most important in the Defect pool. Removes Ethereal -- avoids permanent loss from bad draw timing.

**[[cards/Seek]] synergy:** Seek guarantees Echo Form in hand Turn 1 (or whenever needed). This Seek + Echo Form combination is the core of the Defect's most consistent winning engine.

**[[relics/Mummified Hand]] synergy:** Playing Echo Form triggers Mummified Hand, making a random card cost 0. Partially offsets 3E setup cost.

**[[bosses/Time Eater]] warning:** Echo Form copies count toward 12-card counter. Each doubled card = 2 plays counted. Effective budget is ~6 real card plays per cycle.

**[[relics/Bottled Lightning]] interaction:** Can make Echo Form innate, but Bottled Lightning on Seek is often better (Seek adapts to specific fights).
