# Malaise

[[characters/Silent]]'s strongest boss debuff. Permanent [[buffs/Strength]] reduction + [[debuffs/Weak]].

**Critical -- X-cost energy trap:** Malaise automatically uses ALL remaining energy. You CANNOT specify X manually. Play block cards and other spells FIRST, then Malaise LAST with only the desired energy remaining — playing it early can consume the energy your block plan needed.

**When to play:** Best in boss fights (the longer the fight, the more the permanent Str reduction pays off), but the exhaust resets BETWEEN fights — using Malaise in a hallway fight costs the boss fight nothing. The only real cost is the energy spent this turn. In a hard hallway fight, a small-X Malaise that prevents real damage is a fine play. Play LAST after all other non-X-cost cards. Do NOT use for [[buffs/Artifact]] stripping.

**Synergies:** High energy decks (more energy = larger X = bigger debuff), [[cards/Wraith Form]] (reduces damage post-[[buffs/Intangible]]), [[cards/Shiv]] burst (neuters offense while Shiv damage scales).

**Draft priority:** High for boss fights. One of the best Silent cards against any single-target boss.
