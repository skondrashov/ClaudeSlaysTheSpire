# Aggregate

Energy burst card. Generates energy based on cards remaining in draw pile.

**When to play:** Play at the START of your turn when the draw pile is largest. Every 4 cards drawn or exhausted before playing reduces gain by 1. Do NOT play [[cards/Coolheaded]] or [[cards/Compile Driver]] before Aggregate.

**Upgrade priority:** Significant. Divisor from 4 to 3. With 12 cards: 3E vs 4E.

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] synergy:** Extra energy fuels the extra cards drawn. When Snecko randomizes costs high, Aggregate provides energy to play them anyway.

**Timing:** Play first each turn (before other draw effects reduce the draw pile).
