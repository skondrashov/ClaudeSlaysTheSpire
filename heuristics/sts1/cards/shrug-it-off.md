# Shrug It Off

Block + draw. Replaces itself while providing defense.

**When to play:** Always. The draw means you get block without losing card advantage. Play early in the turn when possible -- the drawn card may change your plan.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium-high. 8->11 block. After upgrading, always use 11 in calculations, not the base 8. Miscalculating SIO+ block as 8 instead of 11 costs 3 block per play -- this exact error has been the survival margin in a boss fight.

**Matchups:** Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], do NOT play (Skill triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]).
