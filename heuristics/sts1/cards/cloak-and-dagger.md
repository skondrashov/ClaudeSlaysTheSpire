# Cloak and Dagger

Dual-purpose: block + damage via [[cards/Shiv]] generation. Shivs benefit from [[cards/Accuracy]], [[buffs/Strength]], and other modifiers.

**Upgrade priority:** High when Guardian is the boss. The upgrade from 1 Shiv to 2 Shivs adds 9+ damage per play (with Accuracy 4 + [[relics/Vajra]] 1) — against Guardian that extra Shiv can be the difference in reaching the [[buffs/Mode Shift]] threshold on the planned turn (see [[layer:heuristics, bosses/The Guardian]]).

**Interactions:** Accuracy (each Shiv gets full bonus), [[cards/After Image]] (each Shiv = +1 block, making upgraded CaD effectively 8+ block), [[relics/Dead Branch]] (Shivs exhaust, triggering random card generation), [[cards/Wraith Form]] Dex penalty (at negative Dex, block drops to 0 but Shiv damage unaffected).

**[[buffs/Sharp Hide]] warning:** Shivs are Attack cards and trigger Guardian Sharp Hide (3 damage per Shiv). During Defensive Mode, each Shiv costs 3 HP.

**Draft priority:** High in Shiv decks. Both offense and defense in a single 1E play.
