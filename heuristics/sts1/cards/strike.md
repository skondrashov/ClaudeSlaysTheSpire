# Strike

Baseline damage card that gets outclassed quickly.

**When to play:** Early floors before better attacks are available. Safe against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] (Attack card).

**Decision points:** Is there a better attack to play instead? Could this energy be spent on block or setup?

**Upgrade priority:** Low. 6->9 damage (13 with [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]). Only worth mass-upgrading via [[events/Ancient Writing]] event.
