# After Image

Flat block per card played. Not affected by [[buffs/Dexterity]] or [[debuffs/Frail]].

**When to play:** Turn 1 or Turn 2 of every fight. Every turn it sits unplayed is block lost. Before the Collector's Turn 4 debuff -- must be active before triple debuff lands. Priority over other Powers except [[cards/Noxious Fumes]].

**Key mechanic:** Uniquely valuable when Frail is active (only block source at full value) and when Dexterity is negative from [[cards/Wraith Form]] (only block source that still works at -5 Dex or worse).

**Combat value:** 5 cards/turn = 5 free block/turn. 8 cards/turn = 8. Over 10 turns: 50-80 total block for 1E investment. Shivs from [[cards/Cloak and Dagger]] trigger After Image (block + damage simultaneously).

**[[relics/Bottled Tornado]] synergy:** Making After Image innate guarantees Turn 1 activation every combat. Top pick for Bottled Tornado in [[characters/Silent]] decks.

**Setup priority in boss fights:** 1. Noxious Fumes+ (win condition), 2. After Image (survival), 3. [[cards/Footwork]] (block multiplier).
