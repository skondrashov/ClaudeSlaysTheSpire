# Whirlwind

AOE that scales with energy. Uses ALL remaining energy.

**When to play:** Play LAST -- uses all remaining energy automatically. You CANNOT specify X manually. To control X, spend energy on other cards first, then Whirlwind last.

**Synergies:** Extra energy ([[relics/Lantern]], [[cards/Seeing Red]], [[cards/Berserk]]), [[buffs/Strength]] (each hit gains bonus).

**Upgrade priority:** Significant. 5->8 per hit.

**Matchups:** [[bosses/Slime Boss]] split -- hits both slimes. Sentries -- 15 damage to all 3. [[enemies/Gremlin Leader]] -- clears summoned gremlins.

**Anti-synergy:** [[enemies/Snake Plant]] [[buffs/Malleable]] -- each hit triggers Malleable, granting increasing block. Do NOT use against enemies with Malleable. Use single large hits instead.
