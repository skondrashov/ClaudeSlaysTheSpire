# Impervious

Best single-card block in the game. One-time use (Exhaust).

**When to play:** Save for the biggest incoming hit of the fight. Using 30-40 block on a 16-damage turn is wasteful -- Defends handle that. Calculate: "If I don't play Impervious now, will I die? If yes, play it. If no, save it."

**Timing discipline:** NEVER play on Guardian's [[buffs/Sharp Hide]] attacks (9-16 damage) unless you would die. Save for the 32-damage single hit. Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], do NOT play (Skill triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]).

**Upgrade priority:** High. 30->40 block. The extra 10 block is the difference between absorbing a big hit cleanly and taking chip.

**Matchups:** [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] -- THE most important card. Hyper Beam deals 45-57 damage. Save for Hyper Beam turns. Guardian -- absorbs the 32-damage attack completely (upgraded takes 0). [[enemies/Gremlin Leader]] -- save for Turn 4-5 when Rally-scaled damage reaches 34+. Collector -- critical for post-STRONG_DEBUFF turns (37-50+ incoming). [[encounters/Transient]] -- save for Turn 3+ (50+ damage), NOT Turn 1. [[enemies/Snake Plant]] -- absorbs 9x3=27 multi-hit. [[bosses/Hexaghost]] -- save for Divider (Turn 2) or Inferno. In a 16-card deck, there is a ~31% chance Impervious is NOT in your Turn 2 hand. [[cards/Headbutt]] can place Impervious on top of draw pile the turn before a predicted Divider/Inferno. If Impervious is the only plan for a specific high-damage turn, the plan is unreliable -- need backup block ([[debuffs/Weak]], [[cards/Flame Barrier]], multiple Defends).

**Synergies:** [[relics/Calipers]] (retains 25 block next turn upgraded), [[cards/Barricade]] (retained permanently), [[cards/Spot Weakness]] (use the safe turn to set up [[buffs/Strength]]).

**Escalating-damage fights:** Against enemies with increasing damage per turn ([[enemies/Book of Stabbing]], [[encounters/Transient]]), save Impervious for the highest-damage turn you can survive until. Book of Stabbing Multi Stab goes 12, 18, 24, 30, 36, 42, 48... Using Impervious on Turn 3 (18 damage) saves only 12 effective HP vs Defends. Using it on Turn 8 (36+ damage) saves 15+. The card exhausts -- it only fires once. Save it for the turn where normal block cards cannot cover the gap.
