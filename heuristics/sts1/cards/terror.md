# Terror

99 [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] = permanent 50% more damage from ALL attacks for the rest of combat.

**When to play:** Play on the first turn you can spare 1E (0E upgraded), then focus on damage. Earlier application = more total bonus damage. Against [[bosses/Awakened One]]: Terror in Phase 1 accelerates kill before Curiosity Str accumulates. Terror in Phase 2 enables the kill without Powers.

**Boss fight value:** Against bosses with 250-300+ HP, the 50% increase saves 3-5 turns of combat, translating to 3-5 fewer turns of incoming damage.

**[[relics/Dead Branch]] synergy:** Terror exhausts, triggering Dead Branch for a random card -- "free" proc on top of devastating debuff.

**Bane synergy:** Bane deals double on Poisoned. With Terror's Vuln 99: (base * 2) * 1.5 = 3x effective damage.

**Single-target limitation:** Only applies to one enemy. Prioritize the highest-HP target.

**Upgrade priority:** Excellent. 1E->0E makes it a free permanent 50% damage boost.
