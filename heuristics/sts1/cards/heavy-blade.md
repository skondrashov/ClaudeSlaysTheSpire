# Heavy Blade

High-cost [[buffs/Strength]] multiplier. Worthless without Strength, game-winning with it.

**When to play:** Only take when the deck has Strength sources ([[relics/Vajra]], [[cards/Inflame]], [[cards/Spot Weakness]], [[cards/Limit Break]], [[cards/Demon Form]]). At 0 Strength, just 14 damage for 2E (terrible). At Str 6+, highest damage-per-play available.

**Upgrade priority:** High in Strength decks. 3x to 5x Strength multiplier is transformative. At Str 6: 32 -> 44 damage.

**Damage formula (VERIFY UPGRADE STATUS):** Heavy Blade = 14 + Str * 3. Heavy Blade+ = 14 + Str * 5. The multiplier difference is massive: at Str 9, unupgraded deals 41 but upgraded deals 59 -- an 18-damage gap. Always check the + suffix before calculating.

**Synergies:** Demon Form+ (by turn 4 at +12 Str, deals 74 damage; by turn 6 at +18 Str, deals 104), Limit Break+ (doubling Str before Heavy Blade = exponential), [[cards/Double Tap]] (plays twice for 64+ damage), Spot Weakness (+3 Str becomes +9/+15 damage), [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (applies to full total).

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] synergy:** 25% chance of costing 0E under Confusion. Free 30-94 damage play.

**Matchups:** Excellent in boss fights where Strength has time to build. Less effective in quick hallway fights where 2E without setup is too slow.

**[[relics/Pen Nib]] transition one-shot:** A Pen-Nib-doubled Heavy Blade, under [[debuffs/Vulnerable]], at high Strength is a phase-break line against transition bosses. At Str 16, Heavy Blade+ is 14 + 16*5 = 94; Pen Nib doubles to ~188 base, and Vulnerable (1.5x) pushes a single hit past 150 actual damage on a fresh target. Fired on the crossing turn of a HP-gated boss ([[bosses/The Champ]]'s 50% Anger line, any "below X% → new phase" enemy), it can carry the boss so deep past the threshold in one turn that the phase-2 scaling (Anger, Execute) never comes online — you skip the dangerous phase entirely instead of racing it. Line up the Pen Nib 10th-attack counter and the Vulnerable application to land on the same turn you intend to cross.
