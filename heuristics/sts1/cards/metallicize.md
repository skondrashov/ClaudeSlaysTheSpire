# Metallicize

Passive block every turn. The block equivalent of [[buffs/Strength]].

**When to play:** Play as early as possible in long fights. Over 10 turns: 30 free block. Two copies provide 6 block/turn. Powers do NOT trigger [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] [[buffs/Enrage]].

**Key interaction:** Metallicize block is NOT affected by [[debuffs/Frail]]. Frail only reduces block from cards. Metallicize's end-of-turn block is flat regardless of debuffs -- especially valuable against [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]] (Frail 5).

**Synergies:** [[cards/Iron Wave]] (passive + card block = solid defense), second Metallicize (6-7 block/turn is exceptional), [[cards/Barricade]] (Metallicize block carries over and stacks -- with Barricade + 2x Metallicize, passive alone reaches 21 by Turn 3, 35 by Turn 5).

**Matchups:** Guardian -- handles many attack patterns with only 1 Defend needed. [[bosses/Hexaghost]] -- critical for the 13-turn fight. Byrds -- essential for long attrition. Spheric Guardian -- Frail-immune block.
