# Dropkick

If target is [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]: gain 1 energy + draw 1 card. Effectively FREE when target is Vulnerable.

**When to play:** Is the target Vulnerable? If yes, always play -- it is free damage + cycling. If no, it is a terrible card (5/8 damage, no benefit). Do NOT play against non-Vulnerable targets.

**Upgrade priority:** 5->8 damage. With Vulnerable: 7->12 damage. The free damage is significantly higher upgraded.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Bash]] (3 turns Vulnerable = 3 free Dropkick plays), [[cards/Thunderclap]] (mass Vulnerable = Dropkick any target).

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] -- Dropkick+ dealt 12 Vuln damage three times as free actions = 36 total for 0 net energy. [[bosses/Slime Boss]] pre-split -- excellent with Bash+ providing 3 turns of Vulnerable. Post-split -- requires re-applying Vulnerable to each slime individually.
