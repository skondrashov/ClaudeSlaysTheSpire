# Spot Weakness

Conditional [[buffs/Strength]] gain. Only works when enemy's current intent is an Attack.

**When to play:** Only play when enemy intent is an Attack. On non-attack turns, this does nothing and wastes 1 energy. Against enemies where intent is hidden ([[relics/Runic Dome]]), this is a gamble.

**Upgrade priority:** Worth upgrading for scaling fights. +3 -> +4 STR.

**Synergies:** Multi-hit attacks ([[cards/Pummel]], [[cards/Twin Strike]], [[cards/Whirlwind]] -- Strength applies per hit), [[cards/Heavy Blade]] (+3 Str becomes +9/+15 due to multiplier), [[cards/Limit Break]] (doubling after Spot Weakness gives massive scaling), long fights where Strength compounds.

**Matchups:** Excellent vs Guardian (predictable attack turns, long fight). Excellent vs [[bosses/Hexaghost]] (frequent attack turns). Hexaghost Turn 2 timing: playing it alongside [[cards/Inflame]] leaves only 1E for block against ~30 damage. Consider playing on Turn 3+ instead. Marginally useful vs [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]] (alternates attack/defend).

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] synergy:** Even at 2-3E cost, the permanent Str gain is often worth it in boss fights.
