# Fiend Fire

Exhausts ALL other cards in hand. Deals 7 damage per card exhausted to a single target.

**When to play:** When burst will kill the target, when remaining cards are expendable, or on free turns. Turns [[cards/Slimed]]/Decay/[[cards/Dazed]]/Burn cards into 7 damage each. After playing, your hand is EMPTY. Only play when: (1) the burst kills, (2) it is a free turn, or (3) remaining cards are expendable.

**Synergies:** Status/Curse cards (become 7+ damage instead of dead draws), [[buffs/Strength]] (each card's damage scales), [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (each hit gets 50% bonus), [[relics/Unceasing Top]] (empties hand, triggers Top draw engine), [[potions/Snecko Oil]] (randomize costs before Fiend Fire), [[cards/Feel No Pain]] (Fiend Fire self-exhausts too, so total FNP triggers = other cards + 1. With N other cards in hand: FNP block = (N+1) x 3).

**Before playing: categorize your hand.** Fiend Fire exhausts EVERYTHING else in hand, permanently. Sort every card into (1) truly expendable -- Strikes, status cards, duplicate copies -- and (2) needed later -- survival cards ([[cards/Impervious]], [[cards/Shockwave]], [[cards/Dark Shackles]]), [[debuffs/Weak]] sources ([[cards/Intimidate]]), debuffs held for a later phase, your remaining block density. If ANY category-2 card is in hand, do NOT play Fiend Fire: play that card first or wait for a turn where only expendable cards remain. The card-efficiency appeal (2 cards for 60+ damage) is an illusion if it costs your only Weak source or emergency defense. One exception: at critically low HP, exhausting everything for survival is correct -- survival > future value.
- **Phased bosses ([[bosses/The Champ]], [[bosses/Awakened One]]):** debuffs ([[cards/Disarm]], Shockwave, [[cards/Exhume]]) held for the post-transition phase are category 2 — exhausted, they are gone forever and cannot be re-applied in Phase 2. Verify all Phase 2 cards are played or safely in the draw/discard pile before firing.
- **Long fights ([[bosses/Time Eater]], any 10+ turn fight):** cards that seem expendable on Turn 2 become critical by Turn 10 when the enemy has scaled (+4-6 Strength from [[debuffs/Time Warp]] stacks). Categorize against the whole fight, not the current turn.
- **[[relics/Runic Pyramid]]:** the retained hand grows, so Fiend Fire exhausts MORE cards -- including everything being held for later. Retained cards never leave the hand naturally: play category-2 cards first or accept that Fiend Fire cannot be used that turn.

**Matchups:** [[bosses/Slime Boss]] -- burst + Slimed exhaust. [[enemies/Chosen]] -- only use when burst will kill or exhausted cards are expendable. If Chosen survives, remaining turns have zero block sources.

**[[buffs/Thorns]] WARNING — LETHAL:** Fiend Fire hits once per exhausted card. Against [[enemies/Spiker]] or any enemy with Thorns, each hit triggers Thorns separately. 4 exhausted cards at Thorns 9 = 36 self-damage. **NEVER use Fiend Fire against Thorns enemies.** Use single-hit attacks ([[cards/Heavy Blade]], [[cards/Carnage]]) instead. Fiend Fire into Thorns 9 deals enough self-damage to kill you outright.

**Guardian warning:** Fiend Fire as a primary engine drains the deck unsustainably in 12-14 turn fights. Use on free turns for burst, do not plan the deck around it as sole damage engine.

**Index shifting danger:** Always reference Fiend Fire by name rather than index number. Earlier card plays can shift indices causing wrong cards to be played.
