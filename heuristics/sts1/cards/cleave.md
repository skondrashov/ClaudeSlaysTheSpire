# Cleave

Cheap AOE damage to all enemies. Does NOT apply [[debuffs/Vulnerable]].

**When to play:** Multi-enemy fights for raw AOE damage. Good on free turns (turn 1 of [[enemies/Cultist]] fights) to soften all targets.

**Decision points:** Cleave vs [[cards/Thunderclap]] -- Thunderclap applies Vulnerable but deals less damage. Usually Thunderclap is better for the Vulnerable.
