# Finisher

Deals damage multiplied by number of Attacks played this turn. Shivs count as Attacks.

**When to play:** Play as the LAST Attack card of the turn. After 4 Shivs: 4 * (6 + Str) damage for 1E. With [[cards/Blade Dance]] (3 Shivs) + 1 extra [[cards/Shiv]]: excellent burst.

**Synergies:** Blade Dance (creates 3 Shivs that build the Attack count), [[cards/Phantasmal Killer]] (Finisher is an Attack -- Double Damage doubles its total output), [[cards/Accuracy]] (enables Shiv plays that build the count).

**Draft priority:** Mid-to-high in Shiv decks. Without Shiv support, deals only 6-12 damage (1-2 attacks before it) -- mediocre.
