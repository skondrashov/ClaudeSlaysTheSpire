# Sweeping Beam

The [[characters/Defect]]'s best early AOE option. Hits all enemies and replaces itself with a draw.

**When to play:** Always in multi-enemy fights for the AOE. In single-enemy fights, slightly below Strike in damage but the draw makes it net-positive. Safe against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] as an Attack.

**Early Defect priority:** High. Addresses the Attack deficit and provides AOE damage, critical for [[acts/Act 1]] [[bosses/Slime Boss]] and [[acts/Act 2]] multi-enemy fights.

**Upgrade priority:** 6->9 damage AOE. Solid improvement.
