# Beam Cell

A 0-cost [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] applicator. The damage is negligible -- the Vulnerable application is the entire value.

**When to play:** Play before [[cards/Barrage]] or other damage cards to maximize Vulnerable amplification. In short fights, even 1 turn of Vulnerable on Barrage+ with 5 orbs adds 17 bonus damage.

**Upgrade priority:** The extra Vulnerable turn (1->2) is significant, keeping enemies Vulnerable longer.
