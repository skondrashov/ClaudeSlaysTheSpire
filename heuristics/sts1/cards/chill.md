# Chill

In multi-enemy fights (3 Shapes, Darklings, [[encounters/Gremlin Gang]]), channels 3 Frost orbs for 0E -- enormous defensive burst.

**When to play:** Play immediately on Turn 1 of multi-enemy fights for maximum Frost generation. In single-enemy fights, play whenever a Frost orb is needed. Exhausts after use.

**Upgrade priority:** Gains Innate. Useful if already in the deck -- guarantees Turn 1 availability.
