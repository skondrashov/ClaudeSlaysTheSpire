# Barrage

[[characters/Defect]]'s primary scaling Attack. Damage scales directly with channeled orbs.

**When to play:** After filling orb slots. With 1 orb: 4 damage for 2E (terrible). With 5 orbs: 20/30 damage (excellent). Always channel orbs before playing Barrage.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 4->6 per orb. At 5 orbs: 20->30 damage (50% increase).

**[[relics/The Boot]] interaction:** Each per-orb hit is separate. The Boot bumps unupgraded hits from 4 to 5. With 5 orbs and The Boot: 25 instead of 20.

**[[cards/Echo Form]] synergy:** Echo Form plays Barrage twice. With 3 orbs: 36 total damage for one card play.

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] synergy:** Can randomize to 0E -- free 20+ damage. Even at 1E, well above rate.

**[[relics/Necronomicon]] synergy:** At 2+ energy, Necronomicon doubles it. With 5 Frost orbs: 40 damage from one card play.

**Vulnerability scaling:** With [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] on target and 5 orbs, Barrage+ deals 45 damage. Primary burst source in first Defect victory.

**Prediction error warning:** Actual values are 4/6 per orb (not 5/7). The Boot bumping may cause confusion.

**Early priority:** Moderate. [[debuffs/Weak]] early with empty slots. Strong once [[cards/Capacitor]]/[[cards/Coolheaded]] fill the board.
