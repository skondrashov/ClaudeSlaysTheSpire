# Corruption

All Skills become free but one-time-use. Extremely powerful but fundamentally changes deck economy.

**Setup cost trap:** At 3E unupgraded, Turn 1 leaves ZERO energy for blocking. Against multi-enemy fights, that leaves you taking 20+ unblocked damage, enough to kill you. Safe to play when: (1) enemy is buffing/defending, (2) you have 4+ energy, (3) HP absorbs the hit, (4) upgraded to 2E.

**Upgrade priority:** HIGH. 3E->2E makes the difference between blocking and not on the setup turn.

**Decision points:** After Corruption, every Skill exhausts -- plan for a finite number of block plays. Before playing, count remaining Skills and estimate remaining fight turns. If (Skills) < (turns needing block), do NOT play.

**Synergies:** [[relics/Dead Branch]] (every exhausted Skill generates a random card -- strongest combo in the game), [[cards/Feel No Pain]] (3 block per exhaust makes Corruption safe), [[cards/Dark Embrace]] (draw per exhaust), [[cards/Second Wind]] (mass exhaust for block), [[cards/Barricade]] (accumulated block persists), [[cards/Sentinel]] (strictly energy-positive under Corruption: 0E play, block, plus the 2E exhaust refund — sequence it FIRST on engine turns to fund the rest).

**Without FNP (same-seed comparison, single seed):** Corruption without FNP, completed by [[cards/Barricade]] as the block accumulator, has carried Act 1 and Act 2 including the Act 2 boss in a controlled same-seed comparison — the engine is real, not just Act 1 economics. Scope limits, both observed directly: the engine's uptime is ~4-7 turns — in a ~20-card deck the Skill pool empties about 3 engine turns in (max block collapses once the Skills are exhausted), so plan the kill BEFORE the pool runs out. [[debuffs/Frail]] taxes ~30% of all engine block (per-card round-down — many small blocks are the worst Frail victim), BUT volume survives the tax where low-block-ceiling decks cap out under it: free-Skill volume has zeroed multi-hit turns through Frail that kill 12-block-ceiling decks outright. Attack-led lines still beat the engine in block-on-block mirrors ([[enemies/Spheric Guardian]]) — that loss held even when the engine deck played attack-led.

**Guardian warning:** Guardian fight lasts 12-14+ turns. Corruption exhausts ALL block Skills. Without Dead Branch or Feel No Pain, do NOT play against Guardian.

**Victory engine:** Barricade + Corruption + FNP + [[cards/Entrench]] + [[cards/Body Slam]] defeated [[bosses/Time Eater]]. Every Skill under Corruption generates FNP block AND exhausts free. Second Wind+ under Corruption can exhaust Normality curse permanently.
