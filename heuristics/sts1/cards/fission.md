# Fission

Critical upgrade difference between removing orbs (unupgraded) and evoking them (upgraded).

**Unupgraded (BAD):** REMOVES orbs WITHOUT evoking. Orbs vanish -- no Frost block, no Lightning damage, no Dark burst. Destroys orb-based defense for Energy and draws that may not compensate. Do NOT play when Frost orbs are providing critical block.

**Upgraded (EXCELLENT):** EVOKES all orbs, triggering effects. With 5 orbs: 5 Frost evokes (25 block), 5 Energy, 5 draws. Massive defensive burst AND enables playing drawn cards with gained energy.

**Upgrade priority:** Extremely high -- top-3 [[characters/Defect]] upgrade target. The difference between removing and evoking is the difference between destroying your own defense and generating a massive burst turn.

**Decision points:** At 0 orbs, does nothing. At 1 orb, minimal. Shines at 3+ orbs. Fission+ is a burst finisher when evoke effects + Energy/draws enable killing or massive block.
