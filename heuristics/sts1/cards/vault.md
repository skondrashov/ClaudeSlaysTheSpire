# Vault

Extra turn. Extremely powerful but expensive. One use per combat (Exhausts).

**When to play:** On the highest-impact turn possible. Best on safe turns (enemy buffing/debuffing) -- the extra turn lets you deal massive damage before the enemy acts again.

**Upgrade priority:** Very high. 3E->2E makes Vault playable alongside other cards on the same turn. At 3E, Vault often consumes your entire energy budget.

**Extra turn value:** The additional turn provides its own draw + energy. Effectively doubles your output for one cycle -- critical for finishing bosses or surviving dangerous phases.

**Decision points:** Use on the turn where doubling your output matters most. Often this is the turn before a boss's big attack, or on a free turn where you can set up and then burst with two full turns of attacks.
