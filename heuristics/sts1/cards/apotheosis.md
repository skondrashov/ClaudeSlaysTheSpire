# Apotheosis

Mass upgrade for the entire deck. The single most impactful card when upgrades are scarce.

**When to play:** Play on a free turn (enemy buffing/debuffing) early in a fight. The earlier it is played, the more turns benefit. In boss fights, Turn 1-2 is ideal. Under [[cards/Corruption]], costs 0E and exhausts -- best possible outcome.

**Decision points:** With [[relics/Fusion Hammer]] (cannot Smith at rest sites), Apotheosis is the ONLY way to upgrade cards. It upgrades everything: [[cards/Barricade]] 3E->2E, Corruption 3E->2E, [[cards/Impervious]] 30->40, [[cards/Feel No Pain]] 3->4, [[cards/Immolate]] 21->28, all simultaneously.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Fusion]] Hammer (the #1 synergy), Corruption (becomes 0E), Feel No Pain (exhaust under Corruption triggers block).

**Source:** Colorless card from events (Sensory Stone "Recall" option). Cannot be obtained from normal card rewards.
