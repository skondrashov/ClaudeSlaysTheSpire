# Berserk

DANGEROUS. Self-[[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (take 50% more damage) for 2 turns. Permanent +1 energy.

**When to play:** ONLY when enemies are not attacking for the next 2 turns AND you can survive the Vulnerable penalty afterward. In practice, almost never safe.

**Decision points:** Can you survive 2 turns of taking 50% more damage from ALL enemies? Against multiple enemies, the combined boosted damage is often lethal. Against bosses at low HP, the self-Vulnerable is suicidal. The energy gain does not matter if you die.

**Matchups:** [[bosses/Hexaghost]] -- NEVER play. Burns + self-damage + Vulnerable is a death spiral. Multi-enemy fights -- NEVER play. Combined 50% boosted damage from all enemies is overwhelming.
