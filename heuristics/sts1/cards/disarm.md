# Disarm

Permanent [[buffs/Strength]] reduction. Extremely powerful against scaling enemies.

**When to play:** Save for the enemy with the most dangerous Strength scaling. Against multi-hit enemies, the value compounds (5-hit attack loses 10 total damage).

**Decision points:** Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], do NOT play (Skill triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]). Against [[enemies/Chosen]], playing Disarm adds a [[cards/Dazed]] via [[debuffs/Hex]] -- weigh permanent -2 Str against deck clog. If fight lasts 4+ more turns, the Str reduction is usually worth one Dazed.

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Gremlin Leader]] -- reduces Rally effectiveness. [[bosses/Hexaghost]] -- permanently reduces all attacks including each Inferno hit. Slavers -- play on the hardest-hitting Slaver. Collector -- play Turn 1 for maximum cumulative value. [[encounters/Transient]] -- essential for survival; two Disarms makes the fight trivial.

**[[bosses/Slime Boss]]:** Disarm applied pre-split does NOT carry over to split slimes. Play post-split on the higher-damage slime.

**[[buffs/Artifact]] interaction:** Against enemies with Artifact, Disarm strips 1 Artifact instead of applying -2 Str.

**[[bosses/The Champ]]:** Disarm applied before Anger is completely wasted (Anger cleanses all debuffs). Hold Disarm for Phase 2 -- the -2 Str reduction is critical against post-Anger +6/+9/+12 Str. Protect Disarm from [[cards/Fiend Fire]] while holding it (see [[layer:heuristics, cards/Fiend Fire]]).
