# Uppercut

Applies BOTH [[debuffs/Weak]] and [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] in one card. The best single-card [[buffs/Artifact]] stripper in the [[characters/Ironclad]] pool.

**When to play:** Against single targets where both debuffs matter. Against Artifact enemies (strips 2 charges). Upgraded version applies 2 turns of each.

**Decision points:** At 2E, is dual-debuff worth the cost? Against [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]] (3 Artifact), strips 2 charges efficiently. Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], safe (Attack card).

**Upgrade priority:** High against Artifact enemies. Extends debuff duration to 2 turns each -- doubles value.

**Synergies:** Artifact stripping (each debuff consumes 1 charge separately), [[cards/Spot Weakness]] (Vulnerable amplifies [[buffs/Strength]]-boosted attacks), [[cards/Demon Form]] (sustained Vulnerability keeps damage high as Strength ramps).

**Matchups:** Spheric Guardian -- strips 2 of 3 Artifact charges in one play.
