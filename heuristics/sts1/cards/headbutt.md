# Headbutt

Put a card from your discard pile on top of your draw pile. Strongest with predictable enemy patterns.

**When to play:** When you have a high-value card in discard you need next turn. Check your discard pile -- what do you need next turn given enemy intent?

**Upgrade priority:** High if Headbutt is a regular play. 9->12 damage. With [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]: 13->18. The extra damage can be the difference between a kill and leaving an enemy at sliver HP for more turns.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Rampage]] (top-deck to keep scaling chain unbroken), [[cards/Carnage]] (guarantee 28-42 next turn), Feed (guarantee kill shot), [[cards/Bash]] (refresh Vulnerable on demand), [[cards/Immolate]] (top-deck for guaranteed 28+ AOE), [[cards/Heavy Blade]] (top-deck for guaranteed finisher).

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] interaction:** Top-decked cards receive a new random cost when drawn. Top-deck a 2E card and it has 25% chance of being free next turn.

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Lagavulin]] -- top Bash+ to refresh Vulnerable. Guardian -- guarantee burst for [[buffs/Mode Shift]] phases. [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] -- top-deck Heavy Blade for post-Hyper Beam stun turn. [[bosses/Donu and Deca]] -- top-deck Immolate+ repeatedly.
