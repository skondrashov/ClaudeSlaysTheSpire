# Sword Boomerang

Hits random enemies 3-4 times for 3 damage each. Targets are random across all enemies.

**When to play:** In single-enemy fights, all hits land on the same target. With high [[buffs/Strength]], each hit gains +Str damage. At +5 Str: (3+5) x 3 = 24 for 1E. Does not Exhaust, so it can be played every cycle.

**Decision points:** At base (3x3 = 9 for 1E), mediocre. Becomes strong at Strength +3 or higher. Against multiple enemies, random targeting makes it unreliable for focus-firing a priority target.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Inflame]]/[[cards/Demon Form]]/[[relics/Brimstone]] (Strength multiplied across 3-4 hits), [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (1.5x on each hit), [[relics/Pen Nib]].

**Anti-synergy:** [[enemies/Snake Plant]] [[buffs/Malleable]] -- each hit triggers Malleable, granting increasing block. Avoid against enemies with Malleable. Random targeting in multi-enemy fights is unreliable.
