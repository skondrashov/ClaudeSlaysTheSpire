# Demon Form

Long-fight scaling engine. Provides compounding damage over many turns.

**When to play:** Turn 1 of boss fights. Turn 1 of any fight where the enemy buffs (free turn). NEVER play when at low HP and facing immediate lethal damage next turn -- spending 3E on future scaling when you need block NOW is how decks die.

**Decision points:** 3E cost means the setup turn produces ZERO block and ZERO damage. Needs 2-3 turns after playing to meaningfully impact outcome. Useless when entering a fight at low HP vs fast-scaling enemies.

**[[cards/Reaper]] synergy warning:** Demon Form as the ONLY [[buffs/Strength]] source makes Reaper healing too slow in early turns. If Reaper is in the deck, pair Demon Form with a front-loaded Strength source ([[cards/Inflame]], [[cards/Spot Weakness]]) so Reaper provides meaningful healing from the first play.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Heavy Blade]] (5x Strength bonus -- at +12 Str, Heavy Blade+ deals 74 damage), [[cards/Limit Break]] (exponential Strength growth), [[relics/Shuriken]] + [[cards/Anger]] (combined Str from both sources), [[cards/Evolve]] (maintains hand quality while Demon Form shortens fights).

**Matchups:** [[bosses/The Champ]] -- needs to be played Turn 1 to accumulate enough before Execute. Dominates 10-15 turn boss fights.

**Deck building note:** If Demon Form is primary scaling, the deck still needs fast burst for emergency hallway fights at low HP.
