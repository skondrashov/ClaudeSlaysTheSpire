# Body Slam

Converts block into damage. Scales with every block source in the deck — and it is a normal Attack on top: [[buffs/Strength]] adds to the hit and [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] multiplies it, so damage = (block + Str) x1.5 on a Vulnerable target. Include both in kill math.

**When to play:** With [[cards/Barricade]]. Body Slam+ costs 0E -- a completely free attack every cycle. With Barricade accumulating 100+ block, deals 100+ damage for 0E. Without Barricade, block resets each turn, limiting damage to 10-20.

**Upgrade priority:** CRITICAL. Body Slam+ costs 0E (from 1E). One of the most impactful upgrades because it costs nothing and leaves full energy for block generation.

**Synergies:** Barricade (block persists = damage grows every turn), [[cards/Entrench]] (doubling block doubles Body Slam damage), [[cards/Impervious]] (30-40 block = 30-40 free damage), [[cards/Corruption]] + [[cards/Feel No Pain]] (exhausted Skills add block that Body Slam converts), [[cards/Metallicize]] (passive block accumulation converted to damage).

**Anti-synergies:** Decks without Barricade (block resets = low damage), [[debuffs/Frail]]-heavy fights.

**[[acts/Act 1]] tier:** LOW. Do not take in Act 1 unless Barricade is already in the deck.
