# Accuracy

Permanent Power adding flat damage to every [[cards/Shiv]]. The most important card in Shiv-based [[characters/Silent]] decks.

**When to play:** Turn 1 setup in every fight. The second copy is also excellent -- going from +4 to +8 per Shiv doubles offensive value of every Shiv-generating card.

**Saturation:** the engine plateaus around 2 copies. Once Shivs one-shot hallway enemies (~14+ each), a third or fourth Accuracy adds almost nothing — overkill damage is wasted. Past that point, discretionary picks, shop slots, and upgrades belong to whatever gap the deck has named (usually block scaling: [[cards/Footwork]], [[cards/After Image]]), not to more engine depth.

**Damage math:** With 2x Accuracy (+8) and Str 11: Shiv = 4 + 8 + 11 = 23 per 0-cost Shiv. [[cards/Blade Dance]] (1E) = 3 Shivs = 69 damage. With [[cards/Phantasmal Killer]]: 46 per Shiv, 138 from 3 Shivs.

**Synergies:** Blade Dance (3 Shivs for 1E), [[cards/Cloak and Dagger]] (2 Shivs + 6 block), [[cards/Infinite Blades]] (free Shiv/turn), Phantasmal Killer (doubles Shiv damage), [[potions/Cultist Potion]] ([[buffs/Ritual]] Str compounds on every Shiv), [[cards/Finisher]] (Shivs build attack count), [[cards/After Image]] (each Shiv = +1 block).

**[[bosses/Time Eater]] interaction:** Shivs count toward 12-card counter, but with Accuracy stacking each is worth 20+ damage, making the counter manageable.

**Draft priority:** High in any Shiv deck. Take early.
