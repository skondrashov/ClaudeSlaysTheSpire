# Immolate

High-damage AOE attack. Hits ALL enemies. The Burn downside is minor.

**When to play:** Multi-enemy fights (Byrds, Gremlin groups, [[bosses/Slime Boss]] split, Collector + Torch Heads). Play freely -- 21+ AOE is worth 2-4 HP of Burn damage across the fight.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 21->28 AOE. With [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]: 42 AOE. With [[relics/Paper Phrog]] (75%): 49 AOE. Most impactful card in multi-enemy boss fights.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Thunderclap]] (applies Vulnerable to all, then Immolate for 31+ AOE), [[cards/Fire Breathing]] (Burns trigger 10 free damage on draw -- bypasses [[buffs/Plated Armor]]; do NOT exhaust Fire Breathing when Immolate is in deck), [[cards/Evolve]] (Burns draw replacement cards -- makes Burn downside pure upside), [[cards/Dual Wield]] (3 copies = 84-102 AOE damage in one turn -- won [[bosses/Donu and Deca]]), [[cards/Headbutt]] (top-deck for guaranteed next turn).

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] synergy:** Frequently costs 0-1E instead of 2E, enabling multiple plays per turn.

**Matchups:** Excellent vs Byrds, Slime Boss split, 3 Cultists, Guardian, [[bosses/The Collector]]. Primary damage source in both consecutive victories.

**Flight damage math:** Against Byrds (Flight 3), damage is halved and rounded down. Include [[buffs/Strength]] in the base BEFORE halving: (21 + Str) / 2 floored. With +2 Str from [[cards/Inflame]]: floor(23/2) = 11 per [[enemies/Byrd]], not 10. Forgetting Strength underestimates damage by 1 per Byrd.
