# Jaw Worm Horde

**Core rule:** This is a DPS race against triple [[buffs/Strength]] scaling. AOE is critical. Kill one quickly to reduce incoming by 33%.

**Danger Level:** High. Triple Strength scaling makes this one of the most dangerous [[acts/Act 3]] hallway fights. Combined Turn 1 damage can be 30+ with no free setup turn.

## Strategy

- AOE is mandatory ([[cards/Die Die Die]], [[cards/Blade Dance]] + [[cards/Accuracy]], [[cards/Dagger Spray]], [[cards/Corpse Explosion]])
- Focus fire one Jaw Worm while AOE chips the others
- Apply [[debuffs/Weak]] or [[cards/Piercing Wail]] to reduce combined incoming on big attack turns
- Kill order: whichever has lowest HP first, then next lowest
- The fight becomes unmanageable after 4-5 turns if all 3 are still alive and scaling

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Silent]]:** [[cards/Noxious Fumes]] applies poison to all 3 simultaneously. Piercing Wail reduces all 3 enemies' Strength. Die Die Die+ for burst AOE. Blade Dance provides multi-target damage. [[cards/Wraith Form]] for emergency [[buffs/Intangible]] if scaling gets out of control.
