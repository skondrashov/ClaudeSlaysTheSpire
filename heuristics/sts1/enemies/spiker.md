# Spiker

**Core rule:** [[debuffs/Poison]] is the ideal damage source -- it bypasses Thorns entirely. If you must attack, use single large hits, not multi-hit attacks.

## Strategy

- Poison ([[cards/Noxious Fumes]], [[cards/Deadly Poison]], [[cards/Bouncing Flask]]) deals damage without triggering Thorns
- If attacking, use single large hits ([[cards/Dash]], [[cards/Predator]]) not multi-hit attacks
- Kill Spikers early before Thorns compound (at Thorns 3-5 attacks are tolerable; at 9+ every attack is significant HP loss)
- In multi-enemy fights, kill Spiker first to stop Thorns from scaling

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Silent]]:** Poison completely solves Spikers. Noxious Fumes + any poison source kills without ever taking Thorns damage. Shivs and [[cards/Blade Dance]] are TRAPS -- each hit triggers Thorns. [[cards/Piercing Wail]] does nothing useful here (Cut damage is low; Thorns is the real threat).

**[[characters/Ironclad]]:** Use single large hits only. [[cards/Heavy Blade]]+ with Strength is ideal — one massive hit minimizes Thorns triggers. **NEVER use [[cards/Fiend Fire]]** — it hits once per exhausted card (4 cards = 4 Thorns triggers = lethal). [[cards/Reaper]] does NOT heal before Thorns resolves — Thorns can kill during attack resolution before healing applies. Fiend Fire at Thorns 9 deals 36 self-damage (4 hits), dropping you from 38 to 2 HP and creating an unrecoverable state as Thorns climbs to 15.

**Danger Level:** HIGH for attack-heavy decks. Low direct damage but Thorns scales +2/buff turn and punishes multi-hit attacks lethally. Poison decks trivialize this fight. Ironclad without poison must kill within 4-5 turns before Thorns exceeds survivable block.
