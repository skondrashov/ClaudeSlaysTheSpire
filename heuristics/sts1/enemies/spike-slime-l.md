# Spike Slime L

**Core rule:** Prepare for the split -- have enough HP and resources to handle 2 medium slimes after the split.

**The split REPLACES its declared move.** Splitting consumes the slime's turn, so a declared Flame Tackle never lands if you push it across the split threshold first. When it has an attack declared and your damage can cross the threshold this turn, that damage IS your block — deliberately timing the split to an attack turn is free damage prevention (see [[bosses/Slime Boss]] for the same tech at the boss).

## Strategy

- In the [[bosses/Slime Boss]] fight, killing this while Acid Slime (L) is at full HP creates a 3-enemy nightmare
- AOE for post-split management
- Exhaust [[cards/Slimed]] cards to maintain hand quality

## What to Bring

- AOE for post-split
- Exhaust for Slimed

## What to Avoid

- Long attrition fights (8+ turns drain HP badly)
- Killing this before softening the other large slime in multi-slime fights

**Danger Level:** Medium.
