# Byrd

**Core rule:** [[cards/Thunderclap]] is CRITICAL. Without it, Flight 3 on three enemies means 9 hits just to strip all Flight once -- at half damage each.

**Benchmark:** With Thunderclap + burst: a ~7-turn fight. Without AOE: 8-10 turns, and the worst cases drain nearly a full health bar. **Damage clock:** each Byrd Pecks 1x5 or Swoops 12, with Caw banking +1 Strength on off-turns — Strength multiplies through Peck's five hits, so the flock's combined 10-25+ per turn climbs as the fight drags; price it by your turns-to-kill against that clock.

## Strategy

- Use Thunderclap to strip 1 Flight from ALL Byrds and apply [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] simultaneously
- Focus fire one Byrd at a time -- going from 3 to 2 removes 33% of incoming damage
- Once Flight is at 0, burst with Vulnerable attacks to kill
- Expect 7-12 turn fights — every extra turn is another round of the clock above

## Engine Setup Trap

Do NOT spend 3 turns setting up powers ([[cards/Evolve]] + [[cards/Metallicize]] + [[cards/Fire Breathing]] + [[cards/Inflame]]). Each setup turn costs 12-23 unblocked HP because Byrds attack for 10-25 per turn and a single Defend + Metallicize only blocks 8. A 3-turn setup burns 40-60 HP before dealing any damage. Instead, play at most 1 power per turn while blocking, or skip setup entirely — kill speed matters more than engine value here, and the 40-60 HP setup tax usually costs more than the engine ever pays back.

## Turn 2 Trap

If all 3 Byrds attack simultaneously (after gaining Str from buff turns), combined damage can reach 38+. With only 1 block card in hand, there is no survival path. The deck needs block density (multiple [[cards/Shrug It Off]], [[cards/Ghostly Armor]], [[cards/Impervious]]).

## What to Bring

- AOE (Thunderclap for Flight stripping + Vulnerable -- THE key card)
- Burst damage ([[cards/Carnage]] nearly one-shots a Byrd)
- Multi-hit attacks ([[cards/Twin Strike]] strips 2 Flight per play)
- Metallicize (essential for long fight)
- [[cards/Reaper]] (sustain healing)

## What to Avoid

- Small single-hit attacks (halved by Flight and inefficient)
- Havoc or unupgraded [[cards/True Grit]] (random exhaust can destroy key cards)

## Chosen+Byrd Fight

Combines Flight mechanics with [[debuffs/Hex]] (Skills add [[cards/Dazed]]). Extremely dangerous -- need Attacks for Byrd Flight stripping but also need block from Skills, which trigger Hex. Price it on the ledger as strictly dearer than a plain Byrd fight — the Hex tax lands on exactly the block cards the flock forces you to play.
