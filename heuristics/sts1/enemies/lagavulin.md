# Lagavulin

**Core rule:** Wake it immediately with [[cards/Bash]] ([[debuffs/Vulnerable]] > chipping through 8 block). Front-load damage on debuff turns (free turns). Kill before [[buffs/Strength]] debuffs accumulate.

**Benchmark:** 7-turn fight typical. Expect 20-36 HP damage. At A9 its attack hits 20 (18 base), and decks whose block hands run 8-10 have paid up to ~50 — if your block can't match its awake cycle, price the fight near the top, not the middle.

**Strategy:** Apply Vulnerable via Bash+ on Turn 1 to wake it, then burst during the stunned turn and subsequent Siphon Soul turns (free damage windows). [[cards/Headbutt]] top-decks Bash+ for Vulnerable refresh. **Make the waking hit reduce HP:** a poke fully eaten by its Metallicize 8 doesn't trigger the damage-wake — lead with the hit that breaks through. The stun window has not always materialized at high ascension, so treat it as a bonus turn, not the plan's foundation.

## What to Bring

- Bash+ for Vulnerable setup
- Headbutt+ (top-deck Bash+ for Vulnerable refresh)
- Burst damage
- [[debuffs/Weak]] source

## What to Avoid

- Slow play. Every debuff turn compounds the problem -- Siphon Soul permanently reduces both damage output and block generation.
