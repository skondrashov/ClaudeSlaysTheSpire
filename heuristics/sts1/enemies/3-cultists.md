# 3 Cultists

**THIS IS THE MOST LETHAL HALLWAY FIGHT IN THE GAME.** Six deaths across all runs -- more than any other single encounter. Deaths at 5%, 30%, 37%, 39%, 52%, and 53% HP entry.

**Core rule:** Enter with 50%+ HP MINIMUM -- this is a hard threshold, not a guideline. Kill one [[enemies/Cultist]] ASAP to reduce scaling by 33%.

## Strategy

- Turn 1 is free -- use it for Powers ([[cards/Metallicize]], [[cards/Inflame]]) and initial damage on one target
- Turn 2-3: Play [[cards/Shockwave]] on the FIRST ATTACK TURN for mass [[debuffs/Weak]] + [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]
- Focus fire one Cultist at a time -- each dead Cultist permanently removes one scaling threat
- Reducing from 3 to 2 cuts incoming by 33%; reducing to 1 makes it trivial

## Why Decks Fail Here

- Slow scaling engines ([[cards/Demon Form]], [[cards/Limit Break]]) need 2-3 free setup turns that do not exist at low HP
- Even strong block tools ([[cards/Impervious]], [[relics/Calipers]]) only delay the inevitable if kill speed is insufficient
- The fight demands BOTH burst damage AND sustained block for 4-6 turns simultaneously

## What to Bring

- Shockwave+ (MUST play on first attack turn)
- Powers for free turn setup (Inflame, Metallicize)
- Single-target burst for focus fire ([[cards/Rampage]], [[cards/Fiend Fire]], [[cards/Iron Wave]])
- Immediate block (Defend, [[cards/Ghostly Armor]])

## What to Avoid

- Entering below 50% HP
- Delaying Shockwave+ past the first attack turn
- Spreading damage across all 3 instead of focus-firing
- Relying solely on slow scaling (Demon Form) without fast burst
