# Orb Walker

**Core rule:** Kill one Orb Walker fast. Focus all damage on one OW to halve incoming damage as soon as possible. This is a DPS race.

**Benchmark:** Single OW killable in 2-3 turns. Do not let the fight go past Turn 5 -- incoming exceeds 30+ per OW and Burns overwhelm the hand.

## Strategy

- Focus fire one OW to halve incoming damage ASAP
- [[debuffs/Poison]] ([[cards/Deadly Poison]], [[cards/Catalyst]]) can handle one OW while you block against the other
- Feed kills are valuable at ~93 HP (+3 max HP)

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Ironclad]]:** [[cards/Inflame]] + [[cards/Immolate]] + Strike+ can kill a single OW in 2 turns. [[cards/Headbutt]] + Immolate+ guarantees a Turn 2 kill.

**[[characters/Silent]]:** Poison engine handles OWs well -- Deadly Poison+ plus [[cards/Noxious Fumes]] ticks kill one in 3-4 turns. [[cards/Piercing Wail]] is critical for reducing combined incoming. [[cards/After Image]] provides passive block scaling. Do NOT use Catalyst+ on Orb Walkers if the [[acts/Act 3]] boss is [[bosses/Awakened One]] (save for Regenerate 10).

## What NOT to Do

- Set up slow Powers on Turn 1 (fight is too short for setup to pay off)
- Let the fight go past Turn 5
- Spend Catalyst+ here if the Act 3 boss is Awakened One
