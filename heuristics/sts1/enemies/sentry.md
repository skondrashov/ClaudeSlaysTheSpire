# Sentry

**Core rule:** AOE is king. Without AOE or meaningful damage scaling, the fight takes 14-20 turns and drains 52-56+ HP.

**Benchmark:** WITH AOE: 7-turn fight, ~22 HP damage. WITHOUT AOE: 18-20 turns, 50-60 HP damage. The AOE benchmark is the only acceptable outcome.

## Elite Readiness Gate

Do NOT fight Sentries with a starter-quality deck (Strikes, Defends, [[cards/Bash]], 0-1 other cards). Without AOE or meaningful damage scaling, effective damage per Strike is only 2 (6 minus [[cards/Metallicize]] 4 absorb) — the fight stretches toward 20 turns and the accumulated chip damage drains most of a health bar.

The Sentries fight requires at least one of: AOE ([[cards/Thunderclap]], [[cards/Whirlwind]], [[cards/Cleave]]), damage scaling ([[cards/Inflame]], [[cards/Spot Weakness]]), or a strong attack card ([[cards/Carnage]], [[cards/Pommel Strike]]).

## Path Timing Gate

Do NOT path into Sentries with only 0-2 monster rooms beforehand. A deck needs 3-4 fights minimum to pick up cards that make Sentries viable.

## Strategy

- Strip [[buffs/Artifact]] with Thunderclap before real debuffs
- Focus fire one Sentry at a time
- Kill before [[cards/Dazed]] overwhelms draws

## What to Bring

- AOE (Whirlwind, Thunderclap, Cleave)
- Damage scaling (Inflame, Spot Weakness)
- Focus fire capability

## What to Avoid

- Slow play (Dazed accumulation cripples draws)
- Fighting with a starter-quality deck

## Act 2 Pairing (Sentry + Spheric Guardian)

Kill the Sentry first (no block to break through, lower HP) to reduce incoming damage count and stop Dazed generation. [[relics/Gremlin Horn]] triggers on Sentry death, providing energy and draw to handle the [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]].

## Silent Act 2 Pairing

Kill order: Sentry first (strip Artifact with [[cards/Neutralize]], apply [[cards/Terror]] for Vuln, burst with [[cards/Backstab]] + attacks). Use [[cards/Piercing Wail]] to zero the Sentry's beam turn while setting up [[cards/Footwork]] Dex. After Sentry dies, poison the Spheric Guardian ([[cards/Deadly Poison]], [[cards/Noxious Fumes]] bypass [[cards/Barricade]] block entirely). However, Dazed from Sentry Bolts combined with SG's Barricade block creates a lethal combination at low HP (<30%).
