# Spire Growth

**Core rule:** Constricted stacks with the attack damage every turn, so effective incoming per turn is (attack + Constricted). Kill fast or out-block the sum — there is no third option.

Spire Growth (170 HP, [[acts/Act 3]]) applies **Constricted X** on its STRONG_DEBUFF turns. Constricted deals X damage at end of every subsequent turn, and this damage IS affected by block.

**At low HP, Constricted is lethal.** Constricted 10 means you need 10 EXTRA block every turn beyond blocking the attack. If the attack is 16 (Weakened), that's 26 block needed per turn — more than a typical hand can generate, turn after turn. Entering at low HP is unsurvivable.

## Strategy

- Enter the fight at high HP. Constricted is manageable if you can absorb a few turns of damage.
- Kill FAST. Every extra turn is another (attack + Constricted) package; burst damage is the cleanest mitigation.
- Do NOT spend the first free debuff turn on power setup if HP is critical — prioritize block infrastructure over engine value.
- [[debuffs/Weak]] reduces the attack component (Constricted is unaffected).

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Watcher]]:** Wrath burst with the full engine (Rushdown 4, Deva Form, Divinity) can output 50-100+ damage per turn — the fastest kill line. [[cards/Wave of the Hand]] + Wallop combo is key: Wallop generates block equal to damage dealt, Wave of the Hand converts attacks into Weak application, reducing the attack component while building block.
