# Looter

**Core rule:** Kill by Turn 3-4 to prevent escape and recover stolen gold.

**Benchmark:** Kill in 3-4 turns to limit theft and prevent escape.

**Blocking does not protect your gold.** The theft lands through full block — block protects HP only. The only way to stop the bleed is to kill it before Escape (or accept the loss and triage elsewhere). Race it; turtling concedes gold every turn.

## Strategy

- Burst damage to kill before [[potions/Smoke Bomb]]/Escape sequence
- If Smoke Bomb intent appears, you have one turn left before it flees

## What to Bring

- Burst damage

## What to Avoid

- Slow play (hemorrhages gold and risks escape)

## Act 2 Pairing (Looter + Mugger)

Combined damage is 20+ per turn with no free setup turns. AOE and fast kills are critical. Do not play expensive setup cards ([[cards/Corruption]] at 3E, [[cards/Barricade]] at 3E) because you cannot afford a turn without block against two enemies attacking simultaneously.
