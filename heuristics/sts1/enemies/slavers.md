# Slavers

**Core rule:** This fight is unwinnable at low HP. Block is mandatory Turn 1.

**Damage clock:** three lanes attack every turn — [[enemies/Red Slaver]] Stab 13 / Scrape 8 (+Vulnerable), [[enemies/Blue Slaver]] Stab 12 / Rake 7 (+Weak), [[enemies/Taskmaster]] always 7 (+Wound) — roughly 27-32 combined, with the riders amplifying everything after. There are no free turns and no way to stall the cost down — price it by your block coverage against that clock and your turns-to-kill before routing in.

## Kill Priority

[[enemies/Red Slaver]] first (highest damage at 13 per Stab, applies [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] which amplifies ALL incoming, Entangle can lock you out of attacks). Then [[enemies/Blue Slaver]] (Weak is annoying but less dangerous). [[enemies/Taskmaster]] last (only 7 damage and Wounds are manageable).

## Strategy

- **Turn 1:** Block is mandatory. Do not set up powers. [[cards/Flame Barrier]] (16 block + 6 counter to all 3 attackers) is the ideal Turn 1 play
- Focus fire on the enemy with highest per-turn damage first
- Rage (0E, 3 block per Attack) is excellent for generating free block while dealing damage
- [[cards/Uppercut]]'s Weak on the hardest-hitting enemy is critical

## Timing Rule

Do NOT fight Slavers within 2 floors of the [[acts/Act 2]] boss — the HP drain stacks directly on top of whatever the boss will cost your deck, and the ledger rarely clears. See pre-boss routing in [[layer:heuristics, map]].

## What to Bring

- AOE ([[cards/Thunderclap]], [[cards/Whirlwind]], [[cards/Immolate]])
- Flame Barrier+ (block + punishes all 3)
- Weak sources
- High block density

## What to Avoid

- Entering at low HP (no free turns, no way to stall)
- This elite within 2 floors of the boss

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Silent]]:** [[cards/Shiv]] burst can eliminate one Slaver quickly ([[relics/Ninja Scroll]] + STR = 21+ free damage Turn 1). [[cards/Piercing Wail]] is excellent Turn 1 (reduces 27 combined incoming to ~9).
