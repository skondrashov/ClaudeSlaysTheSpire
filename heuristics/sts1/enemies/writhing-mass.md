# Writhing Mass

**Core rule:** Use single large hits, not multi-hit attacks. [[buffs/Malleable]] gains block per hit, punishing [[cards/Whirlwind]]/[[cards/Pummel]] severely.

**Expected cost:** 25-40 HP played well over a long fight (6-8 turns).

## Strategy

- [[cards/Hemokinesis]] (15 damage, 1 hit) is far better than Pummel (2x4, gives 2 stacks of Malleable block)
- Apply [[debuffs/Weak]] early (multi-hit attacks at 75% damage are much more survivable)
- [[cards/Disarm]] helps but does not solve the fight -- fight length is the real problem

## What NOT to Do

- Play Whirlwind, Pummel, or other multi-hit attacks
- Expect a quick kill (plan for 6-8 turns minimum)

## Engine Approach

[[cards/Barricade]] + [[cards/Corruption]] + FNP engine handles Writhing Mass effectively. Block accumulation makes the fight survivable, and [[cards/Immolate]] (single large hit) bypasses Malleable efficiently. [[cards/Spot Weakness]] works on Attack turns.

## Danger Warning

This enemy is a major HP drain in [[acts/Act 3]]. Entering the next fight at low HP after Writhing Mass is the real danger.
