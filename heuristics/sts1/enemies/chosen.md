# Chosen

**Core rule:** Minimize Skill usage after [[debuffs/Hex]] is applied. Front-load damage with Attacks. Kill before [[buffs/Strength]] buffs make attacks lethal.

**Benchmark:** Kill in 4-5 turns. **Expected cost:** 30-40 HP played well — and the Strength scaling means a slow deck pays a multiple of this; price it by your turns-to-kill.

## Kill Priority (Multi-Enemy Fights)

**Kill Chosen FIRST.** Hex's [[cards/Dazed]] flooding is CATASTROPHIC -- every Skill played after Turn 2 adds a Dazed to the draw pile, and by Turn 4-5 the deck is so diluted that critical turns draw 2-3 Dazed. Killing Chosen first stops Hex immediately.

**Exception:** If the [[enemies/Cultist]] can be killed in 1-2 turns ([[potions/Fire Potion]] burst, or it has <15 HP), killing it first is acceptable because Hex only applies for 1-2 turns.

**Whichever branch applies, COMMIT.** Splitting damage between Chosen and the Cultist is strictly worse than either committed line — neither dies before the fight's damage converges (at A9 the pairing's combined incoming reaches ~60/turn by turns 6-7 if neither target has died — that clock outpaces any block plan). Convert damage potions EARLY on the committed target; a potion that dies unspent in this fight was the missing kill-turn.

## Strategy

- Attacks and Powers are safe (do not trigger Hex)
- Play Defend only when absolutely necessary (each one adds Dazed)
- [[cards/Iron Wave]] is excellent (Attack that blocks)
- [[cards/Metallicize]] is excellent (passive block without triggering Hex -- Power, not a Skill)
- Kill speed is critical -- Strength scaling is lethal

## What to Bring

- Attack-heavy deck
- Iron Wave (Attack that blocks)
- [[cards/Inflame]] (Strength overcomes [[debuffs/Weak]] penalty)
- [[cards/Bash]] (Attack, applies [[debuffs/Vulnerable]])
- Metallicize (passive block without triggering Hex)

## What to Avoid

- Playing Defend unless absolutely necessary
- Slow kill (Strength scaling is lethal)
- [[cards/Fiend Fire]] unless the burst will kill (exhausts ALL other cards in hand including defensive cards)

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Silent]]:** Hex is disproportionately devastating. Silent decks are naturally Skill-heavy ([[cards/Backflip]], [[cards/Footwork]], [[cards/Survivor]], [[cards/Deflect]], [[cards/Blur]], [[cards/Piercing Wail]]). Powers (Footwork, [[cards/After Image]], [[cards/A Thousand Cuts]]) do NOT trigger Hex. Front-load damage with Attacks and Shivs. Use Piercing Wail Turn 1 BEFORE Hex is applied. Avoid curse-heavy decks ([[relics/Du-Vu Doll]] strategy) when Hex encounters are likely.

**[[characters/Watcher]]:** Hex is extremely dangerous. The stance dance engine relies on Skills for Calm entry (Vigilance, Inner Peace, Empty Body, Meditate) and blocking (Defend). Every Skill played adds a Dazed to the draw pile, diluting the deck and destroying draw consistency. Mitigation: prioritize Attack-based Wrath entries (Eruption, Tantrum) over Skill-based Calm entries. Set up Powers (Rushdown, Mental Fortress, Battle Hymn) early — Powers do NOT trigger Hex. Kill Chosen as fast as possible with Wrath burst damage. Accept taking some damage to avoid playing Skills. A Chosen+Byrd hallway can collapse the Watcher's Skill-heavy engine through Dazed flooding.
