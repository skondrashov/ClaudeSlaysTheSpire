# The Maw

**Core rule:** Setup on free turns. The Maw gives 3-4 free turns across the fight. Use every one for Power cards ([[cards/Barricade]], [[cards/Metallicize]], [[cards/Inflame]], [[cards/Evolve]]). This is a defensive setup fight, not a burst fight.

**Benchmark:** 8-9 turn fight. Must have block scaling online before the big hits arrive (33-56+ damage).

## Strategy

- **Barricade + Metallicize is the solution.** Block from early turns carries over, stacking higher each turn. By the time damage escalates to 33-56, the block wall exceeds incoming.
- [[relics/Mummified Hand]] + Power-heavy decks excel (multiple Power plays on free turns trigger Mummified Hand repeatedly)
- [[cards/Headbutt]] + [[cards/Immolate]] loop for damage
- Feed is safe to exhaust early (Feed cannot kill the Maw at 300 HP)

## What NOT to Do

- Waste [[cards/Bloodletting]] on Turn 1 (the free turn -- [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] increases future damage taken)
- Ignore the [[debuffs/Frail]] debuff (block values are reduced by 25% during Frail turns)
- Rush the kill (300 HP means 8-9 turns minimum; defensive setup is more important than early burst)
