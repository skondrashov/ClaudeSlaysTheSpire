# Exhaust Strategy

- **Before Exhausting, Ask**: (1) Can remaining deck survive the enemy's biggest attack? (2) Is the card truly expendable? (3) Am I in first half (thin=good) or second half (thin leaves too little damage/block to survive)?
- **Random Exhaust Is Run-Ending**: Unupgraded [[cards/True Grit]] and Havoc both cause random exhaust. Rule: Never play Havoc or unupgraded True Grit when deck contains irreplaceable cards. Upgrade True Grit IMMEDIATELY if taken.
- **Safe Exhaust Targets**: Strikes (once better attacks exist), extra Defends, status cards ([[cards/Slimed]], Wound, Burn, [[cards/Dazed]]).
- **Never Exhaust**: Last copy of best damage card, last high-block card, core combo pieces ([[cards/Bash]] when Vuln matters), synergy engines ([[cards/Fire Breathing]] when [[cards/Immolate]] is in deck).
- **The Long Fight Problem**: Exhaust cards front-load power into first 5 turns. After that, deck plays like starter. Against scaling enemies, first 5 turns aren't enough. If 30%+ of deck exhausts/is Ethereal, must win in 5-6 turns.
- **[[cards/Fiend Fire]] + [[relics/Unceasing Top]] Engine**: Exception to long fight problem. After Fiend Fire exhausts hand, Top draws a card. Cycles through draw pile in one mega-turn. With energy from [[cards/Bloodletting]]/[[cards/Offering]], deals 50-100+ damage. Strongest combo observed for [[characters/Ironclad]].
- **Boss Exhaust Trap**: Mass exhaust (Fiend Fire, [[cards/Corruption]]) permanently destroys cards. This is dangerous in two scenarios:
  - **Phased bosses ([[bosses/The Champ]], [[bosses/Awakened One]]):** Phase transitions cleanse debuffs. Debuffs exhausted by Fiend Fire in Phase 1 are gone forever — cannot be re-applied in Phase 2.
  - **Long fights ([[bosses/Time Eater]], any 10+ turn fight):** Cards exhausted early are gone for the remaining 8-10 turns. Emergency tools ([[cards/Dark Shackles]] -15 Str, [[cards/Intimidate]] [[debuffs/Weak]], block cards) that seem redundant on Turn 2 become the difference between life and death when the enemy has +4-6 Str by Turn 10. Before mass-exhausting, separate every card in hand into "truly expendable" (Strikes, status, duplicates) and "needed later" (debuffs, emergency defense, block). Only mass-exhaust when hand contains NO category-2 cards.
