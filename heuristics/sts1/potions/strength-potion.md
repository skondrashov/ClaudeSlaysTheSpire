# Strength Potion

Best used Turn 1 of boss fights (compounds over all attacks all fight). In hallway fights, use for kill turns or when Full Block needs extra damage to kill an attacker.

In hard fights, drink it turn 1 at any HP — do not save it for a crisis. A [[buffs/Strength]] Potion shortens fights by 2-3 turns against high-HP enemies ([[enemies/Shelled Parasite]], [[enemies/Centurion]]+[[enemies/Mystic]]), which means 2-3 fewer turns of incoming damage. An unused Strength Potion on a death screen means 10-20 damage was left on the table.

Against [[enemies/Giant Head]] with [[cards/Metallicize]] 8, even +2 Str on a Strike turns it from 6 damage (bounces off Metallicize) to 8 damage (exactly breaks through). In any fight approaching lethal, use ALL offensive potions before the window closes. Potions do not count as card plays (no Slow interaction).
