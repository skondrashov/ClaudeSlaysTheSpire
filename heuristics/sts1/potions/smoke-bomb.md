# Smoke Bomb

Use when the fight is unwinnable and continuing will result in death. Use when estimated incoming damage on the next turn exceeds your current HP + maximum possible block. Do not save it for "a worse situation" -- dying to an avoidable fight is worse than losing rewards from one combat.

Carry one into [[acts/Act 2]] whenever possible as insurance against dangerous hallway fights (3 Cultists, Byrds, [[enemies/Snake Plant]]) at low HP. Also valuable in [[acts/Act 1]] against [[encounters/Gang of Gremlins]] fights at low HP -- 4 gremlins deal 22+ combined damage on Turn 1, which is lethal at sub-30% HP without strong AOE.

**Hold-value: it converts coin-flip elites into win-or-walk-away.** You always act before the enemy, so a burst-to-the-floor race with Smoke Bomb in reserve has no losing branch you must accept: commit to the race, and if the decisive draw doesn't come, bomb out with the HP you still have. Define the abort threshold BEFORE the fight (e.g. "below N HP with more than X enemy HP left"), not at 1 HP. Does not work in boss fights.

A single Smoke Bomb can extend a run by multiple floors. An unused Smoke Bomb on a death screen is a strategic failure.
