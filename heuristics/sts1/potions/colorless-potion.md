# Colorless Potion

A card-granting potion, same shape as [[potions/Power Potion]]/[[potions/Skill Potion]]: the picked card is combat-only and costs 0 the turn it arrives. The Colorless pool swings wide — from premium ([[cards/Apotheosis]]-class) to filler — so the value is the free tempo, not a specific answer. Drink it on a turn with spare safety (a free enemy turn, a setup turn) so a weak roll costs nothing and a strong roll is a free play.
