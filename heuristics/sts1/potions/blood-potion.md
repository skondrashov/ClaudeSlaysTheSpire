# Blood Potion

Use before a dangerous fight to increase survival odds, or mid-fight when HP is critical. It is also a fine favorable trade outside a crisis: drinking it any time the heal will not overcap (topping up before a planned elite, after a draining hallway fight) converts an expiring slot into permanent HP — see Potion Economy in [[layer:heuristics, combat]]. Scales with Max HP -- more valuable when Feed has increased the pool. At 80 Max HP heals 16; at 100 Max HP heals 20.
