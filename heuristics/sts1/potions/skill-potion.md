# Skill Potion

Best during boss fights when you need a specific answer (mass debuff, big block). Can find [[cards/Impervious]] (30 block), [[cards/Shockwave]] (mass debuff), or other clutch Skills. The 0 cost means it's free value on any turn.

The granted card is combat-only and costs 0 only the turn it arrives — drink it on the turn you can play the answer, not in advance.
