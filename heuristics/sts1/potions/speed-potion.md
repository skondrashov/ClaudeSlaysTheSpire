# Speed Potion

Use before a big incoming damage turn where you play multiple block cards. Defend goes from 5 to 10 block with +5 Dex. Best when you can play 2-3 block cards on the same turn (10-15 extra block total). Use when you need to survive a massive attack (Inferno, Guardian 32). Against multi-enemy fights with high combined damage, Speed Potion on a defensive turn can be fight-saving.
