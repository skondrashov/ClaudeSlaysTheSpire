# Cultist Potion

Use Turn 1 of long fights (bosses, elites). The earlier it is used, the more turns of [[buffs/Strength]] accumulation. In a 9-turn boss fight used Turn 1, provides +1, +2, +3... up to +8 Str by Turn 9. Comparable to [[cards/Demon Form]] (+2/turn) over 4 turns.

Synergies: [[cards/Rampage]] (Strength compounds with per-play scaling for multiplicative damage growth), [[cards/Sword Boomerang]] (multi-hit multiplies Strength bonus), Shivs + [[cards/Accuracy]] ([[buffs/Ritual]] Str stacks with Accuracy bonus on every [[cards/Shiv]] -- by Turn 10, each 0-cost Shiv deals 23 damage).

Best targets: [[bosses/Hexaghost]] (13-turn fight, massive scaling value), [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] (long fight, DPS race), [[bosses/Time Eater]] (10-turn fight). Save it for LONG fights — which includes hard hallway fights ([[enemies/Byrd]]s, 3 Cultists run 7-12 turns), not just bosses. Only short hallway fights end before the scaling pays off.

**Premium shop buy when the visible act boss is long.** Over a 10+ turn boss fight the sustained Ritual (+1 Str compounding every turn) tends to out-scale what any single defensive potion saves — at a shop before Hexaghost, Time Eater, or Bronze Automaton, take the Cultist Potion over a defensive potion of similar price.
