# Distilled Chaos

Use when draw pile contains high-cost cards ([[cards/Immolate]], [[cards/Barricade]], [[cards/Demon Form]]) that would normally consume your entire turn's energy. The free play is incredible value. Also good when you need an extra action but have 0 energy.

The card played is random (top of draw pile), so outcomes vary. Best used when draw pile is stacked with good options. After [[cards/Battle Trance]] or other draw effects thin the draw pile, remaining cards are more predictable.

Best for emergency big-block or big-damage on critical turns. Synergizes with decks that have expensive Power cards or heavy AOE in draw pile.
