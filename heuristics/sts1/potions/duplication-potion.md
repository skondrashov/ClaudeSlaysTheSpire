# Duplication Potion

Use before a high-value card play. [[cards/Rampage]] at 40 damage = 80 total. [[cards/Carnage]] at 28 = 56 total. [[cards/Iron Wave]] = double block + double damage (14/14 total). Best with damage cards for burst or Iron Wave+ for combined offense/defense.

Under Confusion ([[relics/Snecko Eye]]), wait for a high-value card to roll 0 cost, then duplicate for double value at zero energy cost. Best confirmed use: Duplication Potion on 0-cost [[cards/Immolate]] dealt 60 AOE to both [[bosses/Donu and Deca]]. This is the highest-value play -- target cards whose cost was randomly set to 0.
