# Power Potion

Best used Turn 1 of a boss fight, especially on a free turn. Powers are permanent, so early setup = maximum value. Can find [[cards/Metallicize]] (passive block), [[cards/Inflame]] ([[buffs/Strength]]), or other strong Powers. Always choose the permanent scaling option -- avoid [[cards/Berserk]] (self-[[debuffs/Vulnerable]]).

**The granted card costs 0 the turn it arrives and is combat-only** (it never joins your deck). That makes the tempo the point: drunk on an elite's free turn ([[enemies/Gremlin Nob]]'s Bellow, a Charging/buff turn), a free 3E Power lands with all your energy still available — this has cheaply won elite fights. Drink it on the turn you can exploit the 0 cost, not in advance.
