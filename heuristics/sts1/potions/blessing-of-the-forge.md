# Blessing of the Forge

Use before a fight, not during. Best used early in the run when most cards are unupgraded (higher chance of upgrading something useful). At rest sites, using this potion gives a free upgrade without spending the rest site action -- use it in addition to the normal Smith action for two upgrades at one rest site.

Value is moderate due to random targeting. Could upgrade a Strike (low value) or a key card like [[cards/Bash]] (high value). Most valuable when the deck has many unupgraded cards, least valuable when most cards are already upgraded.
