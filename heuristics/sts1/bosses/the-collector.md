# The Collector

**Core rule:** Enter at 70%+ HP or have [[relics/Pantograph]]. The Mega Debuff on Turn 4 creates a 3-turn crisis where incoming spikes by 50% and block is reduced by 25%. AOE is the highest priority card type.

## Preparation Checklist

1. Enter at 70%+ HP or Pantograph
2. AOE damage ([[cards/Immolate]] is the MVP -- hits Collector + both Torch Heads)
3. Mass debuff ([[cards/Shockwave]] reduces combined incoming from 3 enemies by 25% for 3 turns)
4. [[cards/Impervious]] (30 block absorbs an entire post-debuff turn)
5. Block density ([[debuffs/Frail]] reduces all block by 25% -- need multiple sources)
6. [[cards/Disarm]] (permanently reduces Collector [[buffs/Strength]])

## Strategy

- **Turn 1 (spawn turn):** [[cards/Thunderclap]] for [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] on all 3 enemies. Disarm on Collector.
- **Turns 2-3:** Maximum damage to Collector while blocking Torch Heads. Shockwave+ for mass [[debuffs/Weak]].
- **Turn 4 (Mega Debuff):** No damage incoming -- use for burst offense (free turn).
- **Turns 5-7 (post-debuff crisis):** Impervious is the best card. Block is reduced by Frail, you take 50% more from Vulnerable.
- **Turns 8-10 (cleanup):** Re-apply Vulnerable, AOE to clear re-summoned Torch Heads.

## What NOT to Do

- Enter below 70% HP without Pantograph (Mega Debuff creates mathematically unwinnable turns at low HP)
- Ignore Torch Heads entirely (14 combined damage/turn adds up)
- Save potions for "later" (post-debuff turns 5-7 are the crisis -- use potions here)
- Play Shockwave+ on Turn 1 before Torch Heads spawn
- Enter without Impervious or Shockwave+ (without a big absorb or mass Weak, the post-debuff crisis turns are unsurvivable)

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Silent]]:** Play defensive powers ([[cards/After Image]], [[cards/Footwork]]) IMMEDIATELY before Turn 4 debuff. [[cards/Noxious Fumes]] is the primary win condition (3 poison/turn to all, ignores player debuffs). [[cards/Piercing Wail]] reduces all enemy Str by 8 for 1 turn. Prioritize killing Torch Heads with Shivs (After Image + Shivs = block + damage). [[cards/Wraith Form]]: do NOT play early (fight lasts 15-20 turns, Dex penalty compounds). [[cards/Catalyst]] for tripling poison. WLP+ to retain key cards.
