# The Champ

**Core rule:** Save debuffs for AFTER the Anger transition (below 50% HP). All debuffs applied before Anger are completely wasted. Burst through Phase 2 quickly.

## Strategy

- **[[cards/Demon Form]] and slow scaling:** Need enough turns to ramp BEFORE Execute triggers. If Demon Form played Turn 1 and Execute happens Turn 5, only +10 [[buffs/Strength]] -- may not be enough.
- **Burst through Phase 2:** Have enough burst to kill quickly after Anger. Slow attrition decks struggle because [[cards/Metallicize]] 5 + cleansed debuffs mean he takes less damage and deals more.
- **Re-apply debuffs after Anger:** [[cards/Shockwave]], [[cards/Disarm]], and other debuffs are wasted if applied before Anger. Save them (or have second copies) for Phase 2.
- **HP management:** Enter at high HP. Post-Anger Strength is +6 (A0) / +9 (A4) / +12 (A19), plus any Gloat stacks from Phase 1 (+2 each). With 1-2 Gloats before transition, expect +8 to +14 Str. Execute (10x2) with +8 Str hits for 18x2=36.
- **Vulnerability window:** After Execute, The Champ spends one turn on the transition. Use this for block and preparation.

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Ironclad]]:**
- Demon Form: good but needs early play (need accumulated Str to outscale Champ's Phase 2 Str and Metallicize 5)
- Shockwave+: save for AFTER Anger if possible
- [[cards/Reaper]]: single-use -- time for post-Anger when attacks are strongest
- [[cards/Dark Shackles]]: excellent emergency post-Anger
- [[cards/Heavy Blade]]: strong finisher if Str is high from Demon Form
- **[[cards/Fiend Fire]] exhaust decks:** do NOT fire Fiend Fire in Phase 1 while debuffs (Disarm, Shockwave, [[cards/Exhume]]) held for Phase 2 are in hand — categorize your hand first; see [[layer:heuristics, cards/Fiend Fire]].

**[[characters/Defect]]:**
- [[cards/Barrage]] is the primary burst card (52+ damage with 5+ orbs and [[debuffs/Vulnerable]])
- [[cards/Echo Form]] doubling Barrage+ can deal 100+ in a single turn
- Frost wall for Phase 1 survival
- Re-apply Vulnerable after Anger ([[cards/Beam Cell]] or [[cards/Sweeping Beam]])
