# Mushrooms

- **Eat (Heal 21 HP + Parasite)** when: HP is below 30% AND a shop is reachable within 2-3 floors for curse removal, OR HP is so critically low that dying before reaching a shop is more likely than the curse killing you. The heal is NOT free -- weigh the Parasite cost. Parasite is an Unplayable card that clogs draws permanently until removed.
- **Stomp (Fight)** when: HP is above 70%, the deck does NOT rely on [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] for damage, AND the boss is Guardian or [[bosses/Slime Boss]] ([[relics/Odd Mushroom]] is less harmful against these bosses).
- **Leave** when: HP is above 40% or no shop is available to remove Parasite within 3 floors. The Parasite curse is worse than 21 HP in most situations.

HEXAGHOST ANTI-SYNERGY: Odd Mushroom reduces Vulnerable from 1.5x to 1.25x on enemies. Against [[bosses/Hexaghost]], where kill speed determines whether Burns overwhelm you, this ~17% damage reduction extends the fight by 1-2 turns. Take Eat or Leave instead.

Priority: Leave if HP above 40% or no shop nearby. Eat only at critical HP with shop access. Stomp only at 70%+ HP with non-Hexaghost boss.
