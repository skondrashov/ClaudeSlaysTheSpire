# Masked Bandits

Pay the toll at low HP where combat risks death. Gold is recoverable; HP is not. If below 60% HP and the alternative is a fight that could cascade into a death spiral, pay without hesitation. Gold has no value if you die.

Fight at high HP with a strong deck. The fight is an [[acts/Act 2]] hallway encounter, but price it like a hard one: at high ascension it has cost ~45 HP at competent play. Put that number on the ledger before choosing to fight — if the deck handles hard hallway fights cleanly and the HP is there, fight to preserve gold for shops.

**Kill order: [[enemies/Bear]] FIRST.** The trio is [[enemies/Romeo]], Bear, and [[enemies/Pointy]]. Bear's Bear Hug strips -2 [[buffs/Dexterity]], which degrades every Block card you play for the rest of the fight — that compounding debuff is what turns this into a ~45-HP grind. Focus-killing Bear before it can stack its debuff (rather than spreading chip across all three) collapses both the incoming damage and the block-loss leak; Romeo (Weak) and Pointy (flat 5x2) are far cheaper once Bear is down. Pre-commit the focus target before T1, not mid-fight.
