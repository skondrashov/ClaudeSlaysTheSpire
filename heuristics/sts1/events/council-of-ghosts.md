# Council of Ghosts

5 turns of invincibility is powerful, but the Max HP loss is devastating. At 100+ Max HP with a strong deck that ends fights quickly, Accept can work. At low Max HP (<80), Refuse -- being at 39 Max HP means one bad fight ends the run.

[[debuffs/Poison]] deck synergy: Apparitions are exceptionally powerful with a poison deck. [[buffs/Intangible]] buys time for poison to tick -- [[cards/Noxious Fumes]] applies damage every turn regardless of Intangible status. [[cards/Catalyst]] tripling a poison stack during Intangible turns is the optimal sequencing: build poison safely, then survive with block after Intangible expires. [[cards/Tools of the Trade]] filters Apparitions to when needed; [[relics/Hovering Kite]] generates energy from discarding unneeded ones.

Against [[debuffs/Hex]] ([[enemies/Chosen]]): Apparitions are Skills, so they trigger Hex (add [[cards/Dazed]]). Playing 3-4 Apparitions adds 3-4 Dazed. Still worth it -- Intangible is more valuable than the Dazed penalty.

Priority: Accept (poison deck with Noxious Fumes/Catalyst+, or 100+ Max HP) > Refuse (default, especially <80 Max HP without poison engine).
