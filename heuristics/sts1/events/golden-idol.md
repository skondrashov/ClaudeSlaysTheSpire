# Golden Idol

Golden Idol increases gold earned by ~25%. The trap costs are ranked: Smash (20 HP, recoverable at rest sites) > Outrun (6 Max HP, permanent loss) > Hide (curse, permanent deck pollution). At full HP, Smash is affordable. Below 50% HP, skip entirely.

Do NOT Smash before elites or bosses. 20 HP lost to Golden Idol is 20 HP less for the next fight. If the path ahead includes an elite within 1-2 floors, skip entirely or choose Outrun (6 Max HP is less dangerous than 20 HP when entering an elite at reduced HP).

Priority: Smash (high HP) > Outrun (if 6 Max HP is affordable) > Leave (low HP). Never choose Hide.
