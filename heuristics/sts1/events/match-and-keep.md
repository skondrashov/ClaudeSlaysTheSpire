# Match and Keep

Matched cards are added to your deck whether you want them or not -- unlike card rewards, you cannot refuse. This means the event can ADD UNWANTED CARDS (observed: [[cards/Bash]] and [[cards/Perfected Strike]] added to a deck that did not want them). Deck dilution from bad matches is a real downside.

Play only when the deck is small enough that a few mediocre additions are tolerable, or when you are desperate for any card quality.

Leave when the deck is already at a good size (14-18 cards) and adding random cards would dilute draws of key cards. This is the safer default choice.
