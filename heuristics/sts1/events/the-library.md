# The Library

Read is the default above ~60% HP or with a rest site adjacent. A pool of 20 cards is the largest selection in the game -- dramatically increases the odds of finding a specific card you need. If the deck still lacks a healing card, this is one of the best chances to find [[cards/Reaper]] or Feed (see the healing priority in [[layer:heuristics, drafting]]).

**Low-HP branch: Sleep heals ~1/3 of max HP.** At low HP with no rest site nearby, that heal can be worth more than a card — a great card in a run that dies two floors later is worth nothing.

Priority picks follow the same card evaluation rules as normal rewards, but the larger pool means you are more likely to find the exact card your deck needs rather than settling for the best of 3.
