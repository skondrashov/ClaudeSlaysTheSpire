# HP Management

- **HP is an act-level resource, not per-fight.** "I can afford 8 damage" thinking is wrong. The goal in EVERY hallway fight is zero damage taken. HP thresholds below are for MAP PATHING decisions.
- **HP Thresholds for Map Pathing**: ANY fight: 20% minimum. Hallway easy: 25%. Hallway hard (3 Cultists, Centurion+Mystic, Snake Plant): 60%. Elite: 60%. Boss: 70% or Pantograph.
- **3 Cultists is the most lethal hallway fight** — 6 deaths at 5-53% HP. Triple +3 Str/turn = +9 combined. Hard-scaler exception. 60% entry NON-NEGOTIABLE.
- **What to Do at Low HP**: Take a different path (event/rest/shop). If forced into fight below threshold, use ALL potions turn 1. POTION CHECK ON EVERY LETHAL TURN. After fight drops below 30%, next room MUST be non-combat. Low HP does NOT change combat behavior — you should already be full blocking.
- **The Act 2 Death Spiral**: Enter Act 2 at reasonable HP → take unnecessary damage (not full blocking) → enter next fight at critical HP → die. After ANY fight below 30%, next room must be non-combat. If map doesn't offer this, run was lost at map selection.
- **What Causes HP Drain**: Byrd fights (36-73 HP), Centurion+Mystic (25-61 HP), Chosen (30-40 HP), Snake Plant (15-42 HP), Spheric Guardian (30-41 HP), no healing between fights, Fairy consumed in wrong fight, Decay curse compound damage, Vampires event Max HP loss.
- **Act 2 Preparation**: Enter at full HP. Upgrades matter more (upgrade over rest when above 50%). Healing sustain is critical — Burning Blood cannot compensate for 30-50 HP fights. Minimum TWO healing sources (not counting rest sites) by Act 2 Floor 5.
- **Act 3 HP Thresholds**: Hallway easy: 40%. Hallway hard: 60%. Transient: 50%. The Maw: 60%. Elite: 70%. Boss: 70% or Pantograph.
