# Sssserpent

Take the gold if a shop is within 2-3 floors (remove curse for 75g, net 100g profit). Refuse if no shop is reachable before a boss or elite.

Doubt is a manageable curse -- Weak on yourself reduces your attack damage by 25%, but does not affect block. If the curse gets stuck in hand for 1-2 fights before removal, the cost is reduced damage output, not death.

Many events that offer gold + curse are designed with the expectation that you can route to a shop to remove the curse. The correct play is often: take the gold, route to a shop, remove the curse, net profit. Do not refuse the gold reflexively because "curses are bad" -- evaluate the map first.
