# Knowing Skull

Default: Leave immediately (pay the 6 HP exit cost and nothing more). The HP cost is rarely worth the reward, especially in Act 2 where HP is the scarcest resource. Even taking one ask (6 HP) and then leaving (6 HP) costs 12 HP total -- entering the dialogue commits you to at least 6 HP loss.

Do not engage near the boss. A 12 HP loss before elite territory or boss can be the difference between winning and dying.

Engage only at high HP (70%+) with a specific, critical deck gap (e.g., need exhaust tools before Book of Stabbing, need AOE before Byrds) AND at least 5 floors before the boss. Limit to one ask maximum. Never take multiple asks -- the escalating cost compounds into a death spiral.

At low HP, always leave immediately (accept the 6 HP exit cost).
