# Mysterious Sphere

Open Sphere when:
- HP is above 70% AND healing is available (rest sites ahead, no Mark of the Bloom)
- The deck can handle 2 Orb Walkers without using irreplaceable boss resources (Catalyst+)
- A Rare Relic could meaningfully improve the boss fight

Leave when:
- Mark of the Bloom is active (any damage taken is permanent)
- HP is below 50%
- The boss fight is imminent and Catalyst+ would need to be spent here
- The deck already has strong relics and the marginal value of another is low

The Rare Relic is not worth the risk if any damage taken here cannot be recovered before the boss. A dead run gets zero value from a Rare Relic.
