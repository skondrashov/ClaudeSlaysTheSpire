# Mind Bloom

**Ginger equipped:** Choose "I am Healthy" (free full heal, Doubt curse is harmless since Ginger prevents Weakened, remove at shop).

**Strong deck:** Choose "I am War" -- an Act 1 boss (Guardian, Slime Boss, Hexaghost) is trivial with an Act 3 deck. The Rare Relic reward is guaranteed and can be game-changing.

**I am Awake is a TRAP.** Mark of the Bloom prevents ALL healing for the rest of the run -- rest sites cannot heal, potions cannot heal, Burning Blood does nothing, Feed does nothing. Every point of damage from this moment forward is permanent. Only consider if ALL of the following are true: (1) multiple unupgraded cards with transformative upgrades, (2) ZERO remaining combat encounters on the path to the boss, AND (3) current HP is near max.

**I am Rich is a trap with Snecko Eye.** Normality limits the hand to 3 cards per turn. With Snecko Eye drawing 7 cards, 4 are immediately wasted. The 999 gold is useless in late Act 3 (few shops remaining).

Priority: Healthy (with Ginger) > War (strong deck) > Awake (almost never) > Rich (never).
