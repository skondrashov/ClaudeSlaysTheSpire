# Council of Ghosts

5 turns of invincibility is powerful, but the Max HP loss is devastating. At 100+ Max HP with a strong deck that ends fights quickly, Accept can work. At low Max HP (<80), Refuse -- being at 39 Max HP means one bad fight ends the run.

Poison deck synergy: Apparitions are exceptionally powerful with a poison deck. Intangible buys time for poison to tick -- Noxious Fumes applies damage every turn regardless of Intangible status. Catalyst+ tripling a poison stack during Intangible turns is the optimal sequencing: build poison safely, then survive with block after Intangible expires. Tools of the Trade filters Apparitions to when needed; Hovering Kite generates energy from discarding unneeded ones.

Against Hex (Chosen): Apparitions are Skills, so they trigger Hex (add Dazed). Playing 3-4 Apparitions adds 3-4 Dazed. Still worth it -- Intangible is more valuable than the Dazed penalty.

Priority: Accept (poison deck with Noxious Fumes/Catalyst+, or 100+ Max HP) > Refuse (default, especially <80 Max HP without poison engine).
