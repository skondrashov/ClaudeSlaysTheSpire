# Masked Bandits

Pay the toll at low HP where combat risks death. Gold is recoverable; HP is not. If below 60% HP and the alternative is a fight that could cascade into a death spiral, pay without hesitation. Gold has no value if you die.

Fight at high HP with a strong deck. The fight is a standard Act 2 hallway encounter. If the deck handles hallway fights cleanly, fight to preserve gold for shops.
