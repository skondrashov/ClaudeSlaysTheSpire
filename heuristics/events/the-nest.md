# The Nest

At low HP (<30), always take gold. 6 HP can be lethal when consecutive fights follow. Ritual Dagger is a long-term investment card -- only worth it if many fights remain AND current HP can absorb the 6 HP loss without entering the next fight below threshold. 99 Gold is reliable value (enables shop purchases, curse removal, potions).

If next room is combat and HP is marginal, gold is correct. The 6 HP loss cannot be recovered before the fight.
