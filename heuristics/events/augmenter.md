# Augmenter

**Transform 2 is usually best.** Removes two of your worst cards (Strikes/Defends) and replaces with random cards from your character pool. The variance is high but the floor removal is guaranteed value.

**Test J.A.X. is niche.** J.A.X. deals 2 damage × hand size (typically 10-14 damage for 0E). Only worth it with large hand sizes ([[relics/Snecko Eye]] draws 7, [[relics/Runic Pyramid]] retains full hand) or when you need a 0-cost exhaust target.

**Leave when:** Deck is already tight and Transform might remove a key card you cannot afford to lose. Or when you're in a deck where random card additions are dangerous (e.g., [[bosses/Awakened One]] boss with a Power-light strategy).
