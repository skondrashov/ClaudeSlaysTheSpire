# The Library

Always choose Read. A pool of 20 cards is the largest selection in the game -- dramatically increases the odds of finding a specific card you need. This is the best opportunity to find [[cards/Reaper]], Feed, or other critical cards that may not have appeared in normal rewards.

At any point where a healing card (Reaper, Feed) has not yet been found, The Library is potentially game-saving. The probability of at least one healing card appearing in 20 random cards is much higher than in the standard 3-card reward.

Priority picks follow the same card evaluation rules as normal rewards, but the larger pool means you are more likely to find the exact card your deck needs rather than settling for the best of 3.
