# Vampires

The Max HP loss is SEVERE. Losing 24 Max HP (from 80 to 56) means every HP threshold shifts drastically. Boss HP thresholds (70% = 39 HP instead of 56 HP) become razor-thin.

The healing from Bites (2 HP per Bite played) is meaningful over long fights but cannot compensate for the reduced HP pool against burst damage. Removing all Strikes is good for deck quality (Bites are strictly better: same cost, similar damage, plus healing).

AVOID if: you are below 70% HP, approaching a boss or elite, or your deck already has healing (Reaper, Feed). The Max HP loss compounds with Act 2's HP drain fights.

CONSIDER if: you have no other healing sources and are at high HP with a safe path ahead. At 56 Max HP, the Gremlin Leader elite (27+ damage/turn with Str scaling) and 3 Cultists become nearly impossible to survive.

Priority: Refuse (most situations) > Accept (only at high HP with no healing sources and a safe upcoming path).
