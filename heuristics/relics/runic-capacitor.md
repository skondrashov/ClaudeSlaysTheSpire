# Runic Capacitor

Doubles starting orb capacity. More slots means more simultaneous passive effects and dramatically higher [[cards/Barrage]] damage.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **Barrage+**: Each additional filled slot adds 7 damage. 3 extra slots = +21 Barrage+ damage.
- **[[cards/Capacitor]] (card)**: Stacks for even more slots. Runic Capacitor + Capacitor+ = 9 orb slots.
- **[[cards/Coolheaded]]**: More slots means Frost orbs persist longer before being pushed out. Frost wall is more stable.
- **[[cards/Blizzard]]**: More Frost orbs channeled = higher Blizzard damage.

With 6 slots filled with Frost orbs at Focus 2: 24 passive block/turn. Enables truly massive orb boards.
