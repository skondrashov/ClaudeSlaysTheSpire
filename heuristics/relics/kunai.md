# Kunai

Passive Dexterity scaling from playing Attacks. In long fights (bosses, elites), compounds: by turn 5-6, block cards generate 3-5 more block each, drastically improving survivability.

Best in Attack-heavy decks with cheap Attacks (Anger at 0E, Pommel Strike, Twin Strike, Headbutt). Pairs naturally with Ironclad's Attack-heavy card pool. Purely upside with no drawback -- always take when offered.

SYNERGY: Shuriken (same trigger -- playing 3 Attacks gives +1 Dex AND +1 Str if both relics held).
