# Kunai

Passive [[buffs/Dexterity]] scaling from playing Attacks. In long fights (bosses, elites), compounds: by turn 5-6, block cards generate 3-5 more block each, drastically improving survivability.

Best in Attack-heavy decks with cheap Attacks ([[cards/Anger]] at 0E, [[cards/Pommel Strike]], [[cards/Twin Strike]], [[cards/Headbutt]]). Pairs naturally with [[characters/Ironclad]]'s Attack-heavy card pool. Purely upside with no drawback -- always take when offered.

SYNERGY: [[relics/Shuriken]] (same trigger -- playing 3 Attacks gives +1 Dex AND +1 Str if both relics held).
