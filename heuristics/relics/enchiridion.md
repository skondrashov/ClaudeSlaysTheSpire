# Enchiridion

Free Power every fight. Can generate [[cards/Metallicize]], [[cards/Inflame]], [[cards/Corruption]], [[cards/Dark Embrace]], or other Powers. The 0 cost means it never competes with other turn 1 plays. High variance -- sometimes generates a duplicate of a Power you already have. Overall excellent value for free.

DANGER -- UNCONTROLLABLE POWER SELECTION: The Power is random. Enchiridion can generate [[cards/Brutality]] (lose 1 HP/turn), which is actively harmful in long fights or at low HP. Before playing an Enchiridion-generated Power, evaluate whether it helps or hurts in THIS specific fight. Do not autopilot "free Power = always play."

DANGEROUS POWERS: Brutality (HP drain in long fights), [[cards/Berserk]] ([[debuffs/Vulnerable]] to self), [[cards/Combust]] (HP drain per turn). Safe powers: Metallicize, Inflame, Dark Embrace, [[cards/Feel No Pain]], Rage.
