# Enchiridion

Free Power every fight. Can generate Metallicize, Inflame, Corruption, Dark Embrace, or other Powers. The 0 cost means it never competes with other turn 1 plays. High variance -- sometimes generates a duplicate of a Power you already have. Overall excellent value for free.

DANGER -- UNCONTROLLABLE POWER SELECTION: The Power is random. Enchiridion can generate Brutality (lose 1 HP/turn), which is actively harmful in long fights or at low HP. Before playing an Enchiridion-generated Power, evaluate whether it helps or hurts in THIS specific fight. Do not autopilot "free Power = always play."

DANGEROUS POWERS: Brutality (HP drain in long fights), Berserk (Vulnerable to self), Combust (HP drain per turn). Safe powers: Metallicize, Inflame, Dark Embrace, Feel No Pain, Rage.
