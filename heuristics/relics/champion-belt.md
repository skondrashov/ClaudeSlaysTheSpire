# Champion Belt

Turns Bash+ into a dual-debuff card (3 Vulnerable + 1 Weak) for free. Every Bash+ play both increases your damage AND reduces incoming damage simultaneously.

SYNERGIES: Bash+ (main trigger), Thunderclap (mass Vuln = mass Weak to all enemies), Paper Phrog (75% Vuln + Weak = massive amplification with damage reduction).

Excellent defensive relic requiring no deck changes. Since Bash+ is played in nearly every combat, provides passive Weak application for free. Reduces the need for dedicated Weak sources (Intimidate, Shockwave, Clothesline).
