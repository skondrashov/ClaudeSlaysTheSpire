# Meat on the Bone

In Act 2, fights routinely drain 30-50 HP and leave the player below 50%. Meat on the Bone provides double the healing of Burning Blood (12 vs 6) when it triggers. Combined with Burning Blood, a fight ending below 50% heals 18 HP total.

Does not trigger when HP is above 50% at fight end. Efficient fights where block prevents most damage get zero value. Does not help DURING combat -- only after.

Partially addresses the Act 2 HP sustain problem. Not a replacement for in-combat healing (Reaper, Feed) but a solid supplementary source. Always take when offered -- unconditional upside with no drawback.
