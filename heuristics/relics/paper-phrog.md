# Paper Phrog

Massively amplifies all damage against Vulnerable targets. Bash+ becomes a much stronger setup card. Over a multi-turn Vulnerable window (Bash+ = 3 turns), adds up to massive extra damage.

MATH (with Paper Phrog):
- Strike (6) vs Vulnerable: 10 (vs 9 without)
- Immolate+ (28) vs Vulnerable: 49 (vs 42 without)
- Twin Strike (5+5) vs Vulnerable: 17 (vs 14 without)

SYNERGIES: Bash+ (3 turns amplified Vulnerable), Thunderclap (mass Vulnerable), Bag of Marbles (free Turn 1 Vulnerable), any high-damage attack card.

One of the best common relics for Ironclad. Since Bash+ applies Vulnerable in every fight, provides permanent damage amplification. Stronger than most uncommon relics.
