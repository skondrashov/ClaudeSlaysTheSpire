# Bottled Lightning

Guarantees a specific Skill on Turn 1 of every fight. Provides perfect consistency for engine-defining cards.

BEST TARGETS:
- **Seek/Seek+** (Defect): Guaranteed Turn 1 tutor that finds Echo Form, Barrage+, or any needed card. Strongest known target for Defect.
- **Offering** (Ironclad): Guaranteed Turn 1 energy burst + draw.
- **Coolheaded+** (Defect): Guaranteed Turn 1 Frost orb + 3 draws.

Confirmed dominant: Bottled Lightning on Seek+ guaranteed Echo Form+ in hand Turn 1 of every boss fight in the first Defect victory. This single relic-card combination was the foundation of the winning engine.
