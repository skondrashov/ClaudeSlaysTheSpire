# Coffee Dripper

Strong energy boost but removes the primary healing mechanism. Rest sites become upgrade-only, leaving potions, events, healing cards, and relics as remaining healing sources.

WHEN TO TAKE:
- Deck nearly complete and rest sites would be used for upgrades anyway.
- Multiple healing sources exist (healing cards, potions, relics).
- Energy-hungry deck benefits greatly from extra energy.
- Ironclad with Burning Blood providing baseline healing per fight.

WHEN TO AVOID:
- Early Act 1 when HP management is uncertain.
- No healing sources beyond rest sites.
- Silent without healing cards or potions (no passive heal equivalent to Burning Blood).

CRITICAL ANTI-SYNERGY -- COFFEE DRIPPER + ECTOPLASM: No healing from rest sites AND no gold to buy potions/healing at shops. Eliminates two of three primary recovery paths. Avoid this combination unless the deck reliably takes zero damage.
