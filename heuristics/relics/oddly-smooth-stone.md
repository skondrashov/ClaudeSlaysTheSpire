# Oddly Smooth Stone

Permanent +1 block on every block card played. Defend generates 6 block instead of 5. [[cards/Shrug It Off]] generates 9 instead of 8. Compounds with multiple block cards per turn. Over a long fight: 10-20 extra block total.

Solid common relic with no downside. Less impactful than [[relics/Vajra]] (+1 [[buffs/Strength]]) for [[characters/Ironclad]] because Ironclad's win condition is typically killing fast, not blocking more. But in [[acts/Act 2]] where chip damage accumulates, the extra block helps reduce HP drain between rest sites.
