# Ginger

Immunity to an entire debuff type is always valuable.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
1. **[[events/Mind Bloom]] "I am Healthy" becomes free.** The Doubt curse (1 [[debuffs/Weak]]/turn) is completely harmless with Ginger. Choose it for a free full heal, then remove Doubt at the next shop.
2. **Enemy Weak applications ignored.** [[enemies/Chosen]]'s [[debuffs/Hex]], Collector's debuff, [[bosses/Time Eater]]'s Ripple -- no effect on damage output.
3. **Block calculations simplified.** Damage output is always at full value.

SYNERGIES:
- [[cards/Shiv]] decks benefit most since Weak would reduce every individual Shiv hit.
- Boss fights with Weak application (Collector, Time Eater) become safer offensively.

High draft priority. The Mind Bloom interaction alone can provide 15-30 HP of free healing.
