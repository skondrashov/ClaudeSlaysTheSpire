# Bird-Faced Urn

Each Power played becomes a small heal. In Power-heavy decks (3+ Powers), adds 6+ HP healing per combat, partially offsetting the Burning Blood gap. Incentivizes taking and playing Power cards.

SYNERGIES: Mummified Hand (free Power plays = free healing), Corruption (frees energy for Powers), multiple cheap Powers (Inflame 1E, Metallicize 1E) maximize triggers per fight.

Best in long fights (bosses, elites) where multiple Powers are played. Less impactful in short hallway fights where only 1 Power is played. Does not replace real healing cards (Reaper, Feed) but supplements them.
