# Horn Cleat

Provides free 14 block on turn 2, which is often the first big attack turn from enemies. Effectively a free Defend + Shrug It Off on the turn you need it most. No action required -- automatic.

14 block covers most single-enemy attacks in Act 1 and partially mitigates Act 2 attacks. Frees up energy on turn 2 to play offensive cards instead of blocking. Helpful against Cultists (buff turn 1, attack turn 2 -- Horn Cleat covers the attack).
