# Pure Water

Effectively +1 energy on Turn 1 of every combat. The [[cards/Miracle]] Retains, so it can be saved for a turn where the energy matters most (e.g., a burst turn exiting Calm into Wrath).

INTERACTION WITH DEUS EX MACHINA: Generates 2-3 additional Miracles when drawn. Combined with Pure Water's Turn 1 Miracle, accumulate 3-4 Miracles. Managing these (not wasting on turns with no useful plays) is critical.

BOSS RELIC SWAP: Losing guaranteed +1E per combat is a real cost, especially early when few energy-generating cards exist. Keep Pure Water unless the boss relic is clearly superior ([[relics/Snecko Eye]], [[relics/Pandora's Box]], etc.).
