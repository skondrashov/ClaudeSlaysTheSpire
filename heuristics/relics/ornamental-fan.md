# Ornamental Fan

Moderate value. Provides passive block in attack-heavy turns without costing energy. Works well in decks that naturally play 3+ attacks per turn.

Less useful in Corruption engine decks where most plays are Skills (which exhaust for block via Feel No Pain instead). The 4 block per 3 attacks is small relative to 30-40 block from Impervious or FNP chains.

SYNERGY: High-attack decks, Snecko Eye (many cheap attacks), Anger (0E attack helps reach 3 plays).
