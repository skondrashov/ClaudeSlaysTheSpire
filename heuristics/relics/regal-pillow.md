# Regal Pillow

Turns rest sites from 24 HP healing to 39 HP healing. In Act 3 where HP management is critical, can restore nearly half of max HP at each rest site. Makes it safer to choose Rest over Smith when HP is below threshold.

Strong utility relic that patches healing gaps. Enabled healing from 53 HP to 80/80 (full HP) at the pre-boss rest site in the victory run, ensuring 100% HP entry that contributed to the win.
