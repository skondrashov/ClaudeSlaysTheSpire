# Slaver's Collar

The extra energy in elite and boss fights is significant -- it allows playing an additional card per turn in the hardest fights. A 4E turn means you can play Bash+ (2E) + Rampage (1E) + Defend (1E) instead of choosing between offense and defense.

High value for elite-heavy paths. If your route includes 2-3 elites and the Act boss, the relic provides value in 3-4 of the hardest fights. Less valuable if avoiding elites.

Pairs well with expensive cards (Bludgeon at 3E, Reaper at 2E) that normally crowd out other plays.
