# Hovering Kite

Effectively raises base energy from 3 to 4 (or 4 to 5) when combined with any discard source.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **[[cards/Tools of the Trade]]**: Discards at start of turn, triggering Kite automatically. +1E every turn with zero effort.
- **[[cards/Survivor]]/Survivor+**: Manual discard trigger. Block + energy in one play.
- **[[cards/Tactician]]**: When discarded, returns 2E. Combined with Kite's +1E, a single TotT discard of Tactician+ generates +3E total.
- **[[cards/Calculated Gamble]]**: Discard entire hand, Kite triggers on first discard.

Excellent when the deck has discard synergy (Tools of the Trade, Survivor+, Tactician+). Also useful for discarding Burns and curses -- turning dead cards into energy.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Decks without discard sources get inconsistent value. [[relics/Snecko Eye]] randomizes costs, making +1E less impactful when cards already cost 0.
