# Nunchaku

In Attack-heavy decks, provides a reliable energy boost every few turns. With Snecko Eye drawing 7 cards/turn and 4-5 being Attacks, triggers every 2-3 turns. The bonus energy enables playing one more card on the trigger turn.

SYNERGIES: Attack-heavy decks (Anger adds copies, increasing density), Snecko Eye (more cards drawn = more Attacks played). Multi-hit attacks like Pummel count as one play toward the counter, not per hit.

Solid common relic. Not flashy but reliable. Counter persists across combats.
