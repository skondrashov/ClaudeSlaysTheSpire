# Data Disk

Focus is the Defect's primary scaling stat. Each point adds 1 to every orb passive (Frost block, Lightning damage, Dark scaling). With 3 Frost orbs: +3 block per turn passively. Over a 10-turn fight: 30+ block from a single relic.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **Frost orbs**: +1 passive block per orb per turn. With 5 Frost: +5 passive block/turn.
- **Defragment**: Stacks for total Focus 2+ making orb passives immediately impactful.
- **Loop/Emotion Chip**: Focus bonus applies to all passive triggers.

Provides baseline Focus that makes Frost orb passives meaningful from Turn 1, before additional Focus scaling is established.
