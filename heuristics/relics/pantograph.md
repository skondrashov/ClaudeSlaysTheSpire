# Pantograph

Strong but not game-changing. 25 HP is significant but does not remove the need to manage HP before bosses. Upgrading at rest sites before bosses is still good, but only if the 25 HP heal brings you above a survivable threshold.

CRITICAL CORRECTION: Pantograph heals exactly 25 HP, NOT to full. Plan boss HP thresholds accordingly -- you need to enter bosses at no more than 25 HP below max to benefit fully. Entering a boss at 30% HP with 87 Max HP means healing to ~54%, not full.

With Sozu (no potions), Pantograph is still valuable as reliable healing, but it is NOT a full heal replacement.
