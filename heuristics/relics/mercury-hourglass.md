# Mercury Hourglass

Free passive AOE damage every turn. Over a 10-turn boss fight: 30 free damage to every enemy. Against multi-enemy fights (Collector + 2 Torch Heads): 3 to each = 9 total per turn.

Best in long fights and multi-enemy encounters. [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] on enemies amplifies it (3 * 1.5 = 4 per enemy). Can finish low-HP enemies at start of turn without triggering additional effects (killed [[enemies/Snake Plant]] without triggering [[buffs/Malleable]] block).
