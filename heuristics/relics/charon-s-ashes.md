# Charon's Ashes

Creates an exhaust damage engine. With True Grit (exhaust per turn) and Dark Embrace (draw on exhaust), every exhaust = 3 AOE damage + draw. Over 14 turns: ~30+ total damage.

DANGER: encourages over-exhausting, which can thin the deck below survival threshold. Don't exhaust cards just for 3 damage -- consider whether you need the cards for later turns.
