# Bag of Marbles

Free Vulnerable on Turn 1 without spending energy or a card play. Enables aggressive Turn 1 plays -- every attack card deals significantly more damage before Bash+ is even needed.

SYNERGIES: Paper Phrog (1.75x damage to all enemies Turn 1 for free), Immolate+ (28 * 1.75 = 49 AOE Turn 1), Heavy Blade (Str multiplied + Vuln), any front-loaded damage strategy.

One of the best common relics. Free damage amplification on Turn 1 of every combat.
