# Whetstone

Equivalent to two rest site upgrades for Attacks specifically, but with no control over which cards are chosen. Best when the deck has multiple high-priority Attack upgrades (Heavy Blade 3x->5x, Carnage 20->28, Twin Strike, Pommel Strike). Worst when key Attacks are already upgraded or only Strikes remain.

Strong early (more Attacks in deck = higher chance of hitting good targets, plus upgrades compound over more fights). Weaker late. Always acceptable as a free relic with no downside.
