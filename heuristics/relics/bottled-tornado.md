# Bottled Tornado

Guarantees a specific Power from Turn 1 of every fight. The value depends entirely on which Power is bottled.

BEST TARGETS (Silent):
1. **After Image** (victory-proven) -- 1 block per card played from Turn 1.
2. **Noxious Fumes+** -- Passive poison from Turn 1.
3. **Footwork+** -- +3 Dex from Turn 1.

BEST TARGETS (Ironclad):
1. **Barricade** -- Block persists from Turn 1. Excellent with Corruption engine.
2. **Metallicize** -- Passive block every turn from Turn 1.
3. **Inflame** -- +2 Str from Turn 1.

High draft priority when you have a Power that you always want Turn 1. After Image innate is game-defining; a situational Power is less impactful.
