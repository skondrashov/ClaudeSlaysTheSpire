# Calipers

Turns any large block turn into sustained defense. Eliminates the "wasted block" problem where overblocking a small attack loses the excess.

MATH:
- [[cards/Impervious]] (40 block): 25 retained next turn.
- Impervious (30 block): 15 retained next turn.
- [[cards/Shrug It Off]] + 2 Defends (18 block): 3 retained next turn.

SYNERGIES: Impervious/Impervious+ (retains massive amounts), [[cards/Metallicize]] (passive block stacks with retention). Strongest in fights with alternating high/low damage turns where you can stack block on low turns and retain for high turns.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: [[cards/Barricade]] (already retains all block -- Calipers redundant). Decks generating exactly 5-15 block per turn see no benefit (all decays away).

Best in decks with Impervious or other large block sources. Does NOT replace the need for healing.
