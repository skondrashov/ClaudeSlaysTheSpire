# Red Mask

Free damage reduction every fight with zero player input. 1 [[debuffs/Weak]] = 25% less enemy damage on turn 1. Against multi-enemy fights (3 Cultists, Byrds), applies to every enemy simultaneously.

**[[buffs/Artifact]] stripping**: Against enemies with Artifact ([[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] Artifact 3, [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]] Artifact 3), Red Mask strips 1 Artifact charge automatically before the player's first turn, reducing the stripping requirement by 1.

Against enemies that buff turn 1 (Cultists, some Fungi Beasts), the Weak is wasted since they don't attack. Provides consistent incremental value across every single fight for the rest of the run.
