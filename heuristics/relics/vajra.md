# Vajra

Permanent, unconditional +1 damage to every Attack play for every fight. Modest on its own, but compounds with Strength-scaling cards and effects. Over a 5-turn fight playing 2 Attacks/turn: +10 total damage. Multi-hit cards benefit especially: Twin Strike gains +2 total, Pummel gains +4-5 total.

SYNERGIES:
- **Heavy Blade** -- +1 Str becomes +3 damage (or +5 upgraded) due to the multiplier.
- **Limit Break** -- doubling Strength that starts at 1 instead of 0 is the difference between the card doing nothing and doing something.
- **Reaper** -- more Strength = more damage to all enemies = more healing.

Safe value from Neow or early chests. No downside. Particularly strong when building toward Strength scaling (Inflame, Limit Break, Heavy Blade).
