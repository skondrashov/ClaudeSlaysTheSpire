# Black Star

Doubles relic acquisition from elite fights. In a run fighting 3-4 elites, provides 3-4 extra relics -- each potentially game-changing.

DECISION FRAMEWORK: Best taken at end of Act 1 (before Act 2/3 elites). Weaker after Act 2 (only Act 3 elites remain). Requires actively routing through elites to realize value -- taking Black Star and avoiding elites wastes the relic slot.

RISK: Boss relics replace the starter relic. Losing Burning Blood's sustain must be weighed against long-term relic advantage. For the Defect, losing Cracked Core is relatively low-cost compared to losing Burning Blood.
