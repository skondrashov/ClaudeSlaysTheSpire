# Mark of Pain

Strong boss relic. 4E per turn is a massive upgrade. The Wound downside is manageable and can become an advantage with the right cards.

SYNERGIES:
- **[[cards/Evolve]] (BEST)**: Each Wound drawn triggers 2 additional draws. Playing attacks generates future card advantage. If Evolve+ is already in deck, Mark of Pain is the clear best boss relic choice.
- **Evolve (unupgraded)**: Each Wound triggers 1 draw. Upgrade Evolve ASAP after taking Mark of Pain.
- **[[cards/Fire Breathing]]**: Wounds trigger Fire Breathing damage to all enemies.
- **[[cards/Second Wind]]**: Exhausts Wounds for 5 block each.
- **[[cards/True Grit]]**: [[enemies/Chosen]] exhaust can target Wounds specifically.

ANTI-SYNERGIES:
- **No Evolve/Status management**: Wounds clog draw pile. By turn 4-5, drawing 2-3 Wounds/hand reduces effective hand to 2-3 real cards.
- **[[cards/Battle Trance]]**: "Cannot draw additional cards" prevents Evolve triggering on Wound draws that turn.

WHEN TO TAKE: When Evolve is already in deck. A 15+ card deck dilutes Wounds better than a 10-card deck.

WHEN TO AVOID: No Wound management (no Evolve, Fire Breathing, Second Wind, True Grit+) and small deck (under 12 cards).
