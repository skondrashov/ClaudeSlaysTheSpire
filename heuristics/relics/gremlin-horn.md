# Gremlin Horn

Transforms multi-enemy fights from resource-draining attrition into snowball opportunities. Against 4 Gremlins, killing 3 provides +3E and +3 cards -- enough for an entire extra turn's worth of actions.

SYNERGIES:
- **AOE damage** (Thunderclap, Cleave, Whirlwind) -- weakens multiple enemies, triggering chain kills.
- **Slime Boss split** -- kills during the split phase refund energy for handling new slimes.
- **Any multi-enemy fight** -- 3 Cultists, Gremlins, Slavers, Centurion+Mystic.

Always take. No downside. In single-enemy fights it does nothing harmful. In multi-enemy fights (the most common dangerous Act 2 encounters), it is one of the strongest relics available.
