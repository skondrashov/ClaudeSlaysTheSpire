# Snecko Eye

Drawing 7 cards per turn means reliably finding key combo pieces every turn. Cost randomization averages 1.5E/card, but high-cost cards (2E+) benefit enormously -- 25% chance of costing 0E each turn. Present in both consecutive Ironclad victories.

WHEN TO TAKE: Deck has multiple 2E+ cards. The more expensive the average card cost, the better. A deck with Immolate+, Heavy Blade, Impervious, Flame Barrier, Bash+ has 5 cards that can become free.

WHEN TO AVOID: Decks built around 0-cost cards (Anger, Rage) or X-cost cards (Whirlwind) get worse. 0-cost cards randomized to 1-3 makes them worse.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **Gambling Chip**: Mulligan bad cost rolls for new ones. Dramatically improves Turn 1.
- **Necronomicon**: Cards randomized to 2+ trigger doubling. Dramatically increases trigger frequency.
- **Headbutt**: Top-decked cards get new cost rolls.
- **Nunchaku**: 7 cards drawn = more Attacks played per turn.

S-tier for Strength-scaling decks with expensive power cards. The extra draws solve consistency problems and cost randomization provides massive energy efficiency. Take as Act 2 boss relic whenever deck has high-cost cards.

DEFECT VALUE: Strong because Defect's orb-based defense partially compensates for bad cost rolls on block cards (orbs generate block automatically without playing cards).
