# Busted Crown

Going from 3E to 4E is transformative. Every turn plays an extra card. On Turn 1, enables [[cards/Bash]] (2E) + Defend (1E) + another card (1E), impossible at 3E.

DOWNSIDE: Card rewards reduced from 3 to 1. Makes finding specific cards ([[cards/Reaper]], [[cards/Impervious]]) much harder.

WHEN TO TAKE: When the deck's core cards are already acquired (by [[acts/Act 1]] boss reward). The extra energy helps play those cards more effectively.

WHEN TO AVOID: If the deck is still missing critical cards (healing, block scaling) and needs card rewards to find them. If [[relics/Snecko Eye]] is also being considered, Busted Crown loses value since Snecko provides its own draw advantage.

The +1E is reliable every turn and immediately impactful. The card reward reduction is real but can be mitigated by shops and [[relics/Dream Catcher]].
