# Eternal Feather

Supplements Burning Blood's flat 6 HP/fight. At 15 cards: +9 HP/fight (total 15 with Burning Blood). At 20 cards: +12 HP/fight. At 25 cards: +15 HP/fight. Rewards larger decks, which conflicts with typical deck-thinning strategy.

Do not take extra cards just to increase Eternal Feather healing -- deck quality matters more than marginal HP gains. In a typical 15-20 card deck, the healing is modest (9-12 HP). Only valuable when the run already has a larger deck and no better relic options. Mediocre overall.
