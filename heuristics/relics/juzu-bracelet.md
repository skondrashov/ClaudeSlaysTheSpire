# Juzu Bracelet

Extremely high value. Unknown rooms become free value (events) instead of potential death traps. Fundamentally changes [[acts/Act 2]] routing -- Unknown rooms become preferable to Monster rooms.

In Act 2, Unknown rooms can spawn [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]] (30-41 HP drain), Byrds (36-73 HP drain), or [[enemies/Snake Plant]] (15-42 HP drain). Juzu Bracelet eliminates this risk entirely.

Worth buying at ~147 gold if gold allows. Route through Unknown rooms for guaranteed safe rooms.
