# Sling of Courage

Free permanent Strength in the two most important fight types. With Heavy Blade (3x multiplier), +2 Str becomes +6 damage per play. With Heavy Blade+ (5x), it becomes +10. Compounds with Spot Weakness+ and Limit Break (doubles all Str including this bonus).

Excellent shop purchase for Strength-scaling decks. Every point of base Strength has outsized value due to multiplicative scaling with Heavy Blade and Limit Break.
