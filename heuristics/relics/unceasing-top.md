# Unceasing Top

Creates a draw engine with any card that empties your hand. One of the strongest relics for exhaust-focused decks. Less useful in decks with large hands that never empty.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **Fiend Fire** (CRITICAL): Exhausts hand -> empty -> draw -> play -> empty -> draw. Infinite draw engine limited only by energy and draw pile size.
- **0-cost cards**: Bloodletting (gain 2 energy, hand empties), Rage (play for free, hand empties), Inflame/powers (play, hand empties) all trigger additional draws.
- **Offering**: Lose 6 HP but gain 2 energy + draw 3. Combined with Top, massive turns.
