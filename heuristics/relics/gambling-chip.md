# Gambling Chip

The mulligan effect lets you find key cards (Bash+, Inflame+, Shockwave, Whirlwind) on Turn 1. Consistently useful in every combat.

FIGHT-SPECIFIC MULLIGANS:
- **Gremlin Leader**: Discard block, keep attacks. Turn economy demands damage from Turn 1.
- **Spheric Guardian**: Discard block, keep Artifact strippers and attacks. DPS check.
- **Hallway fights**: Discard expensive Powers, keep Bash+ and cheap attacks.
- **Chosen/Hex**: Discard Skills (trigger Hex adding Dazed). Keep Attacks and Powers.
- **Boss fights**: Discard based on boss pattern.

SYNERGIES: Lean decks (15-17 cards, mulligan more likely to find specific answers). Cards with fight-specific value can be safely included because they can be discarded when not needed.

**SNECKO EYE SYNERGY**: When Snecko assigns expensive costs (3E) to key cards, Gambling Chip lets you discard and redraw with new cost rolls. Dramatically improves Turn 1 consistency.
