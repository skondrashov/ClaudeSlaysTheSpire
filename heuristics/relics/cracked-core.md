# Cracked Core

Provides a guaranteed Lightning orb from Turn 1 of every fight without spending a card play or energy. The Lightning orb deals 3 passive damage at end of turn. Combined with Zap (0E, channel another), Defect can have 2 Lightning orbs dealing 6 passive damage per turn from Turn 1.

Also means Dualcast always has a target on Turn 1 (16 burst damage if used immediately). If swapped for a boss relic, Dualcast is dead Turn 1 unless another orb is channeled first.
