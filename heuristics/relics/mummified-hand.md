# Mummified Hand

Excellent in Power-heavy decks. Each Power play enables a free card -- effectively +1 energy per Power. In a deck with 4-5 Powers, generates 4-5 free plays per combat.

SYNERGIES:
- **Multiple Powers**: More Powers = more free plays.
- **[[cards/Barricade]] + [[cards/Metallicize]] setup turns**: Playing Barricade triggers a free Defend+ or Strike+ on the setup turn. Partially offsets Barricade's cost.
- **[[cards/Immolate]]**: A free Immolate+ is 28-31 damage for zero energy.

DEFECT SYNERGY: Excellent Power density ([[cards/Echo Form]], Loop, [[cards/Capacitor]], [[cards/Defragment]], [[cards/Creative AI]]). Mummified Hand + Echo Form + Loop generates multiple free plays during setup, offsetting Echo Form's 3E cost.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Decks with 0-1 Powers (trigger too rare). [[cards/Corruption]] (Skills already cost 0, so free card is less impactful if it hits a Skill).

Scales in value as more Powers are added. Confirmed dominant across both [[characters/Ironclad]] and [[characters/Defect]] Power-heavy archetypes.
