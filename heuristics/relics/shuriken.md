# Shuriken

Incentivizes playing 3+ Attacks per turn. Over a long fight, provides 3-5+ Strength passively -- comparable to Inflame but requires no card slot or energy. High pick priority in Attack-heavy decks.

SYNERGIES:
- **Anger**: 0-cost Attack that copies itself. Playing 3x Anger triggers Shuriken for free.
- **Demon Form**: +2 Str/turn stacks with Shuriken's passive gains. By turn 5, total Strength can be +12-15 combined.
- **Rage**: 0-cost Power that gives block per Attack. Every Attack-heavy turn generates both block and Strength.
- **Multi-attack turns**: Thunderclap + Strike + Anger = 3 attacks = +1 Str. Very easy to trigger naturally.

Excellent in long fights (bosses, elites) where 5-8 triggers accumulate to significant Strength.
