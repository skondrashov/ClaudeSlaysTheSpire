# The Champ

**Core rule:** Save debuffs for AFTER the Anger transition (below 50% HP). All debuffs applied before Anger are completely wasted. Burst through Phase 2 quickly.

## Strategy

- **Demon Form and slow scaling:** Need enough turns to ramp BEFORE Execute triggers. If Demon Form played Turn 1 and Execute happens Turn 5, only +10 Strength -- may not be enough.
- **Burst through Phase 2:** Have enough burst to kill quickly after Anger. Slow attrition decks struggle because Metallicize 5 + cleansed debuffs mean he takes less damage and deals more.
- **Re-apply debuffs after Anger:** Shockwave, Disarm, and other debuffs are wasted if applied before Anger. Save them (or have second copies) for Phase 2.
- **HP management:** Enter at high HP. Post-Anger attacks with +8-12 Strength hit for 27x2 or more.
- **Vulnerability window:** After Execute, The Champ spends one turn on the transition. Use this for block and preparation.

## Character Matchups

**Ironclad:**
- Demon Form: good but needs early play (need accumulated Str to outscale Champ's +8 Str and Metallicize 5)
- Shockwave+: save for AFTER Anger if possible
- Reaper: single-use -- time for post-Anger when attacks are strongest
- Dark Shackles: excellent emergency post-Anger
- Heavy Blade+: strong finisher if Str is high from Demon Form

**Defect:**
- Barrage+ is the primary burst card (52+ damage with 5+ orbs and Vulnerable)
- Echo Form doubling Barrage+ can deal 100+ in a single turn
- Frost wall for Phase 1 survival
- Re-apply Vulnerable after Anger (Beam Cell or Sweeping Beam)
