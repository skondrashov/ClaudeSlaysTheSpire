# Donu and Deca

**Core rule:** AOE is king (every point does double duty). Focus fire Donu first (stops Strength scaling). Strength scaling + Heavy Blade for the finisher.

## Winning Strategy (confirmed in 2 victories)

1. **AOE is king.** Immolate+ hits BOTH bosses every cast. Three Immolate+ in one turn dealt 102 AOE.
2. **Focus fire Donu.** All single-target attacks go to Donu. AOE handles Deca passively.
3. **Strength scaling is the finisher.** Spot Weakness+ -> Limit Break -> Heavy Blade (50+ damage) as lethal finisher.
4. **Snecko Eye is exceptional.** 7 cards/turn with random costs. Multiple 0-cost Immolate+ plays win the fight.
5. **Dual Wield+ on Immolate+** creates three copies in hand for 100+ AOE.

## Preparation Checklist

1. AOE damage (Immolate+ is the best card)
2. Strength scaling (Spot Weakness+, Inflame, Limit Break, Heavy Blade)
3. Enter at 70%+ HP (ideally full). Combined incoming is 20-32/turn.
4. Block density for 6-10 turns
5. Potions: Snecko Oil Turn 1, Duplication Potion on 0-cost Immolate+, Strength Potion compounds with Limit Break

## Key Cards

- **Immolate+:** Best card in the fight (28-34 AOE per play)
- **Heavy Blade+:** 5x Str multiplier finisher
- **Dual Wield+:** Copies Immolate+ for triple AOE turns
- **Limit Break+ (no exhaust):** Most impactful upgrade for this fight -- enables repeated Str doubling
- **Flex+ with Limit Break+:** 4 temporary Str doubled by Limit Break+ = effectively 10 permanent Str
- **Evolve:** Converts Burns from Immolate into card draws

## Potion Timing

- Snecko Oil Turn 1 (expands hand to 10 cards)
- Strength Potion Turn 2 (compounds with every attack)
- Duplication Potion on 0-cost Immolate+ (wait for favorable Snecko roll)
- Liquid Memories retrieves Immolate+ from discard at new Snecko cost
