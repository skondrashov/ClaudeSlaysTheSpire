# Time Eater

**Core rule:** Deliberate card counting is mandatory. Before playing any card, know the exact count (e.g., "9/12"). Plan your turn around the count. Play high-impact cards, minimize card count per turn.

**HP Threshold:** Enter at 50%+ HP. The fight is long (10-15 turns) with escalating damage from Time Warp Str stacks.

## Strategy

- **Track the 12-card count explicitly every turn.** State count before and after each play.
- **Play high-impact cards, minimize count per turn.** Body Slam+ (0E, damage = block) is ideal. Immolate+ (one card, 28+ AOE) is excellent.
- **Barricade is the best card in this fight.** Block persists through Time Warp turn resets.
- **Entrench+ doubles block for Body Slam+ scaling.** 2 cards for massive damage.
- **Intentional phasing:** Choose WHEN Time Warp triggers. Plan to trigger it when your last 1-2 plays push past 12, so forced turn-end costs nothing.
- **Corruption + Feel No Pain:** Excellent but be card-count conscious. Every free Skill counts toward the 12-card limit.

## Confirmed Winning Approaches

**Ironclad:** Barricade + Corruption + FNP + Entrench + Body Slam engine. Accumulate 69 block, Entrench to 138, Body Slam+ for 146 damage.

**Silent:** Accuracy x2 (+8 Shiv damage) + After Image + Wraith Form+ + Phantasmal Killer. Shivs deal 36 each doubled. Cultist Potion Turn 1 for Ritual scaling. Malaise X=5 for late-game Str/Weak reduction. Finisher scales with attacks played.

**Defect:** Echo Form+ + Seek+ + Coolheaded+ + Barrage+. Barrage doubled by Echo Form for lethal. Track that Echo Form doubles count toward Time Warp (1 card = 2 counted plays). Frost wall persists through Time Warp.

## What NOT to Do

- Play many small cards per turn without purpose (accelerates Time Warp with low damage output)
- Exhaust Impervious early (save for high-damage turns after Str stacks)
- Enter below 40% HP
- Waste Malaise on Artifact stripping (use Neutralize for that)
- Lose track of the 12-card count

## Potion Timing

- Cultist Potion Turn 1 (maximum Str scaling over the long fight)
- Fear Potion on Phantasmal Killer burst turns
- Snecko Oil as emergency draw
- Save Lizard Tail / Fairy as death insurance
