# Spike Slime M

**Core rule:** Mostly uses Lick (70% of turns), so blocking needs are moderate. Kill in 3-4 turns.

## Strategy

- Exhaust [[cards/Slimed]] cards when possible
- [[debuffs/Frail]] reduces block effectiveness -- account for this in block math

## What to Bring

- Exhaust tools for Slimed cards

## What to Avoid

- Long fights where Slimed cards accumulate

**Danger Level:** Low.
