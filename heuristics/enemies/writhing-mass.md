# Writhing Mass

**Core rule:** Use single large hits, not multi-hit attacks. Malleable gains block per hit, punishing Whirlwind/Pummel severely.

**Benchmark:** Expect a long fight (6-8 turns) and 25-40 HP drain.

## Strategy

- Hemokinesis (15 damage, 1 hit) is far better than Pummel (2x4, gives 2 stacks of Malleable block)
- Apply Weak early (multi-hit attacks at 75% damage are much more survivable)
- Disarm helps but does not solve the fight -- fight length is the real problem

## What NOT to Do

- Play Whirlwind, Pummel, or other multi-hit attacks
- Enter below 40% HP (drains 25-40 HP even with strong play)
- Expect a quick kill (plan for 6-8 turns minimum)

## Engine Approach

Barricade + Corruption + FNP engine handles Writhing Mass effectively. Block accumulation makes the fight survivable, and Immolate+ (single large hit) bypasses Malleable efficiently. Spot Weakness works on Attack turns.

## Danger Warning

This enemy is a major HP drain in Act 3. Entering the next fight at low HP after Writhing Mass is the real danger.
