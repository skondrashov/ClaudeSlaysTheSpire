# Repulsor

**Core rule:** Kill after Exploders but before Spikers. Not directly dangerous (low attack damage, low HP) but degrades deck quality, making OTHER enemies more dangerous.

## Strategy

- Low HP (29-35) means they die quickly to any focused damage
- Evolve converts Dazed into card draws (if available)
- Minor annoyance for strong decks; can cripple thin decks that rely on specific card draws

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** Dazed clogging is extra painful since key combo pieces (Catalyst+, Wraith Form) need to be drawn at the right time. Tools of the Trade and Well-Laid Plans+ help. Poison ignores deck clogging once applied.

**Danger Level:** Low. Direct threat is minimal. Danger is indirect -- Dazed clogging makes companion enemies harder to deal with.
