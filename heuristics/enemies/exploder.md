# Exploder

**Core rule:** KILL FIRST in any multi-enemy fight. You have exactly 2 turns to kill or eat 30 damage. Kill priority is highest among the Shapes.

## Strategy

- Front-load all damage on Exploders (even at the cost of taking Slam damage, preventing the 30 explosion is correct)
- 30 HP is very low -- most decks can kill in 1-2 turns
- Poison can work if applied heavily on Turn 1 (need 30+ total ticks in 2 turns)
- Block the Explosion if you cannot kill in time (it does NOT bypass block)

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** Deadly Poison+ (12 poison) on Turn 1 needs supplemental damage (12+11=23 by end of Turn 2, need ~7 more). Noxious Fumes alone is too slow. Best approach: direct attacks + Backstab for burst.

**Danger Level:** High if not killed quickly. Low if you have burst damage or AOE.
