# Snake Plant

**Core rule:** Use single large attacks rather than many small hits per turn (Malleable gains increasing block per hit). The debuff turn (Enfeebling Spores) is a free turn for offense.

**Benchmark:** With Strength scaling: 4-5 turn fight, 15-25 HP lost. Without Strength scaling: 7-turn fight, 40+ HP lost. This fight is a Strength scaling check.

## Strategy

- Apply Vulnerable early
- Clothesline/Uppercut for Weak reduces 7x3=21 to ~5x3=15
- Kill in 4-5 turns before debuffs stack
- The debuff turn is a free turn for offense (enter Wrath for Watcher)

## What to Bring

- Burst damage (Immolate+, Carnage+, Rampage)
- Clothesline/Uppercut for Weak
- Single large hits
- Strength scaling is critical (without it, the fight drains 40+ HP)

## What to Avoid

- Multi-hit attacks (Whirlwind, Twin Strike, Sword Boomerang, especially Pummel which generates 3+4+5+6 = 18 block from Malleable alone)
- Entering at low HP -- 21-27 damage/turn with Frail making block weaker is a death spiral

## Character Matchups

**Watcher:** Talk to the Hand triggers on each of Snake Plant's 3 Chomp hits = 6-9 free block per attack turn. Use Wrath burst on Enfeebling Spores (free) turns. Exit Wrath via Fear No Evil on Chomp turns. Flying Sleeves (4x2) triggers Malleable twice -- prefer single large hits instead.

## Unknown Room Warning

Cannot be avoided if map is routed through Unknown rooms at low HP. Unknown rooms below 30% HP are effectively lethal when Snake Plant spawns.
