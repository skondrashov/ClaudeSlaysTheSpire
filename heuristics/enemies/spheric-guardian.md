# Spheric Guardian

**Core rule:** This is a DPS check disguised as a hallway fight. Must kill within 6-8 turns or block becomes insurmountable. Strip 3 Artifact charges first, then apply Vulnerable, then burst through remaining block + HP.

**Benchmark:** Expect 30-50+ HP lost from Frail-reduced blocking vs 10x2 attacks. At low Strength without burst, this fight is a guaranteed loss through attrition.

## Strategy

1. Strip 3 Artifact charges with cheap debuffs (Thunderclap ideal)
2. Apply Vulnerable
3. Burst through remaining block + HP

## What to Bring

- Uppercut+ (strips 2 Artifact charges in one play)
- Thunderclap (strip remaining Artifact)
- Pen Nib (double damage to break block)
- Burst damage (Fiend Fire, Immolate, Bludgeon)
- FAST Strength scaling (Inflame, Spot Weakness, Limit Break -- NOT Demon Form)
- Potions (Strength Potion, Fire Potion)

## What to Avoid

- Chip damage with Strikes (block regenerates faster than you can deplete it)
- Demon Form as sole Strength source (too slow -- 13 Str by turn 9 still lost to 65+ block)
- Random exhaust effects (Havoc, unupgraded True Grit)
- Exhausting Spot Weakness (permanent Strength gain is needed to break through Barricade)
- Playing Brutality (1 HP/turn self-damage over an 8-13 turn fight)
- Entering at low HP (<50%) -- routinely drains 30-50 HP
- Entering with zero potions

## Winning Formulas

- Corruption + Feel No Pain engine (12 HP lost)
- Immolate + Impervious+ + Calipers + Spot Weakness (20 HP lost)
- Front-loaded Str (Inflame/Spot Weakness) + burst (Fiend Fire, Bludgeon) + potions

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** Dex stacking (Footwork+) + Shiv damage (Accuracy + Blade Dance) works but expect 26+ HP drain. Poison bypasses Barricade entirely (Deadly Poison, Noxious Fumes). Piercing Wail+ strips Artifact while negating damage. Block density under Frail 5 is precarious.

## Danger Warning

Single most dangerous Act 2 hallway fight. 5 deaths confirmed. Even with correct play and front-loaded Strength, expect 30-40 HP drain.
