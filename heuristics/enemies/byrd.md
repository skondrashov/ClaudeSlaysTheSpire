# Byrd

**Core rule:** Thunderclap is CRITICAL. Without it, Flight 3 on three enemies means 9 hits just to strip all Flight once -- at half damage each.

**Benchmark:** With Thunderclap + burst: 7-turn fight, ~20 HP lost. Without AOE: 8-10 turns, 36+ HP lost. Worst case observed: 73 HP lost (74 to 1).

## Strategy

- Use Thunderclap to strip 1 Flight from ALL Byrds and apply Vulnerable simultaneously
- Focus fire one Byrd at a time -- going from 3 to 2 removes 33% of incoming damage
- Once Flight is at 0, burst with Vulnerable attacks to kill
- Expect 7-12 turn fights and 20-73 HP lost

## Engine Setup Trap

Do NOT spend 3 turns setting up powers (Evolve + Metallicize + Fire Breathing + Inflame). Each setup turn costs 12-23 unblocked HP because Byrds attack for 10-25 per turn and a single Defend + Metallicize only blocks 8. A 3-turn setup burns 40-60 HP before dealing any damage. Instead, play at most 1 power per turn while blocking, or skip setup entirely if HP is below 60%. Kill speed matters more than engine value when entering below 70% HP.

## Turn 2 Trap

If all 3 Byrds attack simultaneously (after gaining Str from buff turns), combined damage can reach 38+. With only 1 block card in hand, there is no survival path. The deck needs block density (multiple Shrug It Off, Ghostly Armor, Impervious).

## What to Bring

- AOE (Thunderclap for Flight stripping + Vulnerable -- THE key card)
- Burst damage (Carnage+ nearly one-shots a Byrd)
- Multi-hit attacks (Twin Strike+ strips 2 Flight per play)
- Metallicize (essential for long fight)
- Reaper (sustain healing)

## What to Avoid

- Small single-hit attacks (halved by Flight and inefficient)
- Entering below 50% HP
- Havoc or unupgraded True Grit (random exhaust can destroy key cards)

## Route Planning Warning

If the map forces a Monster room immediately after a Byrd fight with no rest/shop/event option, the run may be lost. When planning Act 2 routes, ensure the first 2-3 floors include at least one non-combat room as a safety valve.

## Chosen+Byrd Fight

Combines Flight mechanics with Hex (Skills add Dazed). Extremely dangerous -- need Attacks for Byrd Flight stripping but also need block from Skills, which trigger Hex. Enter at 50%+ HP minimum.
