# Jaw Worm

**Core rule:** Block Turn 1 (always Chomp for 11). Burst during Bellow turns (free turns).

**Benchmark:** 4-turn kill typical. 10-17 damage per turn with Vulnerable.

## Strategy

- Turn 1: Block the Chomp (11 damage)
- Turn 2: Likely Bellow (free turn) -- apply Vulnerable, deal damage
- Kill fast before Strength accumulates

## What to Bring

- Bash for Vulnerable
- Strikes for follow-up

## What to Avoid

- Slow decks that let Strength accumulate

**Danger Level:** Low. Standard Act 1 opener.
