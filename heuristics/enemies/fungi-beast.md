# Fungi Beast

**Core rule:** Kill fast. Fungi Beasts scale with time due to +3 Str per Grow.

**Benchmark:** Must kill within 5-6 turns or Str scaling makes incoming unblockable.

## Strategy

- Kill one when the other is NOT attacking to avoid Spore Cloud Vulnerability + attack in the same turn
- AOE is excellent (both are low-HP, AOE shortens the fight before scaling gets dangerous)
- Focus fire one target at a time

## What to Bring

- Enough burst to kill one in 3-4 turns
- Block for escalating later turns
- AOE (shortens fight before scaling ramps)

## What to Avoid

- Killing one on a turn when the other attacks (Spore Cloud Vulnerability amplifies the hit)
- Long fights (Str scaling is the real threat, not raw HP)
- Curse clogging (reduces effective hand size against scaling enemies)

## Act 2 Pairing (Fungi Beast + Shelled Parasite)

Kill Fungi Beast first to avoid giving Shelled Parasite a damage amplifier from Spore Cloud Vulnerability, but be prepared to block heavily on the turn it dies (Vulnerable from Spore Cloud + Shelled Parasite attack).
