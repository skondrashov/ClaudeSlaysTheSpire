# Giant Head

**Core rule:** This is a DPS race against escalating damage. Must have either Demon Form (Strength scaling) or Barricade + Body Slam (block scaling). Without either engine, the fight is unwinnable.

**HP Threshold:** Enter above 60% HP. The fight lasts 10+ turns with increasing damage.

## Strategy (Ironclad)

- **Demon Form is critical.** Play Turn 1-2. At 500 HP, exponential Strength scaling is needed
- **Slow HELPS you** -- play many cards per turn to maximize damage
- Immolate+ with high Strength is the best damage card
- Entrench+ is essential for surviving high-damage turns
- Impervious: save for turns where attacks spike past normal block capacity
- Reaper is critical for sustain in this long fight

## Barricade Engine Approach

Barricade + Corruption + FNP + Entrench + Body Slam defeats Giant Head without Demon Form. Body Slam+ with 66-83 accumulated block deals massive single-card damage. The engine completed the kill in 8 turns with zero HP lost.

## Strategy (Silent)

Poison bypasses Metallicize 8 entirely (ticks at end of enemy turn, ignoring block). Apply poison early, then triple with Catalyst+ for lethal total. The key math: tripling 16+ poison produces 48+ poison = 1176+ total damage over time.

**Critical timing window:** After Catalyst+ triples poison, survive 2-3 more turns for poison to finish. Block aggressively. Use ALL available potions on survival turns -- a potion can be the difference between enemy dying this turn vs surviving at 1 HP.

## What NOT to Do

- Delay Demon Form past Turn 2-3
- Fail to use offensive potions before the kill window closes (confirmed death: Giant Head survived at 1 HP with Strength Potion and Explosive Potion unused)
- Play purely defensively without damage scaling
- Enter below 60% HP
