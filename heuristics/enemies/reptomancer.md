# Reptomancer

**Core rule:** KILL DAGGERS IMMEDIATELY. Each Dagger that explodes deals 25 damage. Kill them on their Stab turn before Explode. AOE is critical.

**Kill Priority:** Daggers about to Explode > Daggers that just Stabbed > Reptomancer

**Danger Level:** Extreme. Combined burst from Dagger Explodes + Reptomancer attacks can deal 75+ in a single turn. Without AOE or fast Dagger removal, this fight ends runs.

## Strategy

- Kill Daggers before they Explode (you have 1 turn per Dagger wave)
- AOE is critical (Die Die Die, Blade Dance + Accuracy, Dagger Spray all hit multiple targets)
- After clearing initial Daggers, focus damage on Reptomancer (she resummons, but reducing her HP means fewer total Dagger waves)
- Big Bite (30 damage) requires serious block -- track her pattern
- Weak from Snake Strike reduces your damage -- re-apply Vulnerable to compensate

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** This is the Silent's best elite matchup in Act 3. Die Die Die+ (17x3 AOE) can kill all Daggers in one play. Blade Dance + Accuracy provides cheap multi-target damage. Piercing Wail reduces all enemy damage (critical for multi-Dagger turns). Corpse Explosion turns killed Daggers into AOE against Reptomancer. Poison on Reptomancer ticks while you manage Daggers.
