# Chosen

**Core rule:** Minimize Skill usage after Hex is applied. Front-load damage with Attacks. Kill before Strength buffs make attacks lethal.

**Benchmark:** Kill in 4-5 turns. Must enter with 40+ HP minimum. This fight routinely drains 30-40 HP.

## Kill Priority (Multi-Enemy Fights)

**Kill Chosen FIRST.** Hex's Dazed flooding is CATASTROPHIC -- every Skill played after Turn 2 adds a Dazed to the draw pile, and by Turn 4-5 the deck is so diluted that critical turns draw 2-3 Dazed. Killing Chosen first stops Hex immediately.

**Exception:** If the Cultist can be killed in 1-2 turns (Fire Potion burst, or it has <15 HP), killing it first is acceptable because Hex only applies for 1-2 turns.

## Strategy

- Attacks and Powers are safe (do not trigger Hex)
- Play Defend only when absolutely necessary (each one adds Dazed)
- Iron Wave is excellent (Attack that blocks)
- Metallicize is excellent (passive block without triggering Hex -- Power, not a Skill)
- Kill speed is critical -- Strength scaling is lethal

## What to Bring

- Attack-heavy deck
- Iron Wave (Attack that blocks)
- Inflame (Strength overcomes Weak penalty)
- Bash+ (Attack, applies Vulnerable)
- Metallicize (passive block without triggering Hex)

## What to Avoid

- Playing Defend unless absolutely necessary
- Slow kill (Strength scaling is lethal)
- Entering below 30 HP
- Fiend Fire unless the burst will kill (exhausts ALL other cards in hand including defensive cards)

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** Hex is disproportionately devastating. Silent decks are naturally Skill-heavy (Backflip, Footwork, Survivor, Deflect, Blur, Piercing Wail). Powers (Footwork, After Image, A Thousand Cuts) do NOT trigger Hex. Front-load damage with Attacks and Shivs. Use Piercing Wail Turn 1 BEFORE Hex is applied. Avoid curse-heavy decks (Du-Vu Doll strategy) when Hex encounters are likely.
