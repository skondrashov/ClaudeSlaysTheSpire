# Acid Slime L

**Core rule:** Killing this creates 2 Acid Slime (M) enemies. Spread damage first before killing.

## Strategy

- In the Slime Boss fight, going from 2 enemies to 3 unless both large slimes are killed in quick succession
- Weak reduces YOUR damage output, making it harder to finish off remaining enemies
- Burst to kill before Weak stacks become crippling

## What to Bring

- Burst damage
- AOE for the medium slimes that spawn on split

## What to Avoid

- Killing this while the other large slime is at full HP
- Spread damage first, then kill in quick succession

**Danger Level:** Medium. Tackle hits hard (16 damage) but Lick turns are free.
