# Spiker

**Core rule:** Poison is the ideal damage source -- it bypasses Thorns entirely. If you must attack, use single large hits, not multi-hit attacks.

## Strategy

- Poison (Noxious Fumes, Deadly Poison, Bouncing Flask) deals damage without triggering Thorns
- If attacking, use single large hits (Dash+, Predator) not multi-hit attacks
- Kill Spikers early before Thorns compound (at Thorns 3-5 attacks are tolerable; at 9+ every attack is significant HP loss)
- In multi-enemy fights, kill Spiker first to stop Thorns from scaling

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** Poison completely solves Spikers. Noxious Fumes + any poison source kills without ever taking Thorns damage. Shivs and Blade Dance are TRAPS -- each hit triggers Thorns. Piercing Wail does nothing useful here (Cut damage is low; Thorns is the real threat).

**Danger Level:** Medium. Low direct damage but Thorns mechanic punishes attack-heavy decks severely. Poison decks trivialize this fight.
