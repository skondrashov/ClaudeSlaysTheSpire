# Centurion + Mystic

**Core rule:** KILL MYSTIC FIRST. Non-negotiable. If you attack [[enemies/Centurion]] while [[enemies/Mystic]] is alive, you're in an unwinnable attrition war (Mystic heals all enemies 16 HP). Never split damage.

**Benchmark:** Kill Mystic in 5-6 turns. Expect 25-61 HP lost even with correct play. Enter with 60%+ HP.

## Strategy

- Focus all damage on Mystic despite Centurion being the bigger per-turn threat
- Apply [[debuffs/Weak]] to Centurion to reduce incoming while you kill Mystic
- Expect 6-12 turns total
- Do NOT spend all potions on the Mystic kill -- save at least one defensive resource for Centurion solo phase

## Centurion Solo Phase Warning

Once Mystic is dead, Centurion uses the 6x3 multi-hit attack (18-30 damage). If you are at low HP after killing Mystic, this is lethal. Always check if you can burst the remaining Centurion in one turn rather than blocking.

## What to Bring

- Burst damage for Mystic (need to break through 15 block/turn)
- Weak for Centurion
- Double [[cards/Metallicize]] is excellent
- [[cards/Headbutt]] (find specific cards)
- [[relics/Stone Calendar]] (52 damage nuke on turn 7 lands automatically after Mystic is dead)

## What to Avoid

- Attacking Centurion while Mystic lives
- Entering below 50% HP (confirmed deaths at 49% and 71% entry)
- Wasting Fairy here when elites are ahead
- Splitting damage between Centurion and Mystic even with temporary [[buffs/Strength]] buffs
- Spending ALL potions on the Mystic kill phase

## Character Matchups

**[[characters/Silent]]:** [[debuffs/Poison]] + Dex stacking works but expect 24+ HP drain. [[cards/Malaise]] on Centurion early (-2 Str, 2 Weak) neuters early attacks. [[cards/Catalyst]] doubles poison for sustained kill. [[cards/Piercing Wail]] on Centurion's 8x3 multi-hit is critical. Fight lasts ~10 turns. At 55% HP entry, survivable but leaves only 21% HP.
