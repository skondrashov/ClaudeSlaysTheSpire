# Gremlin Gang

**Kill priority order:** Sneaky (9 dmg/turn, glass cannon) > Wizard (25 dmg nuke on timer) > Fat (4 dmg + Weak) > Mad (4 dmg, scales with Angry) > Shield (7 block to ally, lowest threat).

## Strategy

- Kill Sneaky Gremlin ASAP (9-10 damage every turn, two turns ignored = 18-20 HP lost)
- AOE is excellent (All-Out Attack, Dagger Spray, Thunderclap hit all 4)
- Noxious Fumes is strong (3 poison/turn on 4 enemies = 12 total poison/turn)
- Focus fire on highest-priority target first
- Kill Wizard before its Ultimate Blast fires (25 damage)

## What NOT to Do

- Ignore Sneaky Gremlin while setting up
- Spread damage evenly without killing anything
- Spend all energy on block when a Sneaky Gremlin has 3 HP (killing it prevents more damage than blocking it)
- Spread incidental damage to Mad Gremlin (Angry makes it stronger when hit)
