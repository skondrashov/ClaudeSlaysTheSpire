# Sentry

**Core rule:** AOE is king. Without AOE or meaningful damage scaling, the fight takes 14-20 turns and drains 52-56+ HP.

**Benchmark:** WITH AOE: 7-turn fight, ~22 HP damage. WITHOUT AOE: 18-20 turns, 50-60 HP damage. The AOE benchmark is the only acceptable outcome.

## Elite Readiness Gate

Do NOT fight Sentries with a starter-quality deck (Strikes, Defends, Bash, 0-1 other cards). Without AOE or meaningful damage scaling, effective damage per Strike is only 2 (6 minus Metallicize 4 absorb). Confirmed: a deck with Strikes/Defends/Bash/Headbutt/SIO spent 20 turns and took 56 damage (76->20 HP).

The Sentries fight requires at least one of: AOE (Thunderclap, Whirlwind, Cleave), damage scaling (Inflame, Spot Weakness), or a strong attack card (Carnage, Pommel Strike+).

## Path Timing Gate

Do NOT path into Sentries with only 0-2 monster rooms beforehand. A deck needs 3-4 fights minimum to pick up cards that make Sentries viable.

## Strategy

- Strip Artifact with Thunderclap before real debuffs
- Focus fire one Sentry at a time
- Kill before Dazed overwhelms draws

## What to Bring

- AOE (Whirlwind, Thunderclap, Cleave)
- Damage scaling (Inflame, Spot Weakness)
- Focus fire capability

## What to Avoid

- Slow play (Dazed accumulation cripples draws)
- Fighting with a starter-quality deck

## Act 2 Pairing (Sentry + Spheric Guardian)

Kill the Sentry first (no block to break through, lower HP) to reduce incoming damage count and stop Dazed generation. Gremlin Horn triggers on Sentry death, providing energy and draw to handle the Spheric Guardian.

## Silent Act 2 Pairing

Kill order: Sentry first (strip Artifact with Neutralize, apply Terror for Vuln, burst with Backstab + attacks). Use Piercing Wail to zero the Sentry's beam turn while setting up Footwork Dex. After Sentry dies, poison the Spheric Guardian (Deadly Poison, Noxious Fumes bypass Barricade block entirely). However, Dazed from Sentry Bolts combined with SG's Barricade block creates a lethal combination at low HP (<30%).
