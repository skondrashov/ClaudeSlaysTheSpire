# Nemesis

**Core rule:** The fight has a rhythm -- non-Intangible turns are your damage windows, Intangible turns are for setup/blocking. Do NOT waste damage cards on Intangible turns.

## Strategy

- Track the Intangible pattern: Turn 1 (no), Turn 2 (yes), Turn 3 (no), Turn 4 (yes), etc.
- On non-Intangible turns: deal maximum damage. Front-load burst here.
- On Intangible turns: block, setup, play Powers, apply debuffs
- Block for Scythe (45 damage). Impervious is ideal. Weak reduces it to 33.
- Apply Weak to survive Scythe turns -- without Weak, 45 damage is near-unsurvivable
- Burns management matters in long fights. Kill speed or exhaust tools to end before Burns overwhelm.
- Poison is partially countered -- each tick only deals 1 on Intangible turns. Still works but takes twice as long.
- High-burst poison (Catalyst+) on a non-Intangible turn is still strong.

## Character Matchups

**Silent:** Wraith Form+ provides Intangible for YOU on dangerous Scythe turns. Piercing Wail reduces the 6x3 multi-hit. Malaise reduces Strength for multiple turns. Well-Laid Plans+ can save burst damage for non-Intangible turns.

**Danger Level:** High. Scythe (45 damage) can one-shot at low HP. Intangible doubles effective fight length. Burns clog draws.
