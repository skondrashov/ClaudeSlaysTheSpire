# Snecko

**Core rule:** Kill fast before cost Confusion ruins multiple turns. Apply Vulnerable early to maximize every successful attack turn.

**HP is high (~114-120).** Plan for a 5-7 turn fight even with good damage.

## Strategy

- With good RNG, Confused turns can be better than normal (0-cost Bash+, 0-cost Fiend Fire)
- With bad RNG, entire turns are wasted on unplayable 3-cost cards
- Metallicize is excellent: Powers are played once and provide guaranteed value every turn regardless of cost rolls
- Demon Form is strong: if drawn at reduced cost under Confusion, play immediately for guaranteed scaling

## What to Bring

- Burst damage
- Vulnerable application
- Powers (guaranteed value regardless of cost rolls)
- Metallicize (passive block every turn)
- Duplication Potion (duplicate a card Confusion set to 0-cost)
- Gambler's Brew (redraws hand with new randomized costs -- re-rolls Confusion RNG)

## What to Avoid

- Long fights where cost confusion compounds
- Relying solely on expensive cards (2-3 cost) since they may be randomly assigned 3 cost anyway

## Escape Option

Smoke Bomb works against Snecko. If Confusion has randomized costs badly and survival is impossible, escaping is correct. Losing rewards is better than dying.
