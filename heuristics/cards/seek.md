# Seek

The most consistent card in the [[characters/Defect]]'s pool. Guarantees access to the exact card needed for the current situation.

**When to play:** Turn 1 of boss fights: find [[cards/Echo Form]]. Hallway fights: find the kill card ([[cards/Barrage]] with full orbs).

**Upgrade priority:** Extremely high. 1 card->2 cards. Seek+ finds 2 cards for 0E -- functionally a perfect draw ignoring deck dilution.

**[[relics/Bottled Lightning]] synergy:** Making Seek innate guarantees a tutor Turn 1 of every combat. The single most consistent opener for the Defect.

**Echo Form synergy:** Echo Form doubles Seek, tutoring 2 (or 4 with Seek+). Enables assembling a complete hand every turn.

**Exhaust note:** One-time tutor per combat. Almost always sufficient because it sets up the engine on Turn 1.

**Nob warning:** Skill card, triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]. Play only if tutored card enables a kill within 1-2 turns.
