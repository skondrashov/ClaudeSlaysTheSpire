# Fiend Fire

Exhausts ALL other cards in hand. Deals 7 damage per card exhausted to a single target.

**When to play:** When burst will kill the target, when remaining cards are expendable, or on free turns. Turns Slimed/Decay/Dazed/Burn cards into 7 damage each. After playing, your hand is EMPTY. Only play when: (1) the burst kills, (2) it is a free turn, or (3) remaining cards are expendable.

**Synergies:** Status/Curse cards (become 7+ damage instead of dead draws), Strength (each card's damage scales), Vulnerable (each hit gets 50% bonus), Unceasing Top (empties hand, triggers Top draw engine), Snecko Oil (randomize costs before Fiend Fire).

**Matchups:** Slime Boss -- burst + Slimed exhaust. Chosen -- only use when burst will kill or exhausted cards are genuinely expendable. If Chosen survives, remaining turns have zero block sources.

**Caution:** Exhausting key cards (Barricade, Clothesline) is permanent. At critically low HP, exhausting everything for survival is correct -- survival > future value.

**Guardian warning:** Fiend Fire as a primary engine drains the deck unsustainably in 12-14 turn fights. Use on free turns for burst, do not plan the deck around it as sole damage engine.

**Index shifting danger:** Always reference Fiend Fire by name rather than index number. Earlier card plays can shift indices causing wrong cards to be played.
