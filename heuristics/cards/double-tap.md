# Double Tap

Doubles the next Attack. The duplicated play counts as a separate play for all purposes.

**When to play:** Play BEFORE your strongest Attack card. At 1E cost, only worth it when the doubled Attack deals significant damage.

**Synergies:** Rampage (double play = double scaling acceleration + massive damage), Pommel Strike+ (20 damage + draw 4), Heavy Blade (at Str 6: 32 x 2 = 64 damage for 3E total), Bash+ (first hit 10 + 3 Vuln, second hit 15 with Vuln active = 25 total).

**Matchups:** Bronze Automaton -- Double Tap is a high-value Stasis target. Orbs can steal it from your hand. Consider playing early before Orbs get a chance to steal it.
