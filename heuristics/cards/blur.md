# Blur

Block that carries over to the next turn. Critical companion to Wraith Form.

**When to play:** On free turns (enemy buffing/debuffing) where block would normally be wasted. On the last Intangible turn to maximize carried block for the first dangerous turn. When After Image generates excess block.

**Wraith Form synergy:** During Intangible turns, generate block freely (enemy damage reduced to 1). Blur carries this to post-Intangible turns when Dex penalty makes new block impossible. Without Blur, the transition to post-Intangible is a cliff.

**Draft priority:** Mid-priority for Silent. Becomes high-priority when deck includes Wraith Form. Without Wraith Form, decent but not essential (5/8 block for 1E is below average, but retention adds conditional value).
