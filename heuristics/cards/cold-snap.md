# Cold Snap

Dual-purpose Attack: damage + Frost orb. Safe against Gremlin Nob.

**When to play:** Play on the highest-priority damage target. The Frost orb channels regardless of whether the damage kills. In Frost-heavy builds, fills orb slots for passive block.

**Early Defect priority:** High. One of the best early pickups because it is an Attack (Nob protection) that also builds Frost defense. Take when offered in Act 1.

**Necronomicon interaction:** When Cold Snap costs 2+ energy (via Snecko randomization) and Necronomicon triggers, it plays twice -- double damage AND two Frost orbs.

**Upgrade priority:** Moderate. 6->9 damage. Frost channel is the primary value.
