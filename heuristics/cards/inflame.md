# Inflame

Cheap, permanent damage scaling. Every Attack deals 2 more damage for the entire fight.

**When to play:** Play as early as possible in long fights (bosses, elites). On free turns, always play. On attack turns with high incoming, play Inflame (1E) and use remaining 2E on block. Do not stack multiple setup cards in one turn. Powers do NOT trigger Gremlin Nob Enrage.

**Synergies:** Reaper (Strength increases damage to ALL enemies = more healing), Heavy Blade (+2 Str becomes +6 or +10 damage due to multiplier), Whirlwind (each hit gains +2), Twin Strike (+2 per hit = +4 total), every Attack card.

**Matchups:** Guardian Defensive Mode -- safe (Power, not Attack, no Sharp Hide retaliation). Bronze Automaton -- play immediately to avoid Stasis theft. Spheric Guardian -- CRITICAL. Without Strength scaling, this fight is mathematically unwinnable. Inflame is the single best card for breaking through Spheric Guardian's defenses.
