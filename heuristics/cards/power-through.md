# Power Through

Massive block (triple Defend) but pollutes hand with 2 unplayable Wounds.

**When to play:** Must-survive turns with massive incoming damage. When you have exhaust tools (True Grit+, Fiend Fire, Second Wind) to remove the Wounds afterward. Short fights (3-4 turns) where the Wounds will not cycle back.

**When it is a trap:** In long fights without exhaust tools. The Wounds reduce effective hand size, meaning fewer block AND fewer damage cards per turn. Over 8+ turns, playing Power Through twice adds 4 Wounds, reducing block density for all subsequent turns.

**Synergy -- Fire Breathing:** The 2 Wounds trigger Fire Breathing when drawn, dealing 6-10 damage to all enemies per Wound. With Evolve, each Wound drawn deals damage AND draws a replacement.

**Slime Boss caution:** Adds 2 Wounds on top of Slimed cards. Without exhaust tools, Wound + Slimed accumulation can make 4 of 5 cards unplayable.

**Draft rule:** Do not take unless the deck has at least one exhaust tool, Fire Breathing, or the deck is fast enough to end fights in 4-5 turns.
