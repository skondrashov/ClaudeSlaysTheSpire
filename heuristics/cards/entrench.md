# Entrench

Doubles all accumulated block. The single most powerful block-scaling card with [[cards/Barricade]].

**Upgrade priority:** VERY HIGH. Entrench+ costs 1E (from 2E). Under [[cards/Corruption]], Entrench+ costs 0E and exhausts -- game-breaking.

**When to play:** With Barricade. Block persists, so Entrench doubles the accumulated total. Typical sequence: Turn 1 generate 20, Turn 2 add 15 (35 total), Turn 3 Entrench doubles to 70. Without Barricade, Entrench doubles only this turn's block (typically 10-20) -- mediocre for 2E.

**Synergies:** Barricade (mandatory pairing), Corruption (becomes 0E + FNP trigger on exhaust), [[cards/Feel No Pain]] (exhaust under Corruption = +3-4 block on top), [[cards/Body Slam]] (doubled block = doubled damage for free), [[cards/Impervious]] (30-40 base doubled to 60-80).

**Anti-synergies:** Decks without Barricade (poor value), fights with [[debuffs/Frail]].

**Do NOT take** without Barricade or a clear path to obtaining Barricade.
