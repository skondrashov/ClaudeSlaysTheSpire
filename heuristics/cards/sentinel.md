# Sentinel

Block card that refunds energy when exhausted.

**When to play:** In exhaust-heavy decks. Sentinel provides block AND energy when fed to [[cards/Fiend Fire]], [[cards/True Grit]], [[cards/Second Wind]], or [[cards/Burning Pact]].

**Decision points:** Base block (5/8) is modest. The value is in the exhaust payoff. Without exhaust tools, Sentinel is just a worse [[cards/Shrug It Off]]. With exhaust tools, it is a net-positive play: block + energy recovery.

**Upgrade priority:** Both halves improve significantly. Upgraded Sentinel exhausted via True Grit+ or Second Wind gives 8 block + 3 energy -- equivalent to a free [[cards/Offering]] without the HP loss.

**Synergies:** Fiend Fire (exhausted for 7+ damage AND gains 2-3E), True Grit+ (exhaust for block + energy), [[cards/Dark Embrace]] (exhaust draws a card), [[cards/Corruption]] (all Skills cost 0 and exhaust -- Sentinel becomes 0E for 5-8 block + 2-3E, a net energy gain).

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] -- do NOT play (Skill triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]).
