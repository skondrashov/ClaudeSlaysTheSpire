# Barricade

Block persists between turns instead of resetting to 0. Transforms block cards from temporary defense into permanent HP shields.

**When to play:** At 3E (2E upgraded), playing Barricade costs an entire turn. Only acceptable if: (1) enemy is buffing/defending, (2) HP is high enough to absorb one unblocked attack, (3) you have 4+ energy, or (4) at 5E budget, Barricade+ leaves 3E for block. NEVER play when incoming will kill or leave critical HP.

**Synergies:** Body Slam (damage = block, scales every turn), Shrug It Off, True Grit+, Iron Wave (all become permanent), Metallicize (passive block stacks permanently -- 2x Metallicize = 21 block by Turn 3, 35 by Turn 5), Entrench+ (doubles accumulated block).

**Anti-synergies:** Low energy decks (3E cost prohibitive), short fights, fights at low HP, Brutality.

**Victory engine:** Barricade + Corruption + Feel No Pain + Entrench + Body Slam defeated Time Eater (480 HP). Block snowball makes hallway fights trivial.

**Matchups:** Guardian -- deploy on free turn (DEFEND/STRONG_DEBUFF intent), then build permanent block wall. Multi-enemy fights with constant damage -- 3E setup often too expensive.
