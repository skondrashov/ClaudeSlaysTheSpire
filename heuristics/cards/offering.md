# Offering

One of the best cards in the game. Trade HP for a massive tempo burst.

**When to play:** Turn 1 of any fight where you need to set up quickly (play a Power + attack in the same turn). At high HP (50+), the cost is negligible. At low HP (15-), the 6 HP loss can be lethal — never play when 6 HP puts you below incoming damage minus available block.

**Synergies:**
- Expensive cards (Bludgeon, Fiend Fire, Demon Form) — makes 3E cards playable alongside other cards
- Unceasing Top — Offering draws 3 and gives 2E; if you play down to empty hand, Top triggers for additional draws
- Fiend Fire — 2 extra energy means Fiend Fire still leaves energy for more plays

**Upgrade priority:** Low. Base version already provides maximum value.

**Key rule:** Exhausts after one use per fight. Plan the turn you play it to maximize the energy and card advantage.
