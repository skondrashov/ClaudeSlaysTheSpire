# Armaments

Block + card upgrade in one play. Upgraded version is transformative.

**When to play:** Armaments+ is one of the best cards in the game -- every turn it upgrades your entire hand permanently for the combat. Play every turn in long fights. Prioritize upgrading Armaments itself first.

**Block value reminder:** Armaments provides 5 block in addition to its upgrade effect. ALWAYS include this 5 block in calculations. This has been forgotten in survival-critical calculations.

**When NOT to play:** In DPS-race fights where every turn of damage matters ([[enemies/Book of Stabbing]], [[enemies/Gremlin Leader]]). Spending 1E on Armaments+ for 5 block + upgrades can cost you the fight if the enemy's damage scaling outpaces your block growth.

**Upgrade priority:** One of the highest-priority upgrades in the game. Single card -> ALL cards in hand.

**Matchups:** Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], do NOT play (Skill triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]).
