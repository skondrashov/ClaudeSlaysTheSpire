# Thunderclap

Cheap mass Vulnerable. The best setup card for multi-enemy fights.

**When to play:** Always in multi-enemy fights. Turn 1 opener to set up 50% more damage on everything. Strips 1 Flight per Byrd hit and 1 Artifact per enemy.

**Decision points:** Against single enemies, worse than Bash+ (1 turn Vulnerable vs 3 turns). Against multiple enemies, one of the best cards in the game.

**Upgrade priority:** Worth upgrading when it is a regular play. 4->7 AOE damage with Vulnerable: ~6->10 damage each.

**Synergies:** Dropkick (sets up free cycling on all Vulnerable targets), all Attack cards (50% more damage to all enemies).

**Matchups:** Byrds -- THE single most important card. Strips 1 Flight from each + applies Vulnerable. Without Thunderclap, Flight 3 on three Byrds requires 9 separate hits. Having Thunderclap before Act 2 is essential. Sentries -- strips 1 Artifact from each. Slime Boss split -- mass Vulnerable on both slimes. Bronze Automaton -- strips Artifact charges (Automaton starts with Artifact 3).
