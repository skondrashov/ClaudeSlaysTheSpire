# Accuracy

Permanent Power adding flat damage to every Shiv. The most important card in Shiv-based Silent decks.

**When to play:** Turn 1 setup in every fight. The second copy is also excellent -- going from +4 to +8 per Shiv doubles offensive value of every Shiv-generating card.

**Damage math:** With 2x Accuracy (+8) and Str 11: Shiv = 4 + 8 + 11 = 23 per 0-cost Shiv. Blade Dance (1E) = 3 Shivs = 69 damage. With Phantasmal Killer: 46 per Shiv, 138 from 3 Shivs.

**Synergies:** Blade Dance (3 Shivs for 1E), Cloak and Dagger+ (2 Shivs + 6 block), Infinite Blades (free Shiv/turn), Phantasmal Killer (doubles Shiv damage), Cultist Potion (Ritual Str compounds on every Shiv), Finisher (Shivs build attack count), After Image (each Shiv = +1 block).

**Time Eater interaction:** Shivs count toward 12-card counter, but with Accuracy stacking each is worth 20+ damage, making the counter manageable.

**Draft priority:** High in any Shiv deck. Take early.
