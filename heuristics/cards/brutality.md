# Brutality

DANGEROUS in long fights. Extra draw at the cost of permanent HP drain.

**When to play:** Short fights (3-4 turns) where the extra draw finds lethal faster. With healing (Reaper, Feed) to offset self-damage. With Rupture, each Brutality provides +1 Strength/turn permanently.

**Decision points:** How long will this fight last? In a 9-turn fight, Brutality costs 9 HP minimum. NEVER play against bosses with status card damage (Hexaghost Burns) or fights expected to last 7+ turns. With Rupture in play, Brutality becomes a scaling engine -- but NEVER play 2 copies at low HP.

**The Brutality Trap:** Feels free at 0 cost and the extra draw seems universally good. But in any fight that might go 7+ turns, the cumulative self-damage becomes lethal. Before playing, ask: "Am I confident this fight ends in 4-5 turns?" If no -- do NOT play.

**Synergies:** Rupture (+1 Str/turn permanently per Brutality). With 2 Brutalities + Rupture = +2 Str/turn but lose 2 HP/turn. Only play multiple copies at 40+ HP.

**Matchups:** Hexaghost -- NEVER play. Book of Stabbing -- NEVER play. Spheric Guardian -- NEVER play (fight extends to 8-13 turns, self-damage seals death).
