# Disarm

Permanent Strength reduction. Extremely powerful against scaling enemies.

**When to play:** Save for the enemy with the most dangerous Strength scaling. Against multi-hit enemies, the value compounds (5-hit attack loses 10 total damage).

**Decision points:** Against Gremlin Nob, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage). Against Chosen, playing Disarm adds a Dazed via Hex -- weigh permanent -2 Str against deck clog. If fight lasts 4+ more turns, the Str reduction is usually worth one Dazed.

**Matchups:** Gremlin Leader -- reduces Rally effectiveness. Hexaghost -- permanently reduces all attacks including each Inferno hit. Slavers -- play on the hardest-hitting Slaver. Collector -- play Turn 1 for maximum cumulative value. Transient -- essential for survival; two Disarms makes the fight trivial.

**Slime Boss:** Disarm applied pre-split does NOT carry over to split slimes. Play post-split on the higher-damage slime.

**Artifact interaction:** Against enemies with Artifact, Disarm strips 1 Artifact instead of applying -2 Str.
