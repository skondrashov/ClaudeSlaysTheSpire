# Twin Strike

Multi-hit attack. Each hit applies Strength separately, making this one of the best Strength-scaling cheap attacks.

**When to play:** Against Flight enemies (strips 2 Flight). For Pen Nib counting (each hit counts toward the 10-hit counter). In Strength-stacking decks -- at Str 6, Twin Strike deals (5+6)*2 = 22 for 1E (or 26 upgraded).

**Decision points:** Against Flight enemies, multi-hit is more valuable than single-hit for the same total. In Strength decks, Twin Strike is often the best finisher for its energy cost.

**Synergies:** Vajra/Inflame/Spot Weakness (Strength applies per hit = double benefit), Vulnerable (50% bonus on each hit), Double Tap (plays twice for massive burst).
