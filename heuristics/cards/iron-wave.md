# Iron Wave

Dual-purpose card. Does two things at once for 1 energy.

**When to play:** When you need both offense and defense but only have 1 energy to spare. Against Chosen with Hex (Attack card, no Dazed penalty). During Guardian Sharp Hide (block absorbs retaliation, then deal damage).

**Decision points:** Is splitting between 5 damage and 5 block better than fully committing to one? Usually yes when you need both. With Strength, only the damage scales. Block is affected by Frail and Dexterity, not Strength.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 5/5->7/7 is a 40% improvement on both halves.

**Synergies:** Metallicize (passive block + Iron Wave block = solid defense per turn), Strength (damage half scales).
