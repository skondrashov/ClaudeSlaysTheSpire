# Iron Wave

Dual-purpose card. Does two things at once for 1 energy.

**When to play:** When you need both offense and defense but only have 1 energy to spare. Against [[enemies/Chosen]] with [[debuffs/Hex]] (Attack card, no [[cards/Dazed]] penalty). During Guardian [[buffs/Sharp Hide]] (block absorbs retaliation, then deal damage).

**Decision points:** Is splitting between 5 damage and 5 block better than fully committing to one? Usually yes when you need both. With [[buffs/Strength]], only the damage scales. Block is affected by [[debuffs/Frail]] and [[buffs/Dexterity]], not Strength.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 5/5->7/7 is a 40% improvement on both halves.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Metallicize]] (passive block + Iron Wave block = solid defense per turn), Strength (damage half scales).
