# Melter

Bypasses enemy Block entirely. Play before other attacks on blocked enemies.

**When to play:** Against enemies with Block (Spheric Guardian, Shield Gremlin, enemies using Defensive Stance). Without Melter, damage into Block is wasted -- Melter removes all Block then deals its damage directly to HP.

**Upgrade priority:** Moderate. 10->14 damage. The Block removal is the main value regardless of upgrade.

**Play order:** Play Melter BEFORE other attacks on blocked enemies. Block removal applies first, then Melter's damage hits HP, then subsequent attacks hit unblocked HP.

**Barrage synergy:** Melter strips Block, then Barrage's full multi-orb damage hits HP. Critical in Spheric Guardian and Gremlin Leader fights.

**Decision points:** If enemy has 15 Block, a normal 10-damage attack does 0 HP damage. Melter removes 15 Block then deals 10 HP damage -- effectively 25 damage advantage over playing another attack first.
