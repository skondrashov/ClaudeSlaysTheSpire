# Carve Reality

Generates a Smite for sustained damage. Effectively 18 total damage (6 + 12 Smite) for 2E total across two plays.

**When to play:** Play on any turn where 1E can be spent on damage. The generated Smite Retains, so it can be held for a burst turn.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium. 6->10 damage improves immediate output but does not change the Smite generation.

**Wrath synergy:** Both Carve Reality and the generated Smite benefit from Wrath doubling. Playing Carve in Wrath = 12 damage + Smite for later.

**Smite Retain:** Generate Smite on a defensive turn, hold it for the next offensive turn in Wrath (24 doubled damage).

**Draft priority:** Medium. Good sustained damage for boss fights but does not directly interact with the stance engine.
