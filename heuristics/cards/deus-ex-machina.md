# Deus Ex Machina

Unplayable card that generates 2/3 Miracles on draw and exhausts itself. Free energy generation + deck thinning.

**When to play:** Cannot be played. Triggers automatically when drawn. No decision needed on the trigger itself.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium-high. 3 Miracles instead of 2 is significant extra energy over a fight.

**Miracle management:** The generated Miracles follow all Miracle rules -- hold them for turns where the energy matters. Do not waste them. See miracle.md.

**Deck thinning:** Exhausts on draw, permanently removing one card from the combat deck. Improves future draw quality.

**Draft priority:** Medium-high. Free energy generation and deck thinning works well in any Watcher deck. No setup required.
