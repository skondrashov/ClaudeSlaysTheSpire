# Immolate

High-damage AOE attack. Hits ALL enemies. The Burn downside is minor.

**When to play:** Multi-enemy fights (Byrds, Gremlin groups, Slime Boss split, Collector + Torch Heads). Play freely -- 21+ AOE is worth 2-4 HP of Burn damage across the fight.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 21->28 AOE. With Vulnerable: 42 AOE. With Paper Phrog (75%): 49 AOE. Most impactful card in multi-enemy boss fights.

**Synergies:** Thunderclap (applies Vulnerable to all, then Immolate for 31+ AOE), Fire Breathing+ (Burns trigger 10 free damage on draw -- bypasses Plated Armor; do NOT exhaust Fire Breathing when Immolate is in deck), Evolve (Burns draw replacement cards -- makes Burn downside pure upside), Dual Wield+ (3 copies = 84-102 AOE damage in one turn -- won Donu and Deca), Headbutt (top-deck for guaranteed next turn).

**Snecko Eye synergy:** Frequently costs 0-1E instead of 2E, enabling multiple plays per turn.

**Matchups:** Excellent vs Byrds, Slime Boss split, 3 Cultists, Guardian, The Collector. Primary damage source in both consecutive victories.
