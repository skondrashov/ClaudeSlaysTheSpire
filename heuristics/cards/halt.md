# Halt

Free block card. Excellent in Wrath (12/14 block for 0E).

**When to play:** After other cards when you have 0E remaining, or in Wrath when you need defensive padding for free. At 0E, there is never a reason not to play it.

**Upgrade priority:** Low. 3->4 base (12->14 in Wrath) is a small improvement.

**In Wrath:** 12/14 block for 0E is extremely strong. If you need to stay in Wrath for one more attack but also need block, Halt provides both without spending energy.

**Decision points:** Always play Halt before ending your turn if it is in hand. Free block is never wasted.
