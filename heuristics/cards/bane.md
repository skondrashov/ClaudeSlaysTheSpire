# Bane

Deals damage TWICE if the target is Poisoned. Effectively 14/20 damage for 1E against any Poisoned enemy.

**When to play:** Against any Poisoned enemy. In Silent decks with poison sources (Noxious Fumes, Deadly Poison, Envenom, Bouncing Flask), Bane is almost always dealing double damage.

**Synergies:** Terror (Vulnerable 99 active: 7*2*1.5 = 21 unupgraded, 10*2*1.5 = 30 upgraded for 1E), all poison sources, Dead Branch (can generate Bane randomly -- excellent against Poisoned Vulnerable boss).

**Limitation:** Against enemies without Poison, only 7/10 damage for 1E -- below average. Do not take unless the deck has reliable poison application.

**Double-hit note:** Counts as two separate attacks for Kunai and Shuriken per-attack counters.
