# Bane

Deals damage TWICE if the target is Poisoned. Effectively 14/20 damage for 1E against any Poisoned enemy.

**When to play:** Against any Poisoned enemy. In [[characters/Silent]] decks with poison sources ([[cards/Noxious Fumes]], [[cards/Deadly Poison]], [[cards/Envenom]], [[cards/Bouncing Flask]]), Bane is almost always dealing double damage.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Terror]] ([[debuffs/Vulnerable]] 99 active: 7*2*1.5 = 21 unupgraded, 10*2*1.5 = 30 upgraded for 1E), all poison sources, [[relics/Dead Branch]] (can generate Bane randomly -- excellent against Poisoned Vulnerable boss).

**Limitation:** Against enemies without [[debuffs/Poison]], only 7/10 damage for 1E -- below average. Do not take unless the deck has reliable poison application.

**Double-hit note:** Counts as two separate attacks for [[relics/Kunai]] and [[relics/Shuriken]] per-attack counters.
