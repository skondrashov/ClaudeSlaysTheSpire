# Bite

Colorless card obtained from the Vampires event. Replaces Strikes (all Strikes removed, 5 Bites added).

**When to play:** Always play when drawn. Better than Strike in every way (similar damage, plus healing). 2 HP per play adds up: in a 5-turn fight playing 1-2 Bites per turn, that is 10-20 HP healed.

**Decision points:** The card itself is fine -- the problem is the cost of obtaining it. The Vampires event removes ~30% of your Max HP. At a reduced Max HP pool, the 2 HP healing per Bite cannot compensate for Act 2 elite burst damage.

**Synergies:** Strength scaling (Inflame, Demon Form) boosts both damage and efficiency. Reaper still outclasses Bite for healing, but Bites provide consistent small heals every turn.

**Anti-synergy:** The Max HP loss from obtaining Bites makes Reaper less effective (less HP to heal back to).

**Note:** Bite is NOT an Ironclad card -- it is colorless. Molten Egg will NOT auto-upgrade Bites.
