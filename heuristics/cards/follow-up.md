# Follow-Up

Conditional 0-cost attack. Free when played immediately after another Attack card.

**When to play:** Immediately after any Attack card to get the free cost. Do not play Skills between attacks if Follow-Up is in hand -- that breaks the chain and costs 1E.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium-high. 7->11 damage makes the free attack significantly more impactful, especially doubled in Wrath (22 damage for 0E).

**Attack chaining:** Strike -> Follow-Up (free) -> Flurry (free) = 3 attacks for 1E total. Enables long burst sequences.

**Kunai/Shuriken synergy:** Free attacks count toward the 3-attack threshold. Follow-Up + Flurry after any attack = 3 attacks for minimal energy, triggering both relics.

**In Wrath:** A free 14/22 damage attack in Wrath is exceptional. Chain attacks to maximize doubled damage output.
