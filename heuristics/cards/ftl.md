# FTL

0-cost attack with conditional draw. When played first each turn, replaces itself (or generates +1 card upgraded).

**When to play:** Always play FTL first if possible to get the draw bonus. If another card is more important first (especially with Echo Form), the draw bonus is lost and FTL becomes simple 5 damage for 0E.

**Upgrade priority:** Moderate. Draw 1->2 makes FTL+ a strong opening play each turn.

**Echo Form note:** Interaction with "first card played" mechanic and Echo Form copies requires careful sequencing.

**Safe against Gremlin Nob** (Attack card).
