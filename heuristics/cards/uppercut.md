# Uppercut

Applies BOTH Weak and Vulnerable in one card. The best single-card Artifact stripper in the Ironclad pool.

**When to play:** Against single targets where both debuffs matter. Against Artifact enemies (strips 2 charges). Upgraded version applies 2 turns of each.

**Decision points:** At 2E, is dual-debuff worth the cost? Against Spheric Guardian (3 Artifact), strips 2 charges efficiently. Against Gremlin Nob, safe (Attack card).

**Upgrade priority:** High against Artifact enemies. Extends debuff duration to 2 turns each -- doubles value.

**Synergies:** Artifact stripping (each debuff consumes 1 charge separately), Spot Weakness (Vulnerable amplifies Strength-boosted attacks), Demon Form (sustained Vulnerability keeps damage high as Strength ramps).

**Matchups:** Spheric Guardian -- strips 2 of 3 Artifact charges in one play.
