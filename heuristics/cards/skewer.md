# Skewer

X-cost attack that scales with remaining energy. High energy produces massive single-target burst.

**Critical -- X-cost energy trap:** Skewer automatically uses ALL remaining energy. Play block cards and other spells FIRST, then Skewer LAST with only desired energy remaining.

**When to play:** Best on free turns (enemy buffing/defending) where all energy can go to damage. Pairs with Energy Potions for massive burst (5E = 35/50 damage).

**Synergies:** [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (50% more damage per hit), high energy sources.

**Draft priority:** Moderate. Strong burst finisher for boss fights and elites. Less useful in hallway fights where energy must split between block and damage.
