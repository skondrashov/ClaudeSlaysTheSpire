# Coolheaded

Defensive cycling card. Frost orb + draw.

**When to play:** Play early in the turn to cycle into better cards. The Frost orb provides ongoing block value for the rest of combat.

**Upgrade priority:** Extremely high. Draw 1->3. The +2 draw dramatically improves hand quality. Coolheaded+ becomes an engine piece rather than utility.

**Echo Form synergy:** Echo Form plays Coolheaded+ twice: 2 Frost orbs and 6 draws. Enormous hand size boost -- primary defensive engine of first Defect victory.

**Nob warning:** Skill card, triggers Enrage. Against Nob, do NOT play unless you need the draw to find a lethal Attack.
