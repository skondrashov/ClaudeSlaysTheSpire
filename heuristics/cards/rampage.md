# Rampage

Scaling attack that gets stronger every time you play it. +8 damage per play (cumulative). Resets each fight.

**When to play:** Play every turn you can afford it. Each play increases all future plays. In short fights (3 turns), only reaches 16-24 total -- mediocre. In long fights, becomes highest damage-per-energy in the deck. On free turns, always play for the scaling even if targeting a full-HP enemy.

**Scaling math:** Play 1: 8, Play 2: 16, Play 3: 24, Play 4: 32, Play 5: 40. The +8 accumulates on the BASE, then Strength and Vulnerable apply ON TOP (multiplicative scaling).

**Synergies:** Double Tap (double play = double scaling acceleration), Headbutt (top-deck Rampage to keep scaling chain unbroken), Vulnerable (50% bonus on already-high damage), Inflame (compounds with scaling base).

**Anti-synergy:** Havoc (can randomly exhaust Rampage permanently), unupgraded True Grit (random exhaust risk). Any random-exhaust effect is devastating because Rampage's value is cumulative.

**Matchups:** Hexaghost -- exceptional in the 13-turn fight (scales to 60+ damage). Guardian -- strong for 10+ turns. 3 Cultists -- play turn 1 (free) to start scaling.
