# Carnage

Massive single-target burst. Must be played or it exhausts permanently (Ethereal).

**When to play:** Boss fights for big burst turns. Against Guardian [[buffs/Mode Shift]] (fast phase transitions). Any fight where 2E for 28+ damage is game-changing.

**Decision points:** Can you afford 2E on damage this turn, or do you need block? If drawn on a must-block turn, you either spend 2E on damage or lose Carnage permanently. This tension is manageable with enough block cards.

**Upgrade priority:** Top priority. 20->28 damage. With [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]: 30->42. One of the highest single-card damage outputs.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Headbutt]] (guarantee Carnage+ on top for next turn -- used repeatedly for consistent 28-42 damage turns), Vulnerable (42 damage).

**Matchups:** Guardian -- 42 Vuln damage triggers Mode Shift fast. Byrds -- 28 damage halved to ~14 nearly one-shots a [[enemies/Byrd]].
