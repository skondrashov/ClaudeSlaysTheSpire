# Burning Pact

Controlled exhaust + card draw. Net +1 card in hand.

**When to play:** Removing status cards ([[cards/Slimed]], [[cards/Dazed]], Wound). Cycling to find key cards. Thinning in-combat deck safely. Critical vs [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] -- exhaust Wounds to keep draws functional. Essential in [[bosses/Slime Boss]] fight -- exhaust Slimed cards to prevent hand clog. With [[cards/Feel No Pain]], each Slimed exhausted generates 3 block AND draws 2 cards.

**Decision points:** What is expendable in hand? Exhaust status cards or extra Strikes. Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] and [[enemies/Chosen]], do NOT play (Skill card triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]/[[debuffs/Hex]]).
