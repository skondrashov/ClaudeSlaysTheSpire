# Burning Pact

Controlled exhaust + card draw. Net +1 card in hand.

**When to play:** Removing status cards (Slimed, Dazed, Wound). Cycling to find key cards. Thinning in-combat deck safely. Critical vs Book of Stabbing -- exhaust Wounds to keep draws functional. Essential in Slime Boss fight -- exhaust Slimed cards to prevent hand clog. With Feel No Pain, each Slimed exhausted generates 3 block AND draws 2 cards.

**Decision points:** What is expendable in hand? Exhaust status cards or extra Strikes. Against Gremlin Nob and Chosen, do NOT play (Skill card triggers Enrage/Hex).
