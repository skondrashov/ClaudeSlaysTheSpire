# Battle Hymn

Sustained damage engine. Generates a free Smite (12/16 damage, Retain, Exhaust) every turn for the rest of combat.

**When to play:** As early as possible on a safe turn. Like all Powers, every unplayed turn is lost value. The free turn against [[bosses/Hexaghost]] (Turn 1 Activate) is ideal.

**Upgrade priority:** High. Upgraded Smites deal 16 instead of 12 -- over a 10-turn boss fight, that is 160 vs 120 total free damage.

**Smite Retain synergy:** Generated Smites can be held for Wrath burst turns. In Wrath: 24/32 doubled damage from a free card.

**Hexaghost relevance:** Free 12-damage card each turn helps end the fight before Burns overwhelm. The [[characters/Watcher]]'s equivalent of [[cards/Demon Form]] for long fights.

**Draft priority:** High. One of the Watcher's best rare cards for boss fights. Take when offered.
