# Sweeping Beam

The Defect's best early AOE option. Hits all enemies and replaces itself with a draw.

**When to play:** Always in multi-enemy fights for the AOE. In single-enemy fights, slightly below Strike in damage but the draw makes it net-positive. Safe against Gremlin Nob as an Attack.

**Early Defect priority:** High. Addresses the Attack deficit and provides AOE damage, critical for Act 1 Slime Boss and Act 2 multi-enemy fights.

**Upgrade priority:** 6->9 damage AOE. Solid improvement.
