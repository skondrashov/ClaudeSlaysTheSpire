# Judgment

Conditional instant-kill finisher. Sets enemy HP to 0 if at or below 30/40 HP threshold.

**When to play:** Save for when an enemy is near the HP threshold. Calculate total damage from other cards first -- if they bring the enemy to 30/40 or below, play attacks first then finish with Judgment.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 30->40 HP threshold is significant. Many boss phases end around 40 HP, making upgraded Judgment a reliable finisher.

**Ignores block:** If the enemy has block but HP is at or below threshold, Judgment still kills. Do not waste other cards removing block before Judgment.

**Useless above threshold:** Against enemies above 30/40 HP, Judgment does literally nothing. Do not waste 1E playing it on a full-health enemy.

**Boss fights:** Excellent finisher. Against Hexaghost, getting the boss to 30-40 HP then using Judgment avoids 1-2 additional turns of Burns damage.
