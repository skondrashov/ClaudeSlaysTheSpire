# Masterful Stab

12 damage for 0 energy. Cost increases by 1 for each HP lost in combat.

**When to play:** Excellent in decks that take zero damage. Dead weight in long fights with chip damage.

**Synergies:** Footwork+/high Dex builds (zero damage taken = permanent 0E), short hallway fights with no chip damage.

**Anti-synergies:** Hexaghost (Burns deal unblockable damage, cost rises 2-4 per turn -- completely unplayable by Turn 12), any fight with unavoidable chip damage (Poison, Burns, Decay curse, Brutality), long fights generally.

**Decision framework:** Take when the deck reliably achieves zero damage in hallway fights AND the Act 1 boss is NOT Hexaghost. If Hexaghost is the boss, Masterful Stab is a trap.
