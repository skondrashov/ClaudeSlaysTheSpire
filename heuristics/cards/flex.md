# Flex

Temporary [[buffs/Strength]] that disappears at end of turn. Standalone, almost never good.

**When to play:** Do not take unless [[cards/Limit Break]] is already in the deck or planned. If already in deck without Limit Break+, remove at next shop.

**Exception -- Limit Break+ combo:** Flex+ becomes a legitimate combo piece with Limit Break+. Play Flex+ for +4 temporary Str, then Limit Break+ to double total. The doubled amount is permanent -- only the 4 temporary Str is lost at end of turn. This turned Flex+ from a dead card into a key boss-kill contributor.

**Decision points:** Without Limit Break+, [[cards/Inflame]] (1E, permanent +2 Str) is strictly better in any fight lasting 2+ turns.
