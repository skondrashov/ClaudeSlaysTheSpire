# Compile Driver

Multi-purpose Attack providing both damage and card draw. Draw scales with orb count.

**When to play:** Best when orbs are channeled. With 3 orbs, draws 3 cards for 1E while dealing 7 damage -- excellent value. With 0 orbs, just a 7-damage Strike equivalent. Channel orbs before playing to maximize draw.

**Early Defect priority:** High. Addresses both the Attack deficit and deck cycling needs. Take when offered in Act 1. Safe against Gremlin Nob as an Attack card.

**Upgrade priority:** 7->10 damage. Useful but the draw is the primary value.
