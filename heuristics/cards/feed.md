# Feed

One of the strongest [[characters/Ironclad]] cards. Permanent Max HP scaling.

**When to play:** Whenever you can land the killing blow on an enemy. Each kill adds 3-4 Max HP permanently. Plan other attacks to soften the target, then Feed for the kill.

**Critical:** Feed exhausts on play regardless of whether it kills. If the enemy survives, you lose the card permanently with zero Max HP gained. Calculate exact damage before committing.

**Common mistake:** Feed in the discard pile when an enemy is at killable HP. Feed only works if YOU play it as the killing blow. Plan Feed kills in advance -- hold Feed in hand when an enemy approaches kill range, or use [[cards/Headbutt]] to place Feed on top.

**Take early, use every fight:** Feed's value compounds over the run. In every multi-enemy fight, plan to Feed-kill at least one enemy. Do NOT save Feed for "the perfect moment."

**Synergies:** Headbutt (place Feed on top when enemy is low), [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (15/18 damage kill threshold), [[buffs/Strength]] (+2 Str makes Feed+ deal 14 base / 21 with Vuln).

**Matchups:** Best targets are low-HP enemies: Byrds (~25 HP), [[enemies/Mystic]] (40 HP), Gremlins, Cultists. [[bosses/Slime Boss]] split is the best Feed+ fight in [[acts/Act 1]].
