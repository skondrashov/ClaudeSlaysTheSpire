# Just Lucky

Free card that does a little of everything: damage, block, and Scry. Always playable at 0E.

**When to play:** Play before ending your turn for free damage, block, and draw pile filtering. At 0E, there is never a reason not to play it.

**Upgrade priority:** Low-medium. 3->4 damage, 2->3 block. Small improvements across the board.

**Wave of the Hand synergy:** The 2-3 block triggers Wave of the Hand's Weak application. Playing Just Lucky after Wave of the Hand = free Weak application. This is a key interaction for the Watcher's Weak strategy.

**Scry 1:** Small but consistent deck filtering. Discard Burns or Curses from the top of the draw pile.

**Shuriken/Kunai:** Counts as an Attack for relic triggers despite its small damage.

**Play order:** Play after Wave of the Hand if active (to trigger Weak). Otherwise, play freely as a bonus before ending turn.
