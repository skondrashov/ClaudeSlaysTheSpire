# Vigilance

Watcher's primary Calm entry and Wrath exit. Provides block and enters Calm for the energy loop.

**When to play:**
- Emergency Wrath exit when enemy is about to attack (most critical use).
- Setting up Calm energy loop on safe turns (enemy buffing/debuffing).
- When you need block and are currently in Wrath.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium-high. 8->12 block makes it significantly better for surviving Inferno-type multi-hit attacks and provides a meaningful buffer when exiting Wrath.

**Play order (CRITICAL):** If Flurry of Blows is in hand, play it BEFORE Vigilance. The stance change (entering Calm) triggers Flurry's return from discard.

**Exits Wrath safely:** Without an exit from Wrath, doubled incoming damage is lethal. Vigilance is the Watcher's primary safety valve. Always know where your Wrath exit is before entering Wrath.

**Enters Calm:** Gain +2 energy when you later leave Calm (via Eruption, Crescendo, or other Wrath-entry cards). This is the setup half of the Calm energy loop.
