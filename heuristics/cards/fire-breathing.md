# Fire Breathing

Passive AOE damage triggered by drawing Status or Curse cards.

**When to play:** Play on free turns (enemy buff turns). At 1E, it competes with block and damage on attack turns. Best set up early when Status cards are expected to accumulate. In fights with no Status cards, Fire Breathing does nothing.

**Key synergy -- [[cards/Evolve]] + Fire Breathing:** The strongest anti-Status engine. Evolve draws replacement on Status draw. Fire Breathing deals damage on Status draw. Together, Status cards become pure upside: free damage with no hand clog. Confirmed [[bosses/Hexaghost]] counter.

**Do NOT exhaust Fire Breathing+ when [[cards/Immolate]] is in the deck.** Burns from Immolate trigger 10 damage on draw -- bypasses [[buffs/Plated Armor]] entirely. Exhausting Fire Breathing+ throws away 30-50+ free damage.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Power Through]] (Wounds trigger on draw), Evolve (Status = damage + replacement draw), Immolate Burns.

**Upgrade priority:** Significant. 6->10 damage per trigger is 67% more damage.

**Matchups:** Excellent vs Hexaghost (neutralizes Burns), Sentries ([[cards/Dazed]] triggers), [[bosses/Slime Boss]] ([[cards/Slimed]] triggers), [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] (Wounds trigger). Irrelevant in fights with no Status cards.
