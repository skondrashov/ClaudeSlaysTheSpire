# Shockwave

Mass debuff. One of the most powerful cards in the game. Exhausts but returns to deck next fight.

**When to play:** Play on the FIRST ATTACK TURN of multi-enemy fights. For 3 Cultists, that is turn 2. For Slavers, that is turn 1. Never save for turn 4+ -- preventing damage with early Weak far exceeds healing from Reaper. Never hold for "a harder fight" -- exhaust resets between fights.

**Critical:** After Shockwave (2E), the remaining 1E MUST go to block at critical HP, NOT damage. Confirmed fatal: Shockwave + Pommel Strike instead of Shockwave + Defend at 19 HP vs Slavers resulted in Turn 1 death.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 2->3 turns of each debuff is 50% more duration.

**Synergies:** Multi-enemy fights (debuffs apply to ALL), Dropkick (Vulnerable on all targets = free cycling).

**Matchups:** Hexaghost -- Weak reduces Inferno from 42 to 28. MANDATORY for survival. Gremlin Leader -- mass debuff. Slime Boss split -- mass debuff on both slimes. The Collector -- play when Torch Heads are present.

**Warning -- The Champ:** Shockwave debuffs applied BEFORE Anger are WASTED. Anger cleanses ALL debuffs at 50% HP. Hold for after Anger. With Runic Pyramid, Shockwave stays in hand across turns -- if Fiend Fire is also in the deck, Shockwave will be permanently exhausted by Fiend Fire along with the rest of the hand. Do NOT fire Fiend Fire while Shockwave is in hand during a Champ fight. Wait until Shockwave has been played post-Anger or is safely in the draw pile.
