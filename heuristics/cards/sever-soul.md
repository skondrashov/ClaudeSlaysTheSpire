# Sever Soul

High damage attack that exhausts every non-Attack card currently in hand.

**When to play:** In exhaust-synergy decks with Feel No Pain or Dark Embrace. When hand is mostly Status cards and you want to purge them all while dealing damage.

**Decision points:** Before playing, count how many non-Attack cards are in hand and evaluate whether you can afford to lose them ALL for the rest of this fight. Play Defends/Skills FIRST, then Sever Soul last to minimize cards exhausted. If any Defend or key Skill is in hand, do NOT play unless the fight will end this turn.

**Critical warning:** Do not take this card as a generic damage source. The exhaust-all-non-attacks cost is severe. It is NOT a substitute for Hemokinesis or Carnage. Only take it in dedicated exhaust decks (Feel No Pain, Dark Embrace, Corruption).

**Matchups:** In long fights (Guardian, bosses), losing Defend cards cripples block density for the rest of combat.
