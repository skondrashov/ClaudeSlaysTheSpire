# True Grit

Upgraded version lets you CHOOSE which card to exhaust. Unupgraded version exhausts RANDOMLY.

**When to play:** True Grit+ is excellent -- block + controlled deck thinning. Exhaust status cards ([[cards/Slimed]], Wound, Burn) while gaining block.

**Decision points:** CRITICAL -- is this upgraded? Unupgraded True Grit is a ticking time bomb. In a thin deck, random exhaust may destroy your best card. True Grit+ lets you choose expendable cards (Strikes, status cards). Before playing unupgraded, check: are there cards in hand you absolutely cannot lose?

**Upgrade priority:** THE most important upgrade in the game. Random->chosen exhaust completely changes the card. Upgrade priority #1 whenever True Grit is in the deck.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Dark Embrace]] (draw on exhaust), [[relics/Charon's Ashes]] (3 AOE on exhaust). Be careful of over-exhausting.

**Anti-synergy:** Unupgraded True Grit with [[cards/Fiend Fire]] or any irreplaceable card. Random exhaust can destroy the deck's best card mid-combat.

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] -- do NOT play (Skill triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]). Guardian -- unupgraded True Grit is particularly dangerous; random exhaust removes key cards from a hand already under pressure.
