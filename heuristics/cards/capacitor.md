# Capacitor

More orb slots = more passive effects and higher [[cards/Barrage]] damage.

**When to play:** Play early in long fights to maximize turns with extra slots. In short hallway fights, the Power setup matters less.

**Upgrade priority:** High. +2->+3 orb slots. The extra slot is worth 2+Focus block/turn (Frost) or 7 damage per Barrage+ play.

**[[relics/Runic Capacitor]] interaction:** With the relic (+3 slots at start) plus Capacitor+, [[characters/Defect]] reaches 9 orb slots. Enables massive Barrage damage (63 per play with Barrage+) and enormous Frost wall.

**Barrage scaling:** Each filled slot adds 4/7 damage to Barrage/Barrage+. Capacitor+ adding 3 slots = +21 Barrage+ damage when all filled.
