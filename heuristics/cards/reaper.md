# Reaper

Essential sustain card. Damage + healing in one. Exhausts after one use per fight.

**When to play:** Multi-enemy fights where you heal from hitting all enemies. With Strength, both damage and healing scale. Timing is critical since it exhausts.

**Decision points:** Reaper heals based on UNBLOCKED damage -- enemies with block reduce healing. In scaling fights, Shockwave+ on the first attack turn is higher priority than Reaper healing. Prevention > healing.

**Critical limitation:** Cannot sustain across consecutive combat rooms. A deck relying solely on Reaper will lose 25-40 HP per fight in Act 2 and recover only 6 from Burning Blood. Pair with Feed, Blood Potion, Regen Potion, or Toy Ornithopter.

**Exhaust warning:** Reaper can be permanently lost through random exhaust effects (unupgraded True Grit, Havoc). Protect Reaper from random exhaust at all costs.

**Synergies:** Inflame (+2 Str boosts damage to all enemies = more healing), Vulnerable (bonus damage = bonus healing), multi-enemy fights.

**Anti-synergy:** Demon Form as the sole Strength source. Reaper needs Strength NOW, not later.

**Matchups:** The Collector -- 3 targets, excellent healing. Spheric Guardian -- WORST MATCHUP (Barricade block means Reaper heals zero). The Champ -- single target, limited healing; save for after Execute.
