# Reaper

Essential sustain card. Damage + healing in one. Exhausts after one use per fight.

**When to play:** Multi-enemy fights where you heal from hitting all enemies. With [[buffs/Strength]], both damage and healing scale. Timing is critical since it exhausts.

**Decision points:** Reaper heals based on UNBLOCKED damage -- enemies with block reduce healing. In scaling fights, [[cards/Shockwave]] on the first attack turn is higher priority than Reaper healing. Prevention > healing.

**Critical limitation:** Cannot sustain across consecutive combat rooms. A deck relying solely on Reaper will lose 25-40 HP per fight in [[acts/Act 2]] and recover only 6 from [[relics/Burning Blood]]. Pair with Feed, [[potions/Blood Potion]], [[potions/Regen Potion]], or [[relics/Toy Ornithopter]].

**Exhaust warning:** Reaper can be permanently lost through random exhaust effects (unupgraded [[cards/True Grit]], Havoc). Protect Reaper from random exhaust at all costs.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Inflame]] (+2 Str boosts damage to all enemies = more healing), [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (bonus damage = bonus healing), multi-enemy fights.

**Anti-synergy:** [[cards/Demon Form]] as the sole Strength source. Reaper needs Strength NOW, not later.

**[[buffs/Thorns]] warning:** Reaper does NOT heal before Thorns resolves. Against [[enemies/Spiker]], Thorns damage applies during attack resolution before healing. At low HP, Reaper + Thorns is lethal — the player dies to Thorns before the heal takes effect.

**Matchups:** [[bosses/The Collector]] -- 3 targets, excellent healing. [[enemies/Spheric Guardian]] -- WORST MATCHUP ([[cards/Barricade]] block means Reaper heals zero). [[bosses/The Champ]] -- single target, limited healing; save for after Execute. [[enemies/Spiker]] -- DANGEROUS: Thorns triggers before healing, can kill at low HP.
