# Loop

Doubles the passive output of the first orb slot. Value scales directly with Focus.

**When to play:** Play after channeling an orb you want to keep in slot 1. Since new orbs push older ones rightward, the first slot is the most stable position. Place the orb type you want Loop to trigger (usually Frost for defense) in slot 1 before playing.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 1E->0E makes Loop a free permanent passive boost.

**Focus scaling:** At Focus 3, a Frost orb passive gives 5 block. Loop adds another 5 at start of turn = 10 passive block/turn from a single orb.

**[[relics/Emotion Chip]] interaction:** If Emotion Chip is active, the first orb triggers three times per cycle -- massive passive value.
