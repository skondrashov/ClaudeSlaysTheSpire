# Wreath of Flame

Grants Vigor (flat bonus damage) to the next Attack played. Persists across turns until consumed.

**When to play:** Before your biggest single-hit attack (Smite, Eruption) for maximum value. On free turns (enemy not attacking), play to bank Vigor for the next turn's attacks.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium. 5->8 additional damage. The difference is meaningful when doubled in Wrath (10->16 bonus).

**Wrath doubling:** Vigor is added to attack base damage BEFORE Wrath doubling. Smite (12) + Vigor 5 = 17, doubled in Wrath = 34. This makes setup-turn Wreath into burst-turn Wrath extremely efficient.

**Best on single large hits:** Vigor applies once to the next attack. Use it before a big single hit (Smite, Eruption) rather than before multi-hit cards where it only applies to the first hit.

**Persists across turns:** Set up Vigor on a safe turn, use it on the next attack turn. No risk of losing it unless you play an attack before your intended target.
