# Headbutt

Put a card from your discard pile on top of your draw pile. Strongest with predictable enemy patterns.

**When to play:** When you have a high-value card in discard you need next turn. Check your discard pile -- what do you need next turn given enemy intent?

**Upgrade priority:** High if Headbutt is a regular play. 9->12 damage. With Vulnerable: 13->18. In one fight, unupgraded left Mystic at 1 HP, extending the fight 2+ turns.

**Synergies:** Rampage (top-deck to keep scaling chain unbroken), Carnage+ (guarantee 28-42 next turn), Feed (guarantee kill shot), Bash+ (refresh Vulnerable on demand), Immolate+ (top-deck for guaranteed 28+ AOE), Heavy Blade (top-deck for guaranteed finisher).

**Snecko Eye interaction:** Top-decked cards receive a new random cost when drawn. Top-deck a 2E card and it has 25% chance of being free next turn.

**Matchups:** Lagavulin -- top Bash+ to refresh Vulnerable. Guardian -- guarantee burst for Mode Shift phases. Bronze Automaton -- top-deck Heavy Blade for post-Hyper Beam stun turn. Donu and Deca -- top-deck Immolate+ repeatedly.
