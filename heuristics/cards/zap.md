# Zap

Free orb channel every time it is drawn. Lightning orb deals 3 passive damage at end of turn (or 8 on evoke).

**When to play:** Always play when drawn -- it costs 0E and generates value. The only exception is Gremlin Nob, where Zap is a Skill and triggers Enrage (+2 Str).

**Upgrade priority:** Low compared to other Defect cards. No functional change.

**Nob warning:** Zap is a Skill card. Defect's starting deck has 7 Skills vs 3 Attacks -- Zap being a Skill is a key reason Nob is the Defect's worst elite matchup.
