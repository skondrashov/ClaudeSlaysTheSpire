# Sword Boomerang

Hits random enemies 3-4 times for 3 damage each. Targets are random across all enemies.

**When to play:** In single-enemy fights, all hits land on the same target. With high Strength, each hit gains +Str damage. At +5 Str: (3+5) x 3 = 24 for 1E. Does not Exhaust, so it can be played every cycle.

**Decision points:** At base (3x3 = 9 for 1E), mediocre. Becomes strong at Strength +3 or higher. Against multiple enemies, random targeting makes it unreliable for focus-firing a priority target.

**Synergies:** Inflame/Demon Form/Brimstone (Strength multiplied across 3-4 hits), Vulnerable (1.5x on each hit), Pen Nib.

**Anti-synergy:** Snake Plant Malleable -- each hit triggers Malleable, granting increasing block. Avoid against enemies with Malleable. Random targeting in multi-enemy fights is unreliable.
