# Doom and Gloom

Damage card that also channels a Dark orb for scaling.

**When to play:** When orb slots are not full of more valuable orbs (Frost for defense). If all slots are Frost and the deck relies on block, channeling Dark pushes a Frost rightward toward evocation. In damage-focused turns or when slots are available, excellent.

**Dark orb mechanics:** Dark orbs gain 6+Focus damage every turn passively. They fire accumulated damage at the lowest-HP enemy when evoked. A Dark orb channeled Turn 1 with Focus 2 gains 8/turn; by Turn 5 it has 40 damage.

**Upgrade priority:** Moderate. 10->14 damage is useful but the Dark orb is the main value regardless.

**Synergies:** Barrage (Dark orbs count toward orb count = +4/7 damage per Barrage play).
