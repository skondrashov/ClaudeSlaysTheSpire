# Inner Peace

Dual-purpose card: draws 3/4 cards when in Calm, enters Calm when not.

**When to play:** Depends on current stance.

**In Calm (draw mode):** Draw 3/4 cards. Use when already in Calm and need to find specific cards (Eruption for burst, Defend for blocking). Play early in the turn to maximize use of drawn cards.

**In Wrath or no stance (Calm entry mode):** Enters Calm. Provides safe exit from Wrath and sets up +2E energy loop for next turn. Use when stuck in Wrath with no Vigilance available.

**Play order (CRITICAL):** If Flurry of Blows is in hand and you are NOT in Calm, play Flurry BEFORE Inner Peace. Inner Peace entering Calm triggers Flurry's return from discard. If already in Calm, play Inner Peace first for the draw.

**Upgrade priority:** High. Drawing 4 instead of 3 significantly improves the chance of finding specific combo pieces.

**Calm draw -> Eruption burst:** Draw 3-4 cards in Calm, then play Eruption to exit Calm (+2E) and enter Wrath for doubled attacks. The drawn cards fuel the burst.
