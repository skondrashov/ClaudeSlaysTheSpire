# Bludgeon

The hardest-hitting single Attack in [[characters/Ironclad]]'s card pool.

**When to play:** Boss fights and elite fights where a single massive hit matters. Play on free turns (enemy defending, buffing, or debuffing) where you would not benefit from blocking.

**Decision points:** At 3E, Bludgeon consumes your entire turn. Against [[bosses/The Guardian]], save for DEFEND or STRONG_DEBUFF intent turns. Against [[bosses/The Collector]], play on the STRONG_DEBUFF turn (Turn 4).

**Synergies:** [[cards/Inflame]] ([[buffs/Strength]] amplified by [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]'s 1.5x), [[cards/Bash]] (3 turns Vulnerable for repeated value), [[cards/Double Tap]] (2x Bludgeon = 96 damage with Vulnerable), [[potions/Liquid Memories]] (retrieve for 0 cost).

**Weaknesses:** 3E cost means no block on the turn it is played. Bad against multi-enemy fights where damage needs splitting.

**Upgrade priority:** Moderate. 32->42 damage is significant but the base card is already strong.
