# Limit Break

Strength multiplier. Converts existing Strength into exponential scaling.

**When to play:** After Inflame or Demon Form has provided base Strength. Mid-fight when Strength is already 3+ for meaningful doubling. Does NOTHING if Strength is 0.

**Upgrade priority:** The most impactful upgrade in Str-scaling decks. Limit Break+ (no exhaust) enables repeated doubling for infinite scaling.

**Flex+ combo:** Limit Break+ doubles ALL current Str including temporary from Flex+. Play Flex+ (4 temp Str) then Limit Break+ to convert into permanent. Example: 1 base + 4 from Flex+ = 5 total, doubled to 10. End of turn lose 4 = 6 permanent.

**Synergies:** Vajra (+1) + Inflame (+2) + Spot Weakness (+3) = Str 6 base. Limit Break+ doubles to 12, then 24. Heavy Blade at Str 6 deals 32 (or 44 upgraded). Demon Form + Limit Break dominates long fights but requires 3+ setup turns.

**Weaknesses:** Requires setup (Inflame or Demon Form first), a Skill card (triggers Hex/Enrage), at low HP vs fast enemies the setup window does not exist. As a Neow random rare, dead until a Str source is found.

**Deck building note:** Always pair with immediate damage/block sources for fights where the engine cannot activate.
