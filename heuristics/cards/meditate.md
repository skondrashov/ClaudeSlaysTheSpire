# Meditate

Powerful card retrieval that ends your turn. Play LAST -- always.

**When to play:** Play as the LAST card each turn. It ends your turn immediately. All attacks, blocks, and 0-cost plays must happen before Meditate.

**Upgrade priority:** High. Retrieving 2 cards instead of 1 lets you set up both attack and defense for next turn, or retrieve two combo pieces.

**Card retrieval with Retain:** Retrieved cards stay in hand through end of turn (Retain). Guarantees specific cards for next turn -- retrieve [[cards/Eruption]] for burst, or [[cards/Vigilance]] for blocking.

**Enters Calm:** Sets up +2E energy bonus for next turn. Combined with retrieved Eruption+, this guarantees the full Calm energy loop.

**Flurry interaction:** Entering Calm triggers [[cards/Flurry of Blows]] return from discard. But since Meditate ends your turn, you cannot play returned Flurry this turn. However, Flurry Retains, so it is available next turn.

**Common mistakes:**
- Playing Meditate early and losing the rest of your plays for the turn.
- Retrieving cards you do not need next turn (retrieve what solves the upcoming enemy intent).
