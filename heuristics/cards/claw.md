# Claw

Defect's 0-cost scaling Attack. Deals increasing damage each time any Claw is played this combat.

**When to play:** Play freely whenever 0E is available. The damage accumulates without cost. First Claw deals 3/5, second 5/7, third 7/9, etc. (+2 per Claw play across all copies).

**Upgrade priority:** Moderate. 3->5 base is a meaningful bump on each play, especially early when scaling is low.

**Akabeko synergy:** Claw at 0E is an ideal first attack for Akabeko's +8 Vigor. Turn 1: 3 + 8 = 11 damage for free.

**Gremlin Nob:** One of the best Turn 1 plays against Nob -- 0E Attack, no Enrage trigger.

**Scaling reality check:** Slow accumulation (+2 per play across all Claws). In a 10-turn fight with 1 Claw played 3-4 times: 3+5+7+9 = 24 total. Decent complementary damage, not a primary engine.

**Decision points:** Never hold Claw. At 0E with no downside, play it every time it appears in hand regardless of other priorities.
