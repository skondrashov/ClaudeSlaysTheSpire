# Defend

Basic block. [[debuffs/Frail]] reduces this to 3, which is terrible.

**When to play:** When you need block and have nothing better. Defend+ at 8 block is actually decent.

**Decision points:** Against [[enemies/Chosen]] with [[debuffs/Hex]] active, every Defend adds a [[cards/Dazed]] to your draw pile. Against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], Defend triggers [[buffs/Enrage]] (+2 Str). Consider whether block is worth the cost.

**Upgrade priority:** Low individual priority but [[events/Ancient Writing]] mass upgrade is excellent. 5->8 block is a 60% increase.
