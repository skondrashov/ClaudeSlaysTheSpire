# Defend

Basic block. Frail reduces this to 3, which is terrible.

**When to play:** When you need block and have nothing better. Defend+ at 8 block is actually decent.

**Decision points:** Against Chosen with Hex active, every Defend adds a Dazed to your draw pile. Against Gremlin Nob, Defend triggers Enrage (+2 Str). Consider whether block is worth the cost.

**Upgrade priority:** Low individual priority but Ancient Writing mass upgrade is excellent. 5->8 block is a 60% increase.
