# Second Wind

Mass exhaust for block. Clears status cards while gaining significant block.

**When to play:** When hand is full of status cards (Burns, Wounds, [[cards/Slimed]], [[cards/Dazed]]). Each exhausted becomes 5-7 block. Also exhausts Defends and other Skills.

**Decision points:** Exhausts ALL non-Attack cards including useful Skills. Check what you are losing before playing. In long attrition fights, exhausting irreplaceable Skills ([[cards/Shockwave]], [[cards/True Grit]]) sealed losses.

**Critical warning:** Only play when you are certain the exhausted Skills are no longer needed.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Corruption]] (Skills cost 0 but exhaust -- Second Wind exhausts them for block value), [[cards/Dark Embrace]] (draw per exhaust), [[cards/Barricade]] (block persists), [[cards/Feel No Pain]] (each card exhausted by SW+ triggers FNP -- hand with 5 status = 25-35 block from SW+ + 15-20 from FNP = 40-55 total).

**Normality curse removal:** Under Corruption, Second Wind+ can permanently exhaust Normality from combat. High-priority play.

**Victory engine role:** Highest-impact single block play. Against 5 Dazes + 1 Burn, SW+ generates 48-66 total block from one 0E card under Corruption.
