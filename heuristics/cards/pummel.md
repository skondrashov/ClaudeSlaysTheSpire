# Pummel

Multi-hit Attack. Excellent Strength-scaling finisher. Exhausts after use.

**When to play:** Only when Strength is high enough to make the hits meaningful. At base 2x4 = 8 for 1E, mediocre. Save for mid-to-late fight after Inflame or Demon Form have built Strength. Since it Exhausts, you get one shot per fight -- make it count. Play on a Vulnerable target.

**Decision points:** At +5 Str: (2+5) x 4 = 28 for 1E. At +7 Str: (2+7) x 4 = 36 for 1E.

**Synergies:** Inflame/Demon Form (Strength multiplied across 4-5 hits), Vulnerable (1.5x on each hit), Pen Nib, Shuriken.

**Anti-synergy:** Snake Plant Malleable -- each hit triggers Malleable, causing increasing block (4-hit Pummel can give 3+4+5+6 = 18 block). Avoid against enemies with Malleable.

**Comparison:** Sword Boomerang has higher base per-hit damage and does not exhaust, but Pummel has more hits for better Strength scaling in single-target burst.
