# Rupture

Converts self-damage into permanent Strength scaling.

**When to play:** As early as possible to maximize Strength accumulation. Rupture does nothing without self-damage cards.

**Decision points:** Without Brutality or Hemokinesis, it is a dead card. With Brutality, it creates a scaling engine that makes long fights easier over time.

**Synergies:** Brutality (automatic +1 Str/turn), Hemokinesis (+1 Str per play), Offering (+1 Str per play). Multiple Ruptures stack (2 Ruptures + Brutality = +2 Str/turn).
