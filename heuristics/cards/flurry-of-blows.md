# Flurry of Blows

The Watcher's core free-damage engine. Returns to hand from discard on every stance change for another free 0-cost play.

**Play order (CRITICAL -- SINGLE MOST IMPORTANT WATCHER RULE):** ALWAYS play Flurry of Blows BEFORE any stance-change card. Flurry must be in the DISCARD PILE when a stance change occurs for the return trigger to work.

**WRONG:** Eruption -> Flurry. Flurry was in hand during the stance change. One play only (4 damage).

**CORRECT:** Flurry (4 damage, goes to discard) -> Eruption (Flurry returns to hand) -> Flurry again (4 more damage). Two plays for 8 total.

With 2 stance changes per turn: correct order yields 3 plays (12 damage) vs wrong order yields 1 play (4 damage). That is 8 free damage per turn wasted by incorrect sequencing.

**In Wrath:** Damage doubles (8 base, 12 upgraded). Always play returned Flurry in Wrath for maximum value.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium-high. 4->6 per play. With 3 plays per turn: 12->18 damage difference.

**Shuriken/Kunai synergy:** Each Flurry play counts as an attack. With 2-3 plays per turn, relic thresholds trigger frequently.

**Multiple copies:** Two Flurry in the deck means two returns per stance change. With 2 copies and 2 stance changes: 6 free plays for 24 damage.

**Draft priority:** High. Take in Act 1 whenever offered. Best common card for the stance-cycling damage engine. A second copy doubles output.
