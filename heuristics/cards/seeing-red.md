# Seeing Red

Free energy. Costs 0 to play (upgraded), gains 2E, exhausts.

**When to play:** Any turn where you have cards to play but not enough energy. Play early in the turn so you can use the 2E on remaining cards.

**Synergies:** Expensive cards ([[cards/Carnage]] 2E, [[cards/Shockwave]] 2E, [[cards/Fiend Fire]] 2E). Enables playing them alongside other cards. Excellent for enabling [[cards/Bash]] 2E + another 2E card in the same turn.
