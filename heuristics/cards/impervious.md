# Impervious

Best single-card block in the game. One-time use (Exhaust).

**When to play:** Save for the biggest incoming hit of the fight. Using 30-40 block on a 16-damage turn is wasteful -- Defends handle that. Calculate: "If I don't play Impervious now, will I die? If yes, play it. If no, save it."

**Timing discipline:** NEVER play on Guardian's Sharp Hide attacks (9-16 damage) unless you would die. Save for the 32-damage single hit. Against Gremlin Nob, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage).

**Upgrade priority:** High. 30->40 block. The extra 10 block is the difference between absorbing a big hit cleanly and taking chip.

**Matchups:** Bronze Automaton -- THE most important card. Hyper Beam deals 45-57 damage. Save for Hyper Beam turns. Guardian -- absorbs the 32-damage attack completely (upgraded takes 0). Gremlin Leader -- save for Turn 4-5 when Rally-scaled damage reaches 34+. Collector -- critical for post-STRONG_DEBUFF turns (37-50+ incoming). Transient -- save for Turn 3+ (50+ damage), NOT Turn 1. Snake Plant -- absorbs 9x3=27 multi-hit. Hexaghost -- save for Divider (Turn 2) or Inferno. In a 16-card deck, there is a ~31% chance Impervious is NOT in your Turn 2 hand. Headbutt can place Impervious on top of draw pile the turn before a predicted Divider/Inferno. If Impervious is the only plan for a specific high-damage turn, the plan is unreliable -- need backup block (Weak, Flame Barrier, multiple Defends).

**Synergies:** Calipers (retains 25 block next turn upgraded), Barricade (retained permanently), Spot Weakness (use the safe turn to set up Strength).
