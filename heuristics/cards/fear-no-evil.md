# Fear No Evil

Conditional Calm entry that doubles as an attack. Only enters Calm when the enemy has attack intent.

**When to play:** On attack turns (enemy intending to attack), Fear No Evil in Wrath deals doubled damage AND exits to Calm safely. On non-attack turns, it is a simple 8/11 damage attack with no stance change.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium. +3 damage (8->11) is solid but not game-changing.

**Play order (CRITICAL):** On attack turns:
1. Play Flurry of Blows first (goes to discard)
2. Play Fear No Evil (deals damage, enters Calm, Flurry returns to hand)
3. Play returned Flurry of Blows again

On non-attack turns: Flurry order does not matter since no stance change occurs.

**Conditional mechanic:** Only enters Calm when the target enemy has ATTACK intent. On BUFF, DEBUFF, DEFEND turns, it deals damage but does NOT change stance. Do not rely on it as your only Wrath exit.

**Decision points:** Ideal for Wrath -> Calm transitions on attack turns. Provides both burst damage (doubled in Wrath) and automatic safety (enters Calm).
