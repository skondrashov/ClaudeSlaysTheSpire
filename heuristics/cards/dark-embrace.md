# Dark Embrace

Draw engine for exhaust-heavy decks.

**When to play:** With multiple exhaust sources. Every exhaust replaces itself in hand. Play on free turns. Powers do NOT trigger Gremlin Nob Enrage. WARNING: encourages over-exhausting, which can thin the deck dangerously.

**Synergies:** True Grit+ (exhaust + draw every turn), Charon's Ashes (exhaust = draw + 3 AOE damage), Fiend Fire (exhaust entire hand = draw many cards enabling a second wave of plays), status cards (exhausting Slimed/Dazed/Wounds draws a real card), Sentinel (exhaust = draw + 2-3E refund).

**Anti-synergy with Burns saturation:** Dark Embrace draws from whatever is in the draw pile. Against Hexaghost, Burns accumulate faster than True Grit+ can exhaust them. Once the deck has 3+ Burns, exhausting a Burn draws another Burn -- the "engine" becomes a wash. Dark Embrace is strong with finite status cards (Wounds, Slimed, Dazed) that don't replenish, but weak against Hexaghost where Burns are continuously added. Do NOT buy Dark Embrace specifically to handle Hexaghost Burns.

**Matchups:** Strong vs Book of Stabbing -- Wounds are exhaust fodder that convert into real draws. Dark Embrace + Burning Pact/True Grit+ turns every Wound into card advantage. Weak vs Hexaghost -- see anti-synergy note above.
