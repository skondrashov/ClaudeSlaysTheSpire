# Dark Embrace

Draw engine for exhaust-heavy decks.

**When to play:** With multiple exhaust sources. Every exhaust replaces itself in hand. Play on free turns. Powers do NOT trigger Gremlin Nob Enrage. WARNING: encourages over-exhausting, which can thin the deck dangerously.

**Synergies:** True Grit+ (exhaust + draw every turn), Charon's Ashes (exhaust = draw + 3 AOE damage), Fiend Fire (exhaust entire hand = draw many cards enabling a second wave of plays), status cards (exhausting Slimed/Dazed/Wounds draws a real card), Sentinel (exhaust = draw + 2-3E refund).

**Matchups:** Strong vs Book of Stabbing -- Wounds are exhaust fodder that convert into real draws. Dark Embrace + Burning Pact/True Grit+ turns every Wound into card advantage.
