# Dual Wield

Creates copies of an Attack or Power card in your hand. Copies go to hand and can be played immediately.

**When to play:** In boss fights and tough hallway fights on a high-value Attack. Timing: play mid-fight when the target card is in hand and you have energy to play the copies.

**Best targets:** [[cards/Immolate]] (84-102 AOE across 3 copies -- won [[bosses/Donu and Deca]]), [[cards/Heavy Blade]] (triple at high [[buffs/Strength]]), [[cards/Impervious]] (90-120 block in one turn), [[cards/Flame Barrier]] (stacking block and return damage).

**[[relics/Snecko Eye]] synergy:** Under Confusion, each copy gets a new random cost. ~44% chance at least one costs 0E with 2 copies.

**Decision points:** A Skill card -- triggers [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]]'s [[buffs/Enrage]] and [[enemies/Chosen]]'s [[debuffs/Hex]]. In those fights, do not play.

**Upgrade priority:** Very high. 1 copy vs 2 copies is the difference between "good" and "game-winning."
