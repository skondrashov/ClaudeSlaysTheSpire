# Dual Wield

Creates copies of an Attack or Power card in your hand. Copies go to hand and can be played immediately.

**When to play:** In boss fights and tough hallway fights on a high-value Attack. Timing: play mid-fight when the target card is in hand and you have energy to play the copies.

**Best targets:** Immolate+ (84-102 AOE across 3 copies -- won Donu and Deca), Heavy Blade+ (triple at high Strength), Impervious (90-120 block in one turn), Flame Barrier (stacking block and return damage).

**Snecko Eye synergy:** Under Confusion, each copy gets a new random cost. ~44% chance at least one costs 0E with 2 copies.

**Decision points:** A Skill card -- triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage and Chosen's Hex. In those fights, do not play.

**Upgrade priority:** Very high. 1 copy vs 2 copies is the difference between "good" and "game-winning."
