# Study

Slow value engine. Generates one Insight per turn (0E, draw 2, Retain).

**When to play:** On a safe turn (enemy not attacking). The 2E cost makes it unsuitable for defensive turns. Must be deployed early to accumulate enough Insights for value.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium. Adding Insight to hand instead of draw pile gives immediate access rather than waiting a draw cycle.

**2E cost warning:** Spending 2E on a Power that does nothing immediately is risky on turns with incoming damage. Only play when survival is not in question.

**Draft priority:** Low-medium. Too slow for most hallway fights. Only valuable in long boss fights where cumulative draw advantage matters. Skip in aggressive decks that end fights quickly.
