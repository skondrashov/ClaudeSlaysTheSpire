# Havoc

Plays the top card of the draw pile for free, then exhausts it. The played card's energy cost is ignored.

**When to play:** When the draw pile contains expendable cards (Strikes, status, Wounds, Burns, [[cards/Dazed]]) and you want a free action. Best when you know what is on top (e.g., after [[cards/Headbutt]]).

**Critical danger:** Havoc exhausts the played card permanently. If the top card is a key scaling card ([[cards/Rampage]], [[cards/Fiend Fire]], [[cards/Bash]]), it is gone forever.

**Decision points:** Before playing, consider what is in the draw pile. Havoc is safe when: (1) draw pile is mostly Strikes/Defends, (2) you tracked what remains, (3) the fight is trivial. Havoc is dangerous when: (1) deck contains irreplaceable cards, (2) you have not tracked draw pile contents.

**Synergies:** Headbutt (place a specific card on top, then Havoc plays it for free -- controlled combo).

**Anti-synergy:** Any deck with irreplaceable scaling cards (Rampage, Fiend Fire, Feed). DO NOT take if the deck depends on specific cards.

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] -- Havoc is a Skill, triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]. Do NOT play.
