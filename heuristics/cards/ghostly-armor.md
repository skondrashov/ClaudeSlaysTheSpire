# Ghostly Armor

Strong block for 1 energy (double Defend). Ethereal means must play or lose permanently.

**When to play:** Whenever it is in hand. Always play -- losing it permanently is worse than wasting block.

**Decision points:** Do you have 1E to spare? If yes, always play. If forced to choose between Ghostly Armor and a critical play, remember: losing Ghostly Armor permanently is a real cost.
