# Evolve

Neutralizes hand-clogging from Status cards. Each Status drawn is immediately replaced by a real card.

**When to play:** Play on free turns. At 1E, it competes with block/damage on attack turns. Best set up early in fights where Status cards are expected. Without [[relics/Mark of Pain]], zero value in fights without Status card generation -- do NOT play in non-Status fights as it wastes 1E.

**Synergies:** Mark of Pain + Evolve+ (Wounds from attacks become massive card advantage -- 4E + bonus draws = 5-7 cards/turn), [[bosses/Hexaghost]] Burns / [[cards/Immolate]] Burns (Burns become cycle-neutral), Sentries [[cards/Dazed]] (draw replacement, Dazed exhausts itself), [[cards/Fire Breathing]] (drawing Status = damage + replacement).

**Upgrade priority:** HIGH with Mark of Pain (2 draws per Wound = dominant card advantage). Moderate without Mark of Pain.

**Matchups:** Excellent vs Hexaghost (top-priority Turn 1 setup). Excellent vs Sentries, [[bosses/Slime Boss]], [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]]. With Mark of Pain, has value in EVERY fight. [[enemies/Chosen]] adds Dazed via [[debuffs/Hex]], so Evolve has value after Hex activation.
