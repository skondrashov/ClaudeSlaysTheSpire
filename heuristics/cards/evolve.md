# Evolve

Neutralizes hand-clogging from Status cards. Each Status drawn is immediately replaced by a real card.

**When to play:** Play on free turns. At 1E, it competes with block/damage on attack turns. Best set up early in fights where Status cards are expected. Without Mark of Pain, zero value in fights without Status card generation -- do NOT play in non-Status fights as it wastes 1E.

**Synergies:** Mark of Pain + Evolve+ (Wounds from attacks become massive card advantage -- 4E + bonus draws = 5-7 cards/turn), Hexaghost Burns / Immolate Burns (Burns become cycle-neutral), Sentries Dazed (draw replacement, Dazed exhausts itself), Fire Breathing (drawing Status = damage + replacement).

**Upgrade priority:** HIGH with Mark of Pain (2 draws per Wound = dominant card advantage). Moderate without Mark of Pain.

**Matchups:** Excellent vs Hexaghost (top-priority Turn 1 setup). Excellent vs Sentries, Slime Boss, Book of Stabbing. With Mark of Pain, has value in EVERY fight. Chosen adds Dazed via Hex, so Evolve has value after Hex activation.
