# Apotheosis

Mass upgrade for the entire deck. The single most impactful card when upgrades are scarce.

**When to play:** Play on a free turn (enemy buffing/debuffing) early in a fight. The earlier it is played, the more turns benefit. In boss fights, Turn 1-2 is ideal. Under Corruption, costs 0E and exhausts -- best possible outcome.

**Decision points:** With Fusion Hammer (cannot Smith at rest sites), Apotheosis is the ONLY way to upgrade cards. It upgrades everything: Barricade 3E->2E, Corruption 3E->2E, Impervious 30->40, Feel No Pain 3->4, Immolate 21->28, all simultaneously.

**Synergies:** Fusion Hammer (the #1 synergy), Corruption (becomes 0E), Feel No Pain (exhaust under Corruption triggers block).

**Source:** Colorless card from events (Sensory Stone "Recall" option). Cannot be obtained from normal card rewards.
