# Phantasmal Killer

Grants Double Damage buff for the NEXT Attack card played. Buff persists until consumed.

**When to play:** The turn BEFORE a burst turn. On a free turn (enemy buffing/defending), play PK. Next turn, play the highest-value Attack for doubled damage. With Shivs: PK then [[cards/Blade Dance]] next turn for 3 doubled Shivs.

**Synergies:** Blade Dance (3 Shivs, each doubled), [[cards/Accuracy]] (+4/+8 per [[cards/Shiv]], all doubled), [[cards/Finisher]] (as an Attack, its total is also doubled), [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] (multiplicative with Double Damage).

**[[debuffs/Time Warp]] trick:** If counter is at 11/12, play PK as the 12th card on a free turn. Time Warp triggers, but Double Damage persists for the fresh cycle's first attack.

**Draft priority:** High when deck has Blade Dance and/or Accuracy. Upgraded version (0E) is strictly better -- free setup any turn.
