# Hemokinesis

High burst for 1 energy. The 2 HP self-damage is minor when healthy.

**When to play:** Opening turns, boss burst damage, finishing blows where HP cost is irrelevant. With [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] on target: floor(15*1.5) = 22 damage for 1E -- exceptional burst.

**Decision points:** Check current HP. At low HP in a long fight, the 2 HP per play adds up. Against [[bosses/Slime Boss]] split phase, the 2 HP cost is especially dangerous -- combined with split damage, even one Hemokinesis can be the difference between survival and death.

**Synergies:** Vulnerable (22 damage for 1E), [[cards/Rupture]] (+1 Str per play from self-damage), kill shots where HP cost is irrelevant.
