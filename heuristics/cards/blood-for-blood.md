# Blood for Blood

Starts at 4 Energy but becomes cheaper as you take damage. After losing 4+ HP instances, costs 0 Energy -- free 18-22 damage.

**When to play:** At 0 cost, always play. At 1-2 cost, evaluate energy vs damage. At 3-4 cost (start of fight before taking damage), hold it and play other cards first. The cost reduction is permanent for the combat.

**Decision points:** In decks that prevent all damage (Barricade + heavy block), the cost may never reduce. At 3-4E, it is dead weight and a prime candidate for True Grit+ exhaust.

**Synergies:** Double Tap (double a 0-cost Attack = 36+ free damage), Inflame/Strength, Brutality's 1 HP/turn self-damage counts as a damage instance (reaches 0E faster).

**Weaknesses:** Terrible on turn 1 of fights where you block all damage. Not reliable as the only damage source.
