# Flame Barrier

Block + thorns effect. Punishes multi-hit attacks.

**When to play:** Excellent against multi-hit enemies. Flame Barrier+ against Hexaghost Inferno (7 hits) = 16 block + 42 counter damage. Against multi-enemy fights (Slavers, Gremlins), each attacker triggers counter damage.

**Decision points:** At 2E, competes with other defensive plays. Worth it when facing multi-hit attacks or multiple attackers. On single-hit attack turns, a Defend may be more energy-efficient.

**Upgrade priority:** High when facing Hexaghost. 12->16 block, 4->6 counter per hit. Single best defensive card for Inferno turns.

**Synergies:** Hexaghost Inferno (7 hits x 6 counter = 42 free damage), multi-enemy fights (each attacker triggers counter), Book of Stabbing (multi-hit attacks trigger counter on every hit, and hit count increases each turn).

**Matchups:** Best-in-class vs Hexaghost. Strong vs Book of Stabbing. Moderate vs single large attackers.
