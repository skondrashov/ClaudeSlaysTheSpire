# Bloodletting

Energy generation at an HP cost. Enables big turns.

**When to play:** When the extra energy enables something critical this turn. Do NOT play if you have nothing impactful to spend the energy on -- 3 HP for wasted energy is a net loss. At low HP (<15), the 3 HP cost becomes dangerous.

**Critical sequencing:** Do NOT batch Bloodletting with `end` in the same action sequence. The generated energy is wasted if the turn ends immediately. Play Bloodletting, evaluate hand, spend the energy, THEN end the turn.

**Upgrade priority:** Significant. 2->3 energy makes Bloodletting+ one of the best energy cards in the game.

**Synergies:** Rupture (lose HP triggers +1 Str permanently -- best Rupture enabler since it costs 0E), Corruption (extra energy helps survive the 3E setup turn), any 2-3E card needing energy flexibility.
