# Heavy Blade

High-cost Strength multiplier. Worthless without Strength, game-winning with it.

**When to play:** Only take when the deck has Strength sources (Vajra, Inflame, Spot Weakness, Limit Break, Demon Form). At 0 Strength, just 14 damage for 2E (terrible). At Str 6+, highest damage-per-play available.

**Upgrade priority:** High in Strength decks. 3x to 5x Strength multiplier is transformative. At Str 6: 32 -> 44 damage.

**Synergies:** Demon Form+ (by turn 4 at +12 Str, deals 74 damage; by turn 6 at +18 Str, deals 104), Limit Break+ (doubling Str before Heavy Blade = exponential), Double Tap (plays twice for 64+ damage), Spot Weakness (+3 Str becomes +9/+15 damage), Vulnerable (applies to full total).

**Snecko Eye synergy:** 25% chance of costing 0E under Confusion. Free 30-94 damage play.

**Matchups:** Excellent in boss fights where Strength has time to build. Less effective in quick hallway fights where 2E without setup is too slow.
