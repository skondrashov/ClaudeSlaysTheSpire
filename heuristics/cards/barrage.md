# Barrage

Defect's primary scaling Attack. Damage scales directly with channeled orbs.

**When to play:** After filling orb slots. With 1 orb: 4 damage for 2E (terrible). With 5 orbs: 20/30 damage (excellent). Always channel orbs before playing Barrage.

**Upgrade priority:** High. 4->6 per orb. At 5 orbs: 20->30 damage (50% increase).

**The Boot interaction:** Each per-orb hit is separate. The Boot bumps unupgraded hits from 4 to 5. With 5 orbs and The Boot: 25 instead of 20.

**Echo Form synergy:** Echo Form plays Barrage twice. With 3 orbs: 36 total damage for one card play.

**Snecko Eye synergy:** Can randomize to 0E -- free 20+ damage. Even at 1E, well above rate.

**Necronomicon synergy:** At 2+ energy, Necronomicon doubles it. With 5 Frost orbs: 40 damage from one card play.

**Vulnerability scaling:** With Vulnerable on target and 5 orbs, Barrage+ deals 45 damage. Primary burst source in first Defect victory.

**Prediction error warning:** Actual values are 4/6 per orb (not 5/7). The Boot bumping may cause confusion.

**Early priority:** Moderate. Weak early with empty slots. Strong once Capacitor/Coolheaded fill the board.
