# Intimidate

Free mass Weak. Zero downside at 0 energy cost.

**When to play:** ALWAYS play when drawn in most fights. Reduces all enemy attack damage by 25% for 1-2 turns.

**Decision points:** Against Gremlin Nob and Chosen, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage/Hex). Bronze Automaton: Do NOT use for Artifact stripping. SAVE for the Hyper Beam turn -- Weak reduces Hyper Beam damage by 25%. Intimidate exhausts after use, so if played early for Artifact stripping, no Weak source exists when Hyper Beam fires.

**Upgrade priority:** Worth upgrading when the deck has few other debuff sources. 1->2 Weak doubles duration.

**Synergies:** Letter Opener (free 5 damage + mass Weak), Corruption (free AND exhausts), Feel No Pain (exhaust triggers 3 block).

**Matchups:** Guardian -- Weak reduces 32-damage attack to 24. Gremlin Leader -- mass Weak on all gremlins + Leader. Bronze Automaton -- SAVE for Hyper Beam turn. Weak on Hyper Beam reduces 51 damage to 38.
