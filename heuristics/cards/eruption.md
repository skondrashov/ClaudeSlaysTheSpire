# Eruption

[[characters/Watcher]]'s primary Wrath entry. The core of the Calm energy loop.

**When to play:** On burst turns when you have attacks ready to benefit from doubled damage. In Calm, Eruption+ is effectively free (spend 1E, gain 2E from Calm exit = net +1E).

**Upgrade priority:** Very high. 2E->1E doubles the efficiency of the Calm energy loop and allows more cards to be played on burst turns. One of the most important Watcher upgrades.

**Play order (CRITICAL):** If [[cards/Flurry of Blows]] is in hand, play it BEFORE Eruption. Flurry needs to be in the discard pile to trigger its return when Eruption changes stance.

**When NOT to play:** Do NOT play Eruption if you cannot exit Wrath this turn and the enemy is attacking. Doubled incoming damage at low HP is lethal. [[bosses/Hexaghost]] Turn 1 (free turn): Do NOT enter Wrath -- Turn 2 Divider would be doubled = instant death.

**Calm energy loop:** In Calm: play Eruption+ (1E) -> exit Calm (+2E) -> enter Wrath (double damage). Net gain: +1E and doubled attacks. This is the Watcher's fundamental engine.
