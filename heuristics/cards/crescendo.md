# Crescendo

Cheap one-time Wrath entry. Exhausts after use -- not a permanent engine card.

**When to play:** On burst turns where you have multiple attacks ready and need Wrath for doubled damage. Also on free turns (enemy not attacking) where entering Wrath is safe. Do NOT play if you have no way to exit Wrath and the enemy attacks next turn.

**Upgrade priority:** Medium. 0E is better than 1E, but since it exhausts, the upgrade only matters once per combat.

**Play order (CRITICAL):** Play [[cards/Flurry of Blows]] BEFORE Crescendo. Crescendo enters Wrath, triggering stance-change effects. Flurry must be in the discard pile to return to hand.

**Exhausts:** Only usable once per combat. Plan Wrath entries around having either Crescendo OR [[cards/Eruption]] available -- do not rely solely on Crescendo for stance cycling.

**Calm energy combo:** Combined with Calm exit energy (+2E), Crescendo enables explosive burst turns at minimal net energy cost.
