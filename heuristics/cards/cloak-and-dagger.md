# Cloak and Dagger

Dual-purpose: block + damage via Shiv generation. Shivs benefit from Accuracy, Strength, and other modifiers.

**Upgrade priority:** UPGRADE WHEN GUARDIAN IS THE BOSS. The upgrade from 1 Shiv to 2 Shivs adds 9+ damage per play (with Accuracy 4 + Vajra 1). A confirmed death occurred because unupgraded CaD produced 1 Shiv instead of 2, leaving damage 8 short of Mode Shift threshold.

**Interactions:** Accuracy (each Shiv gets full bonus), After Image (each Shiv = +1 block, making upgraded CaD effectively 8+ block), Dead Branch (Shivs exhaust, triggering random card generation), Wraith Form Dex penalty (at negative Dex, block drops to 0 but Shiv damage unaffected).

**Sharp Hide warning:** Shivs are Attack cards and trigger Guardian Sharp Hide (3 damage per Shiv). During Defensive Mode, each Shiv costs 3 HP.

**Draft priority:** High in Shiv decks. Both offense and defense in a single 1E play.
