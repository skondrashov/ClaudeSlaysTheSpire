# Body Slam

Converts block into damage. Scales with every block source in the deck.

**When to play:** With Barricade. Body Slam+ costs 0E -- a completely free attack every cycle. With Barricade accumulating 100+ block, deals 100+ damage for 0E. Without Barricade, block resets each turn, limiting damage to 10-20.

**Upgrade priority:** CRITICAL. Body Slam+ costs 0E (from 1E). One of the most impactful upgrades because it costs nothing and leaves full energy for block generation.

**Synergies:** Barricade (block persists = damage grows every turn), Entrench+ (doubling block doubles Body Slam damage), Impervious (30-40 block = 30-40 free damage), Corruption + Feel No Pain (exhausted Skills add block that Body Slam converts), Metallicize (passive block accumulation converted to damage).

**Anti-synergies:** Decks without Barricade (block resets = low damage), Frail-heavy fights.

**Act 1 tier:** LOW. Do not take in Act 1 unless Barricade is already in the deck.
