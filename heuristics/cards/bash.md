# Bash

Primary Vulnerable applicator. Opens every fight.

**When to play:** Always valuable. Vulnerable for 2-3 turns means all subsequent attacks deal 50% more damage. On free turns (enemy buffing/debuffing), Bash is ideal. On high-damage incoming turns, 2E on Bash means less block. Safe against Gremlin Nob (Attack card).

**Upgrade priority:** Top priority in any deck. 3 turns Vulnerable vs 2 is enormous — the extra turn means one more full hand of boosted attacks.

**Synergies:** Headbutt (put Bash+ back on top to refresh Vulnerable), Dropkick (free cycling when target is Vulnerable), all Attack cards (50% more damage).

**Matchups:** Lagavulin — refresh Vulnerable after it wears off via Headbutt. Guardian — apply on free turns (DEFEND/STRONG_DEBUFF intent). Gremlin Nob — safe to play (Attack card).
