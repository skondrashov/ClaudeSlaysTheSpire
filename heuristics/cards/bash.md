# Bash

Primary [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] applicator. Opens every fight.

**When to play:** Always valuable. Vulnerable for 2-3 turns means all subsequent attacks deal 50% more damage. On free turns (enemy buffing/debuffing), Bash is ideal. On high-damage incoming turns, 2E on Bash means less block. Safe against [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] (Attack card).

**Upgrade priority:** Top priority in any deck. 3 turns Vulnerable vs 2 is enormous — the extra turn means one more full hand of boosted attacks.

**Synergies:** [[cards/Headbutt]] (put Bash+ back on top to refresh Vulnerable), [[cards/Dropkick]] (free cycling when target is Vulnerable), all Attack cards (50% more damage).

**Matchups:** [[enemies/Lagavulin]] — refresh Vulnerable after it wears off via Headbutt. Guardian — apply on free turns (DEFEND/STRONG_DEBUFF intent). Gremlin Nob — safe to play (Attack card).
