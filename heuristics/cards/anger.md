# Anger

Free damage card that copies itself into discard when played. 0-cost means it never competes with other plays.

**When to play:** Every turn you draw it. There is no reason to hold Anger since it costs 0 energy. In multi-enemy fights, target the enemy closest to death for the free kill.

**Synergies:** Shuriken (each Anger counts toward the 3-Attack threshold), Rage (3 block per Attack for free), Demon Form (free attacks benefit greatly from Strength scaling -- at +10 Str, each Anger deals 16 for 0E), Vulnerable (50% bonus on a free card).

**Matchups:** Great vs Hexaghost (long fight, copies accumulate, Strength scaling applies). Great vs Gremlin Nob (Attack card, avoids Enrage trigger).

**Caution:** Anger adds copies to the discard pile, inflating deck size. In decks relying on drawing specific cards, extra copies can dilute draws. Rarely a problem since Anger replaces itself with damage.
