# Miracle

Free +1E that Retains and Exhausts. ONLY play when you have a useful way to spend the energy.

**When to play:** AFTER determining your turn plan. Count the energy needed for planned plays, then play exactly enough Miracles to cover the deficit. Excess Miracles should be held (Retain keeps them safe) for future turns where the energy matters.

**Good uses:**
- Enables playing one more block card to survive an attack turn.
- Enables an attack + block card instead of just block.
- Funds the Calm -> Wrath energy loop when short 1E.
- Enables a Power setup on a free turn.

**BAD use:** Playing all Miracles with no plan for the energy. A confirmed death occurred with 4 Miracles in hand -- player played all 3 Miracles for +3E, had no useful cards to spend energy on (only Meditate+ and Rushdown), and died with 0 block against Snake Plant's 21-damage attack.

**Upgrade priority:** N/A (generated card, cannot be upgraded directly).

**Stance interaction:** Miracle does not change stance but Retains. Available on the critical Calm-exit burst turn when extra energy is needed for attacks in Wrath.
