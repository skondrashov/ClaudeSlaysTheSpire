# Leap

Straightforward block card. 9 block for 1E is good value (better than Defend's 5).

**When to play:** When you need block. No special mechanics. Fills the [[characters/Defect]]'s need for reliable turn-by-turn block before Frost orbs + Focus are online.

**Upgrade priority:** Decent. 9->12 block. Lower priority than Focus cards or [[cards/Coolheaded]].

**Nob warning:** Skill card, triggers [[buffs/Enrage]]. Against Nob, only play if survival requires it.
