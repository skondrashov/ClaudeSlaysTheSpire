# Malaise

Silent's strongest boss debuff. Permanent Strength reduction + Weak.

**Critical -- X-cost energy trap:** Malaise automatically uses ALL remaining energy. You CANNOT specify X manually. Play block cards and other spells FIRST, then Malaise LAST with only the desired energy remaining. A confirmed death occurred when Malaise consumed all 4E leaving 0E for Defend at 19 HP.

**When to play:** Save for boss fights. Exhaust means single use per combat. Do not waste on hallway fights unless genuinely lethal. Play LAST after all other non-X-cost cards. Do NOT use for Artifact stripping.

**Synergies:** High energy decks (more energy = larger X = bigger debuff), Wraith Form+ (reduces damage post-Intangible), Shiv burst (neuters offense while Shiv damage scales).

**Draft priority:** High for boss fights. One of the best Silent cards against any single-target boss.
