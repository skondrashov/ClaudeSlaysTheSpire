# Strength Potion

Best used Turn 1 of boss fights (compounds over all attacks all fight). In hallway fights, use for kill turns or when Full Block needs extra damage to kill an attacker.

If HP is below 35% entering a fight, use this potion IMMEDIATELY on turn 1. Do not save it. A Strength Potion shortens fights by 2-3 turns against high-HP enemies (Shelled Parasite, Centurion+Mystic), which means 2-3 fewer turns of incoming damage. An unused Strength Potion on a death screen means 10-20 damage was left on the table.

Against Giant Head with Metallicize 8, even +2 Str on a Strike turns it from 6 damage (bounces off Metallicize) to 8 damage (exactly breaks through). In any fight approaching lethal, use ALL offensive potions before the window closes. Potions do not count as card plays (no Slow interaction).
