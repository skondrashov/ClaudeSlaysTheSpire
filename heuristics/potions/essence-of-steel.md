# Essence of Steel

Best used Turn 1 of long fights (bosses, elites). Plated Armor 4 provides 4 free block every turn. Over 9 turns = 36 total block. Functionally similar to Metallicize. The earlier it's used, the more total block generated. Note that Plated Armor degrades on unblocked hits, so pair with strong block cards to preserve the stacks.
