# Fear Potion

Use when Bash+ or other Vulnerable sources are not available or not in hand. Excellent at the start of boss fights to guarantee Vulnerable on Turn 1 without spending energy on Bash. Also valuable mid-fight when Vulnerable has expired and Bash is in the discard pile.

Vulnerable 3 means 3 turns of 50% increased damage. On a turn with Bludgeon (32 base), Vulnerable adds 16 damage. Over 3 turns of attacks, the total bonus can be 30-60+ damage. Synergizes with Bludgeon (48 vs 32), Immolate (31 vs 21), Reaper (more damage = more healing). Any high-damage turn benefits enormously.
