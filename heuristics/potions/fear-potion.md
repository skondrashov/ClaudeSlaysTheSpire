# Fear Potion

Use when [[cards/Bash]] or other [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] sources are not available or not in hand. Excellent at the start of boss fights to guarantee Vulnerable on Turn 1 without spending energy on Bash. Also valuable mid-fight when Vulnerable has expired and Bash is in the discard pile.

Vulnerable 3 means 3 turns of 50% increased damage. On a turn with [[cards/Bludgeon]] (32 base), Vulnerable adds 16 damage. Over 3 turns of attacks, the total bonus can be 30-60+ damage. Synergizes with Bludgeon (48 vs 32), [[cards/Immolate]] (31 vs 21), [[cards/Reaper]] (more damage = more healing). Any high-damage turn benefits enormously.
