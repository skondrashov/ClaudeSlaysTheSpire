# Fairy in a Bottle

Keep as safety net for boss fights or dangerous elites. Do not waste in hallway fights -- Fairy is often consumed in Centurion+Mystic hallway fights. Plan paths so Fairy is available for the most dangerous fight, not consumed early.

Can be intentionally sacrificed when the math works: if an enemy's attack will trigger Fairy and your Thorns/Bronze Scales will kill the enemy from the revival, this is a legitimate strategy. Calculate exactly before committing.

In scaling fights (Gremlin Leader), Fairy buys one extra turn but does NOT solve the underlying problem of insufficient damage output vs scaling. Plan to win before Fairy is needed.

**Fairy triggers on ANY lethal damage, including end-of-turn effects.** Burns in hand deal damage at end of turn. If your HP equals or is less than total Burn damage, Fairy triggers on the Burns, not on the boss's next attack. This wastes the safety net on chip damage instead of saving it for the actual lethal hit. Against Hexaghost, account for Burn EOT damage when calculating whether Fairy will survive to the Inferno turn.
