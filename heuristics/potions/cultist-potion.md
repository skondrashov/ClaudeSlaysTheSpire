# Cultist Potion

Use Turn 1 of long fights (bosses, elites). The earlier it is used, the more turns of Strength accumulation. In a 9-turn boss fight used Turn 1, provides +1, +2, +3... up to +8 Str by Turn 9. Comparable to Demon Form (+2/turn) over 4 turns.

Synergies: Rampage (Strength compounds with per-play scaling for multiplicative damage growth), Sword Boomerang (multi-hit multiplies Strength bonus), Shivs + Accuracy (Ritual Str stacks with Accuracy bonus on every Shiv -- by Turn 10, each 0-cost Shiv deals 23 damage).

Best targets: Hexaghost (13-turn fight, massive scaling value), Bronze Automaton (long fight, DPS race), Time Eater (10-turn fight). Do NOT waste on hallway fights -- the fight ends before meaningful scaling occurs.
