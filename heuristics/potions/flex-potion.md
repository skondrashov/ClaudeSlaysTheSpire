# Flex Potion

Use on burst turns where you play multiple Attack cards. Each Attack benefits from +2 damage, but only this turn. Best with Bash+ or high-damage cards on the same turn. Do NOT play on a turn where you only play 1 Attack -- the value is wasted. Plan to play 2-3 Attacks on the Flex turn. Much weaker than Strength Potion due to 1-turn limitation.
