# Gambler's Brew

Use when the current hand lacks what you need to survive the turn. Discard attack cards to draw block cards (or vice versa). Particularly valuable in boss fights where a specific card type is needed on a critical turn.

Best used when you can identify which cards in hand are useless for the current turn. Discard all damage cards when you need block to survive, or discard all block when you have a free turn for burst. The more cards you discard, the higher the variance -- but at 2 HP needing block, maximum redraw is correct.

Never discard block cards ([[cards/Shrug It Off]], Defend, [[cards/Ghostly Armor]]) when facing an attack intent, even if the current plan is to burst damage. Block cards are insurance -- discarding them gambles the entire run on the redraw.

Exceptional under Confusion ([[enemies/Snecko]] fight). Confusion randomizes card costs, and a bad roll can make the entire hand unplayable (all 3-cost cards with 3 energy). Gambler's Brew redraws the hand with NEW randomized costs, effectively re-rolling the Confusion RNG.
