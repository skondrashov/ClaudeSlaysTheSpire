# Duplication Potion

Use before a high-value card play. Rampage at 40 damage = 80 total. Carnage+ at 28 = 56 total. Iron Wave+ = double block + double damage (14/14 total). Best with damage cards for burst or Iron Wave+ for combined offense/defense.

Under Confusion (Snecko Eye), wait for a high-value card to roll 0 cost, then duplicate for double value at zero energy cost. Best confirmed use: Duplication Potion on 0-cost Immolate+ dealt 60 AOE to both Donu and Deca. This is the highest-value play -- target cards whose cost was randomly set to 0.
