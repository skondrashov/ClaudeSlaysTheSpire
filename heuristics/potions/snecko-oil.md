# Snecko Oil

Very high variance -- use as a desperation play or burst turn. Can give incredible turns (multiple 0-cost cards) or unplayable hands. Best as a last resort when the standard hand can't win, or when you need a miracle turn.

Do NOT use when your survival depends on playing specific cards -- use it only when the default hand is already insufficient. At low HP, the variance can seal a loss by making block cards unplayable on critical survival turns.

With Snecko Eye synergy: when already Confused, the cost randomization is redundant -- cards are already randomized each draw. The draw 5 effect becomes pure upside with zero additional downside. Use Turn 1 of a boss fight to expand the hand from 7 to approximately 10 cards, enabling massive burst openings.
