# Defect

## Identity

The Defect scales through Focus and orb manipulation. One proven winning engine:
1. **Echo Form+ orb engine:** Echo Form+ + Seek+ (Bottled Lightning) + Coolheaded+ + Barrage+ + Mummified Hand

## Key Heuristics

- **Focus is everything.** Defragment is the #1 priority card — equivalent of Inflame for Ironclad. Without Focus, orb passives are too weak for Act 2.
- **Frost orbs replace Defend cards.** With Focus 3+, each Frost orb gives 5 passive block/turn. Three Frost orbs = 15 free block/turn.
- **Channel order matters.** Leftmost orb evokes first. Channel damage orbs you want to evoke (Lightning) before defensive orbs you want to keep (Frost).
- **Fission MUST be upgraded.** Unupgraded removes orbs without evoking. Upgraded evokes all orbs AND gives Energy + draws. Critical distinction.

## Weaknesses

- **Worst Gremlin Nob matchup.** 6 Skills vs 4 Attacks in starting deck. Zap and Dualcast are both Skills. Do NOT path into Nob without picking up Attack cards.
- **Cold Snap is the best Nob card** — Attack that also channels Frost (orb passives don't trigger Enrage).
- **Echo Form copies count toward Time Eater's counter.** Each doubled card = 2 toward the 12-card limit. Plan accordingly.

## Deck Building

- Prioritize Attack card pickups in first 3-4 floors (Compile Driver, Sweeping Beam, Cold Snap, Ball Lightning).
- Mummified Hand offsets the cost of 3E Powers (Echo Form, Capacitor, Machine Learning).
- Seek+ with Bottled Lightning guarantees finding your key card Turn 1 of every fight.
