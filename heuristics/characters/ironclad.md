# Ironclad

## Identity

The Ironclad's power comes from Strength scaling and exhaust synergies. Two proven winning engines:
1. **Strength engine:** Snecko Eye + Limit Break + Heavy Blade + Immolate+ for AOE
2. **Block engine:** Barricade + Corruption + Feel No Pain + Entrench + Body Slam+

## Key Heuristics

- **Burning Blood buffer:** At full HP, up to 6 damage is "free" in hallway fights. Below max HP, this buffer doesn't exist.
- **Healing cards are mandatory:** Reaper is the most important card in the game for Ironclad. Take it over almost anything. Feed is #2.
- **Strength resets between combats.** Only Vajra persists. Don't carry Strength expectations between fights.
- **Act 1 tier list exists** — see heuristics/strategy.md for the full list.

## Deck Building

Pursue whichever engine the offerings support. If Barricade + Corruption + FNP appear by mid-Act 2, commit to block. If Snecko Eye is offered as Act 2 boss relic and Limit Break exists, commit to Strength.

## Weaknesses

- Skill-heavy starting deck is balanced (5 Attacks) but Nob is still dangerous without additional Attack pickups.
- No innate Weak source (depends on Bash for Vulnerable, needs Shockwave/Clothesline for Weak).
- Self-damage cards (Offering, Hemokinesis, Brutality) are powerful but compound with HP attrition if not managed.
