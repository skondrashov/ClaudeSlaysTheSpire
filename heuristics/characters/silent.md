# Silent

## Identity

The Silent wins through many small card plays with stacked multipliers, not single massive hits. Two proven winning engines:
1. **Shiv burst:** Accuracy x2 + After Image + Wraith Form+ + Phantasmal Killer
2. **Dead Branch exhaust:** Dead Branch + Terror + Storm of Steel + After Image + Footwork

## Key Heuristics

- **Ring of the Snake makes Turn 1 strong.** 7-card opening hand gives more options than other characters. Use Turn 1 aggressively.
- **Neutralize is free Weak.** 0E Attack — always play it. Safe against Nob (Attack card).
- **Poison scales passively.** Once applied, poison does its damage without further card plays. Good for fights where you need to block heavily.
- **Shivs are card plays.** Each Shiv counts toward Time Eater's 12-card limit, Velvet Choker's 6-card limit, etc.

## Weaknesses

- Most Skill-heavy early deck (9+ Skills vs 5 Attacks). Gremlin Nob is extremely punishing.
- No innate Strength scaling. Relies on Accuracy (for Shivs) or Poison for damage scaling.
- Wraith Form is a finisher, not early defense. -1 Dex/turn is permanent and devastating in long fights.

## Boss Matchups

- **Time Eater:** Shivs with Accuracy deal massive damage per card play, making the 12-card counter manageable.
- **Awakened One:** Dead Branch + exhaust engine beats it by dumping Powers in Phase 1 (Str resets) and playing zero Powers in Phase 2.
