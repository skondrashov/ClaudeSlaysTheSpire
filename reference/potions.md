# Potions

All potions observed across runs. Key distinction: some give permanent effects for the combat, others are temporary (1 turn).

---

## Combat Potions

### Strength Potion
EFFECT: Gain +2 Strength for the rest of combat (PERMANENT within the fight).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1, especially on free turns (Gremlin Nob buff turn, Cultist Ritual turn). Every Attack card for the rest of the fight gains +2 damage. The earlier it's used, the more value it provides.
NOTE: This is PERMANENT for the combat. Different from Flex Potion (1 turn only). Strictly better -- use as early as possible.

### Flex Potion
EFFECT: Gain +2 Strength for THIS TURN ONLY. Strength is removed at end of turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: On burst turns where you play multiple Attack cards. Each Attack benefits from +2 damage, but only this turn. Best with Bash+ or high-damage cards on the same turn. Do NOT play on a turn where you only play 1 Attack -- the value is wasted.
NOTE: TEMPORARY -- 1 turn only. Much weaker than Strength Potion. Plan to play 2-3 Attacks on the Flex turn.

### Speed Potion
EFFECT: Gain Dexterity (adds block to all block-gaining cards for the rest of combat). Likely +5 Dexterity.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Before a big incoming damage turn where you play multiple block cards. Defend goes from 5 to 10 block with +5 Dex. Use when you need to survive a massive attack (Inferno, Guardian 32).

### Weak Potion
EFFECT: Apply Weak to an enemy (reduces their attack damage by 25%).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Against enemies with big attacks when no other Weak source is available. Against Hexaghost Inferno: reduces 42 to ~28. Against Lagavulin: reduces 18 to ~13. Use on the turn BEFORE a big attack lands.

### Fire Potion
EFFECT: Deal direct damage to an enemy (exact amount unconfirmed).
OPTIMAL TIMING: To finish off an enemy or break through block. Good for killing Mystic when it has block up.

### Skill Potion
EFFECT: Choose 1 of 3 random Skill cards and add it to your hand. It costs 0 energy this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: During boss fights when you need a specific answer (mass debuff, big block). Can find Impervious (30 block), Shockwave (mass debuff), or other clutch Skills. The 0 cost means it's free value.

### Power Potion
EFFECT: Choose from 3 random Power cards and add one to your hand. It costs 0 energy this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1 of a boss fight, especially on a free turn. Powers are permanent, so early setup = maximum value. Can find Metallicize (passive block), Inflame (Strength), or other strong Powers. Always choose the permanent scaling option -- avoid Berserk (self-Vulnerable).

### Snecko Oil
EFFECT: Draw 5 cards. Randomize the cost of ALL cards in hand (0-3 energy each).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Desperation plays or burst turns. Very high variance -- can give incredible turns (multiple 0-cost cards) or unplayable hands. Best as a last resort when the standard hand can't win, or when you need a miracle turn.

### Essence of Steel
EFFECT: Gain Plated Armor N (passive block at end of each turn, PERMANENT for combat). N=4 observed.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1 of long fights (bosses, elites). Plated Armor 4 provides 4 free block every turn. Over 9 turns = 36 total block. Functionally similar to Metallicize. Note: Plated Armor decreases by 1 each time you take unblocked damage.

### Entropic Brew
EFFECT: Fills all empty potion slots with random potions. Consumed on use.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Before a boss fight or dangerous elite to maximize potion count. Can be used on the map (not in combat). Random potions received can include any potion type.

### Blood Potion
EFFECT: Heal floor(maxHP * 0.2) HP. (Healed 16 HP at 83 Max HP = ~20%.)
OPTIMAL TIMING: Mid-fight when HP is critical. Or save for between fights at low HP. Scales with Max HP.

## Passive/Triggered Potions

### Fairy in a Bottle
EFFECT: When HP reaches 0, automatically revive with ~30% Max HP. Triggers automatically, no manual use needed.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Keep as safety net for boss fights or dangerous elites. Triggers when you would die, reviving at ~30% Max HP. WARNING: Fairy is often consumed in Centurion+Mystic hallway fights. If possible, save it for elites/bosses -- don't waste it in hallway fights.
NOTE: This is a one-time safety net. Once consumed, it's gone. Plan paths so Fairy is available for the most dangerous fight, not consumed early.

### Fruit Juice
EFFECT: Gain +5 Max HP (PERMANENT, persists after combat).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Use immediately -- +5 Max HP is equally valuable whenever used. Best at start of combat for immediate HP buffer. No reason to hold it.

### Regen Potion
EFFECT: Gain Regeneration. Heals HP each turn, decrementing: 5, 4, 3, 2, 1 = 15 total HP over 5 turns.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1 of a long fight (boss) for maximum total healing. The earlier it's used, the more turns of healing. Total: ~15 HP over 5 turns.

### Duplication Potion
EFFECT: This turn, your next card is played twice.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Before a high-value card play. Rampage at 40 damage = 80 total. Carnage+ at 28 = 56 total. Bash+ = double Vulnerable application (redundant). Best with damage cards for burst. Similar to Double Tap but works on any card type (Skills, Powers too).

### Energy Potion
EFFECT: Gain 2 Energy this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: On turns where you have more cards to play than energy allows. Enables playing expensive combos (Bash+ 2E + Shockwave 2E in one turn with only 3 base energy). Use on critical turns, not throwaway turns.

### Liquid Memories
EFFECT: Choose a card from your discard pile, return it to your hand. It costs 0 this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: When a key card is in the discard pile and you need it NOW. Retrieve Impervious for a big incoming attack, Bash+ to refresh Vulnerable, or a kill card for a lethal opportunity. The 0 cost makes it free value.

### Attack Potion
EFFECT: Choose 1 of 3 random Attack cards and add it to your hand. It costs 0 energy this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: When you need more damage output or a specific answer. Can find burst damage, AOE, or scaling. The 0 cost means whatever you get is free. Best during boss fights when extra cards provide more options.

### Elixir
EFFECT: Exhaust all cards in your hand that you choose.
OPTIMAL TIMING: When hand is clogged with status cards (Burns, Wounds, Slimed, Dazed). Mass-exhaust garbage cards to improve future draws. Also useful for controlled deck thinning mid-combat (exhaust extra Strikes/Defends).
