# Relics

All relics observed across runs.

---

## Starter Relic

### Burning Blood
EFFECT: Heal 6 HP at the end of every combat.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Provides baseline healing between fights. At +6/fight, it cannot compensate for fights that drain 30-50 HP (Byrds, Centurion+Mystic). Do not rely on Burning Blood alone for Act 2 sustain -- you need Reaper, Feed, or rest sites.

## Common Relics

### Lantern
EFFECT: Gain 1 energy on turn 1 of each combat (4 energy turn 1 instead of 3).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Enables stronger openers. Play a setup Power AND still have energy for damage/block on turn 1. Weaker than Ancient Tea Set (+2E but only after rest sites) but more consistent. Use the extra energy on turn 1 -- don't waste it.

### Anchor
EFFECT: Gain 10 block at the start of each combat.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Free block on turn 1 reduces opening damage. Equivalent to a free Defend + half a Defend. Reduces the need to play block cards on turn 1, freeing energy for Bash+ or other setup. Compounds over the run -- less HP lost to turn 1 attacks in every fight.

### Happy Flower
EFFECT: Every 3 turns, gain 1 energy.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Passive energy gain in long fights. In a 9-turn fight: 3 extra energy total. Less impactful than Lantern but provides sustained value. Plan big plays on the turns when the extra energy arrives.

### Toy Ornithopter
EFFECT: Whenever you use a potion, heal 5 HP.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Turns potion usage into healing. Each potion heals an extra 5 HP. With 3 potion uses in a run: +15 HP total. Minor but free. Consider using potions slightly more aggressively when this relic is active.

### Strawberry
EFFECT: Gain 7 Max HP.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Always take. 7 Max HP is free permanent value -- equivalent to a free heal of 7 + larger HP buffer for the rest of the run.

### Torii
EFFECT: Whenever you would receive 5 or less unblocked damage, reduce it to 1.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Exceptional against chip damage. Burns (2 damage each) become 1 damage. Multi-hit attacks where each hit is small (5 or less after block) are drastically reduced. Against Hexaghost, Torii halves Burns attrition. Against Sentries, small hits are reduced to 1. Does NOT help against large single hits (Guardian 32, Hexaghost Inferno hits at 6 each are already above 5 per hit without Weak).

### White Beast Statue
EFFECT: Potions always drop after combat.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Guarantees a steady potion supply. With full potion slots, every fight generates a potion. Extremely valuable for boss preparation -- enter with a full potion belt. Pairs well with Potion Belt (+2 slots) and Toy Ornithopter (+5 HP per potion used).

### Pear
EFFECT: Gain +10 Max HP (permanent).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Always take. +10 Max HP is enormous -- equivalent to a free heal of 10 + a permanently larger HP buffer. One of the best common relics.

## Uncommon Relics

### Pen Nib
EFFECT: Every 10th Attack card played deals double damage. Counter persists across combats.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Track the counter. Time the 10th Attack to coincide with high-damage cards (Fiend Fire, Carnage+, Headbutt+) or Vulnerable targets. Pen Nib + Strike = 12 damage. Pen Nib + Fiend Fire (4 cards) = 56 damage. Plan your Attacks across fights to line up the 10th hit on the right card.

### Letter Opener
EFFECT: Whenever you play a Skill card, deal 5 damage to a random enemy.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Turns every Defend into Defend + 5 damage. In fights where you play 2-3 Skills per turn, adds 10-15 free damage. Over a 12-turn fight: ~40-120 free damage. Conflicts with Gremlin Nob (can't play Skills) and Chosen (Skills trigger Hex). Synergizes with Intimidate (0E Skill = free 5 damage + Weak).

### Liquid Bronze
EFFECT: Whenever you take damage from an Attack, deal 3 damage back to the attacker (Thorns 3).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Passive retaliation. Against multi-hit attacks (Hexaghost 7x6): 7*3 = 21 thorns damage from one Inferno. Against multiple attackers: 3 damage to each. However, damage reduction (Weak) is more important than damage reflection. Don't rely on thorns as a primary damage source.

### Bottled Flame
EFFECT: Choose an Attack card to become Innate (always in opening hand, turn 1).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Guarantees a specific Attack on turn 1 every fight. Best target: Bash+ (guaranteed Vulnerable turn 1 in every fight). Other good targets: Carnage+ (guaranteed burst), Feed (guaranteed kill setup). The consistency of always having your best Attack turn 1 is enormous.

### Dream Catcher
EFFECT: When you rest at a rest site, you may add a card to your deck (card reward screen).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Turns resting into resting + card reward. Reduces the opportunity cost of choosing Rest over Smith. Excellent when forced to rest at low HP -- you still get deck-building value. Changes rest site calculus: resting is now less punishing.

### Potion Belt
EFFECT: +2 potion slots (total 4-5 potion capacity).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: More potions means more options for boss fights and dangerous elites. Pairs with White Beast Statue (guaranteed potion drops fill more slots) and Toy Ornithopter (more potions = more healing). Stock up for bosses.

## Rare Relics

### Pantograph
EFFECT: Heal to FULL HP at the start of boss fights.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Game-changing. Removes the need to conserve HP before the boss -- take risky elite fights, spend HP at events, and still enter the boss at full. Changes rest site calculus: ALWAYS Smith (upgrade) before boss when you have Pantograph, since HP doesn't matter. One of the best relics in the game.

### Golden Idol
EFFECT: Increases gold earned by ~25% (not fully confirmed from gameplay).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: More gold over the run means more shop purchases (card removal, relics, key cards). The value compounds over many fights. Worth the acquisition cost if you can afford the HP/Max HP loss.

### Lizard Tail
EFFECT: When HP reaches 0, heal to 50% Max HP. One-time use, then becomes inactive.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: A safety net similar to Fairy in a Bottle but heals significantly more (50% Max HP vs ~30%). Save it for boss fights or dangerous elites. Unlike Fairy, Lizard Tail is a relic -- it doesn't take a potion slot. Once triggered, it's gone for the rest of the run.

### Molten Egg
EFFECT: All Attack cards obtained for the rest of the run are automatically upgraded.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Massive value if obtained early (Acts 1-2). Every Attack card from rewards, events, and transforms is automatically upgraded. Rampage becomes Rampage+, Pommel Strike becomes Pommel Strike+, etc. Reduces the need to spend rest site upgrades on Attacks. Prioritize picking Attack cards when this relic is active.

### Enchiridion
EFFECT: At the start of each combat, add a random Power card to your hand. It costs 0 this turn.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Free Power every fight. Can generate Metallicize, Inflame, Corruption, Dark Embrace, or other Powers. The 0 cost means it never competes with other turn 1 plays. High variance -- sometimes generates Berserk (dangerous) or a duplicate of a Power you already have. Overall excellent value for free.

## Boss Relics / Special

### Ancient Tea Set
EFFECT: Gain 2 energy on turn 1 if the previous room was a Rest site (5 energy turn 1 instead of 3).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: ALWAYS rest/smith at the rest site before a boss when you have this relic. The 2E bonus on turn 1 is worth more than most upgrades. With 5E turn 1: set up Metallicize + Dark Embrace + play damage, or Bash+ + 3 Attacks for massive burst. Don't waste the energy -- plan to spend ALL 5E on turn 1.

### Charon's Ashes
EFFECT: Whenever a card is exhausted, deal 3 damage to ALL enemies.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Creates an exhaust damage engine. With True Grit (exhaust per turn) and Dark Embrace (draw on exhaust), every exhaust = 3 AOE damage + draw. Over 14 turns: ~30+ total damage. DANGER: encourages over-exhausting, which can thin the deck below survival threshold. Don't exhaust cards just for 3 damage -- consider whether you need the cards for later turns.

### Neow's Lament
EFFECT: Enemies in your next 3 combats have 1 HP. Gives 3 completely free fights.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: One of the best Neow offerings. 3 free fights = 3 card rewards + 3 gold drops + potential potions + zero HP lost. Equivalent to ~50 gold and 3 card choices with no risk. Take this whenever offered.

### Odd Mushroom
EFFECT: Unknown -- possibly +1 Dexterity or modifies Vulnerable interaction.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Uncertain. Effect not confirmed from gameplay.

### Maw Bank
EFFECT: Unknown -- likely gold-related (gain gold over time).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Uncertain. Effect not confirmed from gameplay.
