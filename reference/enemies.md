# Enemies

Non-boss enemies encountered across all runs.

---

## Act 1 Hallway

### Jaw Worm (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~40-44)
PATTERN: Turn 1 uncertain (may attack or buff). Has buff/defend turns where it curls up (gains ~5 block) and/or buffs Strength. Attacks for 7-11 damage, increasing over time from Strength buffs.
KEY MECHANIC: Strength scaling. Damage increases each turn the fight goes on.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast. Apply Vulnerable turn 1-2, burst during buff turns.
BENCHMARKS: 4-turn kill typical. 10-17 damage per turn with Vulnerable.
BRING: Bash for Vulnerable, Strikes for follow-up.
AVOID: Slow decks that let Strength accumulate.

### Cultist (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~50)
PATTERN: Turn 1: Ritual (gains +3 Strength, does NOT attack). Subsequent turns: attacks with escalating damage (base 6 + 3 per turn of Ritual).
KEY MECHANIC: Ritual +3 Strength per turn. Turn 1 is completely free.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Go all-in turn 1 (Bash + Strikes for 17+ damage). Kill by turn 3-4 before attacks become dangerous. By turn 4, Cultist attacks for 15+.
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns. Turn 1 free damage: 17-21.
BRING: Bash for turn 1 Vulnerable setup, burst attacks.
AVOID: Slow play. Every turn increases incoming damage.

### Louse (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~11-17, appears in groups of 2-3)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~5-7 damage per turn. Has Curl Up passive (gains 5-7 block when first attacked).
KEY MECHANIC: Curl Up absorbs first hit. In groups, combined damage adds up.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Focus fire one at a time. The first attack triggers Curl Up block, so follow up with more attacks that turn.
BENCHMARKS: Kill one per turn with Vulnerable.
BRING: AOE if facing 3. Focus fire.
AVOID: Splitting damage across all Louses.

### Red Slaver (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~50)
PATTERN: Attacks for 12-13 damage. Has debuff turns (Entangle or similar). Straightforward fight.
KEY MECHANIC: Moderate damage per turn with occasional debuffs.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Apply Vulnerable, burst down. 3-turn kill typical.
BENCHMARKS: 3 turns with Vulnerable.
BRING: Standard Bash + attacks.
AVOID: Nothing special -- straightforward fight.

### Looter (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~44-48)
PATTERN: Steals 15 gold per turn. Attacks for 10+ damage. May try to escape after stealing enough.
KEY MECHANIC: Gold theft. Each turn it lives costs 15 gold.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast to minimize gold loss. Prioritize over non-stealing enemies in multi-enemy fights.
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns to limit theft.
BRING: Burst damage.
AVOID: Slow play (hemorrhages gold).

### Fungi Beast (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~19, appears in pairs)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~9 damage. Has buff turns (free damage windows). On death, applies Spore Cloud: 2 Vulnerable to the player.
KEY MECHANIC: Spore Cloud on death. Killing one makes you take 50% more damage from the survivor.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill one when the other is NOT attacking. Block on the turn you expect to kill one, because Vulnerable from Spore Cloud amplifies the next hit.
BENCHMARKS: Low HP, but the Spore Cloud makes kill order important.
BRING: Enough block to handle post-kill Vulnerable.
AVOID: Killing one on a turn when the other attacks.

### Spike Slime M (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~28)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~8 damage. Has debuff turns (applies Frail instead of attacking). Adds Slimed status cards to deck.
KEY MECHANIC: Frail + Slimed card generation.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Exhaust Slimed cards when possible. Block is reduced by Frail.
BENCHMARKS: Standard fight. Kill in 3-4 turns.
BRING: Exhaust tools for Slimed cards.
AVOID: Long fights where Slimed cards accumulate.

### Spike Slime L (hallway, Act 1, HP: high)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~16 damage. Applies Frail. Adds Slimed cards. Splits into 2x Spike Slime M when killed.
KEY MECHANIC: Split on death. Extends the fight significantly.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Prepare for the split -- have enough HP and resources to handle 2 medium slimes after killing the large one.
BRING: AOE for post-split. Exhaust for Slimed.
AVOID: Long attrition fights (8+ turns drain HP badly).

### Acid Slime L (hallway, Act 1, HP: moderate)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~11-12 damage. Applies Weak. Has Split ability. Adds Slimed cards.
KEY MECHANIC: Weak + Split + Slimed.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Similar to Spike Slime L but with Weak instead of Frail. Weak reduces YOUR damage output.
BRING: Burst to kill before Weak stacks become crippling.
AVOID: Prolonged fights with Weak active.

## Act 1 Elites

### Lagavulin (elite, Act 1, HP: 110)
PATTERN: Starts asleep with 8 block/turn. Wakes when attacked or after 3 turns. After waking: alternates between attack (18 damage) and debuff (-1 Strength, -1 Dexterity permanently).
KEY MECHANIC: Permanent -1 Str/-1 Dex debuffs. These stack and never go away. Each debuff turn makes all your attacks and block cards permanently worse.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Wake it immediately with Bash (Vulnerable > chipping through 8 block). Front-load damage on debuff turns (free turns). Kill before Strength debuffs accumulate. After 2+ debuff turns, recalculate all damage values: Strike goes from 6 to 5 to 4.
BENCHMARKS: 7-turn fight typical. Expect 20-36 HP damage. Use Weak Potion to reduce 18 to 13.
BRING: Bash+ for Vulnerable, Headbutt+ (top-deck Bash+ for Vulnerable refresh), burst damage, Weak source.
AVOID: Slow play. Every debuff turn compounds the problem.

### Gremlin Nob (elite, Act 1, HP: 85)
PATTERN: Turn 1: buffs (no attack). Subsequent turns: attacks for 14+ damage, scaling with Enrage stacks. When player plays a Skill card, gains +2 Strength (permanent, stacking).
KEY MECHANIC: Enrage. Every Skill card played gives Nob +2 Strength permanently. Powers do NOT trigger Enrage.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Play ZERO Skills. No Defend, no True Grit, no Shrug It Off, no Shockwave, no Impervious. Only Attacks and Powers. Use potions for block instead of Skill cards. Turn 1 is free -- go all-in on damage.
BENCHMARKS: 3-4 turn kill with zero Skills. Strength Potion on free turn 1 maximizes value. Playing 3 Skills gives Nob +6 Str, turning a manageable fight into near-death.
BRING: Attack-heavy hand. Bash (Attack, safe). Potions for block/survival instead of Skills.
AVOID: ANY Skill card. The difference is massive: zero Skills = clean kill, 3 Skills = near-death.

### Sentry (elite, Act 1, HP: ~42 each, appears as group of 3)
PATTERN: Two Sentries attack (9 damage each = 18 total) while one adds Dazed status cards to deck. The attacking pair rotates each turn. Each starts with 1 Artifact.
KEY MECHANIC: Dazed card generation + Artifact protection. Dazed cards (Unplayable, Ethereal) clog draws. Artifact blocks first debuff application.
WHAT THIS MEANS: AOE is king -- Whirlwind for 15 damage to all 3 (45 total) is the best turn 1. Focus fire one at a time. Strip Artifact with Thunderclap before real debuffs. Kill before Dazed overwhelms draws.
BENCHMARKS: 7-turn fight. ~22 HP damage. Block on 2-Sentry attack turns (18 damage).
BRING: AOE (Whirlwind, Thunderclap). Focus fire.
AVOID: Slow play (Dazed accumulation cripples draws).

## Act 2 Hallway

### Byrd (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~25-30 each, appears in groups of 3)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~5-10 damage each. Has buff turns where they gain Strength. Flight mechanic halves all damage and decrements by 1 per hit. Byrds can regain Flight on buff turns.
KEY MECHANIC: Flight N. All damage is halved while Flight > 0. Each hit reduces Flight by 1. Multi-hit and AOE attacks strip Flight faster.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Use Thunderclap to strip 1 Flight from ALL Byrds and apply Vulnerable simultaneously. Focus fire one Byrd at a time -- going from 3 to 2 removes 33% of incoming damage. Once Flight is at 0, burst with Vulnerable attacks to kill. Expect 7-12 turn fights and 20-58 HP lost.
BENCHMARKS: With Carnage+: 7-turn fight, ~20 HP lost. Without Carnage+: 10-12 turns, 36-58 HP lost. This fight is the primary Act 2 HP drain.
BRING: AOE (Thunderclap for Flight stripping + Vulnerable), burst damage (Carnage+ nearly one-shots a Byrd), Metallicize (essential for long fight), Reaper (sustain healing).
AVOID: Small single-hit attacks (halved by Flight and inefficient). Entering below 50% HP.
NOTE: Byrds can appear alongside Chosen (Chosen+Byrd fight). This combines Flight mechanics with Hex (Skills add Dazed). Extremely dangerous -- you need Attacks for Byrd Flight stripping but also need block from Skills, which trigger Hex. Enter at 50%+ HP minimum.

### Chosen (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~95)
PATTERN: Applies Hex (adds Dazed to draw pile when you play Skills). Applies Weak to player. Gains +3 Strength over the fight. Attacks escalate from ~13 to 16+ to 36+.
KEY MECHANIC: Hex. Every Skill card played adds a Dazed to draw pile. Blocking is actively costly -- each Defend clogs the deck.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Minimize Skill usage. Front-load damage with Attacks. Bash+ is safe (Attack). Kill before Strength buffs make attacks lethal. With Weak on you, recalculate damage: Strike = floor((6+Str)*0.75).
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 5-6 turns. Damage output drops from Weak -- plan accordingly.
BRING: Attack-heavy deck. Iron Wave (Attack that blocks). Inflame (Strength overcomes Weak penalty). Bash+ (Attack, applies Vulnerable).
AVOID: Playing Defend unless absolutely necessary (each one adds Dazed). Slow kill (Strength scaling is lethal).

### Centurion + Mystic (hallway, Act 2, Centurion HP: ~65, Mystic HP: ~49)
PATTERN: Mystic heals Centurion ~12 HP/turn and gains ~15 block. Centurion attacks for 12-18 damage per turn. Both can have buff/defend turns (free damage windows).
KEY MECHANIC: Mystic heals Centurion. Centurion is unkillable while Mystic lives.
WHAT THIS MEANS: KILL MYSTIC FIRST. Non-negotiable. If you attack Centurion while Mystic is alive, you're in an unwinnable attrition war. Focus all damage on Mystic despite Centurion being the bigger per-turn threat. Apply Weak to Centurion to reduce incoming while you kill Mystic. Expect 6-12 turns.
BENCHMARKS: Kill Mystic in 5-6 turns. Expect 25-42 HP lost even with correct play. Enter with 50+ HP. Fairy in a Bottle is often consumed in this fight -- save it for elites if possible.
BRING: Burst damage for Mystic (need to break through 15 block/turn). Weak for Centurion. Double Metallicize is excellent. Headbutt+ (12+ damage, 13 Vuln through block).
AVOID: Attacking Centurion while Mystic lives. Entering below 50% HP. Wasting Fairy here when elites are ahead.

### 3 Cultists (hallway, Act 2, 3 Cultists with Ritual 3 each)
PATTERN: Turn 1: ALL three use Ritual (+3 Str each, 0 damage). Subsequent turns: attacks that escalate +9 total Str/turn. By turn 4: ~15 damage each. By turn 7: ~21 damage each.
KEY MECHANIC: Independent Ritual +3 Str EACH per turn. This is the fastest-scaling fight in the game.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Turn 1 is free -- use it for AOE damage + Vulnerable setup (Thunderclap). Turn 2: play Shockwave+ IMMEDIATELY for mass Weak + Vulnerable. Focus fire one Cultist at a time -- each dead Cultist removes one scaling threat. The fight becomes unwinnable if it goes past turn 7.
BENCHMARKS: Turn 2 combined damage: ~27. Turn 4: ~45. Turn 7: ~63. Enter with 50%+ HP.
BRING: Shockwave+ (MUST play turn 2), AOE (Thunderclap), single-target burst for focus fire.
AVOID: Delaying Shockwave+ past turn 2. Entering below 50% HP. Spreading damage across all 3 instead of focus-firing.

### Snake Plant (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~60-75)
PATTERN: Attacks for 15+ damage per turn. Applies Frail and Weak as debuffs. Has Malleable mechanic.
KEY MECHANIC: **Malleable** -- gains increasing block each time it is attacked in a single turn. First hit in a turn: gains ~3 block. Second hit: more block. This punishes multi-hit strategies and rewards single large hits per turn. Also applies Frail (block reduced 25%) and Weak (damage reduced 25%), making the fight harder over time.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Use single large attacks rather than many small hits per turn. Apply Vulnerable early. Shockwave is critical to reduce incoming damage (Weak cancels its attacks, Vulnerable boosts your damage through its Malleable block). Kill in 4-5 turns before debuffs stack.
BRING: Burst damage (Carnage+, Rampage), Shockwave for Weak+Vulnerable, single large hits.
AVOID: Multi-hit attacks (Whirlwind, Twin Strike) which trigger Malleable block repeatedly. Entering at low HP -- the Frail+Weak debuffs make recovery difficult.

### Spheric Guardian (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~20, effectively 60+)
PATTERN: Starts with 40 block + Barricade (block persists) + 3 Artifact + applies Frail 5. Attacks 10x2 = 20 per turn. Gains more block each turn.
KEY MECHANIC: Barricade means block never expires. 40 starting block + 3 Artifact + Frail 5. A DPS check disguised as a hallway fight.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Strip 3 Artifact charges first with cheap debuffs (Thunderclap ideal -- 1 charge per enemy). Then apply Vulnerable. Then burst through remaining block + HP. With Frail 5, your block cards give 25% less for 5 turns (Defend gives 3 instead of 5).
BENCHMARKS: 6-turn fight. Expect 30+ HP lost from Frail-reduced blocking vs 10x2 attacks.
BRING: Thunderclap (strip Artifact), Pen Nib (double damage to break block), burst damage (Fiend Fire), Headbutt (find specific cards).
AVOID: Chip damage with Strikes (block regenerates faster than you can deplete it).

### Snecko (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~60)
PATTERN: Attacks for moderate damage. Has a debuff that confuses card costs (randomizes hand costs to 0-3 each turn).
KEY MECHANIC: Cost confusion debuff. When applied, all cards in hand are randomly assigned costs of 0-3 each turn. This can make expensive cards free or cheap cards unplayable.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast before the cost confusion ruins multiple turns. With good RNG, confused turns can be better than normal (0-cost cards). With bad RNG, entire turns are wasted. Vulnerable application helps end the fight faster.
BRING: Burst damage, Vulnerable. Potions to compensate for bad cost rolls.
AVOID: Long fights where cost confusion ruins many hands.

### Shelled Parasite (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~60-70)
PATTERN: Attacks for moderate damage. Has Plated Armor (passive block each turn). When Plated Armor is stripped, may become more aggressive.
KEY MECHANIC: Plated Armor provides passive block each turn, making chip damage ineffective. Need to deal enough unblocked damage to strip the armor, then burst.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Apply Vulnerable, use large single hits to break through Plated Armor. Once armor is stripped, the enemy takes full damage.
BRING: Burst damage, Vulnerable application.
AVOID: Small multi-hit attacks that get absorbed by Plated Armor.

## Act 2 Elites

### Gremlin Leader (elite, Act 2, HP: high)
PATTERN: Summons Sneaky Gremlins (~10-12 HP each). Uses DEFEND_BUFF (Rally): gives ALL enemies +3 Strength permanently and stacking. Gremlins attack for ~9 base + accumulated Str.
KEY MECHANIC: Rally gives +3 Str to ALL enemies (Leader + all gremlins). Permanent and stacking. After 2 rallies: every enemy has +6 Str. The fight's damage scales exponentially with turn count.
WHAT THIS MEANS: KILL GREMLINS FAST. Dead gremlins don't benefit from future rallies. AOE is critical. Apply Shockwave early for mass Weak + Vulnerable. The fight becomes mathematically unwinnable once Strength stacks too high (by turn 8+, incoming damage is 49+).
BENCHMARKS: Must win by turn 6-7 or exhaust cards run out and scaling overwhelms you. Enter with 60%+ HP minimum. At 13% HP, the fight is unwinnable regardless of play quality.
BRING: AOE (Thunderclap, Whirlwind), Shockwave+ (mass debuff), Disarm (reduce Leader Str). Non-exhaust damage cards for sustained fighting.
AVOID: Entering below 60% HP. Exhaust-heavy decks (once Impervious/Shockwave/Disarm are gone, remaining deck is too weak). If below 50% HP, take any alternative path.

### Book of Stabbing (elite, Act 2, HP: 161)
PATTERN: Multi-hit attacks that INCREASE in hit count each turn. Turn 1: ~8 damage. Turn 2: ~18. Turn 3: ~21. Turn 4: ~24. Each attack adds Wound status cards (Unplayable, persist in deck).
KEY MECHANIC: Scaling multi-hit + Wound generation. The hit count increases every turn AND Wounds clog draws, preventing you from finding block cards. This creates a death spiral.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Front-load damage -- every turn the fight goes on, attacks get worse AND Wounds clog draws. Apply Vulnerable immediately for Dropkick+ cycling. Use exhaust tools for Wound removal. Enter with HIGH HP (50+ minimum).
BENCHMARKS: Even with strong burst, expect 5+ turns. 140 damage dealt in 5 turns still left Book at 21 HP in one fight. At 6 HP entry, the fight is unwinnable.
BRING: Dropkick+ (free cycling when Vulnerable), burst damage, Vulnerable application, exhaust tools for Wounds (True Grit+, Burning Pact), Weak (reduces each individual hit).
AVOID: Entering below 50% HP. Slow play (each turn is worse than the last).
