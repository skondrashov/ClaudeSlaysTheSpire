# Ironclad Cards

Card evaluations based on actual gameplay. Entries cite the run(s) where they were observed.

## Starter Cards

### Strike
- Type: Attack, 1 energy, 6 damage
- Evaluation: Baseline damage card. Not great, not terrible. You want to eventually replace these with better attacks, but early on they're your only damage.
- Confidence: HIGH (standard starter)

### Defend
- Type: Skill, 1 energy, 5 block
- Evaluation: Baseline block card. Frail reduces this to 3, which is terrible. Like Strikes, you want to replace or supplement these.
- Confidence: HIGH (standard starter)

### Bash
- Type: Attack, 2 energy, 8 damage, applies 2 Vulnerable
- Evaluation: Strong opener in fights. Vulnerable for 2 turns means your next 2 turns of attacks deal 50% more. Best when you have multiple attack cards to follow up. Upgrading to Bash+ gives 3 turns of Vulnerable, which is a significant improvement.
- Confidence: MEDIUM (used in Run 1, seemed strong)

## Cards Seen in Run 1

### Berserk
- Type: Power, 0 energy (?)
- Effect: Gain 1 energy per turn, but apply Vulnerable to yourself for 2 turns
- Evaluation: DANGEROUS. The self-Vulnerable is extremely punishing, especially against multiple enemies or high-damage bosses. In Run 1, we played this against the split Slime Boss and the extra incoming damage from Vulnerable was near-fatal (we were already at 23 HP). The extra energy didn't matter because we were spending it all on block to survive the Vulnerable.
- When to use: Maybe only when you're sure you can block all incoming damage for 2 turns, or when enemies are debuffing only (no attacks). Even then, risky.
- Confidence: LOW (only used once, went badly — needs more data to know if it's ever worth it)

### Hemokinesis
- Type: Attack, 1 energy, 15 damage, costs 2 HP to play
- Evaluation: High damage for 1 energy, but the self-damage adds up. At 21 HP in Run 1, the 2 HP cost felt bad but the burst damage was needed. Better when you're healthy and need to end a fight quickly.
- Confidence: LOW (used once, seemed okay for burst but risky when low)

### Clothesline
- Type: Attack, 2 energy, 12 damage, applies 1 Weak
- Evaluation: Expensive at 2 energy, but Weak on the enemy reduces their attack damage by 25%. Good against single big attackers. Against multiple enemies, you can only Weak one.
- Confidence: LOW (used once in Run 1)

### Shrug It Off
- Type: Skill, 1 energy, 8 block (6 when Frail), draw 1 card
- Evaluation: Very good. Block + draw is strong because it replaces itself — you get defense without losing card advantage. The draw can find the damage card you need to close out a turn.
- Confidence: MEDIUM (used in Run 1, the draw was valuable)
