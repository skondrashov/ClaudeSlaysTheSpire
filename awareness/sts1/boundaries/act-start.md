# Act Start

Consider, when the act's map appears, before floor 1:

- The boss is already visible — its own page states what it asks of a deck, its damage clock, and what to draft toward; every choice this act answers to it.
- The act's hard fights: the encounter pool is on the act's page ([[acts/Act 1]], [[acts/Act 2]], [[acts/Act 3]]), and the per-enemy and elite pages give each fight's clock and levers — your deck's kill speed prices them for the ledger.
- The whole map, read once: count elites, rests, shops, and unknowns on every viable route, and pick a full route now — [[layer:heuristics, map]].
- Each route priced on the live ledger before preferring the aggressive one; among routes that clear, the elite-heavy one is the default — [[layer:heuristics, hp-management]], [[layer:heuristics, map]].
- What the deck must acquire before the boss — scaling, AOE, healing, block density — and which route's rewards (elites, shops, events) can supply the missing pieces: [[layer:heuristics, drafting]].
- Rest placement against the act's shape: a rest in the last 1-2 floors before the boss, and enough smiths for the upgrade queue — [[layer:heuristics, rest-sites]].
- A burning elite, if the final act is unlocked: its key against its extra buff — [[rules/Burning Elite]].
