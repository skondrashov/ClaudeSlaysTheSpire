# Card Reward

Consider, at any add-a-card choice:

- The deck's weakest answer right now — damage, block scaling, AOE, healing, draw — and whether any offer addresses it. A pick that fixes the weakest link outvalues a stronger card that doesn't: [[layer:heuristics, drafting]].
- The act's remaining exams: which elites and which boss are ahead, and what they ask of a deck — the act route you planned, and the boss's own page.
- What the card displaces: it becomes a permanent share of every future draw. Deck size sets the dilution price; a small deck pays less.
- The upgrade this card will want, against the 2-3 smiths an act actually offers — an unupgraded pick quietly joins that queue.
- The card's cost against your energy ceiling, and any relic that re-prices costs — the relic's page knows (e.g. [[relics/Snecko Eye]]).
- That skipping is itself a pick, with its own considerations: [[layer:heuristics, drafting]].
- How late in the act this is: a pick now may never be drawn before the boss; weigh it against the NEXT act's asks.
