# Rest Site

Consider, with the campfire options on screen:

- The heal-vs-smith comparison's inputs: rests remaining this act, the derived price of the path to the next recovery (this deck vs those fights), and whether current HP covers it with spike margin — [[layer:heuristics, rest-sites]], [[layer:heuristics, hp-management]].
- The smith queue: which upgrade is next in priority and what it compounds into — an upgrade lasts the run; a rest buys back roughly one fight's cost — [[layer:heuristics, rest-sites]].
- What stands between here and the next recovery: an elite or the boss puts its full derived price on this decision (its page gives the clock; your kill speed gives the price) — and check this rest's CHILDREN: a rest whose only child is the elite forces the fight at rest-capped HP.
- Whether resting heals into waste: healing capped by max HP, or a path with no real fights before the next recovery.
- Relics that change the menu — [[relics/Shovel]], [[relics/Peace Pipe]], [[relics/Girya]], a rest-heal ban like [[relics/Coffee Dripper]] — re-rank the options.
- Keys, if the final act is in reach: [[bosses/Corrupt Heart]] demands all three, and the Ruby Key is bought with one of these rests — count whether the act's remaining rests can spare it.
