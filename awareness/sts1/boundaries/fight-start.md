# Fight Start

Consider, when combat begins, before turn 1 is played:

- Who is present — each enemy's own page: its expected HP cost, its pattern, and in multi-enemy fights the kill order (the scaler or support usually dies first).
- The fight's type, which sets the doctrine: hallway means zero damage taken; boss means HP is spent to kill faster; a hard-scaler means kill speed outranks block — [[layer:heuristics, combat]].
- The win condition before the first card: what actually kills this fight, and which cards in the deck carry it.
- What the opening must answer: the turn-1 spike, debuff, or summon the enemy's page warns about, against the hand actually drawn.
- Each held potion's conversion in THIS fight, and whether this elite or boss is the fight it was held for — [[domain:sts1, category:recognitions, potion-use|the potion questions]].
- Whether the fight has structural thresholds — a split, a phase change at half HP, a re-summon — that the plan must cross deliberately, not stumble over.
