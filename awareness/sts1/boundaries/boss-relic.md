# Boss Relic

Consider, at the boss relic choice:

- Each offer's own page first — the relic's heuristics entry knows the deck it wants and the downside's real price (e.g. [[layer:heuristics, relics/Sozu]], [[layer:heuristics, relics/Coffee Dripper]], [[layer:heuristics, relics/Snecko Eye]]).
- What +1 energy re-prices: roughly one extra card played every turn of every fight for the rest of the run — the baseline any downside is weighed against.
- The downside priced against the deck you HAVE, not an average deck: no-potions is priced by your healing plan ([[layer:heuristics, drafting]]); no-rest-healing by your ledger ([[layer:heuristics, hp-management]]); cost randomization by your curve.
- The next act's asks: bigger fight costs, a known boss pool — whether the offer helps pass those exams or only flattered the act just finished.
- Whether an offer is a commit signal for an archetype the deck has already started — [[layer:heuristics, archetypes]] names the pairings.
- That taking nothing is also a choice: a relic whose downside the deck cannot pay is worse than no relic.
