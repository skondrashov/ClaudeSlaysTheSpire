- The four-option structure — a safe small gain, a card option, a gain-with-cost trade, the boss-relic swap — and the current leanings on each: [[layer:heuristics, events/Neow]].
- The resource ordering holds at floor 0: HP > deck quality > gold > held potions ([[layer:heuristics, hp-management]]) — gold here buys nothing before the first shop, and HP paid here is paid before any recovery exists.
- The act-1 exams this pick prepares for: two elites worth fighting and a boss from a known pool ([[layer:heuristics, map]], [[acts/Act 1]]) — the pick that helps pass them outvalues the merely comfortable one.
- What each shape sets up: max HP is margin in every fight of the run; a rare card can be the engine the run builds around; Neow's Lament is three free fights that want the first three floors routed into Monster rooms — the Neow page weighs them.
- Which engines have actually won for this character, and whether an option here seeds one: [[layer:heuristics, archetypes]].
- What the boss-relic swap stakes: the starter relic — for [[characters/Ironclad]] the only built-in healing, [[relics/Burning Blood]] — against a random boss relic that can roll a downside ([[relics/Sozu]], [[relics/Cursed Key]]).
