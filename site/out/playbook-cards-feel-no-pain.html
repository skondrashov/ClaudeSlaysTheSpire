Feel No Pain (1E, Power, gain 3 block whenever a card is exhausted)

Passive block engine that triggers on every exhaust. Permanent for the rest of combat.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Any deck with exhaust sources. Converts Slimed/Wound/Dazed/Burn exhaustion into block. Turns status card removal from a tempo loss into a defensive gain. Strongest in fights that generate status cards (Slime Boss, Sentries, Chosen under Hex).

DECISION POINTS: Play on a free turn (enemy buffing/debuffing) for zero opportunity cost. At 1E, worth playing even when only 2-3 exhaust triggers are expected (6-9 block over a fight). Priority increases with more exhaust sources in the deck.

SYNERGIES: True Grit+ (each exhaust = block from True Grit + 3 from FNP), Burning Pact (exhaust + draw + 3 block), Fiend Fire (exhaust entire hand = 3 block per card), Corruption (all Skills exhaust = constant block triggers -- FNP is critical for surviving Corruption's setup turns by generating block from every subsequent Skill play), Corruption + Dead Branch (the triple combo: every Skill exhausts for free, generates 3 block from FNP, and adds a random card from Dead Branch -- this is the strongest defensive engine in the game), Second Wind (mass exhaust = massive FNP block burst).

SLIME BOSS SYNERGY: Slimed cards cost 1E to exhaust. With Feel No Pain active, each Slimed exhausted generates 3 block, partially offsetting the 1E tempo cost. In a typical Slime Boss fight with 5+ Slimed cards, this generates 15+ total block across the fight. FNP is one of the most important cards for surviving the Slime Boss split because the post-split phase generates the most Slimed cards.

FRAIL INTERACTION (UNCONFIRMED): Feel No Pain's 3 block is generated by a Power effect, not directly from playing a card. Frail says "block gained from cards is reduced by 25%." It is uncertain whether FNP block counts as "block from a card" (and is reduced by Frail) or "block from a Power trigger" (and is unaffected). This matters in the Slime Boss fight because Spike Slime L applies Frail.

SPHERIC GUARDIAN: Corruption + Feel No Pain is the first confirmed engine to defeat Spheric Guardian (only 12 HP lost). FNP generates block from every exhausted Skill under Corruption, providing sustained defense while burst damage breaks through Barricade block. This is the recommended configuration for the hardest Act 2 hallway fight.

VICTORY ENGINE (CONFIRMED): Feel No Pain is a mandatory component of the Barricade + Corruption engine. With Corruption making all Skills free and exhausting them, FNP triggers on every Skill play. In the Time Eater victory, FNP+ (upgraded to 4 block/exhaust by Apotheosis) generated 40-60+ block per fight from exhaust triggers alone. Combined with Barricade (block persists) and Entrench (block doubles), this creates an impenetrable block wall. FNP is the connective piece that makes Corruption safe to play -- without it, exhausting all Skills leaves no block generation for future turns.

ANTI-SYNERGY: Weak in decks with few exhaust sources. If the deck only exhausts 1-2 cards per fight, 3-6 block total is poor value for a deck slot.
