Skewer

Silent Attack, X cost.

Effect: Deal 7 damage X times to one enemy.

Base: 7 * X damage.

Upgraded: 10 * X damage.

Key Mechanic

Skewer is an X-cost attack that scales with remaining energy. High energy (4-5E) produces massive single-target burst. With Vulnerable, each hit deals 10 (base) or 15 (upgraded) damage.

CRITICAL -- X-COST ENERGY TRAP: Skewer automatically uses ALL remaining energy. You CANNOT specify X manually. If you have 4E and play Skewer first, X=4 and you have 0E left for block cards. To control X: play block cards and other spells FIRST, then play Skewer LAST with only the desired energy remaining. See mechanics.md "X-Cost Cards" section.

Optimal Usage

  • Play LAST on your turn after all block cards and non-X-cost attacks. Skewer consumes ALL remaining energy automatically.
  • Best on free turns (enemy buffing/defending) where all energy can go to damage without sacrificing block.
  • Pairs with Energy Potions for massive burst (5E Skewer = 35/50 damage).
  • Pairs with Vulnerable for 50% more damage per hit.

Observed Usage

Confirmed as primary Hexaghost kill card -- Skewer at 5E (with Sozu + Energy Potion) dealt 36 Vulnerable damage in a single turn.

Draft Priority

Moderate priority. Strong burst finisher for boss fights and elites. Less useful in hallway fights where energy must be split between block and damage.
