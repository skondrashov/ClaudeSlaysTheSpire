Terror (Silent Skill, 1E / 0E upgraded, Exhaust)

Apply 99 Vulnerable to target enemy. Exhausts.

EFFECT: The target takes 50% more damage from ALL attacks for the rest of combat. 99 turns of Vulnerable means it never wears off -- this is effectively permanent Vulnerable for the entire fight.

UPGRADE: Terror+ costs 0E instead of 1E. The upgrade is excellent -- a free permanent 50% damage boost to any enemy.

WHY IT'S EXCELLENT:

  • 99 Vulnerable is the single strongest damage multiplier in the game for sustained fights. Every attack for the rest of combat deals 50% more damage.
  • Exhausts, so it does not clog the deck after use. The exhaust also triggers Dead Branch (random card generation) and Feel No Pain (block).
  • 1E cost (0E upgraded) for a permanent 50% damage increase is the best energy-to-value ratio of any damage-amplifying card.

BOSS FIGHT VALUE:

  • Against bosses with 250-300+ HP, the 50% damage increase applied to every subsequent attack saves 3-5 turns of combat. This translates to 3-5 fewer turns of incoming damage.
  • Against the Awakened One: Terror in Phase 1 accelerates the kill before Curiosity Str accumulates. Terror in Phase 2 enables the kill without playing Powers. Confirmed MVP in a victory.
  • Against any boss: play Terror on the first turn you can spare 1E, then focus on damage. Earlier application = more total bonus damage.

DEAD BRANCH SYNERGY: Terror exhausts on play, triggering Dead Branch for a random card. This makes Terror a "free" Dead Branch proc on top of its devastating debuff. In the Awakened One victory, Terror was played in both Phase 1 and Phase 2 (generated by Dead Branch in Phase 2), each time providing Vuln 99 + a random card.

BANE SYNERGY: Bane deals double damage to Poisoned enemies. With Terror's Vuln 99 active, Bane on a poisoned target deals (base 2) 1.5 = 3x effective damage. Confirmed strong interaction.

SINGLE-TARGET LIMITATION: Terror only applies to one enemy. In multi-enemy fights, the other enemies are unaffected. Prioritize the highest-HP target (usually the boss or elite).
