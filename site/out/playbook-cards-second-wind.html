Second Wind (1E, Skill, exhaust all non-Attack cards in hand, gain 5 block per card exhausted | +: 7 block per card)

Mass exhaust for block. Clears status cards while gaining significant block.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: When hand is full of status cards (Burns, Wounds, Slimed, Dazed) -- each one exhausted becomes 5-7 block. Also exhausts Defends and other Skills for block. Excellent in fights that generate status cards (Hexaghost Burns, Slime Boss Slimed).

DECISION POINTS: Exhausts ALL non-Attack cards including useful Skills. Check what you're losing before playing. If hand has 3 status cards + 1 useful Skill, the 20 block from exhausting 4 cards may not be worth losing the Skill. Best when the non-Attack cards are expendable.

CRITICAL WARNING: In long attrition fights (Spheric Guardian, bosses), Second Wind can exhaust irreplaceable Skills like Shockwave+, True Grit+, and block cards. Once exhausted, these cards are gone for the rest of the fight. Against Spheric Guardian specifically, exhausting Shockwave+ and True Grit+ sealed a loss because the remaining deck had no way to apply debuffs or manage status cards. Only play Second Wind when you are certain the exhausted Skills are no longer needed.

SYNERGIES: Corruption (Skills cost 0 but exhaust -- Second Wind exhausts them for block value instead). Status-heavy fights. Dark Embrace (draw per exhaust). Barricade (block from SW+ persists), Feel No Pain (each card exhausted by SW+ triggers FNP -- a hand with 5 status cards = 25-35 block from SW+ + 15-20 block from FNP = 40-55 total from one card).

NORMALITY CURSE REMOVAL: Second Wind+ under Corruption can permanently exhaust the Normality curse from your deck during combat. Normality (limit 3 plays/turn) is devastating for Corruption engine decks that need many plays. Exhausting it with SW+ removes it for the rest of the combat AND generates 5-7 block + FNP trigger. This is a high-priority play whenever Normality is in hand with SW+ and Corruption active.

VICTORY ENGINE ROLE: In the Barricade + Corruption + FNP engine, Second Wind+ is the highest-impact single block play. Against a hand with 5 Dazes + 1 Burn, SW+ generates 30-42 block + 18-24 FNP block = 48-66 total block from one 0E card under Corruption. Observed: 94 total block from a single Second Wind+ play (including FNP triggers) against Chosen with Hex-generated Dazes.
