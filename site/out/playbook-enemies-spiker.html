Spiker (hallway, Act 3, HP: 42-56)

PATTERN: Each turn, 50/50 chance of using Cut (attack) or Spike (buff Thorns). Cannot use Cut twice in a row. Cannot use Spike after it has been used 6 times (forces Cut instead).

KEY MECHANIC: Thorns. Starts with Thorns 3. Each Spike adds +2 Thorns. Every time you deal attack damage to a Spiker, you take damage equal to its current Thorns. As Thorns stack (3 -> 5 -> 7 -> 9 -> 11 -> 13 -> 15), attacking becomes increasingly costly. After 6 Spikes, Thorns reach 15 -- every attack card played costs 15 HP.

DAMAGE:

  • Cut: 7 damage (9 at A2)
  • Thorns retaliation: starts at 3 per attack hit, grows by 2 per Spike turn

BUFFS: Thorns 3 (innate), gains +2 Thorns per Spike use

WHAT THIS MEANS: Poison is the ideal damage source -- it bypasses Thorns entirely. Multi-hit attacks (Blade Dance, Dagger Spray) are extremely punished since each hit triggers Thorns separately. A 4-hit Blade Dance against Thorns 9 costs 36 HP. Single large attacks are better than many small ones if you must use attacks. Kill Spikers before Thorns stack too high.

STRATEGY:

  • Poison is king. Noxious Fumes, Deadly Poison, Bouncing Flask all deal damage without triggering Thorns
  • If you must attack, use single large hits (Dash+, Predator) not multi-hit attacks
  • Kill Spikers early before Thorns compound. At Thorns 3-5, attacks are tolerable. At 9+, every attack card is a significant HP loss
  • In multi-enemy fights (3 Shapes), kill the Spiker first to stop Thorns from scaling

SILENT-SPECIFIC: Poison completely solves Spikers. Noxious Fumes + any poison source kills without ever taking Thorns damage. Shivs and Blade Dance are TRAPS -- each hit triggers Thorns. If Shivs are the only damage source, accept the Thorns cost but prioritize killing Spikers fast. Piercing Wail does nothing useful here (Spikers' Cut damage is low; Thorns is the real threat).

DANGER LEVEL: Medium. Low direct damage but the Thorns mechanic punishes attack-heavy decks severely. Poison decks trivialize this fight.

APPEARS WITH: Other Shapes (Exploders, Repulsors) in the 3 Shapes encounter. Can also appear in mixed Act 3 hallway fights.
