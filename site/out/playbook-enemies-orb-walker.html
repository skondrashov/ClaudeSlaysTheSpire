Orb Walker (Act 3 Hallway, HP: 90-96)

Fast-scaling enemy. Gains +3 Strength at end of each turn (Strength Up 3). Must burst down before damage becomes unsurvivable. Can appear as a single enemy or as a pair (2 Orb Walkers).

PATTERN: Each turn: 60% Laser, 40% Claw. Cannot use either move 3x in a row.

DAMAGE:

  • Laser: 10 damage + adds 1 Burn to draw pile + 1 Burn to discard pile (11 at A2)
  • Claw: 15 damage (16 at A2)

BUFFS: Strength Up 3 -- gains 3 Strength at end of each turn (5 at A17)

HP: 90-96 at A0, 92-102 at A7

Attacks every turn with increasing damage as Strength accumulates. Turn 1: 10-15 damage. Turn 2: 13-18. Turn 3: 16-21. Generates Burns that clog the hand and deal end-of-turn damage. No free turns.

KEY MECHANICS:

  • Strength scaling (+3/turn per Orb Walker): By Turn 3, a single OW attacks for 19+. With 2 Orb Walkers, combined incoming reaches 38+ by Turn 3 and 50+ by Turn 5. This makes the fight a DPS race.
  • Burns: Orb Walkers add Burns to the draw pile. Burns clog the hand, reduce effective draws, and deal 2 damage each at end of turn (4 damage for upgraded Burns). In long fights, 4+ Burns in hand can deal 8-16 end-of-turn damage on top of the Orb Walker attacks.
  • Moderate HP (~93 each): A single OW is killable in 2-3 turns. Two OWs require focused fire (kill one fast to halve incoming).

TWO ORB WALKER FIGHT (DANGEROUS):

The 2-OW variant is one of the most dangerous Act 3 hallway fights. Combined Str scaling is +6/turn, and combined incoming grows from 20 (Turn 1) to 44+ (Turn 4). With Burns clogging draws, block density drops as incoming damage rises. Confirmed: 2 Orb Walkers drained 66 HP (69 to 3) in a fight where the deck had After Image+, Footwork+, Piercing Wail+ x2, and strong block cards.

STRATEGY:

  • Kill one Orb Walker fast. Focus all damage on one OW to halve incoming damage as soon as possible. Poison (Deadly Poison+, Catalyst+) can handle one OW while you block against the other.
  • Ironclad: Kill fast. Inflame+ + Immolate+ + Strike+ can kill a single OW in 2 turns. Headbutt + Immolate+ guarantees a Turn 2 kill. Bloodletting+ enables Turn 1 burst.
  • Silent: Poison engine handles OWs well -- Deadly Poison+ plus Noxious Fumes+ ticks kill one OW in 3-4 turns. Piercing Wail+ is critical for reducing combined incoming (-8 per OW per turn). After Image+ provides passive block scaling. Do NOT use Catalyst+ on Orb Walkers unless you have another copy or do not need it for the boss. Catalyst+ exhausts after one use and is the primary tool for defeating Regenerate 10 on Awakened One.
  • Feed kills are valuable. At ~93 HP, Feed can land the killing blow for +3 max HP. Prioritize Feed as the last hit.

WHAT NOT TO DO:

  • Set up slow Powers on Turn 1 (Barricade, Metallicize, Evolve). The fight is too short for setup to pay off.
  • Let the fight go past Turn 5. By then, incoming damage exceeds 30+ per OW and Burns overwhelm the hand.
  • Spend Catalyst+ here if the Act 3 boss is Awakened One. Catalyst+ is irreplaceable for defeating the boss's Regenerate 10. The Orb Walker fight is survivable without it; the boss fight may not be.
  • Enter the fight at low HP when Mark of the Bloom is active. With no healing available, even a "safe" 69 HP entry can become 3 HP exit. Avoid optional combat (Mysterious Sphere, Unknown rooms) when Mark of the Bloom prevents recovery.
