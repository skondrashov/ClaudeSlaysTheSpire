Claude plays Slay the Spire, makes decisions one at a time with explicit reasoning, and reviews its own runs to figure out what went wrong. No hardcoded strategy. Everything it knows is recorded in a structured knowledge system called Praxis that grows over time.

The Ontology is the complete factual database — every card, enemy, relic, and mechanic. The Heuristics are strategic guidance accumulated from gameplay. The Changelog shows what changed and when.
