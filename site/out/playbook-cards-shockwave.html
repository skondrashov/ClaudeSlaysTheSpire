Shockwave (2E, Skill, applies Weak + Vulnerable to ALL enemies, Exhaust | +: 3 turns each)

Mass debuff. One of the most powerful cards in the game. Exhausts after use — but returns to your deck next fight. Play it every fight you draw it.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Multi-enemy fights and boss fights. Mass Weak reduces all incoming damage by 25%. Mass Vulnerable boosts all attacks by 50%.

DECISION POINTS: TIMING IS CRITICAL. Play on the FIRST ATTACK TURN of multi-enemy fights. For 3 Cultists, that's turn 2 (turn 1 is free -- all Cultists use Ritual). For Slavers, that's turn 1 (all 3 attack immediately). Never save for turn 4+ -- by then enemies have already dealt massive unmitigated damage. Preventing damage with early Weak far exceeds healing from Reaper. Priority: Shockwave+ on first attack turn > Reaper > single-target damage. NEVER hold Shockwave "for a harder fight" -- exhaust resets between fights, so you'll have it again next fight regardless. If not drawn by the first attack turn, surviving until it's drawn becomes the priority -- invest in block and draw cards to find it faster.

CRITICAL: Shockwave costs 2E. After Shockwave, the remaining 1E MUST go to block (Defend) at critical HP, NOT damage. Weak reduces incoming by 25% but does not prevent lethal damage at sub-30% HP. The 5 block from Defend can be the survival margin. Confirmed fatal: Shockwave + Pommel Strike (damage) instead of Shockwave + Defend (block) at 19 HP vs Slavers resulted in Turn 1 death.

UPGRADE: 2->3 turns of each debuff. Massive improvement -- 50% more duration. High priority.

SYNERGIES: Multi-enemy fights (debuffs apply to ALL), Dropkick (Vulnerable on all targets = free cycling on any of them).

MATCHUPS: Hexaghost -- Weak reduces 7x6=42 Inferno to 7x4=28. MANDATORY for Hexaghost survival. Gremlin Leader -- mass debuff on Leader + all gremlins. 3 Cultists -- play turn 2, not turn 4. Slime Boss split -- mass debuff on both slimes. The Collector -- play Turn 1 or Turn 2 when Torch Heads are present. Mass Weak reduces combined incoming from Collector + 2 Torch Heads. Mass Vulnerable boosts Reaper healing and Bludgeon/Immolate damage against all 3 targets.

WARNING — The Champ: Shockwave debuffs applied BEFORE Execute are WASTED. The Champ's Execute (at ~50% HP) cleanses ALL debuffs. If Shockwave has already been played and exhausted before Execute triggers, you lose the debuffs AND cannot re-apply them. Ideally, hold Shockwave for after Execute. If that is not possible (Shockwave drawn early, need Weak to survive phase 1), accept that the debuffs will be cleansed and plan accordingly.
