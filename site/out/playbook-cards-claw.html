Claw (0E, Attack, 3 damage, +2 Claw damage this combat | +: 5 damage, +2 Claw damage)

Defect common Attack. Deals 3 damage (5 upgraded) and permanently increases ALL future Claw damage by 2 for this combat. Each Claw played makes subsequent Claws hit harder.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: A 0-cost Attack that scales within combat. The first Claw deals 3 (5+), the second deals 5 (7+), the third deals 7 (9+), etc. In a Defect deck with 2 Claws and a long fight, the scaling is meaningful. At 0E, Claw is free damage on any turn with spare card plays.

AKABEKO SYNERGY: Akabeko adds Vigor 8 to the first Attack each combat. Claw at 0E is an ideal first attack: 3 + 8 = 11 damage for free on Turn 1.

LIMITATIONS: The scaling is slow (only +2 per play across all Claws in the deck). In a 10-turn fight with 1 Claw, it might be played 3-4 times for 3+5+7+9 = 24 total damage. This is decent but not an engine card. Claw is a complementary damage source, not a primary one.

DECISION POINTS: Play Claw freely whenever 0E is available. The damage accumulates without cost. Against Gremlin Nob, Claw is one of the best Turn 1 plays -- it's an Attack (no Enrage trigger) that costs 0E.
