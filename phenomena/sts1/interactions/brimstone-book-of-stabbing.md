# Brimstone + Book of Stabbing (trap)

- **Applies when:** You hold [[relics/Brimstone]] and could path into or are fighting [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] (or other multi-hit, self-scaling enemies).

ANTI-SYNERGY. [[relics/Brimstone]] gives you +2 [[buffs/Strength]] but ALL enemies +1 Strength each turn. [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]] already escalates (Multi Stab gains a hit per use); the per-turn Strength compounds its multi-hit damage fast. Avoid the Book of Stabbing elite (and similar multi-hit scalers) while holding Brimstone, especially at reduced HP — a confirmed death trap.

Related: [[relics/Brimstone]], [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]], [[buffs/Strength]]
