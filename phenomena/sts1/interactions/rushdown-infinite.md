# Rushdown stance infinite

- **Applies when:** Watcher deck is thin and contains [[cards/Rushdown]] plus a cheap card that enters [[buffs/Wrath]] and one that enters [[buffs/Calm]] (costing about 2 energy combined).

[[cards/Rushdown]] draws 2 cards whenever you enter [[buffs/Wrath]]. With a thin deck and a Wrath-enter + Calm-enter pair cheap enough to replay each cycle, every stance swap draws back what you spent — an infinite loop that scales block/damage per cycle ([[cards/Eruption]]/[[cards/Vigilance]] are the starter pieces). Requires deck thinning to assemble reliably.

Related: [[cards/Rushdown]], [[buffs/Wrath]], [[buffs/Calm]], [[cards/Eruption]], [[cards/Vigilance]]
