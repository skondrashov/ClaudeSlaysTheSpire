# Mental Fortress vs per-card damage

- **Applies when:** You have a Watcher stance-swapping loop (or any frequent stance changes) and face damage dealt per card played — [[buffs/Beat of Death]] on the [[bosses/Corrupt Heart]], or Thorns enemies.

[[cards/Mental Fortress]] grants Block on every stance change. In a stance-swap loop that produces effectively unlimited Block, directly offsetting chip damage from playing many cards. It is the answer to [[buffs/Beat of Death]] (take damage per card) and to [[buffs/Thorns]] — it lets a high-card-count or infinite turn survive what would otherwise punish the card volume.
