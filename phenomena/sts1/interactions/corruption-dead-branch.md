# Corruption + Dead Branch

- **Applies when:** Ironclad deck contains [[cards/Corruption]] and you hold [[relics/Dead Branch]] — or you are offered one while you have the other.

[[cards/Corruption]] makes all Skills cost 0 and Exhaust when played. [[relics/Dead Branch]] adds a random card to your hand whenever you Exhaust a card. Each 0-cost Skill you play exhausts → Dead Branch hands you another card → often another free Skill, a self-sustaining engine of free cards, block, and exhaust triggers. Add energy/draw to keep it rolling ([[cards/Sentinel]] refunds energy when exhausted — ideal here; [[buffs/Feel No Pain]] turns the exhausts into block). One of the strongest Ironclad engines.

Related: [[cards/Corruption]], [[relics/Dead Branch]], [[cards/Sentinel]], [[buffs/Feel No Pain]]
