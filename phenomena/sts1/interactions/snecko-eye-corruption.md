# Snecko Eye + Corruption

- **Applies when:** Ironclad deck has both [[relics/Snecko Eye]] and [[cards/Corruption]] — or you are choosing whether to take one with the other already in the deck.

[[relics/Snecko Eye]] draws +2 cards but randomizes every drawn card's cost (0-3). [[cards/Corruption]] forces all Skills to cost 0 regardless, so the random costs only ever land on your Attacks and Powers. You get Snecko's extra draw with the downside neutralized on your whole Skill suite. Strong, low-risk combination.

Related: [[relics/Snecko Eye]], [[cards/Corruption]]
