# Electrodynamics (Lightning AoE)

- **Applies when:** Defect orb deck channels Lightning and has or is offered [[cards/Electrodynamics]], especially when multi-enemy fights are common on the path ahead.

[[cards/Electrodynamics]] changes Lightning orbs' passive AND evoke from hitting one random enemy to hitting ALL enemies. It converts a single-target orb build into reliable AoE, trivializing multi-enemy encounters (Reptomancer, Sentries, slime splits). Scales with Focus and orb slots.
