# Snecko Eye + Runic Pyramid (trap)

- **Applies when:** You are offered [[relics/Snecko Eye]] while holding [[relics/Runic Pyramid]], or the reverse.

ANTI-SYNERGY. [[relics/Snecko Eye]]'s value comes from churning your whole hand each turn (+2 draw, randomized costs). [[relics/Runic Pyramid]] lets you retain your hand instead of discarding. Retaining cards defeats Snecko's draw-churn and you accumulate expensive-rolled cards you cannot play. Do not take both — pick almost any other boss relic.

Related: [[relics/Snecko Eye]], [[relics/Runic Pyramid]]
