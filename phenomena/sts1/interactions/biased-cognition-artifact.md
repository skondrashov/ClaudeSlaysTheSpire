# Biased Cognition + Artifact

- **Applies when:** Defect deck runs [[cards/Biased Cognition]] and has a source of [[buffs/Artifact]] (notably [[cards/Core Surge]]).

[[cards/Biased Cognition]] grants +4 (+5 upgraded) Focus immediately, then loses 1 Focus at the start of each turn. An [[buffs/Artifact]] charge absorbs that first Focus-loss tick, delaying or (with repeated Artifact) cancelling the downside while you keep the big Focus spike. [[cards/Core Surge]] deals damage and grants 1 Artifact — a clean pairing.

Related: [[cards/Biased Cognition]], [[buffs/Artifact]], [[cards/Core Surge]]
