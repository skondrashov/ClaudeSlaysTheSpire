# Orichalcum + Ornamental Fan (trap)

- **Applies when:** You hold both [[relics/Orichalcum]] and [[relics/Ornamental Fan]].

ANTI-SYNERGY. [[relics/Orichalcum]] grants Block at end of turn ONLY if you have no Block. [[relics/Ornamental Fan]] grants Block after every 3 Attacks played. The Fan's Block prevents Orichalcum from triggering; conversely, playing defensively to keep Orichalcum eligible suppresses the Fan. The two cancel — neither reliably fires when both are present.

Related: [[relics/Orichalcum]], [[relics/Ornamental Fan]]
