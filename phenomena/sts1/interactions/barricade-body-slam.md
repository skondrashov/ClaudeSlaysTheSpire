# Barricade + Body Slam

- **Applies when:** Ironclad deck contains [[cards/Barricade]] and [[cards/Body Slam]] ([[cards/Entrench]] strongly amplifies it). Especially relevant approaching a long boss fight or the Heart.

[[cards/Barricade]] stops Block from expiring at the start of your turn, so Block accumulates across turns. [[cards/Body Slam]] deals damage equal to your current Block for 1 energy without consuming it. [[cards/Entrench]] doubles your Block. Stack Block over several turns (to the 999 cap if needed), then Body Slam for a massive single hit — a defensive deck's win condition. Great against high-HP single targets like the Heart.
