## DRIFT / PHANTOM detail

### Existence (PHANTOM)
- `[HIGH][VERIFY]` ontology/sts1/relics/strike-dummy.md — No base-StS relic named **"Strike Dummy"**
  exists. The recorded effect ("Whenever you play a card with a base cost of 0, deal 6 damage to a random
  enemy") closely mirrors the Silent boss relic **Wrist Blade** (0-cost Attacks deal +4) repurposed as a
  random-target nuke, i.e. a fabricated relic in the same family as the a–c phantoms (brass-ring/cables).
  FIX: confirm against the relic list; if it does not exist, DELETE ontology/sts1/relics/strike-dummy.md
  (and any companion / wikilinks). If a real relic was intended, rename to the correct relic and re-derive
  effect/rarity from canonical text.

### Effect-text fidelity (DRIFT)
- `[HIGH][VERIFY]` ontology/sts1/relics/paper-crane.md — Canonical Paper Crane (a generic/any-class relic)
  is **"Enemies that are Weakened deal 25% less damage"** (i.e. Weak's reduction deepens from 25% to ~40%
  on affected enemies). The entry instead records **"Enemies deal 40% less damage while you are in Wrath
  form"** AND tags **Character: Watcher** — conflating Paper Crane with a Wrath/stance effect. Wrong effect
  + wrong (phantom) class restriction. FIX: Effect → "While an enemy is Weakened, it deals 25% less damage
  (Weak ramps from 25% to 40% total)." Remove the `Character: Watcher` line.
- `[MED][VERIFY]` ontology/sts1/relics/strange-spoon.md — Canonical Strange Spoon: cards that would
  Exhaust instead have a **50% chance to be discarded (sent to the discard pile) instead of exhausting**.
  Entry says the card "will only Exhaust 50% of the time," which omits the crucial outcome of the other
  50% (it discards and is reusable, not "stays"/vanishes). Numerically right, mechanically incomplete.
  FIX: "Cards that would Exhaust have a 50% chance to be discarded instead (placed in the discard pile)."
