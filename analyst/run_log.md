# Run Log

## Run 0 — Ironclad A0, Victory (Guardian)
DECK: True Grit+, Shrug It Off+, Pommel Strike
CAUSE OF DEATH: N/A — defeated The Guardian
KEY MOMENTS: Block-and-cycle strategy with multi-purpose cards carried the boss fight. True Grit+ (controlled exhaust) was key.
LESSONS: Quality over quantity — cards that do double duty (block+exhaust, block+draw, damage+draw) outperform single-purpose cards.

## Run 1 — Ironclad A0, Death Floor 16 (Slime Boss)
DECK: 4 Strikes, 4 Defends, Bash+, Berserk, Hemokinesis, Clothesline, Shockwave+, Shrug It Off, Ghostly Armor (15 cards)
CAUSE OF DEATH: Slime Boss split — no AOE to handle split slimes, Berserk self-Vulnerable compounded damage, Slimed cards overwhelmed hand
KEY MOMENTS: Berserk played at 23 HP against split slimes dealing 36 combined damage. Deck too thick (15 cards) to draw key cards reliably.
LESSONS: Need AOE for Slime Boss split. Don't take cards you don't understand. Don't play Berserk in dangerous fights. Deck thinning matters.

## Run 2 — Ironclad A0, Death Floor 16 (Guardian)

## Run 9 — Ironclad A0, Death Floor 16 (Hexaghost)
DECK: Bash+, Iron Wave+, Clothesline, Flame Barrier, Offering, Whirlwind, Inflame
RELICS: Potion Belt
CAUSE OF DEATH: Burns attrition over 13+ turn Hexaghost fight. Had Weak source (Clothesline) but no passive block (Metallicize) and insufficient damage output to kill before Burns overwhelmed. Hexaghost still had HP remaining when Burns + attacks killed the player.
KEY MOMENTS: Clothesline provided Weak for Inferno reduction, but the fight lasted too long. Burns accumulated to 3-4 cards dealing 6-8 HP/turn in self-damage. Inflame was present but damage output still insufficient.
LESSONS: Weak source alone is not enough for Hexaghost -- need damage scaling to shorten the fight. Metallicize is critical for the long fight. Burns are the real killer, not just Inferno.

DECK: Headbutt, Shockwave, Pommel Strike, Intimidate, Metallicize, Burning Pact, Hemokinesis, Impervious

## Run 11 — Ironclad A0, Victory (Hexaghost), Death Floor 27 (Chosen+Byrd)
DECK: Bash+ (Innate via Bottled Flame), Inflame, Reaper, Disarm, Thunderclap, Clothesline+, Armaments+, Shockwave, Shrug It Off, Flame Barrier
RELICS: Bottled Flame, Torii, Dream Catcher, Pear
CAUSE OF DEATH: HP drained through Act 2 fights -- Byrds (36-58 HP lost), Snake Plant (Malleable mechanic, Frail+Weak debuffs), Centurion+Mystic. Entered Chosen+Byrd at 18 HP, could not survive combined damage.
KEY MOMENTS: First Hexaghost victory this session. Bottled Flame made Bash+ Innate (guaranteed Vulnerable turn 1). Disarm (-2 Str on Hexaghost) was very effective. Torii reduced Burns to 1 damage each. Pear (+10 Max HP) and Dream Catcher (cards on rest) provided excellent value. Discovered Snake Plant Malleable mechanic (gains block per hit in a turn).
LESSONS: Hexaghost is solvable with Disarm + Weak + Torii. Act 2 HP drain from Byrds and Snake Plant is the real run-ender. Bottled Flame -> Bash+ is one of the best relic picks. Snake Plant punishes multi-hit attacks (Malleable).

## Run 12 — Ironclad A0, Death Floor 16 (Hexaghost)
DECK: Bash+, Battle Trance, Iron Wave, Clothesline+, Pommel Strike x2, Disarm
RELICS: Torii
CAUSE OF DEATH: Insufficient damage output despite having both Weak (Clothesline+) and Disarm (-2 Str). Hexaghost still at 83+ HP when Burns attrition killed the player after 11+ turns. Torii reduced Burns to 1 damage each but the fight simply lasted too long.
KEY MOMENTS: Had the best defensive setup of any Hexaghost attempt (Clothesline+ for Weak, Disarm for -2 Str, Torii for Burns reduction). But the deck had no damage scaling (no Inflame, no Rampage, no Reaper). Pure Strikes and Pommel Strikes couldn't kill a 250 HP boss fast enough.
LESSONS: CRITICAL -- defense alone cannot beat Hexaghost. Must have damage scaling (Inflame, Rampage, Reaper) to shorten the fight. Burns accumulate linearly -- every extra turn adds more self-damage. The prep checklist needs "damage output" as a required category alongside Weak source and passive block.

## Run 13 — Ironclad A0, Victory (Hexaghost), Death Floor 27 (Fungi Beast+Shelled Parasite)
DECK: Bash+, Thunderclap, Shrug It Off, Shockwave, Double Tap, Pommel Strike+, Rampage+, Brutality x2, Rupture, Second Wind, Whirlwind+, Corruption (from Enchiridion)
RELICS: Molten Egg, Lizard Tail, Enchiridion
CAUSE OF DEATH: Played 2 Brutalities in one fight at 10 HP. Combined self-damage (2 HP/turn from 2x Brutality) plus enemy damage killed the player. The Brutality+Rupture scaling engine worked brilliantly (+2 Str/turn) but requires a large HP buffer to sustain.
KEY MOMENTS: Hexaghost victory via Rampage+ scaling (12->20->28->36->48->60 damage per play). Double Tap doubled Rampage for massive burst. Molten Egg auto-upgraded all Attacks. Lizard Tail revived at 50% HP during an earlier fight. Enchiridion gave free Corruption (all Skills cost 0 but exhaust). Brutality+Rupture engine provided +1 Str/turn per Brutality copy. Fatal error: playing both Brutalities at 10 HP.
LESSONS: Rampage+ is one of the best Hexaghost-killing cards. Brutality+Rupture is a powerful scaling engine but NEVER play multiple copies at low HP (<20). Molten Egg is game-changing when obtained early. Enchiridion provides free random Powers that can be transformative (Corruption).
