# Run Log

## Runs 0-13 Summary (archived)

20 runs total. 7 Act 1 boss victories (Guardian x4, Slime Boss x2, Hexaghost x3). 13 deaths. Key lessons absorbed into playbook:
- True Grit unupgraded is run-ending (Runs 2, 32, 44)

- Act 2 HP drain from Byrds/Centurion+Mystic triggers death spirals

## Run 32 — Ironclad A0, Death Floor 16 (The Guardian)
DECK: Bash+, Uppercut, True Grit (unupgraded), Shrug It Off, Thunderclap, Fiend Fire, Ghostly Armor, Metallicize, Rampage, Iron Wave+, Strike x2, Defend x2
RELICS: Burning Blood, Blood Vial
CAUSE OF DEATH: The Guardian at 32 HP remaining. Entered boss at 62 HP (86%) after rest heal. Guardian fight lasted 12+ turns. Exhaustion via Fiend Fire + True Grit (unupgraded random exhaust) thinned deck to 8-10 playable cards by turn 10. On the final 32-damage attack turn, available block sources were Defend and Shrug It Off (9 block total vs 32 required). Guardian deal 32 damage at 5 HP remaining. Died unblocked.
KEY MOMENTS: (1) Entered boss well-prepared (62 HP, solid deck, multiple debuff sources). (2) Lagavulin elite drained 20 HP unnecessarily (suboptimal play). (3) Inflame, Shockwave+, and Rampage added strong scaling. (4) Fiend Fire turn 4 exhausted Ghostly Armor (Ethereal, intentional). (5) Fiend Fire turn 7 exhausted 4 cards for 28 damage — hand empty, recovery slow. (6) True Grit played 3+ times, random exhaust destroyed Bash+ on turn 4 — lost primary Vulnerable source. (7) By turn 10, remaining hand: Strike, Defend, Shrug It Off, Uppercut only. (8) Guardian Mode Shift to Defensive with Sharp Hide active. (9) Turn 12: Guardian executed 32-damage attack. Maximum available block: 9 (5 from Defend + 5 from Shrug It Off, reduced by Sharp Hide). Took 32 damage at 5 HP = death.
LESSONS: CRITICAL PATTERN — exhaustion-heavy decks fail on The Guardian. Fiend Fire's massive damage (28-39 per play) front-loads power into turns 3-7 but leaves a thin deck for turns 8-14. Block density collapses when you need 32+ block on late turns. Unupgraded True Grit compounds the problem: random exhaust removes best cards (Bash+, Uppercut) while you're trying to thin intentionally. With only 9-10 cards remaining, block sources appear every other turn at best. The Guardian is a long fight (12-14 turns typical) where sustain and consistency matter more than burst. Next attempt: avoid exhaust synergy entirely, prioritize Impervious or double Metallicize + Weak for the 32-damage turn, keep 12-15 cards in deck minimum. Fiend Fire can appear but only on free turns (DEFEND/STRONG_DEBUFF intents) — never as primary engine. True Grit must be upgraded or not played at all.
