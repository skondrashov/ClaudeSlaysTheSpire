# Run Log

## Runs 0-45 Summary (archived)

37 runs total. 7 Act 1 boss victories (Guardian x4, Slime Boss x2, Hexaghost x3). Key lessons absorbed into playbook:
- True Grit unupgraded is run-ending (Runs 2, 32, 44)
- Index shifting kills (Runs 3, 21, 22, 57) — 4 confirmed deaths
- Low HP entry is the #1 cause of death (19 of 22 documented deaths)
- Hexaghost needs Weak + damage scaling (not just Weak)
- AOE mandatory for Slime Boss split; both large slimes split on death
- Exhaust-heavy decks collapse in long fights (Guardian)
- Act 2 HP drain from Byrds/Centurion+Mystic triggers death spirals
- 3 Cultists is the most lethal hallway fight (3 deaths at 30-39% HP entry)
- Random exhaust (Havoc, unupgraded True Grit) destroyed critical cards in 3 runs
- Demon Form + Limit Break scaling is too slow for emergency fights at low HP
- Fiend Fire + Unceasing Top is the strongest combo observed
- Unknown rooms are NOT safe at low HP (Snake Plant appearances)
- Burning Blood alone cannot sustain Act 2 (confirmed across 10+ runs)

Sozu's 4th energy is powerful but losing potions removes the backup healing plan entirely.

## Run 73 — Ironclad A0, Victory (Guardian), Death Floor 19 (Looter + Mugger, Act 2)
DECK: Corruption, Barricade, Rupture, Feel No Pain x2, Bash+, Rampage, Dropkick, basics
RELICS: Burning Blood, Dead Branch, Wing Boots
CAUSE OF DEATH: Played unupgraded Corruption (3E) on turn 1 of Looter + Mugger fight. Zero energy remaining for block. Took 20+ unblocked damage from both enemies combined. Died before the Corruption + Dead Branch engine could activate.
KEY MOMENTS: (1) Assembled Corruption + Dead Branch + 2x Feel No Pain -- theoretically a game-winning engine. (2) Beat The Guardian in Act 1 successfully. (3) First hallway fight of Act 2 (Floor 19), played Corruption into multi-enemy fight without block. (4) The engine never activated -- died on the setup turn.
LESSONS: 23rd low-HP death. Corruption at 3E is a TRAP in multi-enemy fights -- spending all energy on a Power with zero block against 20+ combined incoming is suicide. Upgrading Corruption to 2E is mandatory before relying on it as an engine piece. The Corruption + Dead Branch combo is the strongest engine in the game but requires surviving the setup turn. Multi-enemy fights are the worst context for expensive Power setup (no free turns, combined damage too high). Dead Branch and Wing Boots documented as new playbook entries. Mugger documented as Act 2 enemy.
