LESSONS: (1) **X-cost cards (Malaise, Whirlwind, Skewer) automatically consume ALL remaining energy.** You CANNOT specify X. To control X, play other cards first to spend energy down to the desired X, then play the X-cost card LAST. This is now a new checklist item (#5) in strategy.md and documented in mechanics.md. (2) **Unknown rooms in Act 2 continue to kill at alarming rates** -- 5th death confirmed. Player chose Unknown at ~27% HP (19/70) reasoning it was safer. It was not. (3) Spheric Guardian Frail penalty is especially punishing for the Silent -- Defend gives 3 block instead of 5, Survivor gives 4 instead of 6. Total block output per turn drops ~40% under Frail. (4) Potion conservation matters -- using both potions on an earlier fight left nothing for the lethal encounter.
