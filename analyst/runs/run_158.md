## Run 158 — The Silent A0, VICTORY Floor 51 (FIRST SILENT WIN)
DECK: ~26 cards — Accuracy x2, After Image (Innate via Bottled Tornado), Phantasmal Killer, Wraith Form+, Malaise, Blade Dance, Finisher, Envenom, Infinite Blades, Crippling Cloud, Bouncing Flask, Neutralize, Survivor, Backflip+, Acrobatics+, Footwork x2, Blur, Blur+, Piercing Wail, Poisoned Stab, Prepared, Endless Agony x2, Dodge and Roll
RELICS: Pandora's Box (boss relic from Neow swap), Bottled Tornado (After Image innate), Ginger (immune to Weak), Lizard Tail, Fusion Hammer, Golden Idol, Happy Flower, Tingsha, The Boot, Kunai, others
VICTORY: Time Eater (Act 3 Boss), Floor 51, Score 636, Final HP 47/70
KEY MOMENTS: (1) Pandora's Box from Neow swapped all starter Strikes/Defends into random cards -- got 2x Accuracy, Envenom, Infinite Blades, Endless Agony x2, Bouncing Flask, Crippling Cloud, Prepared, Dodge and Roll. Critical block deficit but massive offensive synergy potential from Turn 0. (2) Fusion Hammer gave 5 energy total. Backflip+ and Acrobatics+ were the only upgrades (rest sites). (3) Bottled Tornado made After Image innate -- guaranteed block generation from Turn 1 of every fight. This was the single most important defensive decision. (4) Ginger (immune to Weak) made Mind Bloom "I am Healthy" completely free: full heal to 70 HP, Doubt curse gives 1 Weak/turn but Ginger negates it entirely. Doubt then removed at shop for clean deck. (5) Cultist Potion Turn 1 of Time Eater gave Ritual 1 -- Str scaled from 0 to 11 by Turn 10, making each Shiv deal 23 damage (4 base + 8 Accuracy + 11 Str). (6) Turn 3 Phantasmal Killer + Fear Potion (Vuln) -- Shivs dealt 36 each (doubled, Vulnerable). 72 burst damage in 2 card plays. (7) Turn 5 Wraith Form+ drawn via Acrobatics at 19 HP -- 3 Intangible bought 3 safe turns. Correct timing: played at crisis point, not early. (8) Turn 8 Malaise X=5 applied -5 Str and 5 Weak to Time Eater, neutering its offense for the final turns. (9) Turn 9 Lizard Tail revival: died to 18 incoming at 3 HP, revived at 35 HP. Snecko Oil drew 5 cards for emergency block+damage. (10) Turn 10 Str 11 + Accuracy 8 = Shivs dealing 23 each. 4 Shivs (92) + Finisher (24) = 116 damage, killing Time Eater from ~139 HP.
LESSONS: (1) SILENT WINNING FORMULA: Accuracy x2 (+8 Shiv damage) + After Image (innate via Bottled Tornado) + Wraith Form+ (Intangible safety) + Phantasmal Killer (double damage burst) + Malaise (boss debuff) + Cultist Potion (Ritual for Str scaling). The Shiv archetype beats Time Eater because each Shiv is a single card play but deals massive damage with Accuracy+Str. (2) Time Eater 12-card counter is manageable with Shivs: each Shiv is 1 card but deals 20+ damage, so 12 card plays can output 200+ damage per cycle. (3) Wraith Form+ timing was CORRECT this run -- played Turn 5 at 19 HP as an emergency measure, not preemptively. 3 Intangible turns + Blur carried the middle of the fight. (4) Ginger + Mind Bloom "I am Healthy" = free full heal. Document this interaction: Ginger prevents Doubt's Weak effect entirely. (5) Lizard Tail in boss fights is a safety net worth 35 HP (50% of 70 max). The revival allowed the final burst to kill Time Eater. (6) Cultist Potion provides permanent Str scaling that compounds with Accuracy on Shivs -- by Turn 10, each Shiv dealt 23 damage. This is comparable to Demon Form for a fraction of the card cost. (7) Pandora's Box for Silent is high-variance but can provide game-defining synergy packages (2x Accuracy + Envenom + Infinite Blades in one swap). (8) Finisher scales with attacks played this turn -- with 4 Shivs preceding it, Finisher dealt 24 damage for 1E. Excellent as a turn-ender after Shiv burst.
