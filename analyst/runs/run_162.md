## Run 162 — The Silent A0, VICTORY Floor 51 (SECOND SILENT WIN -- vs Awakened One)

DECK: ~25 cards — After Image, Footwork x2, Well-Laid Plans, Caltrops, Noxious Fumes, Terror, Endless Agony, Piercing Wail, Distraction, Adrenaline, Storm of Steel, Bane, Neutralize, Survivor, Backflip+, Cloak and Dagger, Deadly Poison, Crippling Cloud, Glass Knife, Masterful Stab, Burst+, Blur, Deflect, Quick Slash, Dagger Throw, others
RELICS: Dead Branch (MVP), Lizard Tail (consumed Phase 2), Mercury Hourglass, Clockwork Souvenir, Slaver's Collar, Pocketwatch, others
VICTORY: Awakened One (Act 3 Boss), Floor 51, Score 623, Final HP 23/67
KEY MOMENTS: (1) Dead Branch acquired from Act 3 treasure chest -- immediately became the run-defining relic. All exhaust cards transformed into Dead Branch fuel. (2) **Phase 1 Power dump strategy**: Played ALL Powers early (After Image, Footwork x2, WLP, Caltrops, Noxious Fumes) since Curiosity Str resets on death. Applied Terror for Vuln 99. Used 2x Flex Potions + Glass Knife for 42-damage burst. Piercing Wail + Weak offset Curiosity Str gains. Phase 1 killed in ~8 turns. (3) **Phase 2 zero-Power discipline**: Deliberately avoided playing any Powers despite having them in hand. All damage from Attacks + Skills + Dead Branch random cards. Terror Vuln 99 in Phase 2. Storm of Steel generated 3 Shivs, each exhausting for Dead Branch procs, creating chain reaction turns with 45+ damage. (4) **Lizard Tail consumed Phase 2 Turn 1**: 41 incoming damage vs 20 block + 18 HP = lethal. Lizard Tail revived to 33 HP. Without Lizard Tail, the run was over. (5) Dex 7 (from Phase 1 Footwork x2 + Footwork+) + After Image + Blur provided 20-40 block per turn in Phase 2 WITHOUT Powers. (6) Mercury Hourglass dealt 3 passive damage per turn to the Awakened One across both phases (~45 total free damage). (7) Nightmare copied Cloak and Dagger 3x for massive block + Shiv generation in Phase 1. (8) Bane dealt double damage on poisoned Awakened One (confirmed interaction). (9) Clockwork Souvenir provided Artifact 1 at combat start, blocking the first debuff. (10) Phase 2 kill via Strike + Sucker Punch + Quick Slash burst on ~Turn 7 at AO ~38 HP.
LESSONS: (1) **Dead Branch + exhaust is a 4th winning formula** for the Silent. Dead Branch transforms Terror, Piercing Wail, Adrenaline, Storm of Steel, Endless Agony, Distraction, and Shivs into card advantage engines. Updated dead-branch.md and strategy.md. (2) **Phase 1 Power dump + Phase 2 zero Powers is the correct Awakened One strategy.** Phase 1 Curiosity Str resets on death, so Powers are "free" in Phase 1. Phase 2 Powers are permanent Str -- avoid at all costs. Dead Branch provides card generation that replaces the need for Powers. Updated awakened-one.md. (3) **Lizard Tail is critical insurance for the Awakened One fight.** Phase 2 Turn 1 Dark Echo (40 damage) is often unsurvivable without either massive pre-built block or a revival relic. (4) **Terror Vuln 99 is the best single-card damage amplifier in the game for boss fights.** 50% more damage from every subsequent attack for the entire combat. Created terror.md. (5) **Bane doubles damage on Poisoned targets** -- with Terror Vuln 99 active, Bane deals 3x effective damage. Created bane.md. (6) **Storm of Steel + Dead Branch creates explosive turns**: discard hand -> get Shivs -> each Shiv exhausts for Dead Branch -> new random cards -> more plays. (7) **Dex from Footwork appears to persist through Phase transition** -- needs confirmation but was observed. (8) **Mercury Hourglass provides meaningful passive damage in long boss fights** (~45 damage across both phases). (9) Five wins now (3 Ironclad, 2 Silent). Win rate improved to 3.1%.
