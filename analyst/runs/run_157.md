## Run 157 — The Silent A0, Defeat Floor 33
DECK: ~20 cards — poison/block hybrid with Bane+, Dash+, Backflip+, Blur+, Well-Laid Plans, Piercing Wail, Neutralize, Survivor, Deadly Poison, Slice, Bouncing Flask, Crippling Cloud, Noxious Fumes, Footwork, Malaise, Poisoned Stab, Strikes, Defends
RELICS: Fusion Hammer (boss relic from Neow swap), Sozu, Meat on the Bone, Golden Idol, others
CAUSE OF DEATH: Bronze Automaton at Floor 33 (Act 2 boss). Automaton at 35 HP with Poison 16 (2 turns from death by poison). Str reached 9 by Turn 14, making attacks unsurvivable at 3 HP. 41 block vs 48 incoming = 7 unblocked, lethal at 3 HP.
KEY MOMENTS: (1) Fusion Hammer from Neow swap gave 4 energy but no upgrades -- rest sites were healing-only. (2) Sozu blocked all potion use, removing emergency healing. (3) Poison strategy was sound -- lethal poison was applied -- but deck could not survive long enough for it to finish. (4) Malaise exhausted on Artifact stripping, leaving only 1 copy for the boss fight. (5) Automaton Str reached 9 by Turn 12+, making late-fight attacks (48+ damage) unsurvivable.
LESSONS: (1) Sozu is a trap for Silent -- no emergency potions in boss fights where survivability matters more than raw power. (2) Poison-only kill strategy requires sufficient block/Intangible to survive the 3-4 turns needed for lethal poison to finish. (3) Malaise should be saved for the boss, not exhausted on Artifact stripping (use Neutralize/Crippling Cloud for Artifact instead). (4) Fusion Hammer without alternate upgrade sources (Apotheosis, Armaments, events) leaves key cards permanently unupgraded.
