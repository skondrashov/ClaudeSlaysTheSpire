# Battle Hymn (Watcher Rare Power, 1E)

At the start of each turn, add a Smite to your hand. Upgraded: the Smite is upgraded (Smite+, 16 damage instead of 12).

Battle Hymn provides a free 12/16 damage Retain Exhaust attack card every turn for the rest of combat.

## Key Interactions

- **Sustained damage engine:** In a 10-turn boss fight, Battle Hymn generates 120-160 total free damage. This is one of the Watcher's best long-fight scaling tools.
- **Smite Retains:** Generated Smites can be held for burst turns in Wrath (24/32 doubled damage).
- **Hexaghost relevance:** In the Burns-heavy Hexaghost fight, a free 12-damage card every turn helps end the fight before Burns overwhelm. Battle Hymn is the Watcher equivalent of the Ironclad's Demon Form for Hexaghost -- sustained scaling that shortens the fight.

## Play Order

Play Battle Hymn on a safe turn as early as possible. Like all Powers, earlier is better. The free turn against Hexaghost (Turn 1 Activate) is ideal.

## Draft Priority

High. Take Battle Hymn when offered. It is one of the Watcher's best rare cards for boss fights.
