# Bash (2E, Attack, 8 damage + 2 Vulnerable | +: 10 damage + 3 Vulnerable)
Primary Vulnerable applicator. Opens every fight.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Always. Vulnerable for 2-3 turns means all subsequent attacks deal 50% more damage.
DECISION POINTS: Is this the right turn to apply Vulnerable? On free turns (enemy buffing/debuffing), Bash is ideal. On high-damage incoming turns, 2E on Bash means less block. With Gremlin Nob, Bash is safe (Attack card).
UPGRADE: 8->10 damage, 2->3 turns Vulnerable. The extra Vulnerable turn is enormous: 3 turns of 50% bonus vs 2 turns. Top upgrade priority in any deck.
SYNERGIES: Headbutt (put Bash+ back on top to refresh Vulnerable), Dropkick (free cycling when target is Vulnerable), all Attack cards (50% more damage).
MATCHUPS: Lagavulin -- refresh Vulnerable after it wears off by using Headbutt to top-deck Bash+. Guardian -- apply on free turns (DEFEND/STRONG_DEBUFF intent). Gremlin Nob -- safe to play (Attack card).
