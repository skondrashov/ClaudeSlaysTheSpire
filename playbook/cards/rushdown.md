# Rushdown (Watcher Uncommon Power, 1E)

Whenever you enter Wrath, draw 2 cards. Upgraded: draw 3 cards.

Rushdown is a key engine Power that rewards Wrath entries with card draw, enabling longer and more explosive burst turns.

## Key Interactions

- **Triggers every Wrath entry:** Every Eruption, Crescendo, or other Wrath-entry card draws 2-3 extra cards. In a stance-cycling turn with 2 Wrath entries, this is 4-6 extra draws.
- **Combines with Flurry of Blows:** Wrath entry triggers both Rushdown (draw 2-3) and Flurry return (from discard). The drawn cards may include more attacks to play in Wrath at doubled damage.
- **Setup priority:** Play Rushdown as early as possible in a fight. Like all Powers, every turn it sits unplayed is lost value.

## Play Order

Play Rushdown on a safe turn (enemy not attacking or block already sufficient). It costs 1E and provides no immediate defense. On a free turn (enemy buffing), Rushdown is the highest priority play.

## Upgrade Priority

High. Drawing 3 cards instead of 2 per Wrath entry significantly increases burst potential and consistency.
