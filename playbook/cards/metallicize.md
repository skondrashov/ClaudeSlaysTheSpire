# Metallicize (1E, Power, gain 3 block at end of each turn permanently)
Passive block every turn. The block equivalent of Strength.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Play as early as possible in long fights. Over 10 turns: 30 free block. Two copies provide 6 block/turn -- a game-changing defensive setup that handles many attack patterns with just 1 Defend card.
DECISION POINTS: Play on the first free turn available. The earlier it's played, the more total block it provides. Powers do NOT trigger Gremlin Nob Enrage.
SYNERGIES: Iron Wave (passive block + Iron Wave block = solid defense), second Metallicize (6 block/turn is exceptional).
KEY INTERACTION: Metallicize block is NOT affected by Frail. Frail only reduces block gained from cards (Defend, Shrug It Off). Metallicize's end-of-turn block is a flat 3 regardless of debuffs, making it especially valuable against Spheric Guardian (Frail 5) and other Frail-applying enemies where card-based block is reduced.

MATCHUPS: Guardian -- 6 block/turn (with 2 copies) handles many attack patterns (8x2=16, 9 damage) with only 1 Defend needed. Hexaghost -- critical for the 13-turn fight. Byrds -- essential for the long attrition fight. Spheric Guardian -- Frail reduces Defend to 3 block, but Metallicize still gives a reliable 3 block/turn unaffected by the debuff.
