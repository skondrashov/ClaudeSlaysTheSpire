# Burning Pact (1E, Skill, Exhaust 1 chosen card from hand, draw 2)
Controlled exhaust + card draw. Net +1 card in hand.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Removing status cards (Slimed, Dazed, Wound). Cycling to find key cards. Thinning in-combat deck safely. Critical vs Book of Stabbing -- exhaust Wounds to keep draws functional while cycling toward damage cards. Essential in Slime Boss fight -- exhaust Slimed cards to prevent hand clog while drawing toward damage/block. With Feel No Pain active, each Slimed exhausted via Burning Pact generates 3 block AND draws 2 cards.
DECISION POINTS: What's expendable in hand? Exhaust status cards or extra Strikes. Against Gremlin Nob and Chosen, do NOT play (Skill card triggers Enrage/Hex).
