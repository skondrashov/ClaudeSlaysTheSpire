# Wreath of Flame (Watcher Common Skill, 1E)

Base: Your next Attack deals 5 additional damage (Vigor). Upgraded: 8 additional damage.

Wreath of Flame grants Vigor, which adds flat damage to the next Attack card played. Vigor persists between turns until consumed by an attack.

## Key Interactions

- **Wrath doubling:** Vigor is added to the attack's base damage BEFORE Wrath doubling. A Smite (12) + Vigor 5 = 17, doubled in Wrath = 34.
- **Best on high-damage single hits:** Apply Vigor before your biggest single attack (Smite, Eruption) for maximum value.
- **Persists across turns:** Set up Vigor on a safe turn, use it on the next attack turn.

## Play Order

Play Wreath of Flame before the Attack card you want to empower. If on a free turn (enemy not attacking), play Wreath of Flame to bank Vigor for the next turn's attacks.
