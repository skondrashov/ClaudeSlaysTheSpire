# Entrench (2E, Skill, double your current block | +: 1E)

Doubles all accumulated block. The single most powerful block-scaling card in the game when combined with Barricade.

UPGRADE PRIORITY: VERY HIGH. Entrench+ costs 1E (down from 2E). Under Corruption, Entrench+ costs 0E and exhausts. A 0E "double your block" is game-breaking. Without the upgrade, 2E is a significant cost that competes with generating block cards.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: With Barricade. Block persists between turns, so Entrench doubles the accumulated total, not just this turn's block. A typical sequence: Turn 1 generate 20 block, Turn 2 add 15 more (35 total), Turn 3 Entrench doubles to 70. This snowball makes the player nearly invulnerable within 3-4 turns. Without Barricade, Entrench doubles only this turn's block (typically 10-20), which is mediocre value for 2E.

SYNERGIES: Barricade (the mandatory pairing -- doubles accumulated block), Corruption (Entrench+ becomes 0E, doubles block AND triggers Feel No Pain on exhaust), Feel No Pain (Entrench+ exhaust under Corruption gives +3-4 block on top of the doubling), Body Slam+ (doubled block = doubled Body Slam+ damage for free), Impervious (30-40 block base doubled to 60-80 in one turn).

OBSERVED: Entrench+ doubled 69 block to 138 against Time Eater, followed by Body Slam+ for 146 damage. Entrench+ doubled 97 block to 194 against The Maw for a 197-block wall that made the entire fight trivial.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Decks without Barricade (doubling 10 block for 2E is poor value), fights with Frail (reduced block generation means less to double).

DO NOT TAKE without Barricade or a clear path to obtaining Barricade. Without block persistence, Entrench is a weak card.
