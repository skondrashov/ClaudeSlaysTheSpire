# Meditate (Watcher Uncommon Skill, 1E)

Base: Put 1 card from discard into hand with Retain. Enter Calm. End your turn. Upgraded: Put 2 cards from discard into hand.

Meditate is a powerful card retrieval tool that also enters Calm and forcibly ends the turn.

## Key Interactions

- **Ends your turn:** After selecting cards, your turn immediately ends. Play all other cards BEFORE Meditate.
- **Enters Calm:** Sets up the +2E energy bonus for next turn.
- **Card retrieval with Retain:** The retrieved cards stay in hand through the end of turn (Retain). This guarantees specific cards for next turn -- retrieve Eruption+ for a burst, or Vigilance for blocking.
- **Stance change triggers:** Flurry of Blows returns from discard when Meditate enters Calm. But since Meditate ends your turn, you cannot play the returned Flurry this turn. However, Flurry of Blows Retains, so it will be available next turn.

## Play Order

**Play Meditate LAST.** It ends your turn. All attacks, blocks, and 0-cost plays must happen before Meditate. The only things that happen after are the stance-change triggers (Flurry returning) and end-of-turn effects.

## Upgrade Priority

High. Retrieving 2 cards instead of 1 lets you set up both an attack and a defensive card for the next turn, or retrieve two key combo pieces.

## Common Mistakes

- Playing Meditate early and losing the rest of your plays for the turn.
- Retrieving cards you do not need next turn (retrieve what solves the upcoming enemy intent).
