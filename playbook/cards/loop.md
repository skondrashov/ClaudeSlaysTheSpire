# Loop (1E, Power | +: 0E)

Defect uncommon Power. At the start of each turn, trigger the passive ability of your first orb.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Doubles the passive output of the first orb slot. With a Frost orb in slot 1 at Focus 2, Loop provides 4 extra block per turn (2+Focus passive triggered again). With Lightning in slot 1, Loop deals an extra 3+Focus damage to a random enemy per turn. The value scales directly with Focus and is permanent for the rest of combat.

UPGRADE: 1E -> 0E. High upgrade priority. At 0E, Loop is a free permanent passive boost, playable on any turn without energy cost.

DECISION POINTS: Play Loop after channeling an orb you want to keep in slot 1. Since new orbs push older orbs rightward and eventually evoke them, the first orb slot is the most stable position. Place the orb type you want Loop to trigger (usually Frost for defense, Lightning for offense) in slot 1 before playing Loop.

FOCUS SCALING: Loop triggers a passive, which means Focus amplifies its value. At Focus 3, a Frost orb passive gives 5 block. Loop adds another 5 block at start of turn = 10 passive block per turn from a single orb. This makes Loop excellent in Focus-heavy builds.

EMOTION CHIP INTERACTION: If Emotion Chip is also active, the first orb triggers three times per cycle: Loop at start of turn, Emotion Chip at start of turn (if HP was lost), and normal passive at end of turn. This is enormous passive value from a single orb.

CONFIRMED: Part of the first Defect victory engine. Loop provided additional Frost passive triggers that supplemented the Coolheaded+ Frost wall.
