# Flying Sleeves (Watcher Common Attack, 1E)

Base: 4x2 damage. Retain. Upgraded: 6x2 damage.

A multi-hit attack that Retains between turns.

## Key Interactions

- **Retain:** Hold Flying Sleeves for a Wrath turn to deal 8x2 = 16 (12x2 = 24 upgraded) doubled damage.
- **Multi-hit:** Triggers Shuriken and Kunai per-attack counters. 2 hits = 2 counts toward the 3-attack threshold.
- **Snake Plant warning:** Multi-hit attacks trigger Malleable block multiple times. Against Snake Plant, Flying Sleeves gives the enemy 3+4 = 7 block from Malleable (two triggers). Use single large hits against Snake Plant when possible.

## Play Order

Play in Wrath for doubled damage. Since it Retains, you can hold it from a safe turn for the next burst turn.
