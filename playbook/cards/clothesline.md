# Clothesline (2E, Attack, 12 damage + 2 Weak | +: 14 damage + 3 Weak)
Expensive but applies Weak. Reduces enemy attack damage by 25% for 2-3 turns.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against single big attackers where Weak saves more HP than alternative plays. Against Hexaghost when no other Weak source is available.
DECISION POINTS: Is 2E for 12 damage + Weak worth more than two 1E cards? Against multiple enemies, you can only Weak one. Consider energy economy.
UPGRADE: 12->14 damage, 2->3 Weak. The extra Weak turn is significant for boss fights. Medium priority.
MATCHUPS: Hexaghost -- Weak reduces 7x6=42 Inferno to 7x4=28. This is a lifesaving application. Bronze Automaton -- Clothesline is the BEST Weak source for this fight because it does not exhaust. Unlike Intimidate or Shockwave, Clothesline can apply Weak on BOTH Hyper Beam turns (turn ~6 and ~12). Reduces Hyper Beam from 57 to 42 each time. Also deals 15 damage with Str 3, contributing to the DPS race. Always save enough energy to play Clothesline on Hyper Beam turns. Slime Boss -- Weak on a large slime post-split reduces its attack by 25% for 2-3 turns. At 2E it is expensive during the split phase when energy is tight, but the Weak duration helps survive combined damage from both slimes.
SLIME BOSS NOTE: Does not help with the core problem (need AOE for split). Clothesline is a supplementary defensive tool in this fight, not a solution.
