# Bane (Silent Attack, 1E, 7 damage / 10 upgraded)

Deal 7 (10) damage. If the enemy has Poison, deal 7 (10) damage again (double damage).

EFFECT: Bane deals its damage TWICE if the target is Poisoned. This is effectively 14 (20) damage for 1E against any Poisoned enemy -- one of the highest damage-per-energy ratios for a common card.

WHY IT'S GOOD:
- In any Silent deck with poison sources (Noxious Fumes, Deadly Poison, Envenom, Bouncing Flask, Poisoned Stab), Bane is almost always dealing double damage.
- 14 damage for 1E (unupgraded) rivals uncommon attack cards. 20 damage for 1E (upgraded) is exceptional.
- The double-hit counts as two separate attacks for relics like Kunai (Dex per 3 attacks) and Shuriken (Str per 3 attacks).

TERROR SYNERGY: With Terror's Vulnerable 99 active, Bane on a Poisoned target deals (7 * 2) * 1.5 = 21 damage unupgraded, or (10 * 2) * 1.5 = 30 damage upgraded, for 1E. This is among the highest single-card damage outputs for 1E in the game.

DEAD BRANCH CONTEXT: Bane can be generated randomly by Dead Branch. When it appears against a Poisoned, Vulnerable boss, it is one of the best possible Dead Branch outcomes.

LIMITATION: Against enemies without Poison, Bane deals only 7 (10) damage for 1E -- below average. Do not take Bane unless the deck has reliable poison application.
