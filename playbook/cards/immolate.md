# Immolate (2E, Attack, 21 AOE damage + adds 1 Burn to discard pile | +: 28 AOE damage)

High-damage AOE attack. Hits ALL enemies for 21 damage. Adds 1 Burn to the discard pile as a downside.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Multi-enemy fights (Byrds, Gremlin groups, Slime Boss split, Collector + Torch Heads). With Vulnerable: floor(21 * 1.5) = 31 damage to ALL enemies. With +2 Strength and Vulnerable: floor(23 * 1.5) = 34 damage. With +4 Strength and Vulnerable: floor(25 * 1.5) = 37 damage. Can one-shot Gremlin groups and clear Byrd flights in a single play. Against bosses, still excellent single-target damage at 21+ base.

DECISION POINTS: The Burn downside is minor in short fights (1-3 turns) and moderate in long fights (10+ turns). Burns deal 2 unblocked damage per turn when in hand. With Torii, Burns deal only 1. In most fights, 21 AOE damage is worth 2-4 HP of Burn damage across the fight. Play freely.

SYNERGIES: Inflame (Strength adds to every hit), Thunderclap (applies Vulnerable to all enemies, then Immolate for 31+ AOE), Rage/Rage+ (Immolate triggers Rage block since it is an Attack), Reaper (AOE softens enemies for Reaper healing from all targets), **Fire Breathing+ (Burns from Immolate trigger 10 free damage on draw -- this applies in EVERY fight, not just Hexaghost. Against Plated Armor enemies like Shelled Parasite, the 10 damage bypasses armor entirely. Do NOT exhaust Fire Breathing when Immolate is in the deck)**, Evolve (Burns from Immolate draw replacement cards, making Immolate's Burn downside pure upside).

MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Byrds (Flight strips from AOE), Slime Boss split (damages both large slimes), 3 Cultists (31 damage each with Vuln). Against Guardian, strong single-target when timed on free turns. Against The Collector, damages Collector + both Torch Heads.

UPGRADE: 21 -> 28 AOE damage. Significant increase. With Vulnerable: floor(28 * 1.5) = 42 AOE damage. High-priority upgrade if Immolate is a key damage source.
