# Doom and Gloom (2E, Attack, 10 damage + Channel 1 Dark | +: 14 damage + Channel 1 Dark)

Defect uncommon Attack. Deals 10 damage (14 upgraded) and channels 1 Dark orb.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: A damage card that also channels a Dark orb for scaling. The Dark orb gains 6+Focus damage per turn passively and deals its accumulated damage when evoked. In long fights, the Dark orb eventually becomes a massive single-target nuke. The upfront 10/14 damage is reasonable for 2E, and the Dark orb is pure upside in multi-turn fights.

DECISION POINTS: Play when orb slots are not full of more valuable orbs (Frost for defense). If all slots are Frost and the deck relies on block, channeling Dark pushes a Frost orb rightward toward evocation. In damage-focused turns or when orb slots are available, Doom and Gloom is excellent.

UPGRADE: 10 -> 14 damage. Moderate upgrade priority -- the 4 extra damage is useful but the Dark orb is the main value regardless of upgrade status.

DARK ORB MECHANICS: Dark orbs gain 6+Focus damage every turn passively. They deal their accumulated damage to the enemy with the lowest HP when evoked. A Dark orb channeled Turn 1 with Focus 2 gains 8 damage per turn; by Turn 5 it has accumulated 40 damage. Evoking it via Dualcast or new orb channeling delivers that 40 damage in a single hit.

BARRAGE SYNERGY: Dark orbs count toward Barrage's orb count. Channeling a Dark orb via Doom and Gloom increases Barrage damage by 4/7 per play.
