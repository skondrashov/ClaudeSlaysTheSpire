# Miracle (Watcher Generated Card, 0E, Retain, Exhaust)

Gain 1 energy. Retain. Exhaust.

Miracle is generated by the Watcher's starting relic Pure Water (1 Miracle at combat start) and by certain cards (Deus Ex Machina generates 2 Miracles when drawn). Miracles Retain between turns, meaning they persist in hand until played.

## When to Play

**ONLY play Miracle when you have a useful way to spend the energy this turn.** Miracle gives +1E, but if you have no cards that benefit from the extra energy, the Miracle is wasted (it exhausts on use -- you only get one play per Miracle).

Good uses:
- Enables playing one more block card to survive an attack turn.
- Enables an attack + block card instead of just block.
- Funds the Calm -> Wrath energy loop when short 1E.
- Enables a Power setup on a free turn.

**BAD use:** Playing all Miracles with no plan for the energy. A confirmed death occurred with 4 Miracles in hand -- the player played all 3 Miracles for +3E, then had no useful cards to spend the energy on (only Meditate+ and Rushdown as options), and died with 0 block against Snake Plant's 21-damage attack. The 3 Miracles were wasted because there was nothing to spend them on.

## Play Order

Play Miracle AFTER determining your turn plan. Count the energy needed for your planned plays, then play exactly enough Miracles to cover the deficit. Excess Miracles should be held (Retain keeps them safe) for future turns where the energy matters.

## Interaction with Stance Changes

Miracle itself does not change stance, but it Retains. This makes it available on the critical Calm-exit burst turn when you need extra energy for attacks in Wrath.
