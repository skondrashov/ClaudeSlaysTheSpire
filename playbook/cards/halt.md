# Halt (Watcher Common Skill, 0E)

Base: 3 block. If in Wrath, gain 9 additional block (12 total). Upgraded: 4 block (14 total in Wrath).

A free block card that becomes excellent in Wrath stance.

## Key Interactions

- **Free in all stances:** 0E for 3-4 block is modest but costs nothing. Good for topping off block after spending all energy.
- **In Wrath:** 12/14 block for 0E is extremely strong. If you need to stay in Wrath for one more attack but also need block, Halt provides both.

## Play Order

Play Halt after other cards when you have 0E remaining, or in Wrath when you need defensive padding for free.
