# Barrage (2E, Attack, 4 damage per channeled orb | +: 6 damage per channeled orb)

Defect common Attack. Deals 4 damage times the number of currently channeled orbs to a target enemy (6 per orb upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: The Defect's primary scaling Attack. With 5 orb slots filled, Barrage deals 20 damage (30 upgraded) for 2E. This scales directly with Capacitor (more orb slots) and any card/relic that fills orbs (Coolheaded, Zap, Cold Snap, Glacier). Barrage is an Attack card, so it is safe against Gremlin Nob.
DECISION POINTS: Play after filling orb slots for maximum value. With 1 orb, Barrage deals only 4 damage for 2E (terrible). With 5 orbs, it deals 20 damage for 2E (excellent). Always channel orbs before playing Barrage.
UPGRADE: 4 -> 6 damage per orb. At 5 orbs, this is 20 -> 30 damage. High upgrade value -- 50% more damage.

THE BOOT INTERACTION: Each per-orb hit is a separate damage instance. The Boot (minimum 5 damage on attacks) bumps each unupgraded hit from 4 to 5. With The Boot and 5 orbs, unupgraded Barrage deals 25 instead of 20. Barrage+ at 6 per orb is above The Boot threshold, so it is unaffected.

ECHO FORM SYNERGY: Echo Form plays Barrage twice. The second play uses the orb count at the time of the copy, which may differ from the first play if orbs were evoked between plays. With 3 orbs and no changes between plays, Echo Form Barrage+ deals 36 damage (18+18) for a single card play. Confirmed: Barrage+ Echo Form'd with 3 orbs dealt exactly 36 damage to an Orb Walker (52 to 16 HP).

SNECKO EYE SYNERGY: Snecko Eye can randomize Barrage to 0E, making it free 20+ damage. Even at 1E, Barrage is well above rate. At 3E it is overcosted. The variance is worth it because Barrage's ceiling is extremely high.

NECRONOMICON SYNERGY: If Barrage costs 2+ energy (its base cost or via Snecko randomization), Necronomicon makes it play twice. With 5 Frost orbs, that is 40 damage for a single card play. This is one of the highest single-card damage outputs available to the Defect.

VULNERABILITY SCALING: With Vulnerable on the target (50% more damage), Barrage+ at 3 orbs deals 6*3*1.5 = 27 damage per play. With 5 orbs: 6*5*1.5 = 45 damage. This was the primary burst damage source in the first Defect victory.

PREDICTION ERROR: The player repeatedly miscalculated Barrage+ as "7 per orb" and unupgraded as "5 per orb" during the first Defect victory. The actual values are 4/6. The confusion likely arose from The Boot bumping unupgraded per-orb hits from 4 to 5, which the player then assumed was the base value. Always use 4 (base) / 6 (upgraded) for Barrage damage calculations.

EARLY DEFECT PRIORITY: Moderate. Barrage is weak early when orb slots are mostly empty (3 slots, often 0-1 filled). It becomes strong once Capacitor, Coolheaded, and Focus cards create a full orb board. Take it in Act 1 if the deck already has orb-channeling cards.
