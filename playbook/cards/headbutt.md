# Headbutt (1E, Attack, 9 damage | +: 12 damage)
Put a card from your discard pile on top of your draw pile.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: When you have a high-value card in discard you need next turn. Strongest with predictable enemy patterns and specific targets (Bash+, Feed, Impervious).

DECISION POINTS: Before playing, check your discard pile. What do you need next turn given enemy intent? Is there a key card -- Bash+ to refresh Vulnerable, Impervious for a big incoming hit, Feed for a low-HP kill -- that beats a random draw?

UPGRADE: 9->12 damage. With Vulnerable: 13->18. High priority if Headbutt is a regular play. In one fight, unupgraded Headbutt (9 damage) left Mystic at 1 HP, extending the fight 2+ turns. Headbutt+ would have killed.

SYNERGIES: Rampage (top-deck Rampage to guarantee playing it next turn, keeping the scaling chain unbroken -- each turn without Rampage is wasted scaling), Carnage+ (guarantee 28-42 next turn), Feed (guarantee kill shot), Bash+ (refresh Vulnerable on demand), small discard pile (more control), **Immolate+ (top-deck for guaranteed 28+ AOE next turn -- this was used repeatedly in the first victory, ensuring Immolate+ was available on every critical turn)**, **Heavy Blade (top-deck for guaranteed high-Str finisher -- under Snecko Eye, the top-decked card gets a new cost roll, potentially costing 0E)**.

SNECKO EYE INTERACTION: Under Confusion, top-decked cards receive a new random cost when drawn. This makes Headbutt exceptionally powerful with Snecko Eye -- top-deck a 2E card, and it has a 25% chance of being free next turn. Headbutt + Immolate+ and Headbutt + Heavy Blade were used this way throughout the first victory.

MATCHUPS: Lagavulin -- top Bash+ to refresh Vulnerable after it wears off. With Lagavulin's -1 Str debuff: floor((12-1)*1.5) = 16 Vuln damage. Guardian -- guarantee burst damage draw for Mode Shift phases. Centurion+Mystic -- guarantee Feed or burst for Mystic kill. Bronze Automaton -- top-deck Heavy Blade for the post-Hyper Beam stun turn. Donu and Deca -- top-deck Immolate+ repeatedly for consistent AOE output.
