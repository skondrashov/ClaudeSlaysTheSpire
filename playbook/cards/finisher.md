# Finisher

Silent Attack, 1E.

**Effect:** Deal 6 damage for each Attack played this turn (8 upgraded).

**Base:** 6 damage per Attack this turn.
**Upgraded:** 8 damage per Attack this turn.

## Key Mechanic

Finisher counts all Attack cards played this turn (before Finisher itself) and deals that count multiplied by 6 (or 8 upgraded) as damage. Shivs count as Attacks. This makes Finisher a powerful turn-ender after a Shiv burst.

## Damage Calculation

With Str and Accuracy modifiers, Finisher's per-hit damage also benefits from Strength:
- Base: 6 per Attack + Str bonus
- After 4 Shivs: 4 * (6 + Str) = significant burst
- In the first Silent victory: 4 attacks played, Finisher dealt ~24 damage for 1E

## Optimal Play Pattern

1. Play all Shivs and other Attacks first
2. Play Finisher as the LAST Attack card of the turn
3. With Blade Dance (3 Shivs) + 1 extra Shiv: 4 attacks = Finisher deals 4 * (6 + Str)

## Synergies

- **Blade Dance:** Creates 3 Shivs that count toward Finisher's multiplier.
- **Accuracy:** Doesn't directly affect Finisher (not a Shiv), but enables Shiv plays that build the Attack count.
- **Phantasmal Killer:** Finisher is an Attack -- Double Damage doubles Finisher's total output.

## Draft Priority

Mid-to-high priority in Shiv decks. Becomes excellent with Blade Dance and any Shiv generation. Without Shiv support, Finisher deals only 6-12 damage (1-2 attacks before it) -- mediocre.
