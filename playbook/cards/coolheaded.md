# Coolheaded (1E, Skill, Channel 1 Frost, Draw 1 | +: Channel 1 Frost, Draw 2)

Defect common Skill. Channels 1 Frost orb and draws 1 card (2 cards upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Defensive cycling card. Frost orb provides 2 passive block per turn (more with Focus). The draw replaces itself in hand, making it a net-positive play for card flow. Upgraded Coolheaded+ draws 2 cards, making it one of the best cycling cards in the Defect's pool.
DECISION POINTS: Play early in the turn to cycle into better cards. The Frost orb provides ongoing block value for the rest of combat.
UPGRADE: Draw 1 -> Draw 2. High upgrade priority -- the extra draw significantly improves hand quality.

NOB WARNING: Coolheaded is a Skill card. It triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage. Against Nob, do NOT play Coolheaded unless you need the draw to find a lethal Attack.
