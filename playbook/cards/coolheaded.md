# Coolheaded (1E, Skill, Channel 1 Frost, Draw 1 | +: Channel 1 Frost, Draw 3)

Defect common Skill. Channels 1 Frost orb and draws 1 card (3 cards upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Defensive cycling card. Frost orb provides 2 passive block per turn (more with Focus). The draw replaces itself in hand, making it a net-positive play for card flow. Upgraded Coolheaded+ draws 3 cards, making it one of the best cycling cards in the game -- better than Battle Trance for deck cycling because it also provides a Frost orb.
DECISION POINTS: Play early in the turn to cycle into better cards. The Frost orb provides ongoing block value for the rest of combat.
UPGRADE: Draw 1 -> Draw 3. Extremely high upgrade priority -- the +2 draw dramatically improves hand quality and makes Coolheaded+ an engine piece rather than a utility card.

ECHO FORM SYNERGY: Echo Form plays Coolheaded+ twice, channeling 2 Frost orbs and drawing 6 cards. This is an enormous hand size boost that frequently draws the entire remaining deck. In the first Defect victory, Echo Form + Coolheaded+ was the primary defensive engine, generating 6+ draws per turn while building a Frost wall.

NOB WARNING: Coolheaded is a Skill card. It triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage. Against Nob, do NOT play Coolheaded unless you need the draw to find a lethal Attack.
