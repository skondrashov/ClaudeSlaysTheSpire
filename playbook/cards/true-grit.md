# True Grit (1E, Skill, 7 block + exhaust RANDOM card | +: 9 block + exhaust CHOSEN card)
Upgraded version lets you CHOOSE which card to exhaust. Unupgraded version exhausts RANDOMLY.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: True Grit+ is excellent -- block + controlled deck thinning. Exhaust status cards (Slimed, Wound, Burn) while gaining block.
DECISION POINTS: CRITICAL -- is this upgraded? Unupgraded True Grit is a ticking time bomb. In a thin deck, random exhaust may destroy your best card. True Grit+ lets you choose expendable cards (Strikes, status cards). Before playing unupgraded, check: are there cards in hand you absolutely cannot lose?

GUARDIAN WARNING: Unupgraded True Grit is particularly dangerous in the Guardian fight. True Grit's random exhaust removes key cards from a hand already under pressure. Playing True Grit 2-3 times (6-8E spent) leads to random exhaust 2-3 times -- each roll removes a critical card (Bash+, Defend, Shrug It Off, Uppercut). By mid-fight, the best block sources are exhausted, leaving only Strike and basic Defend cards. On 32-damage turns (turns 8-12), the deck cannot generate sufficient block. If you have True Grit unupgraded in a Guardian deck, DO NOT PLAY IT. Upgrade is mandatory.

UPGRADE: 7->9 block, random->chosen exhaust. THE most important upgrade in the game. Random exhaust destroyed Whirlwind, Bash, and Pommel Strike in one fight, directly causing death. True Grit+ with chosen exhaust won the same boss fight in a different run. Upgrade priority: #1 whenever True Grit is in the deck, especially if Guardian is the next boss.
SYNERGIES: Dark Embrace (draw on exhaust), Charon's Ashes (3 AOE on exhaust). But be careful of over-exhausting -- keep enough cards to survive the enemy's biggest attack.
ANTI-SYNERGY: Unupgraded True Grit with Fiend Fire+ or any irreplaceable card. Random exhaust can destroy the deck's best card mid-combat with no recourse. Unupgraded True Grit randomly exhausted Fiend Fire+ during a Byrd fight, removing the deck's primary burst damage source and directly leading to death two fights later.
MATCHUPS: Gremlin Nob -- do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage).
