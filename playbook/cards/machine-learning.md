# Machine Learning (1E, Power | +: 0E, Innate)

Defect rare Power. Draw 1 additional card each turn.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Permanent +1 card draw per turn for the rest of combat. Over a 10-turn boss fight, this is 10 extra cards drawn. More cards means more options each turn and faster cycling through the deck to find key pieces (Echo Form, Barrage+, Coolheaded+). The effect is subtle but compounds significantly in long fights.

UPGRADE: 1E -> 0E and gains Innate (starts in hand every combat). Extremely high upgrade priority. At 0E Innate, Machine Learning+ is always free to play Turn 1, guaranteeing the +1 draw from Turn 2 onward with zero opportunity cost.

DECISION POINTS: Play Machine Learning as early as possible in any fight expected to last 3+ turns. The sooner it is active, the more extra draws it generates. In hallway fights that end in 2-3 turns, the 1E cost may not be worth the 1-2 extra draws.

MUMMIFIED HAND SYNERGY: As a Power, Machine Learning triggers Mummified Hand for a free card play. At 0E upgraded, it is already free, so the Mummified Hand trigger is pure bonus value.
