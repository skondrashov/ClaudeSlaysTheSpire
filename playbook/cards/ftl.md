# FTL (0E, Attack, 5 damage, Draw 1 if first card play | +: 5 damage, Draw 2 if first card play)

Defect common Attack. Deals 5 damage. If this is the first card played this turn, draw 1 card (2 upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: A 0-cost attack with conditional draw. When played first each turn, FTL replaces itself in hand (or generates +1 card upgraded). At 0E, the floor is a free 5 damage, and the ceiling is 5 damage + 2 draws. Safe against Gremlin Nob as an Attack card.

UPGRADE: Draw 1 -> Draw 2. Moderate upgrade priority. The extra draw makes FTL+ a strong opening play each turn.

ECHO FORM NOTE: If Echo Form is active, Echo Form's copy is the "first card played" mechanic. FTL played as the first card gets doubled by Echo Form (10 damage + draw effect triggers on the first copy). The interaction with "first card played" requires confirmation on whether both copies trigger the draw or only the first.

DECISION POINTS: Always play FTL first if possible to get the draw bonus. If another card is more important to play first (especially with Echo Form), the draw bonus is lost and FTL becomes a simple 5 damage for 0E.
