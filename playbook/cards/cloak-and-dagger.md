# Cloak and Dagger

Silent Skill, 1E.

**Effect:** Gain 6 Block. Add 1 Shiv to your hand (2 Shivs upgraded).

**Base:** 6 Block + 1 Shiv.
**Upgraded:** 6 Block + 2 Shivs.

## Key Mechanic

Cloak and Dagger is a dual-purpose card: block + damage via Shiv generation. The Shivs benefit from Accuracy, Strength, and other Shiv modifiers.

## Upgrade Priority

**UPGRADE THIS CARD WHEN THE GUARDIAN IS THE BOSS.** The upgrade from 1 Shiv to 2 Shivs adds 9+ damage per play (with Accuracy 4 + Vajra 1). Against the Guardian, this extra Shiv is the difference between triggering Mode Shift (cancelling a lethal attack) and dying 8 damage short. A confirmed death occurred because unupgraded Cloak and Dagger produced 1 Shiv instead of 2, leaving total damage 8 short of the 40-damage Mode Shift threshold.

General upgrade priority: medium-high. The upgrade doubles offensive output while block stays the same. In Shiv-focused decks with Accuracy, the upgrade is high priority because each additional Shiv carries the full Accuracy bonus.

## Interactions

- **Accuracy:** Each Shiv gains the full Accuracy bonus. Upgraded CaD with Accuracy = 2 Shivs at 8-10+ damage each.
- **After Image:** Each Shiv played triggers +1 block from After Image, making upgraded CaD effectively generate 8+ block (6 base + 2 from After Image).
- **Dead Branch:** Shivs exhaust after use, triggering Dead Branch for a random card per Shiv.
- **Wraith Form Dex penalty:** At negative Dex, the 6 block from CaD drops toward 0, but Shiv damage is unaffected by Dex. At -6 Dex, CaD gives 0 block but still generates Shivs.
- **Sharp Hide (Guardian Defensive Mode):** Shivs are Attack cards and trigger Sharp Hide (3 damage per Shiv). During Defensive Mode, each Shiv costs 3 HP.

## Draft Priority

High in Shiv decks. The card is both offense (Shiv generation) and defense (block) in a single 1E play. Take early for sustained value across many fights.
