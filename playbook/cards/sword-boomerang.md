# Sword Boomerang (1E, Attack, 3 damage x3 | +: 3 damage x4)

Hits a random enemy 3 times (4 upgraded) for 3 damage each. Targets are random across all enemies.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: With high Strength, each hit gains +Str damage. At +5 Str: (3+5) x 3 = 24 damage for 1E. Multi-hit means it scales well with any Strength source (Inflame, Demon Form, Brimstone, Spot Weakness). In single-enemy fights, all hits land on the same target. Does not Exhaust, so it can be played every time it is drawn.

DECISION POINTS: Against single enemies, it functions as reliable multi-hit damage. Against multiple enemies, the random targeting makes it unreliable for focus-firing a priority target. At base (3x3 = 9 damage for 1E), it is mediocre. Becomes strong once Strength reaches +3 or higher.

SYNERGIES: Inflame/Demon Form/Brimstone (Strength multiplied across 3-4 hits), Vulnerable (1.5x on each hit), Pen Nib (doubles one hit).

ANTI-SYNERGY: **Snake Plant Malleable** -- each of the 3-4 hits triggers Malleable, causing Snake Plant to gain increasing block. Avoid playing against enemies with Malleable. Random targeting in multi-enemy fights is unreliable for killing priority targets.

WEAKNESSES: 3 base damage per hit is low without Strength. Random targeting against multiple enemies. Outclassed by Pummel for single-target burst (but Pummel Exhausts while Sword Boomerang does not).
