# Shrug It Off (1E, Skill, 8 block + draw 1 | +: 11 block + draw 1)
Block + draw. Replaces itself while providing defense.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Always. The draw means you get block without losing card advantage.
DECISION POINTS: Play early in the turn when possible -- the drawn card may change your plan. Against Gremlin Nob, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage).
UPGRADE: 8->11 block. Significant improvement. Medium-high priority. **After upgrading, always use 11 in calculations, not the base 8.** Miscalculating SIO+ block as 8 instead of 11 costs 3 block per play -- this exact error has been the survival margin in a boss fight.
