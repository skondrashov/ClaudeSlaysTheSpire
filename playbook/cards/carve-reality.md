# Carve Reality (Watcher Common Attack, 1E)

Base: 6 damage. Add a Smite to your hand. Upgraded: 10 damage.

Carve Reality deals damage and generates a Smite card (12 damage, Retain, Exhaust) in hand.

## Key Interactions

- **Smite generation:** Smite deals 12 damage, Retains between turns, and Exhausts. This is excellent sustained damage in long boss fights -- Carve Reality effectively deals 18 total damage (6 + 12 Smite) for 2E total (1E Carve + 1E Smite).
- **In Wrath:** Both Carve Reality and the generated Smite benefit from Wrath doubling. Playing Carve in Wrath = 12 damage + Smite for later.
- **Smite Retains:** You can generate Smite on a defensive turn and hold it for the next offensive turn.

## Draft Priority

Medium. Good sustained damage for boss fights but does not directly interact with the stance engine.
