# Malaise

Silent Skill, X cost (Exhaust).

**Effect:** Apply X Weak and reduce enemy Strength by X. Exhaust.

**Base:** X Weak + X Str Down, Exhaust.
**Upgraded:** X+1 Weak + X+1 Str Down, Exhaust.

## Key Mechanic

Malaise is the Silent's strongest boss debuff. It consumes all remaining energy and applies that amount as both Weak and permanent Strength reduction. Unlike Piercing Wail (temporary -6 Str for 1 turn), Malaise's Strength reduction is permanent for the combat.

## Optimal Usage

- **Save for boss fights.** Malaise exhausts -- single use per combat. Do not waste on hallway fights or elites unless the fight is genuinely lethal.
- **Play LAST on your turn** after all other non-X-cost cards. Malaise consumes all remaining energy. Play block cards, Powers, and 0-cost attacks first, then Malaise with remaining E.
- **Maximum value at high energy.** With 5E and Malaise as the only play: X=5 gives -5 Str and 5 Weak. Combined, this reduces enemy damage by ~50-70% for multiple turns.
- **Do NOT use for Artifact stripping.** Malaise is too valuable to spend on removing 1 Artifact charge. Use Neutralize, Crippling Cloud, or other cheap debuffs for Artifact stripping.

## Victory-Proven Usage

First Silent victory, Turn 8 vs Time Eater: Malaise X=5 applied -5 Str and 5 Weak. Time Eater's damage was reduced from ~39 to ~18 per turn. This debuff enabled survival through the critical final turns with Lizard Tail as backup. The Str reduction is particularly valuable because it is permanent -- Time Eater's subsequent attacks were all reduced for the rest of the fight.

## Synergies

- **High energy decks (Fusion Hammer, Happy Flower):** More energy = larger X = bigger debuff.
- **Wraith Form+:** Malaise reduces the damage you need to block after Intangible wears off.
- **Shiv burst:** Malaise neuters the boss's offense while your Shiv damage scales via Accuracy/Str.

## Draft Priority

High priority for boss fights. One of the best Silent cards against any single-target boss. Take it when it appears in Act 2-3 card rewards, especially with Time Eater or Awakened One as the Act 3 boss.
