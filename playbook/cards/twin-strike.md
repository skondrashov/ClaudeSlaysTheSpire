# Twin Strike (1E, Attack, 5+5 = 10 total | +: 7+7 = 14 total)
Multi-hit attack. Each hit applies Strength separately, making this one of the best Strength-scaling cheap attacks.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against Flight enemies (strips 2 Flight). For Pen Nib counting (each hit counts toward the 10-hit counter). In Strength-stacking decks -- at Str 6, Twin Strike deals (5+6)*2 = 22 damage for 1E (or (7+6)*2 = 26 upgraded). Better damage-per-energy than most 2E attacks at high Strength.
DECISION POINTS: Against Flight enemies, multi-hit is more valuable than single-hit for the same total damage. In Strength decks, Twin Strike is often the best finisher for its energy cost.
SYNERGIES: Vajra/Inflame/Spot Weakness (Strength applies per hit = double benefit), Vulnerable (50% bonus on each hit), Double Tap (plays twice for massive burst).
