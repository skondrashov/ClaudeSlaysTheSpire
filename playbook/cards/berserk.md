# Berserk (0E, Power, gain 2 Vulnerable on YOURSELF, gain 1 energy per turn)
DANGEROUS. Self-Vulnerable (take 50% more damage) for 2 turns. Permanent +1 energy.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: ONLY when enemies are not attacking for the next 2 turns AND you can survive the Vulnerable penalty afterward. In practice, almost never safe.
DECISION POINTS: Can you survive 2 turns of taking 50% more damage from ALL enemies? Against multiple enemies, the combined boosted damage is often lethal. Against bosses at low HP, the self-Vulnerable is suicidal. The energy gain doesn't matter if you die.
MATCHUPS: Hexaghost -- NEVER play. Burns + self-damage + Vulnerable is a death spiral. Multi-enemy fights -- NEVER play. Combined 50% boosted damage from all enemies is overwhelming.
