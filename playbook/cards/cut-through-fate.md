# Cut Through Fate (Watcher Common Attack, 1E)

Base: 7 damage, Scry 2, draw 1 card. Upgraded: 9 damage, Scry 3, draw 1 card.

A damage + draw + deck manipulation card. Scry lets you look at and optionally discard the top 2-3 cards of your draw pile.

## Key Interactions

- **Scry filters bad draws:** Discard Burns, Curses, or unwanted cards from the top of the draw pile. Against Hexaghost, scrying away Burns is critical for maintaining hand quality.
- **Draw 1:** Replaces itself in hand, making it effectively free card advantage.
- **Decent damage:** 7/9 damage for 1E is reasonable, especially in Wrath (14/18 doubled).

## Play Order

Play early in the turn when you need to find specific cards. The Scry + draw can reveal and deliver key cards for the rest of the turn.
