# Bloodletting (0E, Skill, lose 3 HP, gain 2 energy | +: lose 3 HP, gain 3 energy)
Energy generation at an HP cost. Enables big turns.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: When you have high-impact cards to spend the energy on (Double Tap + a big Attack, Corruption setup, Shockwave+ on an early turn). The 3 HP cost is trivial if the extra energy enables a fight-winning play. Best in decks with expensive key cards (3E Powers, 2E Skills) that need energy flexibility.
DECISION POINTS: Play when the extra energy enables something critical this turn. Do NOT play if you have nothing impactful to spend the energy on -- 3 HP for wasted energy is a net loss. At low HP (<15), the 3 HP cost becomes dangerous.
UPGRADE: 2 energy -> 3 energy. Significant improvement. The extra energy makes Bloodletting+ one of the best energy cards in the game.

CRITICAL SEQUENCING: Bloodletting generates energy for the current turn. Do NOT batch Bloodletting with `end` (end turn) in the same action sequence -- the generated energy is wasted if the turn ends immediately. Play Bloodletting, evaluate hand, spend the energy, THEN end the turn. This is especially important when combined with Battle Trance: playing Battle Trance + Bloodletting + `end` in one sequence wastes both the drawn cards AND the generated energy.

SYNERGIES: Rupture (lose HP triggers +1 Str permanently -- Bloodletting is the best Rupture enabler since it costs 0E and triggers Rupture every play). Corruption (the extra energy helps survive the 3E Corruption setup turn). Any 2-3E card that needs energy flexibility.
ANTI-SYNERGY: Low-HP situations where 3 HP is a significant fraction of remaining health.
