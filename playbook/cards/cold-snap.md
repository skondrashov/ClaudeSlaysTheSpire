# Cold Snap (1E, Attack, 6 damage + Channel 1 Frost | +: 9 damage + Channel 1 Frost)

Defect common Attack. Deals 6 damage to a target enemy and channels 1 Frost orb.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Dual-purpose Attack that provides both damage and defense in one card. The Frost orb gives 2 passive block per turn (more with Focus), making Cold Snap a sustained-value card. As an Attack, it is safe against Gremlin Nob. Excellent early Defect pickup because it addresses the Attack deficit while building Frost orb defense.
DECISION POINTS: Play on the highest-priority damage target. The Frost orb channels regardless of whether the damage kills the target. In Frost-heavy builds, Cold Snap fills orb slots that fuel Barrage (4 damage per orb) and provide passive block.
UPGRADE: 6 -> 9 damage. Solid damage increase. Upgrade priority is moderate -- the Frost channel is the primary value.

NECRONOMICON INTERACTION: When Cold Snap costs 2+ energy (naturally or via Snecko Eye randomization) and Necronomicon triggers, it plays twice -- dealing double damage AND channeling two Frost orbs. This is extremely high value because it advances both offense and defense simultaneously.

EARLY DEFECT PRIORITY: High. One of the best early Defect pickups because it is an Attack (Nob protection) that also builds Frost orb defense. Take it when offered in Act 1.
