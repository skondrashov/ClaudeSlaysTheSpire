# Reaper (2E, Attack, deals damage to ALL enemies, heals HP equal to unblocked damage)
Essential sustain card. Damage + healing in one.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Multi-enemy fights where you heal from hitting all enemies. With Strength, both damage and healing scale.
DECISION POINTS: Reaper heals based on UNBLOCKED damage -- enemies with block reduce healing. In scaling fights (Cultists, Gremlin Leader), Shockwave+ on the first attack turn is higher priority than Reaper healing. Prevention > healing.
CRITICAL LIMITATION: Reaper exhausts after one use per fight. It cannot sustain across consecutive combat rooms. A deck relying solely on Reaper for healing will lose 25-40 HP per fight in Act 2 and recover only 6 from Burning Blood between fights. Reaper is necessary but not sufficient as a lone healing source -- pair with Feed, Blood Potion, Regen Potion, or Toy Ornithopter.
SYNERGIES: Inflame (+2 Str boosts damage to all enemies = more healing), Vulnerable (bonus damage = bonus healing), multi-enemy fights (heal from sum of all damage dealt).
