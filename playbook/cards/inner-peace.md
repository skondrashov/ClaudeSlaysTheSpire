# Inner Peace (Watcher Common Skill, 1E)

In Calm: draw 3 cards (4 upgraded). Otherwise: enter Calm.

Inner Peace is a dual-purpose card that adapts based on your current stance.

## Modes

**In Calm (draw mode):** Draw 3 cards (4 upgraded). This is a powerful draw engine that digs for key cards. Use this mode when you are already in Calm and need to find specific cards (Eruption for a burst turn, Defend for blocking).

**In Wrath or no stance (Calm entry mode):** Enter Calm. This provides a safe exit from Wrath and sets up the Calm energy loop for the next turn. Leaving Calm later grants +2E.

## Play Order

**CRITICAL:** If Flurry of Blows is in hand and you are NOT in Calm, play Flurry BEFORE Inner Peace. Inner Peace entering Calm triggers Flurry's return from discard.

If you ARE already in Calm, play Inner Peace first for the draw -- you might draw Flurry of Blows or other useful cards.

## Key Interactions

- **Calm draw -> Eruption burst:** Draw 3-4 cards in Calm, then play Eruption to exit Calm (+2E) and enter Wrath for doubled attacks. The drawn cards fuel the burst.
- **Wrath exit:** When stuck in Wrath with no Vigilance, Inner Peace enters Calm (safe stance).

## Upgrade Priority

High. Drawing 4 cards instead of 3 significantly improves the chance of finding the specific cards you need for the turn plan.
