# Offering (0E, Skill, lose 6 HP, gain 2 energy, draw 3 cards, Exhaust)

Trade HP for a massive tempo burst. One of the best cards in the game.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Turn 1 of any fight where you need to set up quickly (play a Power + attack in the same turn). Excellent with expensive cards (Bludgeon, Fiend Fire, Demon Form) that normally consume your entire energy budget. The 3 extra draws also dig for key cards.

DECISION POINTS: The 6 HP cost is real. At 15 HP, Offering drops you to 9 -- potentially lethal if the enemy attacks for 10+. At high HP (50+), the cost is negligible. Never play Offering when the 6 HP loss would put you below the enemy's incoming damage minus your available block.

SYNERGIES:
- **Unceasing Top**: Offering draws 3 and gives 2 energy. If you can play down to an empty hand, Top triggers for additional draws. The energy surplus makes this very achievable.
- **Fiend Fire**: 2 extra energy means Fiend Fire still leaves energy for cards drawn via Unceasing Top.
- **Expensive cards**: Makes 3E cards (Bludgeon, Demon Form, Impervious) playable alongside other cards in the same turn.

UPGRADE: Not typically a priority -- the base version already provides maximum value.
CAUTION: Exhausts after one use per fight. Plan the turn you play it to maximize the energy and card advantage.
