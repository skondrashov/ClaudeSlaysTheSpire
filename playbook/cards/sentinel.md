# Sentinel (1E, Skill, 5 block, if exhausted gain 2E | +: 8 block, if exhausted gain 3E)
Block card that refunds energy when exhausted.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: In exhaust-heavy decks. Sentinel provides block AND energy when fed to Fiend Fire, True Grit+, Second Wind, or Burning Pact. With Dark Embrace in play, exhausting Sentinel draws a card AND gains energy -- massive tempo.

DECISION POINTS: Base block (5/8) is modest. The value is in the exhaust payoff. Without exhaust tools, Sentinel is just a worse Shrug It Off. With exhaust tools, it's a net-positive play: block + energy recovery.

UPGRADE: 5->8 block, 2E->3E on exhaust. Both halves improve significantly. Upgraded Sentinel exhausted via True Grit+ or Second Wind gives 8 block + 3 energy -- equivalent to playing a free Offering without the HP loss.

SYNERGIES: Fiend Fire (exhausted for 7+ damage AND gains 2-3E), True Grit+ (exhaust for block + energy), Dark Embrace (exhaust draws a card), Second Wind (exhaust all Skills = Sentinel provides block + energy refund), Burning Pact (exhaust Sentinel, draw 2, gain 2-3E). Corruption (all Skills cost 0 and exhaust -- Sentinel becomes 0E for 5-8 block + 2-3E, a net energy gain).

MATCHUPS: Gremlin Nob -- do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage).
