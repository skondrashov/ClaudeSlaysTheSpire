# Follow-Up (Watcher Common Attack, 1E)

Base: 7 damage. If the last card played was an Attack, this costs 0 energy. Upgraded: 11 damage.

Follow-Up is a conditional 0-cost attack that rewards attack chaining.

## Key Interactions

- **Free after attacks:** When chained after another Attack card, Follow-Up is free. This enables long attack chains: Strike -> Follow-Up (free) -> Flurry (free) = 3 attacks for 1E.
- **Kunai / Shuriken synergy:** Free attacks count toward the 3-attack threshold for Kunai (+1 Dex) and Shuriken (+1 Str). Follow-Up + Flurry of Blows after any attack = 3 attacks for minimal energy, triggering both relics.
- **In Wrath:** All damage doubled. A free 14/22 damage attack in Wrath is exceptional.

## Play Order

Play Follow-Up immediately after any Attack card to get the free cost. Do not play Skills between attacks if Follow-Up is in hand -- that breaks the chain and costs 1E.

## Upgrade Priority

Medium-high. 11 damage (from 7) makes the free attack significantly more impactful, especially doubled in Wrath (22 damage for 0E).
