# Fire Breathing (1E, Power, deal 6 damage to ALL enemies when a Status or Curse is drawn | +: 10 damage)

Passive AOE damage triggered by drawing Status cards (Burns, Wounds, Dazed, Slimed) or Curses. Each Status drawn deals 6 (10 upgraded) damage to every enemy.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against any enemy that adds Status cards to your deck. Hexaghost Burns become a damage SOURCE rather than a liability. Power Through Wounds trigger damage on draw. Chosen's Hex Dazed cards trigger it. The more Status cards in the deck, the more damage Fire Breathing deals passively.

KEY SYNERGY -- EVOLVE + FIRE BREATHING: The strongest anti-Status engine in the game. Evolve draws a replacement card when a Status is drawn. Fire Breathing deals damage when a Status is drawn. Together, every Status card drawn both deals damage AND replaces itself with a real card. Status cards become pure upside: free damage with no hand clog. This is a confirmed Hexaghost counter -- Burns deal 6-10 damage to Hexaghost on draw while cycling into real cards via Evolve.

OTHER SYNERGIES:
- **Power Through**: Adds 2 Wounds to hand. When these Wounds are drawn in subsequent turns, Fire Breathing triggers.
- **Second Wind**: Exhausts Status cards, but Fire Breathing triggers on DRAW, not exhaust. Both can coexist -- Fire Breathing gets value on draw, Second Wind cleans them up.
- **Corruption**: Makes Skills cost 0 and exhaust, but does not directly synergize. However, if Corruption leads to playing more Power Through / status-generating cards, Fire Breathing benefits.

DECISION POINTS: Play on free turns (enemy buff turns, setup turns). At 1E, it competes with block and damage on attack turns. Best set up early when Status cards are expected to accumulate. In fights with no Status cards, Fire Breathing does nothing -- it is a dead card.

MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Hexaghost (neutralizes Burns), excellent vs Sentries (Dazed triggers damage), good vs Slime Boss (Slimed triggers), good alongside Power Through. Irrelevant in fights with no Status cards.

UPGRADE: 6 to 10 damage per trigger. Significant upgrade -- 67% more damage per Status drawn. Worth upgrading when the deck generates multiple Status cards per cycle.
