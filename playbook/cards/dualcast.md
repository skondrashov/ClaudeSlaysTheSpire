# Dualcast (1E, Skill, Evoke your front orb twice | +: 0E)

Defect starter card. Evokes the frontmost orb slot twice, triggering its evoke effect two times.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Burst damage (Lightning: 8x2 = 16 damage) or burst block (Frost: 5x2 = 10 block) for 1E. Excellent finisher when a Lightning orb is in the front slot. With Focus scaling, evoke values increase, making Dualcast scale multiplicatively.
DECISION POINTS: Only valuable when an orb is channeled. With an empty orb slot, Dualcast does nothing. Plan orb channeling before using Dualcast -- channel a Lightning orb with Zap (0E) then Dualcast it for 16 damage.
UPGRADE: 1E -> 0E. Massive upgrade -- free double evoke. Upgrade priority is high for the Defect.

NOB WARNING: Dualcast is a Skill card. It triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage. Against Nob, only play Dualcast if the evoke damage justifies the +2 Str cost (e.g., killing Nob that turn).
