# Bite (1E, Attack, 7 damage, heal 2 HP)

Colorless card obtained from the Vampires event. Replaces Strikes (all Strikes are removed, 5 Bites added).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Provides sustained healing across every fight. 2 HP per play adds up: in a 5-turn fight playing 1-2 Bites per turn, that is 10-20 HP healed. Better than Strike in every way (similar damage, plus healing).

WHEN IT'S BAD: The card itself is fine -- the problem is the cost of obtaining it. The Vampires event removes ~30% of your Max HP. At a reduced Max HP pool (e.g., 56 instead of 80), the 2 HP healing per Bite cannot compensate for the burst damage of Act 2 elites and hard hallway fights.

DAMAGE COMPARISON: Bite deals 7 damage vs Strike's 6 damage. With Vulnerability on the target, Bite deals 10 (floor(7*1.5)) vs Strike's 9. Slightly better damage output.

SYNERGIES: Strength scaling (Inflame, Demon Form) boosts both the damage and makes Bite a more efficient card. Reaper still outclasses Bite for healing, but Bites provide consistent small heals every turn rather than one big heal.

ANTI-SYNERGY: The Max HP loss from obtaining Bites makes Reaper less effective (less HP to heal back to). Cards that rely on high HP thresholds become weaker.

NOTE: Bite is NOT an Ironclad card -- it is colorless. Molten Egg will NOT auto-upgrade Bites.
