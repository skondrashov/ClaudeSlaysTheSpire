# Empty Body (Watcher Common Skill, 1E)

Base: 7 block. Exit your current stance (enter no stance). Upgraded: 10 block.

Empty Body provides block and leaves you in no stance (neutral). Useful as a safe exit from Wrath when Calm entry is not desired.

## Key Interactions

- **Wrath exit without Calm:** Unlike Vigilance (enters Calm), Empty Body leaves you in no stance. This means you do NOT get the +2E Calm exit bonus next turn, but you also are not locked into the Calm -> Wrath cycle.
- **Stance change triggers:** Leaving any stance triggers Flurry of Blows return. Play Flurry BEFORE Empty Body.

## Play Order

Play Flurry of Blows before Empty Body for the stance-change trigger. Otherwise, play whenever you need to exit Wrath safely.

## Draft Priority

Low-medium. Vigilance is generally better (block + Calm energy), but Empty Body provides flexibility.
