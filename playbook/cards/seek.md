# Seek (0E, Skill, Choose 1 card from draw pile | +: Choose 2 cards)

Defect rare Skill. Search the draw pile and add 1 card to hand (2 cards upgraded). Exhausts.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: The most consistent card in the Defect's pool. Seek guarantees access to the exact card needed for the current situation -- Echo Form on setup turns, Barrage+ on damage turns, Coolheaded+ on defense turns. No other Defect card provides this level of adaptability. At 0E, the tutor effect is free in terms of energy.

UPGRADE: 1 card -> 2 cards. Extremely high upgrade priority. Seek+ finds 2 cards for 0E, which is functionally a perfect draw that ignores deck dilution. In an Echo Form deck, Seek+ doubled by Echo Form tutors 4 cards from the draw pile -- the player effectively chooses their entire hand.

DECISION POINTS: Use Seek to find the highest-impact card for the current turn. In boss fights, Turn 1 Seek should almost always find Echo Form (or the most important setup piece). In hallway fights, Seek should find the kill card (Barrage+ with full orbs) to end the fight quickly.

BOTTLED LIGHTNING SYNERGY: Making Seek innate via Bottled Lightning guarantees a tutor on Turn 1 of every combat. This is the single most consistent opener available to the Defect -- every fight starts with the player choosing their best card from the draw pile. With Echo Form in the deck, this means Turn 1 always has Echo Form available.

ECHO FORM SYNERGY: Echo Form doubles Seek, tutoring 2 cards (or 4 with Seek+). This enables assembling a complete hand every turn -- finding both the damage card AND the defense card simultaneously.

EXHAUST NOTE: Seek exhausts after use. It is a one-time tutor per combat (unless returned via Hologram or similar effects). The single use is almost always sufficient because it sets up the engine on Turn 1, and the engine sustains itself afterward.

NOB WARNING: Seek is a Skill card. It triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage. Against Nob, play Seek only if the tutored card enables a kill within 1-2 turns.

CONFIRMED DOMINANT: Core card of the first Defect victory. Seek+ (Bottled Lightning innate) guaranteed Echo Form+ on Turn 1 of every boss fight, enabling the full orb engine from the first turn of combat.
