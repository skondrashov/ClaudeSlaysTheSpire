# Echo Form (3E, Power | +: 3E, Ethereal removed)

Defect rare Power. The first card you play each turn is played twice.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: The single most powerful card in the Defect's pool. Echo Form doubles the value of the strongest card played each turn -- Barrage+ becomes 70 damage, Coolheaded+ becomes 6 draws + 2 Frost orbs, Seek+ becomes a 4-card tutor. The doubling is unconditional and permanent for the rest of combat. Any card that is already strong becomes game-defining when doubled.

CRITICAL MECHANIC -- ETHEREAL: Unupgraded Echo Form is Ethereal, meaning it exhausts at end of turn if not played. If drawn on a turn where 3E cannot be spent on it (because block or damage is needed), the card is permanently lost. Upgrading Echo Form removes Ethereal, allowing it to stay in hand until affordable. This makes Echo Form one of the highest upgrade priorities in the game.

DECISION POINTS: Play Echo Form as early as possible in long fights (bosses, elites). The doubling compounds over many turns -- playing it Turn 1 doubles 9+ cards across a 10-turn fight. Playing it Turn 5 doubles only 5+ cards. The 3E setup cost means the turn Echo Form is played, only 0-1E remains for other cards. Plan for a low-pressure turn (e.g., after building a Frost wall) to deploy it.

UPGRADE: Removes Ethereal. This is arguably the most important upgrade in the Defect card pool. Without the upgrade, a bad draw timing can permanently lose the card.

SEEK SYNERGY: Seek/Seek+ guarantees Echo Form is in hand on Turn 1 (or whenever needed). With Bottled Lightning on Seek, the player always opens with a tutor that can find Echo Form. This Seek + Echo Form combination is the core of the Defect's most consistent winning engine.

MUMMIFIED HAND SYNERGY: Echo Form is a Power. Playing it triggers Mummified Hand, making a random card in hand cost 0. This partially offsets the 3E setup cost -- on a 4E turn, play Echo Form (3E) and get a free card play (0E from Mummified Hand).

TIME EATER WARNING: Echo Form copies count toward the Time Warp 12-card counter. Every doubled card is 2 plays counted, not 1. This means the effective card budget per cycle is roughly 6 real card plays (each counting as 2). Track the counter precisely and account for every Echo Form copy.

BOTTLED LIGHTNING INTERACTION: Bottled Lightning can make Echo Form innate, guaranteeing it starts in hand every combat. However, Bottled Lightning on Seek is often better because Seek can find Echo Form AND adapt to the specific fight.

CONFIRMED DOMINANT: Core card of the first Defect victory. Echo Form+ doubled Barrage+ (36 damage with 3 orbs, 60 with 5 orbs), Coolheaded+ (6 draws + 2 Frost), and Seek+ (4 cards tutored) across all 3 boss fights. The card transformed an orb-synergy deck into a dominant engine that could burst, defend, and cycle simultaneously.
