# Fission (0E, Skill, Remove ALL orbs. Gain 1 Energy + Draw 1 per orb removed | +: EVOKE ALL orbs. Gain 1 Energy + Draw 1 per orb evoked)

Defect uncommon Skill. Removes all channeled orbs and grants 1 Energy and 1 card draw per orb removed.

CRITICAL UPGRADE DIFFERENCE:
- **Unupgraded Fission: REMOVES orbs WITHOUT evoking them.** The orbs vanish. No Frost block, no Lightning damage, no Dark burst. The orbs are simply deleted. You still gain the Energy and draws, but the orb effects are lost entirely.
- **Fission+: EVOKES all orbs, triggering their evoke effects.** Frost orbs grant block, Lightning orbs deal damage, Dark orbs fire their accumulated damage. Then you gain Energy and draws on top. This is a massive burst play.

THIS IS ONE OF THE MOST IMPORTANT UPGRADES IN THE DEFECT'S CARD POOL. Unupgraded Fission is a trap in Frost-heavy decks: it destroys the Frost orbs providing passive block without generating any evoke block, leaving the player defenseless. Upgraded Fission+ is one of the strongest burst cards in the game.

WHEN IT'S GOOD (UPGRADED): Fission+ with 5 orbs = 5 Frost evokes (25 block), 5 Energy, 5 draws. This is a massive defensive burst AND enables playing the drawn cards with the gained energy. The ceiling is enormous.

WHEN IT'S BAD (UNUPGRADED): Destroys orb-based defense for Energy and draws that may not compensate. Playing unupgraded Fission with 3 Frost orbs deletes 6 passive block per turn and 15 evoke block, getting only 3E and 3 draws in return. If those draws do not include sufficient block cards, the player takes the full incoming damage. This exact scenario caused significant HP loss in a Snake Plant fight.

DECISION POINTS:
- **Never play unupgraded Fission when Frost orbs are providing critical block** unless the Energy and draws will enable a lethal turn.
- **Upgraded Fission+ is a burst finisher.** Use it when the evoke effects plus the Energy/draws enable killing the enemy or generating massive block.
- At 0 orbs, Fission does nothing. At 1 orb, the payoff is minimal. Fission shines at 3+ orbs.

UPGRADE PRIORITY: Extremely high. This is a top-3 Defect upgrade target. The difference between removing and evoking is the difference between destroying your own defense and generating a massive burst turn.
