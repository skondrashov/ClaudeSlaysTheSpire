# Capacitor (1E, Power, +2 Orb Slots | +: +3 Orb Slots)

Defect uncommon Power. Permanently increases maximum orb slots by 2 (3 upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: More orb slots means more simultaneous passive effects and higher Barrage damage. The Defect starts with 3 orb slots. Capacitor+ brings it to 6, enabling 6 simultaneous Frost passives (12+ block/turn at Focus 0, 30+ at Focus 3) or 6 orbs for Barrage+ (42 damage). Orb slots are one of the Defect's core scaling axes.

UPGRADE: +2 -> +3 orb slots. High upgrade priority. The extra slot is worth 2+Focus block per turn (Frost) or 7 damage per Barrage+ play.

DECISION POINTS: Play Capacitor early in long fights to maximize the number of turns with extra orb slots. In short hallway fights, the Power setup cost matters less because the fight ends before the orb slots are fully utilized.

RUNIC CAPACITOR INTERACTION: The Runic Capacitor relic provides +3 orb slots at combat start (6 total). With Capacitor+ on top, the Defect reaches 9 orb slots, enabling massive Barrage damage (63 per play with Barrage+) and an enormous Frost wall.

BARRAGE SCALING: Each additional orb slot (when filled) adds 4/7 damage to Barrage/Barrage+. Capacitor+ adding 3 slots = +21 Barrage+ damage when all slots are filled.
