# Sever Soul (2E, Attack, 16 damage, Exhaust all non-Attack cards in hand | +: 22 damage)
High damage attack that exhausts every non-Attack card currently in hand (Defends, Skills, Powers, Status cards).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: In exhaust-synergy decks with Feel No Pain or Dark Embrace, where exhausting cards generates value. When hand is mostly Status cards (Slimed, Wounds, Dazed) and you want to purge them all at once while dealing damage.

WHEN IT'S DANGEROUS: In any fight where blocking matters on future turns. Sever Soul exhausts ALL Defend cards in hand, permanently removing them from the fight. In long fights (Guardian, bosses), losing Defend cards cripples block density for the rest of combat. Playing Sever Soul during a Guardian fight exhausted 2 Defend cards, directly reducing survivability on critical turns.

DECISION POINTS: Before playing, count how many non-Attack cards are in hand and evaluate whether you can afford to lose them ALL for the rest of this fight. If any Defend or key Skill is in hand, do NOT play Sever Soul unless the fight will end this turn. Play Defends/Skills FIRST, then Sever Soul last to minimize cards exhausted.

CRITICAL WARNING: Do not buy or take this card as a generic damage source. The exhaust-all-non-attacks cost is severe. It is NOT a substitute for Hemokinesis or Carnage. Only take it in dedicated exhaust decks (Feel No Pain, Dark Embrace, Corruption) where exhausting cards is the strategy.

UPGRADE: 16 -> 22 damage. The upgrade improves damage but does not change the dangerous exhaust mechanic.
