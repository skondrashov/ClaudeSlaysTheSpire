# Wave of the Hand (Watcher Uncommon Skill, 1E)

Whenever you gain Block this turn, apply 1 Weak to ALL enemies. Upgraded: apply 2 Weak.

Wave of the Hand converts block generation into Weak application for the rest of the turn.

## Key Interactions

- **Multiple triggers:** Every block source triggers Weak application. Playing Wave of the Hand then 2 Defends = 2 separate Weak applications (2 Weak unupgraded, 4 Weak upgraded).
- **Just Lucky synergy:** Just Lucky gives 2 block, triggering Wave of the Hand.
- **Vigilance synergy:** Vigilance gives 8-12 block, triggering a large Weak application.
- **Hexaghost Inferno:** Play Wave of the Hand before block cards on the Inferno turn. Each block card applies Weak, reducing Inferno's multi-hit damage significantly.

## Play Order

Play Wave of the Hand FIRST, before any block-generating cards. It must be active before block is gained to trigger Weak. Playing it after block cards wastes the triggers.

## Draft Priority

Medium-high. This is the Watcher's primary Weak source. Since Weak is critical for surviving Inferno and other large attacks, Wave of the Hand fills a mandatory slot in boss preparation.
