# Demon Form
COST: 3E
TYPE: Power
EFFECT: At the start of each turn, gain 2 Strength (3 Strength upgraded).

ROLE: Long-fight scaling engine. Provides compounding damage over many turns. By turn 5, effectively +8-10 Strength which makes every attack devastating.

STRENGTHS:
- Dominates boss fights (10-15 turns = +20-30 Strength)
- Turns mediocre attacks into kill shots late-fight
- Pairs with Limit Break for exponential Strength growth
- No HP cost, no downside once played

WEAKNESSES:
- 3E cost means the setup turn produces ZERO block and ZERO damage
- Needs 2-3 turns after playing to meaningfully impact fight outcome
- Useless when entering a fight at low HP vs fast-scaling enemies
- Against 3 Cultists at 30% HP, the 3E setup turn and 2 turns of ramp are 3 turns of unblocked incoming damage -- fatal
- Does nothing to help survive the current turn

WHEN TO PLAY: Turn 1 of boss fights. Turn 1 of any fight where the enemy buffs (free turn). NEVER play when at low HP and facing immediate lethal damage next turn -- spending 3E on future scaling when you need block NOW is how decks die.

DECK BUILDING NOTE: If Demon Form is your primary scaling, the deck still needs fast burst (Fiend Fire, Rampage, Immolate) for emergency hallway fights at low HP. Demon Form solves bosses but not the Act 2 death spiral.
