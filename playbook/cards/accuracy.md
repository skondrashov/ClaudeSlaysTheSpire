# Accuracy

Silent Power, 1E (0E upgraded).

**Effect:** Your Shivs deal 4 additional damage (6 upgraded).

**Base:** +4 Shiv damage.
**Upgraded:** +6 Shiv damage.

## Key Mechanic

Accuracy is a permanent Power that adds flat damage to every Shiv played for the rest of combat. The bonus stacks with multiple copies: 2x Accuracy = +8 per Shiv. Combined with Strength from any source (Cultist Potion, Strength Potion, etc.), the per-Shiv damage becomes devastating.

## Victory-Proven Damage Math

With 2x Accuracy (+8) and Str 11 (from Cultist Potion Ritual by Turn 10):
- Shiv base: 4 + 8 (Accuracy) + 11 (Str) = 23 damage per 0-cost Shiv
- Blade Dance (1E) = 3 Shivs = 69 damage
- With Phantasmal Killer (double): 46 per Shiv, 3 Shivs = 138 damage
- With Vulnerable: 36 per Shiv (doubled + 1.5x), 2 Shivs = 72 damage

This scaling makes Accuracy the most important card in a Shiv-based Silent deck.

## Synergies

- **Blade Dance:** 3 Shivs for 1E, each benefiting from Accuracy bonus.
- **Cloak and Dagger+:** 2 Shivs + 6 block, with Accuracy bonus on each Shiv.
- **Infinite Blades:** Free Shiv each turn with Accuracy bonus = free 8+ damage/turn.
- **Phantasmal Killer:** Doubles Shiv damage for one devastating burst turn.
- **Cultist Potion:** Ritual Str compounds with Accuracy on every Shiv. Key contributor to the first Silent victory.
- **Finisher:** Scales with attacks played; after 3-4 Shivs, Finisher deals massive damage.
- **After Image:** Each Shiv played gives +1 block from After Image, making Shivs both offense and defense.

## Time Eater Interaction

Shivs count as individual card plays toward the 12-card Time Warp counter. However, with Accuracy stacking, each Shiv deals enough damage that 12 card plays per cycle output 200+ total damage. The Time Warp counter is manageable when each card play is worth 20+ damage.

## Draft Priority

High priority in any Silent deck building toward Shivs. Take the first Accuracy early (Turn 1 setup in every fight). The second copy is also excellent -- going from +4 to +8 per Shiv doubles the offensive value of every Shiv-generating card in the deck.
