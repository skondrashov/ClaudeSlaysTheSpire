# Dropkick (1E, Attack, 5 damage | +: 8 damage)
If target is Vulnerable: gain 1 energy + draw 1 card. Effectively FREE when target is Vulnerable.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: With reliable Vulnerable application (Bash+, Thunderclap). Infinite cycling tool -- costs 1E, refunds 1E, deals damage, draws a card.
DECISION POINTS: Is the target Vulnerable? If yes, always play -- it's free damage + cycling. If no, it's a terrible card (5/8 damage, no benefit). Do NOT play against non-Vulnerable targets.
UPGRADE: 5->8 damage. With Vulnerable: 7->12 damage. The upgrade matters because the free damage is significantly higher.
SYNERGIES: Bash+ (3 turns Vulnerable = 3 free Dropkick plays), Thunderclap (mass Vulnerable = Dropkick any target).
MATCHUPS: Book of Stabbing -- Dropkick+ dealt 12 Vuln damage three times as free actions = 36 total damage for 0 net energy. Slime Boss (pre-split) -- excellent with Bash+ providing 3 turns of Vulnerable. Free cycling helps find block cards for the split transition. Post-split -- requires re-applying Vulnerable to each slime individually, which is energy-intensive without Thunderclap.
