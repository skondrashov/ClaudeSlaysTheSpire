# Pommel Strike (1E, Attack, 9 damage, draw 1 | +: 10 damage, draw 2)
Damage + draw. Replaces itself in hand while dealing more than Strike.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Always. The draw is the real value -- it cycles through the deck to find key cards.
DECISION POINTS: Play early in the turn to draw into more options. Against Byrds, the 9 damage is halved by Flight but the draw still finds better answers.
UPGRADE: 9->10 damage, draw 1->draw 2. The double draw makes this one of the best cycling cards in the game. High priority upgrade.
SYNERGIES: Any deck that wants to find specific cards (Bash+, Fiend Fire, Feed). Snecko Oil (draw into 0-cost cards).
