# Impervious (2E, Skill, 30 block, Exhaust)
Best single-card block in the game. 30 block for 2E.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against the biggest attack of the fight. Guardian's 32-damage attack (take only 2). Hexaghost Inferno.
DECISION POINTS: Save for the biggest incoming hit. Using 30 block on a 16-damage turn is wasteful -- Defends handle that. One-time use means timing matters. Against Gremlin Nob, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage).
MATCHUPS: Guardian -- absorbs the 32-damage attack (take only 2). Gremlin Leader -- turn 1 opener for 30 block safety.
