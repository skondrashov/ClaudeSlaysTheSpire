# Flurry of Blows (Watcher Common Attack, 0E)

Base: 4 damage. Upgraded: 6 damage. Returns to hand from discard whenever you change stance.

Flurry of Blows is the Watcher's core free-damage engine. Every stance change brings it back to hand for another free 0-cost play. In a deck that cycles stances 2-3 times per turn, Flurry generates 8-18 free damage per turn.

## PLAY ORDER (CRITICAL)

**ALWAYS play Flurry of Blows BEFORE any stance-change card.** This is the single most important Watcher sequencing rule.

The return trigger works as follows:
1. Flurry of Blows must be in the DISCARD PILE when a stance change occurs.
2. When you change stance (Eruption, Vigilance, Crescendo, Inner Peace, Fear No Evil, Empty Body, etc.), Flurry returns from discard to hand.
3. You can then play it again for free.

**WRONG order:** Eruption (enter Wrath) -> Flurry of Blows. Flurry was in hand during the stance change, so it did NOT trigger. You get only one play.

**CORRECT order:** Flurry of Blows (4 damage, goes to discard) -> Eruption (enter Wrath, Flurry returns to hand) -> Flurry of Blows again (4 more damage). You get two plays for 8 total damage instead of one play for 4.

With 2 stance changes per turn, the correct sequence yields 3 plays (12 damage) vs 1 play (4 damage) from the wrong sequence. **This is 8 free damage per turn wasted by incorrect ordering.**

## In Wrath

Flurry of Blows damage is doubled in Wrath (8 base, 12 upgraded). Play it in Wrath whenever possible for maximum value.

## Synergies

- **Shuriken / Kunai:** Each play counts as an attack. With 2-3 Flurry plays per turn, Shuriken and Kunai trigger frequently.
- **Multiple copies:** Two Flurry of Blows in the deck means two returns per stance change. With 2 copies and 2 stance changes per turn, that is 6 free plays for 24 damage.
- **Rushdown:** Draw 2 cards each time you enter Wrath. More draws = more stance changes = more Flurry triggers.

## Draft Priority

High. Take Flurry of Blows in Act 1 whenever offered. It is the Watcher's best common card for building the stance-cycling damage engine. A second copy doubles the free damage output.
