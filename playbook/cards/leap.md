# Leap (1E, Skill, Gain 9 Block | +: Gain 12 Block)

Defect common Skill. Provides 9 block (12 upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Straightforward block card. 9 block for 1E is good value (better than Defend's 5 block). Fills the Defect's need for reliable turn-by-turn block before Frost orbs + Focus are online.
DECISION POINTS: Play when you need block. No special mechanics.
UPGRADE: 9 -> 12 block. Decent upgrade but lower priority than Focus cards or Coolheaded.

NOTE: Leap is a Skill card. It triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage. Against Nob, only play if survival requires it (incoming damage > remaining HP).
