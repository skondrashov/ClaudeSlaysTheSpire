# Compile Driver (1E, Attack, 7 damage + Draw 1 per orb slot filled | +: 10 damage + Draw 1 per orb slot filled)

Defect common Attack. Deals 7 damage and draws 1 card for each orb currently channeled.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Multi-purpose Attack that provides both damage and card draw. With 3 orbs channeled, it draws 3 cards for 1E while dealing 7 damage -- excellent value. The draw scales with orb count, rewarding orb-heavy builds. As an Attack card, it is safe against Gremlin Nob.
DECISION POINTS: Best played when orbs are channeled. With 0 orbs, it is just a 7-damage Strike equivalent. With 3+ orbs, it becomes one of the best draw engines available. Channel orbs before playing Compile Driver to maximize draw.
UPGRADE: 7 -> 10 damage. The damage increase is useful but the draw is the primary value.

EARLY DEFECT PRIORITY: High. The Defect's starting deck is extremely Skill-heavy (7 Skills vs 3 Attacks). Compile Driver is an Attack that also draws cards -- it addresses both the Attack deficit and the need for deck cycling. Take it when offered in Act 1.
