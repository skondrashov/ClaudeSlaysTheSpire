# Blizzard (1E, Attack, Deal damage = 2x Frost channeled this combat | +: 3x Frost channeled)

Defect uncommon Attack. Deals damage equal to 2 times the total number of Frost orbs channeled this combat (3 times upgraded). Hits ALL enemies.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: AOE attack that scales with Frost orb count over the course of the fight. In a Frost-heavy deck that channels 2-3 Frost per turn (Coolheaded, Chill, Cold Snap, Glacier), Blizzard deals 10-18 AOE damage by Turn 3, scaling to 30-50+ by mid-fight. Excellent in multi-enemy fights where Frost defense and AOE damage are both needed.

UPGRADE: 2x -> 3x multiplier. High upgrade priority -- 50% more damage from the same Frost count.

DECISION POINTS: Play Blizzard after channeling several Frost orbs for maximum damage. The card is weak in early turns (2-6 damage) but becomes devastating in turns 5+. Best in decks that naturally channel many Frost orbs for defense.
