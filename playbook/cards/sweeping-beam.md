# Sweeping Beam (1E, Attack, 6 damage to ALL enemies + Draw 1 | +: 9 damage to ALL enemies + Draw 1)

Defect common Attack. Deals 6 AOE damage and draws 1 card.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: The Defect's best early AOE option. Hits all enemies and replaces itself with a draw. Fills the AOE gap that every character needs for multi-enemy fights (Gremlin Gang, Louse pairs, Act 2 multi-fights). As an Attack, it is safe against Gremlin Nob.
DECISION POINTS: Always play in multi-enemy fights for the AOE. In single-enemy fights, it functions as a 6-damage Attack with a draw -- slightly below Strike in damage but the draw makes it net-positive.
UPGRADE: 6 -> 9 damage AOE. Solid improvement for multi-enemy fights.

EARLY DEFECT PRIORITY: High. Addresses the Defect's Attack deficit and provides AOE damage, which is a critical gap for Act 1 Slime Boss and Act 2 multi-enemy fights.
