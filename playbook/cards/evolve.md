# Evolve (1E, Power)
Whenever you draw a Status card, draw an additional card.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against enemies that add Status cards to your deck. Evolve neutralizes hand-clogging from Burns (Hexaghost), Slimed (Slimes), Dazed (Sentries, Chosen), and Wounds (Book of Stabbing). Each Status card drawn is immediately replaced by a real card, so Status cards no longer reduce hand quality.

DECISION POINTS: Play on free turns (enemy buff turns). At 1E, it competes with block/damage on attack turns. Best set up early in fights where Status cards are expected.

SYNERGIES:
- **Hexaghost Burns**: Burns draw replacement cards, so accumulating Burns does not reduce hand size. Combined with the draw, Evolve effectively makes Burns cycle-neutral.
- **Sentries Dazed**: Dazed draws replacement. Since Dazed is Ethereal (exhausts end of turn), the Evolve draw is pure upside -- you get a real card, Dazed exhausts itself.
- **Slimed cards**: Draw replacement when Slimed is drawn. Still costs 1E to exhaust Slimed, but hand quality is maintained.
- **Fire Breathing**: If both Powers are active, drawing Status cards both deals damage (Fire Breathing) and draws replacements (Evolve).

MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Hexaghost (top-priority setup on Turn 1 free turn). Excellent vs Sentries (Dazed management). Excellent vs Slime Boss (Slimed card management -- confirmed: Evolve was critical to surviving the split phase at 3 HP by maintaining hand quality through Slimed flood). Moderate value in fights without Status cards (does nothing if no Status cards are present). Zero value in fights without Status card generation (Byrds, Chosen, etc.) -- do NOT play in non-Status fights as it wastes 1E for nothing.

UPGRADE: Evolve+ draws 2 cards per Status instead of 1. Moderate upgrade priority -- only worth upgrading if facing heavy Status card fights.
