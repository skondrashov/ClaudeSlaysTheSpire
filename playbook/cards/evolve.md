# Evolve (1E, Power)
Whenever you draw a Status card, draw an additional card.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against enemies that add Status cards to your deck. Evolve neutralizes hand-clogging from Burns (Hexaghost), Slimed (Slimes), Dazed (Sentries, Chosen), and Wounds (Book of Stabbing). Each Status card drawn is immediately replaced by a real card, so Status cards no longer reduce hand quality.

DECISION POINTS: Play on free turns (enemy buff turns). At 1E, it competes with block/damage on attack turns. Best set up early in fights where Status cards are expected.

SYNERGIES:
- **Mark of Pain + Evolve+**: Mark of Pain gives 4 energy but adds Wounds to draw pile when attacks are played. Evolve+ draws 2 cards per Wound drawn, converting the downside into massive card advantage. This combination was confirmed effective: 4E + bonus draws meant playing 5-7 cards per turn reliably. The Wounds are Status cards, so they trigger Evolve. This is one of the strongest boss relic + card synergies for Ironclad.
- **Hexaghost Burns / Immolate Burns**: Burns draw replacement cards, so accumulating Burns does not reduce hand size. Combined with the draw, Evolve effectively makes Burns cycle-neutral. When Immolate+ is played repeatedly (as in the Donu and Deca boss fight with Dual Wield+ copies), multiple Burns enter the discard pile. Evolve converts each subsequent Burns draw into a fresh card, preventing the deck from clogging over long fights.
- **Sentries Dazed**: Dazed draws replacement. Since Dazed is Ethereal (exhausts end of turn), the Evolve draw is pure upside -- you get a real card, Dazed exhausts itself.
- **Slimed cards**: Draw replacement when Slimed is drawn. Still costs 1E to exhaust Slimed, but hand quality is maintained.
- **Fire Breathing**: If both Powers are active, drawing Status cards both deals damage (Fire Breathing) and draws replacements (Evolve).

MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Hexaghost (top-priority setup on Turn 1 free turn). Excellent vs Sentries (Dazed management). Excellent vs Slime Boss (Slimed card management -- confirmed: Evolve was critical to surviving the split phase at 3 HP by maintaining hand quality through Slimed flood). Excellent with Mark of Pain (see synergies). Moderate value in fights without Status cards (does nothing if no Status cards are present). With Mark of Pain, Evolve has value in EVERY fight since attacks generate Wounds. Without Mark of Pain, zero value in fights without Status card generation (Byrds, Chosen pre-Hex, etc.) -- do NOT play in non-Status fights as it wastes 1E for nothing. Note: Chosen DOES add Dazed via Hex, so Evolve has value after Hex is applied (confirmed: Evolve+ was played after Hex activation to manage both Wounds and Dazed).

UPGRADE: Evolve+ draws 2 cards per Status instead of 1. HIGH upgrade priority with Mark of Pain (2 draws per Wound = dominant card advantage). Moderate upgrade priority without Mark of Pain -- only worth upgrading if facing heavy Status card fights.
