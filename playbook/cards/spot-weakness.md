# Spot Weakness (1E, Skill, +3 Strength if enemy intends to Attack | +: +4 Strength)
Conditional Strength gain. Only works when the enemy's current intent is an Attack.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against enemies with predictable attack patterns. Against bosses with alternating attack/defend cycles (Guardian, Hexaghost). +3 permanent Strength compounds over a long fight -- at 5 attacks remaining, that is +15 total damage.

DECISION POINTS: Only play when the enemy's intent is an Attack. On non-attack turns, this does nothing and wastes 1 energy. Against enemies where intent is hidden (Runic Dome), this is a gamble. In multi-enemy fights, target an enemy that is currently showing an Attack intent.

UPGRADE: +3 STR -> +4 STR. Worth upgrading for scaling fights.

SYNERGIES: Multi-hit attacks (Pummel, Twin Strike, Whirlwind) -- Strength applies per hit. Heavy Blade -- +3 Str becomes +9 damage (or +15 upgraded) due to the multiplier. Limit Break -- doubling Strength after Spot Weakness gives massive scaling. Long fights (bosses) where Strength compounds over many attack plays.

MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Guardian (predictable attack turns, long fight). Excellent vs Hexaghost (frequent attack turns in a long fight -- two applications of Spot Weakness+ gave +8 Str that scaled all remaining attacks). **Hexaghost Turn 2 timing**: Spot Weakness triggers on Turn 2 (Attack intent), but playing it alongside Inflame+ on Turn 2 (2E on setup) leaves only 1E for block against ~30 damage. If possible, play Spot Weakness on Turn 3+ or a later attack turn to avoid the Turn 2 blocking deficit. Good vs any boss with visible attack intents. Useless on turns where enemy is buffing/defending. Marginally useful vs Spheric Guardian (enemy alternates attack/defend, so triggers every other turn, but the Str gain may still be insufficient to break through Barricade block).

VICTORY PERFORMANCE: Spot Weakness+ was the primary Strength source in the first-ever win. Played in nearly every combat, it provided the +4 Str foundation that Limit Break doubled and Heavy Blade multiplied. Key applications:
- Bronze Automaton: Two SW+ applications across the fight gave +8 Str base, which Limit Break doubled to 16. This enabled Heavy Blade+ at 94 damage.
- Donu and Deca: SW+ at 0E (Snecko Eye) on Turn 3 gave +4 Str. Combined with Strength Potion (+2), total Str 6 was doubled to 12 by Limit Break on Turn 6 for the kill.
- In multi-enemy fights, targeting the attacking enemy for SW+ while other enemies buff is the correct play. Against Byrds + Chosen, SW+ on the attacking Byrd gave +4 Str before Immolate+ AOE hit both.

SNECKO EYE SYNERGY: At 1E base, Spot Weakness+ already costs only 1E. Under Confusion, it can cost 0E (25% chance), making it a free +4 Str. Even at 2-3E cost, the permanent Str gain is often worth it in boss fights. The card's value under Snecko Eye is "always good" regardless of the cost roll.
