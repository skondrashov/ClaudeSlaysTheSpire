# Fiend Fire (2E, Attack, Exhaust)
Exhausts ALL other cards in hand. Deals 7 damage per card exhausted to a single target.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: When burst will kill the target, when remaining cards are expendable (Strikes, status cards), or on free turns. Turns Slimed cards into 7 damage each.
DECISION POINTS: After playing Fiend Fire, your hand is EMPTY. Only play when: (1) the burst kills, (2) it's a free turn, or (3) remaining cards are expendable. With 4 other cards: 4x7=28 damage. With Vulnerable: 4x10=40. With +2 Str: 4x13=52.
SYNERGIES: Status cards (Slimed becomes 7 damage instead of dead draw), Strength (each exhausted card's damage scales), Vulnerable (each hit gets 50% bonus).
MATCHUPS: Slime Boss -- burst + Slimed exhaust. Mystic -- burst through block.
