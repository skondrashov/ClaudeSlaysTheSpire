# Armaments (1E, Skill, 5 block, upgrade a card in hand | +: 5 block, upgrade ALL cards in hand)
Block + card upgrade in one play. Upgraded version is transformative.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Armaments+ is one of the best cards in the game -- every turn it upgrades your entire hand. Over a long fight, every card in the deck gets upgraded. Unupgraded version is decent (5 block + 1 upgrade per turn).
DECISION POINTS: Play every turn in long fights where you can afford a setup turn. The upgrades are permanent for the combat. Prioritize upgrading Armaments itself first if given the choice (Armaments+ is enormously better than Armaments). Against Gremlin Nob, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage).

WHEN NOT TO PLAY: In DPS-race fights where every turn of damage matters, spending 1E on Armaments+ for 5 block + upgrades can cost you the fight. Specifically: against Book of Stabbing (incoming grows +7/turn, lost damage turns are fatal), Gremlin Leader (Rally scaling adds +3 Str to all enemies per cycle), or any fight where the enemy's damage scaling outpaces your block growth. In run 100, playing Armaments+ on turn 2 against Book of Stabbing (for 5 block + upgrades) instead of dealing ~30 damage cost a critical turn. By turn 5, Book was still alive at 53 HP and dealt lethal damage. The upgrades never mattered because the fight ended before upgraded cards could be drawn again.
UPGRADE: Single card upgrade -> ALL cards in hand. This is one of the highest-priority upgrades in the game.
