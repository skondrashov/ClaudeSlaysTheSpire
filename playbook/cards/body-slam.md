# Body Slam (1E, Attack, deal damage equal to your current block | +: 0E)

Converts block into damage. Scales with every block source in the deck.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: With Barricade. Body Slam+ costs 0E, meaning it is a completely free attack every time it cycles through the deck. With Barricade accumulating 100+ block across turns, Body Slam+ deals 100+ damage for 0E. This is the highest damage-per-energy card in the game when block is high. Without Barricade, block resets each turn, limiting Body Slam to whatever was generated that turn (typically 10-20 damage).

UPGRADE PRIORITY: CRITICAL. Body Slam+ costs 0E (down from 1E). This is one of the most impactful upgrades in the game because: (1) it costs nothing, so it can always be played, (2) it does not count as energy spent, leaving full energy for block generation. Without the upgrade, Body Slam costs 1E -- which is 1E that could have generated 5-11 more block instead.

SYNERGIES: Barricade (block persists, so Body Slam damage grows every turn), Entrench+ (doubling block doubles Body Slam damage), Impervious/Impervious+ (30-40 block = 30-40 damage from Body Slam for free), Corruption + Feel No Pain (every exhausted Skill adds block that Body Slam converts to damage), Metallicize (passive block accumulation that Body Slam converts to damage every cycle).

COMBO: The strongest observed turn was Body Slam+ with 146 block against the Time Eater for 146 damage at 0E cost. The Barricade + Corruption + Feel No Pain + Entrench + Body Slam engine defeated the Time Eater (480 HP boss) with this card as the primary damage source.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Decks without Barricade (block resets, so damage is low), Frail-heavy fights (reduced block generation reduces Body Slam damage).

ACT 1 TIER: LOW. Without Barricade, Body Slam is a weak card. Do not take it in Act 1 unless Barricade is already in the deck.
