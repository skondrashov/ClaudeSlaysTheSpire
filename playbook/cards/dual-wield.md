# Dual Wield (1E, Skill, Copy an Attack or Power in hand | +: Copy TWICE)

Creates copies of an Attack or Power card in your hand. Unupgraded: 1 copy. Upgraded: 2 copies. Copies go to your hand and can be played immediately.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: When the deck has a single high-impact card worth duplicating. Dual Wield+ on Immolate+ creates THREE Immolate+ in hand (original + 2 copies). At 28+ damage each, that is 84-102 AOE damage from a single Dual Wield+ play. This was the defining play of the first victory -- Turn 4 triple Immolate+ dealt 102 AOE to both Donu and Deca.

BEST TARGETS:
- **Immolate+**: 84-102 AOE across 3 copies. Best target in multi-enemy fights. Won the Donu and Deca boss fight.
- **Heavy Blade+**: Triple Heavy Blade+ at high Strength is overkill single-target but guarantees a kill.
- **Impervious**: Triple Impervious = 90-120 block in one turn. Ultimate defensive play.
- **Flame Barrier**: Multiple Flame Barriers stack block and return damage.

SNECKO EYE SYNERGY: Under Confusion, each copy gets a new random cost (0-3). With 2 copies from Dual Wield+, there is a ~44% chance at least one copy costs 0E and ~75% chance at least one costs 0-1E. This makes Dual Wield+ into a massive value engine under Snecko Eye.

DECISION POINTS: A Skill card, so it triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage (+2 Str) and Chosen's Hex (Dazed). In those fights, do not play it. In boss fights and tough hallway fights, Dual Wield+ on a high-value Attack is one of the strongest plays available. Timing: play mid-fight when the target card is in hand and you have energy to play the copies.

UPGRADE PRIORITY: Very high. 1 copy vs 2 copies is the difference between "good" and "game-winning." Unupgraded Dual Wield is mediocre; upgraded is S-tier with the right targets.
