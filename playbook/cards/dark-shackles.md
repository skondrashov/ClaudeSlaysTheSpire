# Dark Shackles (0E, Skill, enemy loses Strength for 1 turn, Exhaust | Colorless)

Emergency defensive tool. Reduces an enemy's Strength until the end of its next turn, then the Strength returns. Costs 0 energy, making it playable in any situation. Exhausts after use.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against high-damage single attacks where you need to survive one turn. Excellent against boss burst turns (The Champ's post-Execute attacks, Hexaghost Inferno). The 0E cost means it never competes with block or damage cards for energy.

DECISION POINTS: Because the Strength reduction is temporary (1 turn only), time it for the enemy's highest-damage turn. Using it on a low-damage turn wastes the exhaust. Against bosses with phase transitions (The Champ Execute), save it for the first big hit after the transition.

MATCHUPS:
- **The Champ**: Strong post-Execute when Champ has +8 Strength. Reducing that spike for one turn can be the difference between dying and stabilizing.
- **Boss fights generally**: 0E emergency button for any turn where incoming damage would be lethal.

SYNERGIES: Works in any deck since it costs 0E. Pairs with other Strength-reduction effects (Disarm) for layered defense.
