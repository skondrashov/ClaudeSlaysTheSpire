# Cleave (1E, Attack, ~8 AOE damage)
Cheap AOE damage to all enemies. Does NOT apply Vulnerable.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Multi-enemy fights for raw AOE damage. Good on free turns (turn 1 of Cultist fights) to soften all targets.
DECISION POINTS: Cleave vs Thunderclap -- Thunderclap applies Vulnerable but deals less damage. Usually Thunderclap is better for the Vulnerable.
