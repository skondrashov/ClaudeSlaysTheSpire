# Deus Ex Machina (Watcher Rare Skill, Unplayable)

When drawn, add 2 Miracles to your hand and exhaust this card. Upgraded: add 3 Miracles.

Deus Ex Machina cannot be played directly. It triggers automatically when drawn, providing 2-3 Miracles (each giving +1E, Retain, Exhaust) and then removing itself from the combat deck.

## Key Interactions

- **Effectively +2/+3 energy when drawn:** Each Miracle is +1E, and they Retain so you can use them on future turns.
- **Thins the deck:** Exhausts itself on draw, removing one card from the combat deck permanently. This improves future draw quality.
- **Miracle management applies:** Do not waste the generated Miracles. Hold them for turns where the energy matters. See miracle.md.

## Draft Priority

Medium-high. Free energy generation and deck thinning. Works well in any Watcher deck.
