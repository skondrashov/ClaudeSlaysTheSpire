# Power Through (1E, Skill, 15 block, adds 2 Wounds to hand)
Massive block (triple Defend) but pollutes hand with 2 unplayable Wounds.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Must-survive turns with massive incoming damage. When you have exhaust tools (True Grit+, Fiend Fire) to remove the Wounds afterward.
DECISION POINTS: Is 15 block worth 2 dead draws in future hands? In long fights without exhaust tools, the Wounds accumulate and ruin draws. In short fights or with exhaust, the 15 block is worth it.
