# Judgment (Watcher Uncommon Attack, 1E)

Base: If the enemy has 30 or less HP, set their HP to 0 (kill). Upgraded: 40 HP threshold.

Judgment is a conditional instant-kill card.

## Key Interactions

- **Finisher:** Does not deal damage normally. Only kills if the enemy is at or below the HP threshold.
- **Ignores block:** If the enemy has block but HP is at or below 30/40, Judgment still kills.
- **Useless above threshold:** Against enemies above 30/40 HP, Judgment does nothing. Do not waste 1E playing it on a full-health boss.

## When to Play

Save Judgment for when an enemy is near the HP threshold. Calculate total damage from other cards first -- if they can bring the enemy to 30/40 or below, play those attacks first, then finish with Judgment.

## Boss Fights

Judgment is excellent as a finisher in boss fights. Against Hexaghost, getting the boss to 30-40 HP then using Judgment avoids 1-2 additional turns of Burns damage.
