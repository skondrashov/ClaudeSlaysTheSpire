# Anger (0E, Attack, 6 damage | +: 8 damage)
Free damage card that copies itself into discard when played.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Always. 0-cost means it never competes with other plays. Adds a copy of itself to the discard pile each time played, so the deck gains more Anger copies over the fight. In long fights, Anger copies accumulate and provide consistent free damage every turn.

DECISION POINTS: Play every turn you draw it. There is no reason to hold Anger since it costs 0 energy. In multi-enemy fights, target the enemy closest to death for the free kill.

SYNERGIES:
- **Shuriken**: Each Anger played counts toward the 3-Attack threshold. Playing 3x Anger in one turn = free +1 Str.
- **Rage**: Rage gives 3 block per Attack played. Anger triggers Rage for free block while dealing free damage.
- **Demon Form**: Free attacks benefit greatly from Strength scaling. At +10 Str, each Anger deals 16 damage for 0 energy.
- **Vulnerable**: 50% damage boost on a free card is excellent value.

MATCHUPS: Great in Hexaghost (long fight, Anger copies accumulate, Strength scaling applies). Great vs Gremlin Nob (Attack card, avoids Enrage trigger from Skills). Solid everywhere due to 0 cost.

CAUTION: Anger adds copies to the discard pile, which inflates deck size over long fights. In decks relying on drawing specific cards (Demon Form, Impervious), the extra copies can dilute draws. This is rarely a problem since Anger replaces itself with damage, but be aware in very long fights with critical draw targets.
