# Vault (Watcher Rare Skill, 3E / 2E+)

Take an additional turn after this one. Exhaust.

Vault gives an extra turn, effectively doubling your output for one cycle. Extremely powerful but expensive.

## Key Interactions

- **Extra turn = extra draw + extra energy:** The additional turn has its own draw and energy. In fights where you need burst damage or extra setup time, Vault is game-changing.
- **Exhausts:** One use per combat. Use on the highest-impact turn.
- **Best on safe turns:** Play Vault when the enemy is buffing or debuffing (free turn). The extra turn lets you deal massive damage before the enemy acts again.

## Upgrade Priority

Very high. 2E instead of 3E makes Vault playable alongside other cards on the same turn.
