# Flex (0E, Skill, gain 2 Strength this turn | +: gain 4 Strength this turn)
Temporary Strength that disappears at end of turn.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Standalone, almost never. Temporary Str dilutes deck for one turn of marginal damage. Inflame (1E, permanent +2 Str) is strictly better in any fight lasting 2+ turns.

**EXCEPTION -- LIMIT BREAK+ COMBO**: Flex+ becomes a legitimate combo piece when Limit Break+ (no exhaust) is in the deck. Play Flex+ for +4 temporary Str, then play Limit Break+ to double total Str. The doubled amount is permanent -- only the original 4 temporary Str is lost at end of turn, leaving a net gain. Example from the second victory: Flex+ gave 4 Str (total ~5 with existing), Limit Break+ doubled to 10. At end of turn, lose 4 temporary = 6 permanent Str remaining. This turned Flex+ from a dead card into a key contributor to the boss kill.

DECISION POINTS: Do not take unless Limit Break+ is already in the deck or planned. If already in deck without Limit Break+, remove at next shop. With Limit Break+, Flex+ is worth keeping and playing on the same turn.

AVOID: Taking from card rewards or shops UNLESS Limit Break+ is present.
