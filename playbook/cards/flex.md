# Flex (0E, Skill, gain 2 Strength this turn | +: gain 4 Strength this turn)
Temporary Strength that disappears at end of turn.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Almost never. Temporary Str dilutes deck for one turn of marginal damage. Inflame (1E, permanent +2 Str) is strictly better in any fight lasting 2+ turns.

DECISION POINTS: Do not take. If already in deck, remove at next shop. If drawn, play turn 1 with 2+ attacks to follow; otherwise ignore.

AVOID: Taking from card rewards or shops.
