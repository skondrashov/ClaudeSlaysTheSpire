# Uppercut (2E, Attack, 13 damage + 1 Weak + 1 Vulnerable | +: 2 Weak + 2 Vulnerable)
Applies BOTH major debuffs in one card. The best single-card Artifact stripper in the Ironclad pool.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against single targets where both debuffs matter. Against Artifact enemies (strips 2 charges). Upgraded version applies 2 turns of each debuff, making it the most efficient debuff card at 2E.
DECISION POINTS: At 2E, is dual-debuff worth the cost? Against Spheric Guardian (3 Artifact), Uppercut strips 2 charges efficiently. Against Gremlin Nob, it's safe (Attack card). Upgraded Uppercut is high priority against any Artifact enemy.
UPGRADE: Extends debuff duration to 2 turns each (confirmed). Significant -- doubles debuff value and makes the 2E cost worthwhile in longer fights.
SYNERGIES: Artifact stripping (each debuff consumes 1 charge separately). Spot Weakness (Vulnerable from Uppercut amplifies Strength-boosted attacks). Demon Form (sustained Vulnerability keeps damage high as Strength ramps).
MATCHUPS: Spheric Guardian -- strips 2 of 3 Artifact charges in one play. Confirmed effective in Run 75: Uppercut+ stripped Artifact charges, enabling subsequent debuffs. However, even with successful Artifact stripping, Spheric Guardian's Barricade block can still be insurmountable if damage scaling is too slow.
