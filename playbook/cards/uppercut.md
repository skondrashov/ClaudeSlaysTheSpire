# Uppercut (2E, Attack, 13 damage + 1 Weak + 1 Vulnerable)
Applies BOTH major debuffs in one card.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Against single targets where both debuffs matter. Against Artifact enemies (strips 2 charges).
DECISION POINTS: At 2E, is dual-debuff worth the cost? Against Spheric Guardian (3 Artifact), Uppercut strips 2 charges efficiently. Against Gremlin Nob, it's safe (Attack card).
UPGRADE: Likely extends debuff duration to 2 turns each.
SYNERGIES: Artifact stripping (each debuff consumes 1 charge separately).
MATCHUPS: Spheric Guardian -- strips 2 of 3 Artifact charges in one play.
