# Feed (1E, Attack, 10 damage, Exhaust, +3 Max HP on kill)
One of the strongest Ironclad cards. Permanent Max HP scaling.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Whenever you can land the killing blow on an enemy. Each kill adds 3 Max HP permanently. Over a full run, Feed accumulates 12-18+ Max HP -- more than any single relic.
DECISION POINTS: Is the enemy low enough for Feed to kill? With Vulnerable: floor(10*1.5) = 15 damage. Plan other attacks to soften the target, then Feed for the kill. In multi-enemy fights, soften one target with other attacks then Feed kill it.
UPGRADE: Unknown exact values.
SYNERGIES: Headbutt (place Feed on top when an enemy is low HP -- guarantee the kill shot), Vulnerable (15 damage kill threshold).
MATCHUPS: Best targets are low-HP enemies: Byrds, Mystic, Gremlins, Cultists. Feed strategy: take it early for maximum kills over the run.
