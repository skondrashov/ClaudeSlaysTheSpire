# Inflame (1E, Power, gain 2 Strength permanently)
Cheap, permanent damage scaling. Every Attack deals 2 more damage for the entire fight.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Play as early as possible in long fights (bosses, elites) for maximum value. At 1E cost, extremely efficient.
DECISION POINTS: Is this turn a setup turn or a survival turn? On free turns (enemy buffing/debuffing), always play Inflame. Damage formula with Inflame + Weak: floor((base+2)*0.75). Powers do NOT trigger Gremlin Nob Enrage.
SYNERGIES: Reaper (Strength increases damage to ALL enemies = more healing), Heavy Blade (+2 Str becomes +6 damage, or +10 upgraded, due to multiplier), Whirlwind (each hit gains +2 damage), Twin Strike (+2 per hit = +4 total), every Attack card.
MATCHUPS: Guardian Defensive Mode -- Inflame is a Power, not an Attack, so no Sharp Hide retaliation. Bronze Automaton -- Inflame is a high-priority Stasis target. Orbs steal cards from hand, and losing Inflame removes permanent Strength scaling. Play Inflame immediately when drawn rather than holding it. Having two copies provides insurance against one being stolen.
