# Chill (0E, Skill, Channel 1 Frost per enemy | +: Innate)

Defect uncommon Skill. Channels 1 Frost orb for each enemy in the fight. Exhausts.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: In multi-enemy fights (3 Shapes, Darklings, Gremlin Gang), Chill channels 3 Frost orbs for 0E -- an enormous defensive burst. Even in single-target fights, 1 free Frost orb is acceptable. The 0E cost means it can always be played without energy conflict.

UPGRADE: Gains Innate (starts in hand). The upgrade guarantees Chill on Turn 1, providing immediate Frost defense. Useful upgrade if the card is already in the deck.

DECISION POINTS: Play Chill immediately on Turn 1 of multi-enemy fights for maximum Frost generation. In single-enemy fights, play it whenever a Frost orb is needed. Exhausts after use, so timing matters less than with reusable cards.
