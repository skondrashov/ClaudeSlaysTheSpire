# Carnage (2E, Attack, 20 damage, Ethereal | +: 28 damage, Ethereal)
Massive single-target burst. Must be played or it exhausts permanently.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Boss fights for big burst turns. Against Guardian Mode Shift (fast phase transitions). Any fight where 2E for 28+ damage is game-changing.
DECISION POINTS: Can you afford 2E on damage this turn, or do you need block? If drawn on a must-block turn, you either spend 2E on damage or lose Carnage permanently. This tension is manageable with enough block cards.
UPGRADE: 20->28 damage. With Vulnerable: 30->42. One of the highest single-card damage outputs. Top priority upgrade.
SYNERGIES: Headbutt (guarantee Carnage+ on top of draw pile for next turn -- used repeatedly for consistent 28-42 damage turns), Vulnerable (42 damage).
MATCHUPS: Guardian -- 42 Vuln damage triggers Mode Shift fast. Byrds -- 28 damage halved to ~14 nearly one-shots a Byrd.
