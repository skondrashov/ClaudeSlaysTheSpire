# Fear No Evil (Watcher Common Attack, 1E)

Base: 8 damage. If the enemy intends to attack, enter Calm. Upgraded: 11 damage.

Fear No Evil is a conditional Calm entry that doubles as an attack. When the enemy is attacking, it provides both damage and a safe stance transition.

## Key Interactions

- **Conditional Calm entry:** Only enters Calm when the target enemy has an ATTACK intent. On non-attack turns (BUFF, DEBUFF, DEFEND), it deals damage but does NOT change stance.
- **Leaves Wrath safely:** On attack turns, playing Fear No Evil in Wrath deals doubled damage AND exits to Calm (safe). This is ideal -- burst damage in Wrath followed by automatic safety.
- **Stance change triggers:** When it enters Calm, Flurry of Blows returns from discard. Play Flurry BEFORE Fear No Evil on attack turns.

## Play Order

On attack turns (enemy intending to attack):
1. Play Flurry of Blows first (goes to discard)
2. Play Fear No Evil (deals damage, enters Calm, Flurry returns to hand)
3. Play returned Flurry of Blows again

On non-attack turns: Fear No Evil is a simple 8/11 damage attack with no stance change. Flurry order does not matter.

## Upgrade Priority

Medium. +3 damage (8 to 11) is solid but not game-changing.
