# Just Lucky (Watcher Common Attack, 0E)

Base: 3 damage, gain 2 block, Scry 1. Upgraded: 4 damage, gain 3 block, Scry 1.

A free card that does a little of everything. Deals damage, provides block, and filters the draw pile.

## Key Interactions

- **Wave of the Hand synergy:** The 2-3 block triggers Wave of the Hand's Weak application. Playing Just Lucky after Wave of the Hand = free Weak application.
- **Free in any situation:** At 0E, Just Lucky is always playable. Play it before ending your turn for free damage, block, and draw pile filtering.
- **Scry 1:** Discard Burns or other bad cards from the top of the draw pile. Small but consistent deck filtering.
- **Shuriken / Kunai:** Counts as an Attack for relic triggers.

## Play Order

Play after Wave of the Hand if Wave is active (to trigger Weak). Otherwise, play before or after other cards as a free bonus.
