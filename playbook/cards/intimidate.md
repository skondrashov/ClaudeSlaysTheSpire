# Intimidate (0E, Skill, apply 1 Weak to ALL enemies, Exhaust | +: apply 2 Weak to ALL enemies, Exhaust)
Free mass Weak. Zero downside.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: ALWAYS play when drawn. 0 energy cost means it never competes with other cards. Reduces all enemy attack damage by 25% for 1 turn (2 turns upgraded).
DECISION POINTS: Play immediately on draw. There is zero reason to hold this card. Against Gremlin Nob and Chosen, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage/Hex).
UPGRADE: 1 Weak -> 2 Weak. Doubles the duration of damage reduction. Worth upgrading when the deck has few other debuff sources.
SYNERGIES: Letter Opener (every Skill played deals 5 damage -- Intimidate is free 5 damage + mass Weak). Corruption (Intimidate becomes free AND exhausts, which it already does -- under Corruption there is zero cost). Feel No Pain (exhaust triggers 3 block).
MATCHUPS: Guardian -- Weak reduces 32-damage attack to 24. Multi-enemy fights -- affects all enemies for free. Gremlin Leader -- mass Weak on all gremlins + Leader reduces Rally-boosted damage significantly. Spheric Guardian -- contributes to debuff strategy after Artifact is stripped.
