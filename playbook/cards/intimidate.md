# Intimidate (0E, Skill, apply 1 Weak to ALL enemies, Exhaust)
Free mass Weak. Zero downside.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: ALWAYS play when drawn. 0 energy cost means it never competes with other cards. Reduces all enemy attack damage by 25% for 1 turn.
DECISION POINTS: Play immediately on draw. There is zero reason to hold this card. Against Gremlin Nob and Chosen, do NOT play (Skill triggers Enrage/Hex).
SYNERGIES: Letter Opener (every Skill played deals 5 damage -- Intimidate is free 5 damage + mass Weak).
MATCHUPS: Guardian -- Weak reduces 32-damage attack to 24. Multi-enemy fights -- affects all enemies for free.
