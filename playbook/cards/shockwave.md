# Shockwave (2E, Skill, applies Weak + Vulnerable to ALL enemies, Exhaust | +: 3 turns each)
Mass debuff. One of the most powerful cards in the game. Exhausts after use — but returns to your deck next fight. Play it every fight you draw it.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Multi-enemy fights and boss fights. Mass Weak reduces all incoming damage by 25%. Mass Vulnerable boosts all attacks by 50%.
DECISION POINTS: TIMING IS CRITICAL. Play on the FIRST ATTACK TURN of multi-enemy fights. For 3 Cultists, that's turn 2 (turn 1 is free — all Cultists use Ritual). Never save for turn 4+ -- by then enemies have already dealt massive unmitigated damage. Preventing damage with early Weak far exceeds healing from Reaper. Priority: Shockwave+ on first attack turn > Reaper > single-target damage. NEVER hold Shockwave "for a harder fight" -- exhaust resets between fights, so you'll have it again next fight regardless. If not drawn by the first attack turn, surviving until it's drawn becomes the priority — invest in block and draw cards to find it faster.
UPGRADE: 2->3 turns of each debuff. Massive improvement -- 50% more duration. High priority.
SYNERGIES: Multi-enemy fights (debuffs apply to ALL), Dropkick (Vulnerable on all targets = free cycling on any of them).
MATCHUPS: Hexaghost -- Weak reduces 7x6=42 Inferno to 7x4=28. MANDATORY for Hexaghost survival. Gremlin Leader -- mass debuff on Leader + all gremlins. 3 Cultists -- play turn 2, not turn 4. Slime Boss split -- mass debuff on both slimes.
