# Overclock (0E, Skill, Draw 2 + Add 1 Burn to discard | +: Draw 3 + Add 1 Burn to discard)

Defect uncommon Skill. Draw 2 cards and add a Burn to the discard pile (Draw 3 upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Free cycling card -- draws 2/3 cards for 0E. The Burn added to discard is a minor downside (2 unblocked damage when drawn and not played). In decks with Fire Breathing (deals damage when status cards are drawn) or Evolve (draws extra cards on status draw), the Burn becomes upside.

UPGRADE: Draw 2 -> Draw 3. High upgrade priority. The extra draw significantly improves the card.

DECISION POINTS: Play Overclock freely when cycling is needed. The Burn dilutes the deck slightly but is manageable in small decks. In large decks, the Burn dilution is negligible.

NOB WARNING: Overclock is a Skill card. Triggers Gremlin Nob Enrage.
