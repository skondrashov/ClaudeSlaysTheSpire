# Aggregate (1E, Skill, Gain Energy equal to floor(cards in draw pile / 4) | +: Gain Energy equal to floor(cards in draw pile / 3))

Defect uncommon Skill. Generates energy based on the number of cards remaining in the draw pile.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Energy burst card that enables big turns. With 12 cards in the draw pile, Aggregate generates 3 Energy (12/4=3). This effectively makes the turn a 6-Energy turn (3 base + 3 from Aggregate), allowing multiple expensive cards to be played. Best played early in the turn before drawing cards reduces the draw pile count.
DECISION POINTS: Play at the start of your turn when the draw pile is largest. Every 4 cards drawn or exhausted before playing Aggregate reduces the Energy gain by 1. In a 17-card deck drawing 7 (Snecko Eye), the draw pile has ~10 cards on Turn 1, yielding 2 Energy (10/4=2). On Turn 2, after discarding and reshuffling, the pile is larger.
UPGRADE: Divisor changes from 4 to 3. With 12 cards in draw pile: 3E unupgraded vs 4E upgraded. Significant improvement.

SNECKO EYE SYNERGY: Snecko Eye draws 7 cards per turn instead of 5, which slightly reduces the draw pile size. However, the extra energy from Aggregate fuels the extra cards drawn. When Snecko Eye randomizes expensive cards to high costs, Aggregate provides the energy to play them anyway.

TIMING: Play first each turn (before other draw effects reduce the draw pile). Do NOT play Coolheaded or Compile Driver before Aggregate -- each card drawn shrinks the draw pile and reduces Aggregate's output.
