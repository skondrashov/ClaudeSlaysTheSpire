# Apotheosis (2E, Colorless Skill, upgrade ALL cards in your hand, draw pile, and discard pile for the rest of combat | +: 1E, Exhaust)

Mass upgrade for the entire deck. The single most impactful card when upgrades are scarce.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: With Fusion Hammer (cannot Smith at rest sites). Apotheosis is the ONLY way to upgrade cards when Fusion Hammer is equipped. It upgrades everything: Barricade 3E to 2E, Corruption 3E to 2E, Impervious 30 to 40 block, Feel No Pain 3 to 4 block per exhaust, Immolate 21 to 28 damage, and every other card simultaneously. The value of upgrading 15-20 cards at once is enormous.

DECISION POINTS: At 2E (or 1E upgraded), Apotheosis is best played on a free turn (enemy buffing/debuffing) early in a fight. The earlier it is played, the more turns benefit from the upgrades. In boss fights, Turn 1-2 is ideal. Under Corruption, Apotheosis costs 0E and exhausts -- the best possible outcome (free upgrades + Feel No Pain trigger).

SYNERGIES: Fusion Hammer (the #1 synergy -- makes Apotheosis the sole upgrade path), Corruption (Apotheosis becomes 0E under Corruption), Feel No Pain (exhausting Apotheosis under Corruption triggers FNP block).

SOURCE: Colorless card from events (Sensory Stone "Recall" option). Cannot be obtained from normal card rewards.

OBSERVED: Against The Maw, Apotheosis upgraded all cards on Turn 3, transforming Metallicize from +3 to +4 block/turn, Impervious from 30 to 40 block, and Feel No Pain from 3 to 4 block per exhaust. The cumulative value over 8 turns was equivalent to 40+ additional block and 30+ additional damage.
