# Masterful Stab (Silent Attack, 0E base)

12 damage for 0 energy. Cost increases by 1 for each HP lost in combat. Upgraded: 16 damage.

EVALUATION: Excellent in decks that take zero damage. Dead weight in long fights with chip damage.

SYNERGIES:
- **Footwork+ / high Dex builds**: When every fight is zero damage taken, Masterful Stab stays at 0E permanently. Free 12 damage per deck cycle is outstanding.
- **Setup+**: Can reset Masterful Stab's cost to 0E by putting it into draw pile. Useful recovery when cost has escalated, but wastes a card play.
- **Short fights**: Hallway fights where zero damage is maintained throughout. Confirmed: multiple hallway fights with free 12 damage per cycle.

ANTI-SYNERGIES:
- **Hexaghost**: Burns deal 2-4 unblockable damage per turn at end of turn. Even with perfect blocking against Hexaghost's attacks, Burns raise Masterful Stab's cost by 2-4 per turn. By Turn 12, the cost reached 4-7E, completely unplayable. In a 23-turn fight, the card contributed damage only in the first 10 turns before becoming dead weight.
- **Any fight with unavoidable chip damage**: Poison on the player, Burns, Decay curse, Thorns reflection, Brutality self-damage. Each point of self-damage raises the cost permanently for that combat.
- **Long fights generally**: Even 1-2 damage per turn over 10 turns raises the cost to 10-12E.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- Take Masterful Stab when the deck reliably achieves zero damage taken in hallway fights AND the Act 1 boss is NOT Hexaghost.
- If Hexaghost is the boss, Masterful Stab is a trap -- it will contribute for the first few turns but become unplayable as Burns accumulate. Prefer cards with fixed energy costs.
- If already holding Masterful Stab against Hexaghost, do not rely on it as a primary damage source. Budget damage from other attack cards.
