# Vigilance (Watcher Starter Skill, 2E)

Base: 8 block, enter Calm stance. Upgraded: 12 block.

Vigilance is the Watcher's primary Calm entry card and Wrath exit. Playing it gives block and switches to Calm, where you take normal damage and gain +2 energy when you leave Calm next turn.

## Key Interactions

- **Exits Wrath when played in Wrath:** This is its most critical use -- ending Wrath before the enemy attacks. Without an exit from Wrath, doubled incoming damage is lethal.
- **Enters Calm:** Gain +2 energy when you later leave Calm (via Eruption, Crescendo, or other Wrath-entry cards).
- **Stance change triggers:** Entering Calm returns Flurry of Blows from discard to hand. Play Flurry of Blows BEFORE Vigilance for the same reason as Eruption.

## Play Order

**CRITICAL:** If Flurry of Blows is in hand, play it BEFORE Vigilance. The stance change triggers Flurry's return from discard. If Flurry is already in hand, playing it first sends it to discard where it can return.

## When to Play

- Emergency Wrath exit when enemy is about to attack.
- Setting up Calm for the energy loop on a safe turn (enemy buffing or debuffing).
- When you need block and are currently in Wrath.

## Upgrade Priority

Medium-high. 12 block (from 8) makes it significantly better for surviving Inferno-type multi-hit attacks and provides a meaningful block buffer when exiting Wrath.
