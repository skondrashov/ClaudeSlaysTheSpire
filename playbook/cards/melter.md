# Melter (1E, Attack, 10 damage, remove enemy Block | +: 14 damage, remove enemy Block)

Defect common Attack. Deals 10 damage (14 upgraded) and removes all Block from the target enemy before dealing damage.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Bypasses enemy Block entirely. Against enemies with Block (Spheric Guardian, Gremlin Leader's Shield Gremlin, enemies using Defensive Stance), Melter ensures all damage is dealt to HP. Without Melter, damage into Block is wasted.

UPGRADE: 10 -> 14 damage. Moderate upgrade priority. The Block removal is the main value regardless of upgrade status.

DECISION POINTS: Play Melter before other attacks on enemies with Block. The Block removal applies first, then the damage hits HP directly. If the enemy has 15 Block, a normal 10-damage attack does 0 HP damage, but Melter removes the 15 Block and then deals 10 HP damage.

SYNERGY: Good with Barrage in fights where enemies gain Block (Gremlin Leader, Spheric Guardian). Melter strips Block, then Barrage's full damage hits HP.
