# Searing Blow (2E, Attack, 12 damage)
Can be upgraded multiple times (unique mechanic). Each upgrade adds ~4 damage. Worth building around only if upgraded 3+ times early.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: If upgraded 3+ times, becomes a primary damage source. Otherwise, a mediocre 2E attack.
DECISION POINTS: Only take if you plan to upgrade at every rest site. At 1-2 upgrades, worse than Carnage or Pommel Strike.
