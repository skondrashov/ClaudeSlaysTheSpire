# Study (Watcher Uncommon Power, 2E)

At the end of your turn, add an Insight to your draw pile. Upgraded: add Insight to hand instead.

Insight is a 0E card that draws 2 cards and Retains.

## Key Interactions

- **Slow value engine:** Generates one Insight per turn. Each Insight draws 2 cards when played. Over a long fight, this is significant card advantage.
- **2E cost is steep:** On turns with incoming damage, spending 2E on a Power that does nothing immediately is risky.

## Play Order

Play on a safe turn (enemy not attacking). The 2E cost makes it unsuitable for defensive turns.

## Draft Priority

Low-medium. Study is too slow for most hallway fights. Only valuable in long boss fights where the cumulative draw advantage matters.
