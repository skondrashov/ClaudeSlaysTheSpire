# Beam Cell (0E, Attack, 3 damage + 1 Vulnerable | +: 4 damage + 2 Vulnerable)

Defect common Attack. Deals 3 damage and applies 1 Vulnerable (4 damage and 2 Vulnerable upgraded).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: A 0-cost Vulnerable applicator. Vulnerable increases all damage by 50% for its duration, making Beam Cell an enabler for Barrage burst turns. At 0E, it fits into any turn without energy conflict. The damage is negligible -- the Vulnerable application is the entire value.

UPGRADE: 3 -> 4 damage, 1 -> 2 Vulnerable. The extra Vulnerable turn is significant, keeping enemies Vulnerable longer for more Barrage/Sweeping Beam damage.

DECISION POINTS: Play Beam Cell before Barrage or other damage cards to maximize Vulnerable amplification. In short fights, even 1 turn of Vulnerable on a Barrage+ with 5 orbs adds 17 bonus damage (35 * 0.5).
