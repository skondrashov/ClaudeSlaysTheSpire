# Thunderclap (1E, Attack, 4 AOE + 1 Vulnerable ALL | +: 7 AOE + 1 Vulnerable ALL)
Cheap mass Vulnerable. The best setup card for multi-enemy fights.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Always in multi-enemy fights. Turn 1 opener to set up 50% more damage on everything. Strips 1 Flight per Byrd hit and 1 Artifact per enemy.
DECISION POINTS: Against single enemies, worse than Bash+ (1 turn Vulnerable vs 3 turns). Against multiple enemies, one of the best cards in the game.
UPGRADE: 4->7 AOE damage. With Vulnerable on targets: ~6->10 damage each. Significant AOE improvement. Worth upgrading when it's a regular play.
SYNERGIES: Dropkick (sets up free cycling on all Vulnerable targets), all Attack cards (50% more damage to all enemies).
MATCHUPS: Byrds -- strips 1 Flight from each + applies Vulnerable. Sentries -- strips 1 Artifact from each. Slime Boss split -- mass Vulnerable on both slimes.
