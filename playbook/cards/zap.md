# Zap (0E, Skill, Channel 1 Lightning | +: Channel 1 Lightning for 0E)

Defect starter card. Channels 1 Lightning orb for 0 energy.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Free orb channel every time it's drawn. Lightning orb deals 3 passive damage at end of turn (or 8 on evoke). At 0E, it never competes with other plays for energy.
DECISION POINTS: Always play Zap when drawn -- it costs 0E and generates value. The only exception is Gremlin Nob, where Zap is a Skill and triggers Enrage (+2 Str). Against Nob, do NOT play Zap.
UPGRADE: No functional change at base (still 0E, still channels 1 Lightning). Upgrade priority is low compared to other Defect cards.

NOB WARNING: Zap is a Skill card. It triggers Gremlin Nob's Enrage. Defect's starting deck already has 7 Skills vs 3 Attacks -- Zap being a Skill (not an Attack) is a key reason Nob is the Defect's worst elite matchup.
