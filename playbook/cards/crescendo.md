# Crescendo (Watcher Common Skill, 1E / 0E+, Exhaust)

Base: Enter Wrath. Exhaust. Upgraded: 0E cost.

Crescendo is a cheap Wrath entry that exhausts after use. It is NOT a permanent engine card like Eruption -- it is a one-time burst enabler.

## Key Interactions

- **Enters Wrath for cheap:** At 1E (0E upgraded), this is the most efficient Wrath entry. Combined with Calm exit energy, it enables explosive burst turns.
- **Exhausts:** Only usable once per combat. Plan your Wrath entries around having either Crescendo OR Eruption available.
- **Stance change triggers:** Flurry of Blows returns from discard. Play Flurry BEFORE Crescendo.

## Play Order

**CRITICAL:** Play Flurry of Blows and any other stance-trigger cards BEFORE Crescendo. Crescendo enters Wrath, triggering all "on stance change" effects. Cards must be in the discard pile to be returned.

## When to Play

- Burst turns where you have multiple attacks ready and need Wrath for doubled damage.
- Free turns (enemy not attacking) where entering Wrath is safe because you plan to exit via Vigilance or similar before the next attack turn.
- Do NOT play Crescendo if you have no way to exit Wrath and the enemy is attacking next turn.

## Upgrade Priority

Medium. 0E is better than 1E, but since it exhausts, the upgrade only matters once per combat.
