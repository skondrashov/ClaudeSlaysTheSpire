# Donu and Deca (Act 3 Boss)

Two enemies. Donu has 250 HP. Deca has 250 HP. Combined 500 HP. Each starts with Artifact 1 (blocks first debuff applied to that enemy). Fight won in 6 turns on first encounter.

## Mechanics

- **Donu** buffs Strength for both enemies. Kill Donu FIRST to stop Strength scaling.
- **Deca** applies debuffs (block-based, Dazed). Lower priority kill target.
- Combined incoming damage starts around 20/turn and scales as Donu buffs Strength.
- Both enemies can have block (observed 8-16 block on various turns).
- Fight length: 6-10 turns depending on deck power. With Snecko Eye and Strength scaling, 6 turns is achievable.

## Winning Strategy (confirmed)

1. **AOE is king.** Immolate+ (28-34 with Str) hits BOTH bosses every cast. Three Immolate+ in one turn dealt 102 AOE damage. Cleave and other AOE cards contribute meaningfully because every point of AOE does double duty.
2. **Focus fire Donu.** All single-target attacks go to Donu. AOE handles Deca passively. Donu dies first, stopping the Strength scaling that would make the fight unwinnable in long games.
3. **Strength scaling is the finisher.** Spot Weakness+ (+4 Str) into Limit Break (double to 12 Str) enables Heavy Blade (14 + 36 = 50 damage) as a lethal finisher. The Str investment pays off in turns 5-6 when both enemies are in kill range.
4. **Snecko Eye is exceptional.** Drawing 7 cards/turn with random costs means high-cost power cards (Immolate+ at 2E, Heavy Blade at 2E, Impervious at 2E) frequently cost 0-1E. Multiple 0-cost Immolate+ plays in the boss fight were the difference between winning and losing.
5. **Dual Wield+ on Immolate+ is devastating.** Copying Immolate+ twice creates three copies in hand. If Snecko gives favorable costs, playing all three deals 100+ AOE in a single turn.

## Key Cards

- **Immolate+**: Best card in the fight. 28-34 AOE per play. Played 5+ times across 6 turns.
- **Heavy Blade+**: 5x Str multiplier finisher. At Str 6: 44 damage. At Str 12: 74 damage. Killed Donu through 16 block.
- **Dual Wield+**: Copies Immolate+ for triple AOE turns. The card that broke the fight open on Turn 4.
- **Limit Break**: Doubled Str from 6 to 12 on the kill turn. Worth saving for the final push.
- **Spot Weakness+**: +4 Str on attacking enemies. Deca's attack turns are the window.
- **Flame Barrier**: 12 block + 4 return damage per hit. Good against multi-hit attack patterns.
- **Evolve**: Converts Burns from Immolate into card draws. Essential in a long fight with repeated Immolate plays.

## Preparation Checklist

1. AOE damage -- Immolate+ is the single best card. Thunderclap+ and Cleave contribute.
2. Strength scaling -- Spot Weakness+, Inflame, Limit Break. Heavy Blade to leverage it.
3. Enter at 70%+ HP (ideally full 80/80). Combined incoming is 20-32/turn.
4. Block density for 6-10 turns. Impervious, Flame Barrier, Metallicize all useful.
5. Potions: Strength Potion compounds with Limit Break. Duplication Potion on Flame Barrier doubles block. Liquid Memories retrieves Immolate+ from discard for another play.

## Potion Timing

- **Strength Potion Turn 2**: +2 Str permanent for combat. Compounds with every attack for remaining turns.
- **Duplication Potion Turn 5**: Double Flame Barrier for 24 block when incoming peaks.
- **Liquid Memories Turn 5**: Retrieve Immolate+ from discard at 0E from Snecko.
- **Toy Ornithopter**: Each potion use heals 5 HP. Three potions = 15 HP healed during the fight.

## Turn-by-Turn Reference (from first victory)

- Turn 1: Immolate+ (0E!) for 28 AOE. Setup Evolve. Focus Donu with single-target. 56 to Donu, 28 to Deca.
- Turn 2: Strength Potion (+2 Str). Headbutt+ to top-deck Immolate+. Heavy Blade 20 to Donu.
- Turn 3: Spot Weakness+ (+4 Str = 6 total). Immolate+ 34 AOE.
- Turn 4: Dual Wield+ copied Immolate+ twice. THREE Immolate+ for 102 AOE. Rampage 19. Donu at 5 HP.
- Turn 5: Heavy Blade (0E!) killed Donu through 16 block. Duplication Potion doubled Flame Barrier. Liquid Memories retrieved Immolate+ at 0E. 69 total to Deca.
- Turn 6: Limit Break doubled Str to 12. Anger+ 20. Heavy Blade 50. Deca dead. VICTORY.
