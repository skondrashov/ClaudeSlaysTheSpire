# Bosses

## Act 1
- [Slime Boss](slime-boss.md) — HP: 140. Splits at 50% into Acid Slime L + Spike Slime L.
- [The Guardian](the-guardian.md) — HP: 240. Mode Shift between offensive/defensive. Sharp Hide in defensive mode.
- [Hexaghost](hexaghost.md) — HP: 250. Burns accumulation. Inferno multi-hit. Long fight.

## Act 2
- [The Collector](the-collector.md) — HP: 282. Spawns Torch Head minions. Turn 4 triple debuff.
- [Bronze Automaton](bronze-automaton.md) — HP: 300. Hyper Beam (45 damage). Orb Stasis steals cards. Artifact 3.
- [The Champ](the-champ.md) — HP: 420. Two-phase fight. Anger clears debuffs + gains 6 Str at 50% HP.

## Act 3
- [Awakened One](awakened-one.md) — HP: 300+300. Two phases. Curiosity (+2 Str per Power played). Regenerate 10 in Phase 2.
- [Donu and Deca](donu-and-deca.md) — HP: 250+250. Donu buffs Str, Deca adds Dazed + Block. Artifact 2 each.
- [Time Eater](time-eater.md) — HP: 456. Time Warp every 12 cards played. Haste heals at 50% HP. DEFEATED 2x (Ironclad Barricade engine, Silent Shiv burst).
