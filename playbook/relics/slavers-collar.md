# Slaver's Collar

EFFECT: Gain 1 additional Energy during Elite and Boss combats (4 Energy instead of 3).

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: The extra energy in elite and boss fights is significant -- it allows playing an additional card per turn in the hardest fights. A 4E turn means you can play Bash+ (2E) + Rampage (1E) + Defend (1E) instead of choosing between offense and defense. Does NOT help in hallway fights, where you still have 3E.

VALUE: High for elite-heavy paths. If your route includes 2-3 elites and the Act boss, the relic provides value in 3-4 of the hardest fights. Less valuable if avoiding elites.

SYNERGY: Pairs well with expensive cards (Bludgeon at 3E, Reaper at 2E) that normally crowd out other plays. With Slaver's Collar, you can play Reaper (2E) + a 1E block card + a 1E attack in the same turn during elite/boss fights.
