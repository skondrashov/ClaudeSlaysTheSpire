# Eternal Feather
RARITY: Common
EFFECT: For every 5 cards in your deck, heal 3 HP after combat.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Supplements Burning Blood's flat 6 HP/fight. At 15 cards: +9 HP/fight (total 15 with Burning Blood). At 20 cards: +12 HP/fight (total 18). At 25 cards: +15 HP/fight (total 21). Rewards larger decks, which conflicts with typical deck-thinning strategy. Do not take extra cards just to increase Eternal Feather healing -- deck quality matters more than marginal HP gains.

EVALUATION: Mediocre. The healing scales with deck size, but larger decks draw key cards less reliably. In a typical 15-20 card deck, the healing is modest (9-12 HP). Compare to Meat on the Bone (12 HP if below 50%) or Burning Blood (6 HP flat). Only valuable when the run already has a larger deck and no better relic options.
