# Ancient Tea Set
EFFECT: Gain 2 energy on turn 1 if the previous room was a Rest site (5 energy turn 1 instead of 3).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: ALWAYS rest/smith at the rest site before a boss when you have this relic. The 2E bonus on turn 1 is worth more than most upgrades. With 5E turn 1: set up Metallicize + Dark Embrace + play damage, or Bash+ + 3 Attacks for massive burst. Don't waste the energy -- plan to spend ALL 5E on turn 1.
