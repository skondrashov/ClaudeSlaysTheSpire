# Black Star (Boss Relic)

EFFECT: Elites drop 2 relics instead of 1.

WHY IT'S STRONG: Doubles relic acquisition from elite fights. In a run that fights 3-4 elites, Black Star provides 3-4 extra relics -- each of which could be a game-changing uncommon or rare relic. The value is proportional to the number of remaining elites the player can reasonably fight.

DECISION FRAMEWORK: Best taken at the end of Act 1 (before Act 2 and 3 elites). Weaker if taken after Act 2 (only Act 3 elites remain, and there are fewer of them). Requires actively routing through elites to realize value -- taking Black Star and avoiding elites wastes the relic slot.

RISK: Boss relics replace the starter relic (Burning Blood, Ring of the Snake, Cracked Core). Losing the starter relic's sustain/utility must be weighed against Black Star's long-term relic advantage. For the Defect, losing Cracked Core (channel 1 Lightning at combat start) is relatively low-cost compared to losing Burning Blood.

CONFIRMED: Present in the first Defect victory. Black Star provided extra relics from elite fights that strengthened the deck significantly through Acts 2 and 3.
