# Mummified Hand (Uncommon Relic)

Whenever you play a Power, a random card in your hand costs 0 for the turn.

EVALUATION: Excellent in Power-heavy decks. With Inflame, Metallicize, Barricade, Evolve, each Power play enables a free card -- effectively +1 energy per Power played. In a deck with 4-5 Powers, this generates 4-5 free plays per combat, representing a massive energy advantage over the course of a fight.

BEST SYNERGIES:
- **Multiple Power cards**: The more Powers in the deck, the more free plays generated. A turn with 2 Power plays gives 2 free cards.
- **Barricade + Metallicize setup turns**: Playing Barricade (2E) triggers Mummified Hand, enabling a free Defend+ or Strike+ on the setup turn. This partially offsets Barricade's setup cost.
- **Inflame+/Evolve+ in long fights**: Powers played on BUFF/DEFEND free turns give free attacks or blocks, converting setup turns into hybrid offense/defense turns.
- **Immolate+**: A free Immolate+ (normally 2E) is 28-31 damage for zero energy. Devastating when triggered.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Decks with 0-1 Powers (trigger is too rare to matter). Corruption (Skills cost 0 already, so the free card is less impactful if it hits a Skill).

CONFIRMED DOMINANT: In a deck with Barricade+, 2x Metallicize, Inflame+, Evolve+, Mummified Hand enabled 2-4 free plays per turn on setup turns, resulting in zero-damage hallway fights against Act 3 enemies (including The Maw at 300 HP). The relic scales in value as more Powers are added to the deck.
