# Torii
EFFECT: Whenever you would receive 5 or less unblocked damage, reduce it to 1.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Exceptional against chip damage. Burns (2 damage each) become 1 damage. Multi-hit attacks where each hit is small (5 or less after block) are drastically reduced. Against Hexaghost, Torii halves Burns attrition. Against Sentries, small hits are reduced to 1. Does NOT help against large single hits (Guardian 32, Hexaghost Inferno hits at 6 each are already above 5 per hit without Weak).
