# Necronomicon (Book Relic, from Cursed Tome event)

EFFECT: The first Attack card costing 2 or more energy played each turn is played a second time automatically. Adds a Necronomicurse to the deck (unplayable, cannot be removed except by special means).

WHY IT'S STRONG: Doubles the first expensive Attack each turn for free. This effectively doubles damage output on the best Attack of each turn. Cards that have secondary effects (channeling orbs, applying debuffs, drawing cards) trigger those effects twice as well.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **Cold Snap (when 2+ cost):** Plays twice = 12 damage + channels 2 Frost orbs. Both damage and the orb channel happen twice.
- **Barrage (2E base):** Always triggers Necronomicon at base cost. With 5 orbs, Barrage plays twice for 40 damage total (50 upgraded). One of the highest single-card damage outputs in the game.
- **Strike+ (when 2+ cost via Snecko Eye):** Even basic Strikes become doubled when Snecko randomizes their cost to 2+.
- **Any Attack with secondary effects:** The entire card effect replays, not just the damage. Draw effects draw again, channel effects channel again, debuff effects apply again.

SNECKO EYE SYNERGY: Snecko Eye randomizes all card costs to 0-3. Cards that normally cost 0-1 energy now have a chance of costing 2+, triggering Necronomicon. This dramatically increases the number of Attacks that can be doubled each turn. Conversely, cards that normally cost 2+ (like Barrage) may roll 0-1 and NOT trigger Necronomicon. The variance works out favorably because Snecko Eye draws 7 cards -- with more cards in hand, the chance of at least one Attack costing 2+ is very high.

NECRONOMICURSE: The curse is an unplayable card that clogs the hand. It cannot be removed at shops. In a Snecko Eye deck drawing 7 cards, the curse is a minor nuisance (1 dead card out of 7). In a standard 5-draw deck, it is more impactful. Manage it by keeping the deck lean so the curse appears less frequently.

DECISION FRAMEWORK: Take Necronomicon from Cursed Tome when:
- The deck has multiple 2+ cost Attack cards (Barrage, Carnage, Bludgeon, etc.)
- Snecko Eye is active (maximizes trigger chance across all Attacks)
- HP can afford the Cursed Tome cost (16 HP total)
Avoid when:
- The deck is entirely 0-1 cost Attacks with no way to increase costs
- HP is critical and cannot afford the 16 HP event cost

ONCE PER TURN: Necronomicon triggers only on the FIRST 2+ cost Attack each turn. Plan the turn so the highest-value Attack is played first to receive the doubling. Do not waste the trigger on a low-impact Attack when a high-impact one is in hand.
