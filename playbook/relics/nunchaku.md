# Nunchaku (Common Relic)

EFFECT: Every 10th Attack card played, gain 1 Energy.

WHY IT'S GOOD: In Attack-heavy decks, provides a reliable energy boost every few turns. With Snecko Eye drawing 7 cards/turn and 4-5 of them being Attacks, Nunchaku triggers every 2-3 turns. The bonus energy enables playing one more card on the trigger turn, which compounds damage output.

SYNERGIES: Attack-heavy decks (Anger adds copies, increasing Attack density). Snecko Eye (more cards drawn = more Attacks played per turn). Multi-hit attacks like Pummel or Whirlwind count each individual play, not each hit, toward the counter.

EVALUATION: Solid common relic. Not flashy but reliable. In the victory run, Nunchaku triggered multiple times per boss fight, enabling extra plays on critical turns. The counter persists across combats.
