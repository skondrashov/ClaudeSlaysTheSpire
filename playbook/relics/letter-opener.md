# Letter Opener
EFFECT: Whenever you play a Skill card, deal 5 damage to a random enemy.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Turns every Defend into Defend + 5 damage. In fights where you play 2-3 Skills per turn, adds 10-15 free damage. Over a 12-turn fight: ~40-120 free damage. Conflicts with Gremlin Nob (can't play Skills) and Chosen (Skills trigger Hex). Synergizes with Intimidate (0E Skill = free 5 damage + Weak).
