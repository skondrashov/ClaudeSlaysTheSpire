# Gremlin Horn (Uncommon Relic)

Whenever an enemy dies, gain 1 Energy and draw 1 card.

Triggers once per enemy death. In multi-enemy fights, each kill refunds 1E and draws 1 card, enabling explosive chain turns where killing one enemy fuels killing the next.

VALUE: Transforms multi-enemy fights from resource-draining attrition into snowball opportunities. Against 4 Gremlins, killing 3 provides +3E and +3 cards -- enough to play an entire extra turn's worth of actions. Against Slime Boss post-split, each Large Slime death into Medium Slimes provides energy and draw to handle the expanding board.

SYNERGIES:
- **AOE damage** (Thunderclap, Cleave, Whirlwind) -- weakens multiple enemies, triggering chain kills.
- **Slime Boss split** -- kills during the split phase refund energy for handling the new slimes.
- **Any multi-enemy fight** -- 3 Cultists, Gremlins, Slavers, Centurion+Mystic.

WHEN TO TAKE: Always. No downside. Even in single-enemy fights, it does nothing harmful. In multi-enemy fights (which are the most common dangerous encounters in Act 2), it is one of the strongest relics available.
