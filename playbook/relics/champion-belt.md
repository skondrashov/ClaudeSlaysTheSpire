# Champion Belt (Uncommon Relic)

EFFECT: Whenever you apply Vulnerable to an enemy, also apply 1 Weak.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Turns Bash+ into a dual-debuff card (3 Vulnerable + 1 Weak) for free. The Weak reduces enemy damage by 25% on top of the 50-75% damage amplification from Vulnerable. This means every Bash+ play both increases your damage AND reduces incoming damage simultaneously.

SYNERGIES: Bash+ (main trigger -- applies Vuln frequently), Thunderclap (mass Vuln to all enemies = mass Weak to all enemies), Paper Phrog (75% Vuln + Weak = massive damage amplification with damage reduction), any Vulnerable source.

EVALUATION: Excellent defensive relic that requires no deck changes. Since Bash+ is played in nearly every combat, Champion Belt provides passive Weak application for free. Reduces the need for dedicated Weak sources (Intimidate, Shockwave, Clothesline) since Bash+ handles both Vulnerable and Weak.
