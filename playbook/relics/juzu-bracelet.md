# Juzu Bracelet
EFFECT: Unknown/Question Mark rooms (?) can no longer contain Monster fights. They always resolve as events.
WHEN IT'S GOOD: Act 2, where Unknown rooms can spawn Spheric Guardian (30-41 HP drain), Byrds (36-73 HP drain), or Snake Plant (15-42 HP drain). Juzu Bracelet eliminates this risk entirely, making Unknown rooms safe for routing.
VALUE: Extremely high. Unknown rooms become free value (events) instead of potential death traps. This relic fundamentally changes Act 2 routing -- Unknown rooms become preferable to Monster rooms. Three Spheric Guardian deaths and multiple Byrd deaths came from Unknown rooms resolving as combat.
SHOP PRICE: ~147 gold. Worth buying if gold allows.
SYNERGY: Pairs well with conservative pathing. Route through Unknown rooms for guaranteed safe rooms.
