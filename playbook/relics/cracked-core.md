# Cracked Core (Defect Starter Relic)

EFFECT: At the start of each combat, Channel 1 Lightning orb.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Provides a guaranteed Lightning orb from Turn 1 of every fight without spending a card play or energy. The Lightning orb deals 3 passive damage at end of turn. Combined with Zap (0E, channel another Lightning), the Defect can have 2 Lightning orbs passively dealing 6 damage per turn from Turn 1.

VALUE: The Defect's equivalent of Burning Blood (Ironclad) or Ring of the Snake (Silent). Provides guaranteed baseline value in every combat. The free Lightning orb also means Dualcast always has a target on Turn 1 (16 burst damage if used immediately).

NOTE: If Cracked Core is swapped for a boss relic (e.g., via Neow), the Defect loses the guaranteed Turn 1 Lightning orb. This removes passive damage and makes Dualcast dead on Turn 1 unless another orb is channeled first.
