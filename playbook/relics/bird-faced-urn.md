# Bird-Faced Urn
EFFECT: Whenever a Power card is played, heal 2 HP.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Each Power played becomes a small heal. In Power-heavy decks (3+ Powers), this adds 6+ HP healing per combat, partially offsetting the Burning Blood gap. Incentivizes taking and playing Power cards (Inflame, Metallicize, Demon Form, Evolve, etc.).
SYNERGIES: Power-heavy decks. Mummified Hand (free Power plays = free healing). Corruption (all Skills become free, freeing energy for Powers). Multiple cheap Powers (Inflame 1E, Metallicize 1E) maximize triggers per fight.
MATCHUPS: Best in long fights (bosses, elites) where multiple Powers are played. Less impactful in short hallway fights where only 1 Power is played. Does not replace real healing cards (Reaper, Feed) but supplements them.
