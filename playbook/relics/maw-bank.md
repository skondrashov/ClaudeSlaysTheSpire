# Maw Bank (Common Relic)

Gain 12 gold after each non-shop floor. Spending gold at a shop disables the effect permanently.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Generates significant gold over time (12 gold x 10 floors = 120 gold). Best taken early for maximum accumulation. Consider skipping shops or only using shop card removal to preserve the gold generation. Most valuable when the run does not need shop purchases urgently.
