# Nilry's Codex

EFFECT: At the end of each turn, you may choose 1 of 3 randomly generated cards to add to your hand. The cards can be from ANY class (not just your own). If you don't want any, you can skip.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Provides a free card every turn, which is excellent in long fights where hand quality matters. Generated cards include powerful options like Impervious and Immolate from any class pool. In a boss or elite fight lasting 8+ turns, Nilry's Codex adds 8+ free cards -- some of which can be game-changing.

SOURCE: Obtained from the Cursed Tome event (costs 16 HP total to complete the event). One of several possible book relics from this event.

VALUE: High in long fights (bosses, elites). The card generation compensates for thin decks or decks lacking specific answers. Lower value in short hallway fights (2-3 turns, only 2-3 extra cards).

OBSERVED: Generated Impervious (12 block, exhaust) and Immolate (21 AOE damage + Burn) during a Gremlin Leader fight. These high-impact cards from the Codex can swing otherwise unwinnable fights.
