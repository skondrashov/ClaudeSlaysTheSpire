# Ginger

EFFECT: You can no longer be Weakened.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Ginger completely negates the Weakened debuff from all sources -- enemy attacks, curses, events. This has several implications:

1. **Mind Bloom "I am Healthy" becomes free.** The Doubt curse (1 Weak/turn) is completely harmless with Ginger. Choose "I am Healthy" for a free full heal to max HP, then remove Doubt at the next shop. This interaction was a key decision in the first Silent victory.

2. **Enemy Weak applications are ignored.** Chosen's Hex, Collector's triple debuff (Weak component), Time Eater's Ripple (Weak component) -- all have no effect on your damage output. This is particularly valuable for the Silent where every point of Shiv damage matters.

3. **Block calculations simplified.** With Ginger, you never need to account for the 25% damage reduction from Weak on your attacks. Your damage output is always at full value.

SYNERGIES:
- **Mind Bloom + Ginger** = free full heal (victory-proven)
- **Shiv decks** benefit most since Weak would reduce every individual Shiv hit
- **Boss fights with Weak application** (Collector, Time Eater Ripple) become safer offensively

DRAFT PRIORITY: High. Immunity to an entire debuff type is always valuable. The Mind Bloom interaction alone can provide 15-30 HP of free healing.
