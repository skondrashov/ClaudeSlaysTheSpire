# Neow's Lament
EFFECT: Enemies in your next 3 combats have 1 HP. Gives 3 completely free fights.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: One of the best Neow offerings. 3 free fights = 3 card rewards + 3 gold drops + potential potions + zero HP lost. Equivalent to ~50 gold and 3 card choices with no risk. Take this whenever offered.
PATHING TRAP: Lament preserves full HP through 3 fights, which creates a false sense of elite readiness. After 3 free fights, the deck may only have 1-2 card rewards added to the starter core. High HP does NOT mean the deck is ready for an elite. Sentries at Floor 5 with a near-starter deck is a confirmed death trap (56 HP drained in a 20-turn fight). When routing with Lament, ensure the card rewards from the 3 free fights actually improve the deck enough for the elite, or delay the elite until more cards are acquired.
