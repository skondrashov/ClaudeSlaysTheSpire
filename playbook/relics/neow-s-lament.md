# Neow's Lament
EFFECT: Enemies in your next 3 combats have 1 HP. Gives 3 completely free fights.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: One of the best Neow offerings. 3 free fights = 3 card rewards + 3 gold drops + potential potions + zero HP lost. Equivalent to ~50 gold and 3 card choices with no risk. Take this whenever offered.
