# Enchiridion
EFFECT: At the start of each combat, add a random Power card to your hand. It costs 0 this turn.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Free Power every fight. Can generate Metallicize, Inflame, Corruption, Dark Embrace, or other Powers. The 0 cost means it never competes with other turn 1 plays. High variance -- sometimes generates Berserk (dangerous) or a duplicate of a Power you already have. Overall excellent value for free.

DANGER -- UNCONTROLLABLE POWER SELECTION: The Power is random. Enchiridion can generate Brutality (lose 1 HP/turn), which is actively harmful in long fights or at low HP. Against Book of Stabbing or other scaling enemies, a free Brutality played carelessly drains HP every turn in a fight that already demands every point. Before playing an Enchiridion-generated Power, evaluate whether it helps or hurts in THIS specific fight. Do not autopilot "free Power = always play."

DANGEROUS POWERS FROM ENCHIRIDION: Brutality (HP drain in long fights), Berserk (Vulnerable to self), Combust (HP drain per turn). Safe powers: Metallicize, Inflame, Dark Embrace, Feel No Pain, Rage.
