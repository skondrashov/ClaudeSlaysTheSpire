# Coffee Dripper (Boss Relic)

+1 Energy per turn. You can no longer Rest at rest sites (upgrade only).

EVALUATION: Strong energy boost but removes the primary healing mechanism. Rest sites become upgrade-only, meaning the only healing sources are potions, events, healing cards (Reaper, Feed), and relics (Toy Ornithopter, Meal Ticket, Regen Potion, Blood Vial, Burning Blood). For the Silent, who lacks Burning Blood's passive healing, Coffee Dripper is significantly more dangerous than for Ironclad.

WHEN TO TAKE:
- Deck is nearly complete and rest sites would be used for upgrades anyway.
- Multiple healing sources already exist (healing cards, potions, relics).
- Energy-hungry deck that benefits greatly from the extra energy (Shiv archetypes, Barricade engine, multi-card turns).
- Ironclad with Burning Blood providing baseline healing per fight.

WHEN TO AVOID:
- Early in Act 1 when HP management is uncertain.
- No healing sources beyond rest sites.
- Silent without healing cards or potions -- the Silent takes more chip damage and has no passive heal equivalent to Burning Blood.

SILENT-SPECIFIC DANGER: The Silent relies heavily on rest sites for HP recovery because she has no innate post-combat healing (no Burning Blood equivalent). Coffee Dripper removes this safety net entirely. Every point of chip damage becomes permanent until a potion or event heals it. In Act 2, fights like Cultist+Chosen (30-40 HP drain from Hex deck clog) can drop HP to critical levels with zero recovery path. Confirmed: Coffee Dripper Silent entered Cultist+Chosen at 30 HP, dropped to 4 HP, then died two fights later because no healing existed.

CRITICAL ANTI-SYNERGY -- COFFEE DRIPPER + ECTOPLASM: Taking both boss relics means no healing from rest sites AND no gold to buy potions or healing items at shops. This double downside eliminates two of the three primary recovery paths (rest, shop, card rewards). The only remaining healing is from card rewards and random events. This combination should be avoided unless the deck is already dominant and can reliably take zero damage in every fight.
