# Horn Cleat

EFFECT: At the start of your 2nd turn in each combat, gain 14 Block.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Provides a free 14 block on turn 2, which is often the first big attack turn from enemies. This is effectively a free Defend + Shrug It Off on the turn you need it most. No action required -- it happens automatically.

VALUE: Solid defensive relic. 14 block on turn 2 covers most single-enemy attacks in Act 1 and partially mitigates Act 2 attacks. Frees up energy on turn 2 to play offensive cards instead of blocking. Less impactful in fights that start with free turns (Cultists buffing turn 1, then attacking turn 2 -- Horn Cleat helps here).

NOTE: Only triggers on turn 2 of each combat. No benefit on turns 1, 3, 4, etc.
