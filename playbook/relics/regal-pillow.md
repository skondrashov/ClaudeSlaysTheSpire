# Regal Pillow (Common Relic)

EFFECT: Heal an additional 15 HP when you Rest at a Rest Site.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Turns rest sites from 24 HP healing to 39 HP healing. In Act 3 where HP management is critical, Regal Pillow can restore nearly half of max HP at each rest site. Makes it safer to choose Rest over Smith when HP is below threshold.

EVALUATION: Strong utility relic that patches healing gaps. In the victory run, Regal Pillow enabled healing from 53 HP to 80/80 (full HP) at the pre-boss rest site before Donu and Deca, ensuring the 100% HP entry that contributed to the win.
