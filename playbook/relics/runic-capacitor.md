# Runic Capacitor (Common Relic, Defect-only)

EFFECT: Start each combat with 3 additional orb slots (6 total instead of 3).

WHY IT'S STRONG: Doubles the Defect's starting orb capacity. More orb slots means more simultaneous passive effects and dramatically higher Barrage damage. With 6 slots filled with Frost orbs at Focus 2, passive block is 24 per turn (6 * 4). With Barrage+, 6 orbs = 42 damage per play.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **Barrage+:** Each additional orb slot (when filled) adds 7 damage to Barrage+. Runic Capacitor's 3 extra slots = +21 Barrage+ damage.
- **Capacitor (card):** Stacks with Capacitor/Capacitor+ for even more orb slots. Runic Capacitor + Capacitor+ = 9 orb slots, enabling truly massive orb boards.
- **Coolheaded+:** More orb slots means Coolheaded+ Frost orbs persist longer before being pushed out by new channels. The Frost wall is more stable.
- **Blizzard:** More Frost orbs channeled means higher Blizzard damage.

CONFIRMED: Present in the first Defect victory. Runic Capacitor enabled 6+ simultaneous orbs, making Barrage+ a reliable 42+ damage card and the Frost wall substantial enough to block most incoming damage.
