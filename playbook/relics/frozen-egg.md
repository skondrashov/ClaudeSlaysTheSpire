# Frozen Egg (Uncommon Relic)

Whenever you obtain a Power card, it is automatically upgraded.

EVALUATION: Strong in decks that plan to take more Powers. Saves rest site upgrades for non-Power cards. Ironclad Powers include Metallicize, Inflame, Evolve, Barricade, Demon Form, Corruption, Dark Embrace, Fire Breathing, Brutality, Feel No Pain -- many are high-priority upgrades.

BEST SYNERGIES:
- **Mid-run Power pickup**: Getting Frozen Egg before taking Metallicize means Metallicize+ (4 block/turn) for free. Barricade+ (2E instead of 3E) is a huge upgrade that would otherwise cost a rest site.
- **Evolve+**: Auto-upgrades Evolve to draw 2 on Status instead of 1. Major power spike.
- **Inflame+**: +3 Str instead of +2 for free.

NOTE: Does not retroactively upgrade Powers already in the deck. Only applies to newly obtained Powers.
