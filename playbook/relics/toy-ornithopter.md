# Toy Ornithopter
EFFECT: Whenever you use a potion, heal 5 HP.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Turns potion usage into healing. Each potion heals an extra 5 HP. With Potion Belt (extra potion slot) and aggressive potion acquisition, this can heal 25-35 HP across a run. In the first victory, Toy Ornithopter healed 15 HP during the Donu and Deca boss fight alone (3 potion uses: Strength Potion, Duplication Potion, Liquid Memories). This healing filled the gap when no Reaper or Feed was available.

HEALING GAP SOLUTION: When Reaper and Feed are not offered (confirmed as a recurring RNG denial pattern), Toy Ornithopter + aggressive potion usage becomes the backup healing plan. Buy healing potions at shops, use them liberally in tough fights, and let Toy Ornithopter convert each use into 5 HP recovery. Three potion uses in a single boss fight = 15 HP healed, equivalent to a medium Reaper hit.

ANTI-SYNERGY: Completely dead with Sozu (which prevents obtaining potions). Do NOT take Toy Ornithopter if you have Sozu. If offered from an event and Sozu is already held, skip it.
