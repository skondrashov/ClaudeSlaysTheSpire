# Ornamental Fan (Common Relic)

Gain 4 Block whenever you play 3 Attacks in a single turn.

EVALUATION: Moderate value. Provides passive block in attack-heavy turns without costing energy. Works well in decks that naturally play 3+ attacks per turn. Less useful in Corruption engine decks where most plays are Skills (which exhaust for block via Feel No Pain instead).

SYNERGY: High-attack decks, Snecko Eye (many cheap attacks), Anger (0E attack helps reach 3 plays).

OBSERVED: Triggered consistently in hallway fights but contributed minimal value in boss fights dominated by the Corruption + Feel No Pain engine. The 4 block per 3 attacks is small relative to the 30-40 block generated by Impervious or FNP chains.
