# Shuriken
EFFECT: Every time you play 3 Attacks in a single turn, gain 1 permanent Strength.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Incentivizes playing 3+ Attacks per turn. Anger (0E) counts as an Attack and is free, making it easy to trigger Shuriken even on tight energy turns. Over a long fight, Shuriken provides 3-5+ Strength passively -- comparable to Inflame but requires no card slot or energy.

SYNERGIES:
- **Anger**: 0-cost Attack that copies itself into discard. Playing 3x Anger in a turn triggers Shuriken for free.
- **Demon Form**: Demon Form's +2 Str/turn stacks with Shuriken's passive Str gains. By turn 5, total Strength can be +12-15 combined.
- **Rage**: 0-cost Power that gives block per Attack. Rage + Shuriken means every Attack-heavy turn generates both block and Strength.
- **Multi-attack turns**: Thunderclap + Strike + Anger = 3 attacks = +1 Str. Very easy to trigger naturally.

WHEN OBTAINED: From elite rewards (relic choice). High pick priority in Attack-heavy decks.

MATCHUPS: Excellent in long fights (bosses, elites) where 5-8 triggers accumulate to significant Strength. Hexaghost was killed in 7 turns partly because Shuriken added +3 Str over the fight alongside Demon Form's +14 Str.
