# Whetstone (Common Relic)

Upon pickup, upgrade 2 random Attack cards in the deck.

One-time effect. Two free Attack upgrades. Value depends entirely on which Attacks are in the deck and whether they have strong upgrades.

VALUE: Equivalent to two rest site upgrades for Attacks specifically, but with no control over which cards are chosen. Best when the deck has multiple high-priority Attack upgrades (Heavy Blade 3x->5x, Carnage 20->28, Twin Strike, Pommel Strike). Worst when the deck has already upgraded its key Attacks or only has Strikes.

WHEN TO TAKE: Strong early (more Attacks in deck = higher chance of hitting good targets, plus upgrades compound over more fights). Weaker late when key Attacks are already upgraded. Always acceptable as a free relic with no downside.
