# Kunai (Uncommon Relic, gain 1 Dexterity every time you play 3 Attacks in a turn)

Passive Dexterity scaling from playing Attacks. Every 3 Attacks played in a single turn grants +1 Dex (permanent for the fight), increasing all block card values by 1.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: In Attack-heavy decks with cheap Attacks (Anger at 0E, Pommel Strike, Twin Strike, Headbutt). The more Attacks per turn, the faster Dex stacks. In long fights (bosses, elites), Kunai compounds: by turn 5-6, block cards generate 3-5 more block each, drastically improving survivability.

SYNERGIES: Whirlwind (counts as 1 Attack, not multiple -- does NOT trigger Kunai per hit), cheap Attack cards (Anger, Pommel Strike), Shuriken (same trigger -- playing 3 Attacks gives +1 Dex AND +1 Str if both relics held), Double Tap (the duplicated Attack counts toward the 3-Attack threshold).

NOTES: Pairs naturally with Ironclad's Attack-heavy card pool. Does not conflict with any boss strategy. Purely upside with no drawback -- always take when offered.
