# Mercury Hourglass (Relic)

At the start of each turn, deal 3 damage to ALL enemies.

VALUE: Free passive AOE damage every turn. Over a 10-turn boss fight, this is 30 free damage to every enemy. Against multi-enemy fights (Collector + 2 Torch Heads), deals 3 to each = 9 total damage per turn for free.

SYNERGIES: Long fights (more turns = more free damage), multi-enemy fights (damage multiplied across targets), Vulnerable on enemies (3 * 1.5 = 4 per enemy). Does NOT trigger Sharp Hide (not an Attack card played). Does NOT count as an attack for Rage block.

OBSERVED: Against Chosen+Byrd fight, Mercury Hourglass dealt 3 to each enemy at start of every turn, contributing ~15-20 free damage over the fight. Against Snake Plant, dealt the killing 3 damage at start of turn to finish off a low-HP enemy without triggering additional Malleable block.
