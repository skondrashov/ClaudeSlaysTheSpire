# Bronze Scales (common relic)

EFFECT: Whenever you take damage from an Attack, deal 3 damage back to the attacker (Thorns 3).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: Every combat. Passive damage that requires no energy, no card plays, no setup. Particularly strong in multi-hit attack fights where the attacker hits many times (each hit triggers 3 Thorns damage). Against enemies with 5-hit multi-attacks, Bronze Scales deals 15 free damage per turn.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **Donu and Deca**: In the second victory, Bronze Scales dealt the final 6 damage to Deca at 4 HP. Deca's own attacks triggered Thorns, killing itself. When the boss is near death, Bronze Scales can deliver the killing blow without requiring a card play or energy.
- **Spikers**: Both sides have Thorns (Spikers have Thorns 3 too). Bronze Scales retaliates when Spikers attack, but attacking Spikers also takes Thorns damage -- net neutral in Thorns exchange.
- **Multi-hit enemies**: Centurion (10x3), Book of Stabbing (6xN), Gremlin Leader minions -- each hit triggers Bronze Scales.

VALUE: Moderate-high. Free passive damage every fight. Excellent as a finishing tool when enemies are at low HP. Scales with fight length (more enemy attacks = more free damage). No downside.
