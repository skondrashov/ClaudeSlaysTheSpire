# Red Mask
Apply 1 Weak to ALL enemies at the start of each combat.

VALUE: Free damage reduction every fight with zero player input. 1 Weak = 25% less enemy damage on turn 1. Against multi-enemy fights (3 Cultists, Byrds), this applies to every enemy simultaneously. Weak duration is 1 turn only (wears off at start of enemy turn 2).

SYNERGIES: Paper Phrog (Vulnerable from Bash lasts longer = more time to exploit the free Weak turn). Fights where enemies attack turn 1 get immediate defensive value. Against enemies that buff turn 1 (Cultists, some Fungi Beasts), the Weak is wasted since they don't attack. **Artifact stripping**: Against enemies with Artifact (Bronze Automaton Artifact 3, Spheric Guardian Artifact 3), Red Mask's Weak application at combat start strips 1 Artifact charge automatically before the player's first turn. This reduces the Artifact stripping requirement by 1 -- against Bronze Automaton, only 2 more debuffs are needed instead of 3.

WHEN ACQUIRED: From boss reward or shop. Provides consistent incremental value across every single fight for the rest of the run.
