# Wing Boots (Rare Relic, can fly over map nodes 3 times)

Grants 3 uses of flight on the map, allowing you to skip a node and move to any connected node on the next row. Useful for avoiding dangerous fights or reaching specific paths (shops, rest sites, elites).

WHEN IT'S GOOD: At low HP to avoid hallway fights that would kill you. To reach a shop or rest site that is otherwise inaccessible. To skip elite fights you are not prepared for.

DECISION POINTS: Use sparingly -- only 3 charges for the entire run. Best saved for Act 2-3 where hallway fights are deadlier and pathing is more critical. Do not waste charges in Act 1 unless absolutely necessary for survival.
