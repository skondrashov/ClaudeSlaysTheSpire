# Unceasing Top (Rare Relic)
Whenever your hand is empty during your turn, draw a card.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Creates a draw engine with any card that empties your hand. Most critically, it combos with Fiend Fire: after exhausting your entire hand, you immediately draw a card. If that card is playable and you still have energy, play it, hand empties again, draw again. This can cycle through your entire draw pile in one turn.

KEY SYNERGIES:
- **Fiend Fire** (CRITICAL): Exhausts hand -> empty -> draw -> play -> empty -> draw. Infinite draw engine limited only by energy and draw pile size.
- **0-cost cards**: Bloodletting (gain 2 energy, hand empties), Rage (play for free, hand empties), Inflame/powers (play, hand empties) all trigger additional draws.
- **Offering**: Lose 6 HP but gain 2 energy + draw 3. Combined with Top, massive turns.

EVALUATION: One of the strongest relics for exhaust-focused decks. Transforms Fiend Fire from a burst card into a sustained damage engine. Less useful in decks with large hands that never empty.
