# Sling of Courage (Shop Relic)

EFFECT: Start each Elite and Boss combat with +2 Strength.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Free permanent Strength that applies to every attack in the two most important fight types. With Heavy Blade (3x multiplier), +2 Str becomes +6 damage per play. With Heavy Blade+ (5x), it becomes +10. Compounds with Spot Weakness+ (+4 Str from attacking enemies) and Limit Break (doubles all Str including this bonus).

EVALUATION: Excellent shop purchase for Strength-scaling decks. In the victory run, Sling of Courage provided the base Strength that made the Bronze Automaton fight manageable -- starting at +2 meant Spot Weakness+ brought Str to 6 instead of 4, and Limit Break doubled to 12 instead of 8. Every point of base Strength has outsized value due to multiplicative scaling with Heavy Blade and Limit Break.
