# Liquid Bronze
EFFECT: Whenever you take damage from an Attack, deal 3 damage back to the attacker (Thorns 3).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Passive retaliation. Against multi-hit attacks (Hexaghost 7x6): 7*3 = 21 thorns damage from one Inferno. Against multiple attackers: 3 damage to each. However, damage reduction (Weak) is more important than damage reflection. Don't rely on thorns as a primary damage source.
