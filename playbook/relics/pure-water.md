# Pure Water (Watcher Starter Relic)

At the start of each combat, add 1 Miracle to your hand.

Miracle is a 0E card that gives +1 Energy, Retains between turns, and Exhausts when played.

## Value

Effectively +1 energy on Turn 1 of every combat. The Miracle Retains, so it can be saved for a turn where the energy matters most (e.g., a burst turn exiting Calm into Wrath). Over a full run, this is one extra energy per fight.

## Interaction with Deus Ex Machina

Deus Ex Machina generates 2-3 additional Miracles when drawn. Combined with Pure Water's Turn 1 Miracle, the Watcher can accumulate 3-4 Miracles. Managing these Miracles (not wasting them on turns with no useful plays) is a critical Watcher skill. See miracle.md.

## Boss Relic Swap

Swapping Pure Water for a random boss relic via Neow is a gamble. Losing the guaranteed +1E per combat is a real cost, especially early when the deck has few energy-generating cards. Consider keeping Pure Water unless the boss relic is clearly superior (Snecko Eye, Pandora's Box, etc.).
