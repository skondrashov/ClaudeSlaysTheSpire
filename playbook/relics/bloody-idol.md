# Bloody Idol
Gain 5 gold whenever you gain gold. Heal 5 HP whenever you gain gold.

VALUE: Provides both economy and sustain. Every combat victory that awards gold also heals 5 HP and gives 5 bonus gold. Events and ? rooms that give gold also trigger both effects. Over a full act (8-12 gold-awarding events), this provides 40-60 bonus gold and 40-60 HP healing.

STRATEGY: Makes combat more sustainable (5 HP heal per fight stacks with Burning Blood's +6 = 11 HP recovered per fight). Improves gold economy for shops. Incentivizes taking fights over avoiding them since each fight provides healing.

SYNERGIES: Maw Bank (gold gain triggers Bloody Idol heal). Golden Idol (+25% gold = more frequent gold triggers). Any relic or event that awards gold.
