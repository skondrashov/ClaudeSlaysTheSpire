# Bottled Tornado

EFFECT: Upon pick up, choose a Power card. That card becomes Innate (starts in your hand at the beginning of every combat).

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Bottled Tornado guarantees a specific Power is available from Turn 1 of every fight. The choice of which Power to make innate is one of the most impactful decisions in a run.

BEST TARGETS (Silent):
1. **After Image** (VICTORY-PROVEN) -- 1 block per card played from Turn 1. This was the #1 defensive choice in the first Silent victory. Guarantees block generation without spending a draw on finding it.
2. **Noxious Fumes+** -- Passive poison from Turn 1 of every fight. Best for poison-focused decks.
3. **Footwork+** -- +3 Dex from Turn 1. Strong for block-heavy decks.

BEST TARGETS (Ironclad):
1. **Barricade** -- Block persists from Turn 1. Excellent with Corruption engine.
2. **Metallicize** -- Passive block every turn from Turn 1.
3. **Inflame** -- +2 Str from Turn 1 of every fight.

NOTE: Bottled Tornado bottles a POWER card (not Attack or Skill). Bottled Flame bottles an Attack. Bottled Lightning bottles a Skill. Do not confuse them.

DRAFT PRIORITY: High when you have a Power that you always want Turn 1. The value depends entirely on which Power is bottled -- After Image innate is game-defining; a situational Power is less impactful.
