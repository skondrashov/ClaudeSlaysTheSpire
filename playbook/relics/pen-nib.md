# Pen Nib
EFFECT: Every 10th Attack card played deals double damage. Counter persists across combats.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Track the counter. Time the 10th Attack to coincide with high-damage cards (Fiend Fire, Carnage+, Headbutt+) or Vulnerable targets. Pen Nib + Strike = 12 damage. Pen Nib + Fiend Fire (4 cards) = 56 damage. Plan your Attacks across fights to line up the 10th hit on the right card.
