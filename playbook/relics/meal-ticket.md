# Meal Ticket
EFFECT: Heal 15 HP whenever you enter a shop.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Makes shop visits double as healing stops. At low HP, pathing through shops provides 15 HP recovery in addition to purchasing options. This is more healing than Burning Blood (6 HP) and stacks with it. Prioritize shop paths on the map when carrying Meal Ticket and low on HP.

VALUE: 15 HP per shop is significant -- roughly one fight's worth of damage absorption. In Act 2 where HP drain is the primary cause of death, Meal Ticket can extend a run by several floors if the map has shop nodes available.

LIMITATION: Only triggers at shops, which are limited on most maps (1-2 per act). Cannot be relied upon as a primary healing source, but excellent as supplemental healing alongside Burning Blood.
