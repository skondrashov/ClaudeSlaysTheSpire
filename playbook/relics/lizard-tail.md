# Lizard Tail
EFFECT: When HP reaches 0, heal to 50% Max HP. One-time use, then becomes inactive.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: A safety net similar to Fairy in a Bottle but heals significantly more (50% Max HP vs ~30%). Save it for boss fights or dangerous elites. Unlike Fairy, Lizard Tail is a relic -- it doesn't take a potion slot. Once triggered, it's gone for the rest of the run.

BOSS FIGHT VALUE (VICTORY-PROVEN): In the first Silent victory, Lizard Tail revived from 0 HP to 35 HP (50% of 70) during the Time Eater fight on Turn 9. This revival enabled 2 more turns of Shiv burst that killed Time Eater from 139 HP. Without Lizard Tail, the run was lost. The key insight: Lizard Tail is not just a safety net -- it can be deliberately "spent" by accepting lethal damage on a turn where you maximize damage output instead of blocking, knowing the revival will sustain you for the killing turns.
