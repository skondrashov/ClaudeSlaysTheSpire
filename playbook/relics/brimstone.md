# Brimstone (Uncommon Relic, at the start of each turn, ALL characters gain 2 Strength)

At the start of every turn, you gain 2 Strength AND every enemy gains 2 Strength. This is a double-edged scaling relic.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: With Demon Form, the combined Strength ramp is +4/turn (2 from Brimstone + 2/3 from Demon Form), making your damage scale extremely fast. Any deck with multi-hit attacks (Pummel, Sword Boomerang, Whirlwind) benefits enormously because each hit gains the accumulated Strength. In short fights (3-4 turns), the enemy Strength gain is manageable.

THE RISK: Enemy Strength stacks identically. A multi-hit enemy like Book of Stabbing with +2 Str/turn becomes devastating: each of their escalating hits deals more damage. Snake Plant's 9x3 becomes (9+2)x3 on turn 2, (9+4)x3 on turn 3, etc. Long fights become increasingly dangerous for BOTH sides. Brimstone turns every fight into a race.

DECISION POINTS: Take Brimstone when your deck has burst damage and can end fights in 4-5 turns. Avoid if your deck relies on slow defensive scaling or long attrition fights. Pairs well with Weak application (Bash+, Thunderclap, Shockwave) to offset enemy Strength gains.

**PURCHASE CHECKLIST (before buying Brimstone at a shop):**
1. Is Book of Stabbing a possible Act 2 elite? If yes, DO NOT BUY. Brimstone + Book of Stabbing has killed the player twice (Run 091, Run 100). The anti-synergy is unsurvivable.
2. Can your deck end fights in 4-5 turns? If no, the enemy Str gain will overwhelm you.
3. Do you have Weak application (Bash+, Thunderclap, Shockwave)? Weak offsets enemy Str gains.
4. Are you in Act 2 already? If yes, evaluate remaining enemy pool before buying. If Snake Plant and Book of Stabbing are both possible encounters, skip Brimstone.

SYNERGIES: Demon Form (+4 Str/turn combined), Reaper (heals more as Strength grows), Heavy Blade (Strength multiplied 3x/5x), Pummel/multi-hit (Strength added per hit), Limit Break (doubles accumulated Strength).

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Slow decks, long fights, enemies with multi-hit attacks (Book of Stabbing, Snake Plant). The enemy Strength gain compounds the danger of every additional turn.

CRITICAL DANGER -- BOOK OF STABBING: Brimstone + Book of Stabbing is one of the most lethal relic-enemy combinations in the game. Book's multi-hit attacks gain +1 hit per turn AND +2 damage per hit per turn from Brimstone. The math escalates catastrophically:
- Turn 1: (7+2)x2 = 18 (vs 14 without Brimstone)
- Turn 2: (7+4)x3 = 33 (vs 21)
- Turn 3: (7+6)x4 = 52 (vs 28)
- Turn 4: (7+8)x5 = 75 (vs 35)
- Turn 5: (7+10)x6 = 102 (vs 42)
By turn 5, incoming is 102 vs 42 without Brimstone -- a 143% increase. This is unsurvivable. In run 100, entered at 57 HP and died on turn 5 despite strong Strength scaling (Brimstone + Inflame+ + Spot Weakness+). If you have Brimstone, AVOID Book of Stabbing entirely. If forced into the fight, you must kill it in 3 turns or die.
