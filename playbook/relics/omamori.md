# Omamori (Uncommon Relic)

Negate the next 2 Curses you would obtain.

EVALUATION: Strong defensive relic. Curses are permanent deck pollution that clog draws. Omamori prevents 2 curses from ever entering the deck. This enables safe event choices that would otherwise add curses (Coffin, various curse-risk events).

WHEN IT'S GOOD:
- Before curse-giving events (open Coffin for free relic, take risky event options).
- Before Vampires event (Bites replace Strikes -- Omamori does NOT help here since Bites are not Curses).
- General insurance against random curse events in Unknown rooms.

CONFIRMED: Used to safely open a Coffin event for a free relic (Ceramic Fish). The curse was negated by Omamori counter, gaining a relic at zero cost.
