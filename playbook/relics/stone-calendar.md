# Stone Calendar (Rare Relic)
At the end of turn 7, deals 52 damage to ALL enemies.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Free 52 AOE damage with no energy or card investment. In long fights (7+ turns), this is a massive damage spike that can finish weakened enemies or push through block. Centurion+Mystic is the ideal fight for this relic -- the fight naturally lasts 7+ turns while you kill Mystic through its block, and the 52 damage nuke lands on Centurion (who should be the last target standing).

WHEN IT MATTERS: Only fires once per fight, only on turn 7. In fights that end in 4-5 turns, the relic does nothing. Evaluate Stone Calendar as a long-fight finisher, not a general damage source.

KEY INTERACTIONS:
- **Centurion+Mystic**: Observed 52 damage to Centurion after 7 turns of focusing Mystic. This is the best Act 2 matchup for Stone Calendar.
- **Boss fights**: Most boss fights last 10+ turns, so the turn 7 nuke always fires. 52 free damage is significant. Against Guardian, Calendar + Spot Weakness Str scaling killed the boss.
- **3 Cultists**: Calendar fired end of turn 6 (counter reached 7) and killed the last Cultist at 23 HP. Combined with Rampage+ scaling and Blood for Blood at 0E, Calendar was the safety net that ended a fight at 15 HP.
- **Shelled Parasite**: Calendar's 52 damage overkilled a 35 HP Parasite through remaining Plated Armor block (~8-9 block). Calendar is the answer to Plated Armor enemies in long fights.
- **Spheric Guardian**: Calendar damage goes through block normally. 52 damage into 65 block only removes 52 block, leaving 13 block -- does NOT kill. Calendar alone is insufficient against Barricade enemies with high block.
- **Short fights**: Useless against enemies that die in 3-5 turns (Cultist, Louse, etc.).

EVALUATION: Strong in decks that expect long fights (defensive/block-heavy builds). Weak in burst-focused decks that aim to kill quickly. Confirmed as a fight-winning condition in 3 fights across one run (Guardian, 3 Cultists, Shelled Parasite). Does NOT solve Spheric Guardian due to block absorption.
