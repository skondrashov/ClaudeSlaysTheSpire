# Hovering Kite

Silent boss relic. The first time you discard a card each turn, gain 1 Energy.

**Effect:** +1 Energy per turn when combined with any discard source. Effectively raises base energy from 3 to 4 (or 4 to 5 with other energy relics).

## Key Synergies

- **Tools of the Trade:** TotT discards a card at start of each turn, triggering Kite automatically. This combo provides +1E every turn with zero effort. Together they give energy + card filtering -- the Silent's core engine needs.
- **Survivor / Survivor+:** Manual discard trigger. Block + energy in one card play.
- **Tactician+:** When discarded (by TotT or Survivor), returns 2E. Combined with Kite's +1E from the discard, a single TotT discard of Tactician+ generates +3E (Kite 1 + Tactician+ 2), bringing a 3E base to 6E per turn.
- **Apparitions (Ethereal):** Unneeded Apparitions can be discarded via TotT for Kite energy instead of exhausting at end of turn.
- **Calculated Gamble:** Discard entire hand, Kite triggers on the first discard.

## When to Take

Hovering Kite is excellent when the deck already has or plans to have discard synergy (Tools of the Trade, Survivor+, Tactician+). The energy boost enables playing more cards per turn, which compounds with A Thousand Cuts (1 damage per card to ALL enemies), After Image (1 block per card), and Kunai/Shuriken (buff per 3 attacks).

## Anti-Synergies

- Decks without discard sources get inconsistent value -- Kite only triggers if a discard actually happens.
- Snecko Eye randomizes card costs, making the +1E less impactful when cards already cost 0.
