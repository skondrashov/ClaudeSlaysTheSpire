# Incense Burner
EFFECT: Every 6 turns, gain 1 Intangible (all damage reduced to 1 for that turn).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: The Intangible turn is a free turn for maximum offense -- play all attacks, use no block. Track the counter to plan around it. Against bosses with predictable big-hit turns (Hexaghost Inferno, Bronze Automaton Hyper Beam), Intangible can negate the most dangerous attack entirely.
SYNERGIES: Long fights (bosses, elites) where the counter triggers 2+ times. A 12-turn fight gets 2 Intangible turns. Combined with Rampage or other scaling, Intangible turns enable all-offense plays that accelerate the kill.
MATCHUPS: Excellent vs Hexaghost (Intangible on Inferno turn = 42 damage reduced to 1). Excellent vs Bronze Automaton (Intangible on Hyper Beam = 45-57 damage reduced to 1). Strong in any fight lasting 6+ turns.
NOTE: The counter increments each turn across ALL combats and does NOT reset between fights. Track it via the relic tooltip.
