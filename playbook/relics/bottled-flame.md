# Bottled Flame
EFFECT: Choose an Attack card to become Innate (always in opening hand, turn 1).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Guarantees a specific Attack on turn 1 every fight. Best target: Bash+ (guaranteed Vulnerable turn 1 in every fight). Other good targets: Carnage+ (guaranteed burst), Feed (guaranteed kill setup). The consistency of always having your best Attack turn 1 is enormous.
