# Fusion Hammer (Boss Relic)

+1 Energy per turn. You can no longer Smith at Rest Sites.

EVALUATION: Excellent when the deck is already well-upgraded. +1 Energy/turn is transformative. The tradeoff is losing all future upgrades -- rest sites become heal-only (and Ancient Tea Set triggers).

WHEN TO TAKE:
- Most key cards are already upgraded (Bash+, Armaments+, Inflame+, core block/damage cards).
- The deck needs energy more than upgrades (playing 4-5 cards per turn instead of 3).
- Blessing of the Forge potion is available (one-time mass upgrade substitute).
- Ancient Tea Set is equipped (rest sites always trigger +2E bonus since you must rest).

WHEN TO AVOID:
- Critical cards are still unupgraded (Bash, Corruption, True Grit, Limit Break).
- Deck was recently assembled and needs 3+ upgrades to function.
- No other energy source exists.

SYNERGIES:
- Ancient Tea Set (rest sites now always trigger it since you can only rest, not smith).
- Armaments+ (upgrade ALL cards in hand, substitutes for smith in-combat).
- Blessing of the Forge potion (one-time all-hand upgrade).
- Regal Pillow (14 extra healing from rest since you always rest).

CONFIRMED VICTORY: Taken as Neow swap (lost Burning Blood). The 4E to 5E upgrade enabled Barricade (3E) + 2E for block on setup turns. Apotheosis (from Sensory Stone event) solved the upgrade problem completely -- upgrading all cards in combat. Blessing of the Forge potions provided 2 early upgrades (Immolate to Immolate+, Defend to Defend+). The Barricade + Corruption + FNP engine defeated all three bosses (Hexaghost, The Collector, Time Eater) without ever upgrading at a rest site.

APOTHEOSIS SYNERGY: With Fusion Hammer, Apotheosis becomes the single most valuable card in the game. It provides the ONLY way to upgrade cards. Under Corruption, Apotheosis costs 0E and exhausts -- free mass upgrade + FNP trigger. Actively seek Apotheosis from colorless card events (Sensory Stone) when Fusion Hammer is equipped.
