# Odd Mushroom
EFFECT: Reduces Vulnerable damage multiplier from 1.5x to 1.25x when applied to YOU (player takes less extra damage from Vulnerable). However, this also applies to YOUR Vulnerable application on enemies -- enemies take only 1.25x instead of 1.5x from your attacks while Vulnerable.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: This is a DOUBLE-EDGED relic. Defensively, it reduces damage taken when you are Vulnerable (from Berserk, Spore Cloud, enemy debuffs). Offensively, it REDUCES your damage output by ~17% against Vulnerable targets. In DPS-race fights (Bronze Automaton, Book of Stabbing), this anti-synergy is significant -- Run 105 had Odd Mushroom and the reduced burst likely contributed to failing the Hyper Beam damage race.

WHEN TO TAKE: Good if your deck relies on block/defense and rarely applies Vulnerable offensively. BAD if your deck relies on Bash+/Thunderclap+ Vulnerable for burst damage (most Ironclad decks). The Mushroom event fight reward is the Odd Mushroom -- consider taking the 22 HP heal (Eat option) instead if your deck is Vulnerable-dependent.

ANTI-SYNERGIES: Bash+ (Vuln 3 is your primary damage amp), Thunderclap+ (mass Vuln for burst), any DPS-race fight (Bronze Automaton Hyper Beam, Book of Stabbing). Confirmed detrimental in Run 105.
