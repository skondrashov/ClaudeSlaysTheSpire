# Cursed Key (Boss Relic)

+1 Energy per turn. Opening non-boss chests adds a Curse to your deck.

EVALUATION: Strong when the deck can handle curse dilution. +1 Energy is transformative (4E to 5E enables playing Barricade/Corruption + block in one turn). The curse downside is manageable: (1) only triggers on chests, which are optional, (2) curses can be removed at shops or via exhaust cards (Second Wind+, True Grit+), (3) some curses (Normality, Clumsy) are more dangerous than others.

WHEN TO TAKE:
- Deck has exhaust tools (Second Wind+, Corruption + Second Wind+ exhausts curses permanently).
- Deck needs energy for 3E Powers (Barricade, Corruption, Demon Form).
- Chest rooms are avoidable on the current map path.

NORMALITY CURSE WARNING: The Normality curse (limit 3 card plays per turn) is the most dangerous curse from Cursed Key. It cripples the Barricade + Corruption engine by limiting skill exhaust chains. Remove Normality at the first shop opportunity. Second Wind+ under Corruption can exhaust Normality permanently during combat.

SYNERGY: Omamori (negates 2 curses from chests), exhaust engines (Corruption makes curse removal trivial).
