# Ectoplasm (Boss Relic)

+1 Energy per turn. You can no longer gain Gold.

EVALUATION: Strong when gold is already abundant or irrelevant. +1 Energy/turn is transformative for energy-hungry decks (Barricade + multiple plays, Whirlwind scaling, Bash+ + 2 more plays). The gold loss is permanent -- no shops, no card removal, no potion purchases for the rest of the run.

WHEN TO TAKE:
- Deck is already mostly built and does not need shop purchases.
- High gold count that can be spent at the current shop before the boss relic screen (but boss relics come AFTER the shop).
- No other boss relic is better for the deck.

WHEN TO AVOID:
- Early in Act 1 when shops are still critical for deck building.
- When card removal is needed (Strikes/Defends polluting the deck).
- When the deck is energy-efficient (3E is enough for the current card pool).

ANTI-SYNERGIES:
- Maw Bank (12g/floor, completely negated by Ectoplasm).
- Ceramic Fish (9g per card added, negated).
- Golden Idol (+25% gold, negated).
- Bloody Idol (5 HP per gold gain, negated).
- Shops in general -- cannot buy potions, relics, or card removal.

NOTE: Gold already in pocket is not lost -- it just cannot be spent (shops are still accessible for browsing but purchases require gold you cannot replenish). Spend all gold BEFORE taking Ectoplasm if possible. In practice, boss relics are offered after the boss fight, so spending gold at a prior shop is the last chance.
