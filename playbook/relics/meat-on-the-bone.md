# Meat on the Bone (Uncommon Relic, heal 12 HP at end of combat if HP is at or below 50%)

Conditional end-of-combat healing. If HP is at or below 50% when a fight ends, heal 12 HP. Otherwise, nothing.

WHEN IT'S GOOD: In Act 2, where fights routinely drain 30-50 HP and leave the player below 50%. Meat on the Bone provides double the healing of Burning Blood (12 vs 6) when it triggers. Combined with Burning Blood, a fight that ends below 50% HP heals 18 HP total -- meaningful recovery between consecutive combat rooms.

LIMITATIONS: Does not trigger when HP is above 50% at fight end. This means efficient fights where block prevents most damage get zero value. The relic rewards taking damage, which creates a tension: you want to be healthy, but the relic only helps when you are not. It also does not help DURING combat -- only after.

STRATEGIC VALUE: Partially addresses the Act 2 HP sustain problem. Burning Blood alone provides +6/fight. Meat on the Bone adds +12 when triggered. Together, post-fight healing becomes 18 HP when below 50%, which partially offsets 30-40 HP drain fights. Not a replacement for in-combat healing (Reaper, Feed) but a solid supplementary healing source.

ALWAYS TAKE when offered. Unconditional upside with no drawback.
