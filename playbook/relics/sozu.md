# Sozu (Boss Relic)
EFFECT: Gain 1 additional Energy each turn. You can no longer obtain potions.

VALUE: The extra energy (3 -> 4 per turn) is extremely powerful -- it enables playing one additional card every turn, which compounds across every fight in the run. However, the potion lockout is a severe tradeoff. Potions provide emergency healing (Blood Potion, Regen Potion), burst damage (Strength Potion), and clutch saves (Fairy in a Bottle). Losing ALL potion access means no safety net for bad draws and no supplemental healing.

ANTI-SYNERGIES:
- **Toy Ornithopter**: Heals 5 HP per potion used. With Sozu, no potions can be obtained, so Toy Ornithopter is completely dead. Do NOT take Toy Ornithopter if you have Sozu.
- **Potion Belt**: Extra potion slots are worthless.
- **White Beast Statue**: Extra potion drops are worthless.
- **Woman in Blue event**: Cannot buy potions.
- **Healing potions as sustain plan**: With Sozu, the backup healing plan (buy Blood Potions at shops) is unavailable. Healing cards (Reaper, Feed) become even MORE critical.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS:
- Skip all potion-related relics and events.
- Healing cards (Reaper, Feed) become absolutely essential since potions cannot supplement healing.
- Conservative play is more important -- no potions means no emergency saves.
- The extra energy partially compensates by enabling more block cards per turn, reducing damage taken.
- When offered as a boss relic swap from Neow, it is a strong pick IF you are confident in finding healing cards. The 4th energy transforms every fight.
