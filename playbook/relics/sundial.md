# Sundial
EFFECT: Every 3 shuffles of the draw pile, gain 2 energy.
NOTES: Observed giving 5 energy on Turn 4 of 3 Cultists fight (normally 3E). The bonus 2 energy enabled a full kill + block turn that would otherwise be impossible. Strong in longer fights (7+ turns) where the deck shuffles multiple times. Pairs well with smaller decks (more frequent shuffles).
