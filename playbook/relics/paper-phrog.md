# Paper Phrog (Common Relic)
Vulnerable now increases damage taken by 75% instead of 50%.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Massively amplifies all damage against Vulnerable targets. Bash+ becomes a much stronger setup card. With Paper Phrog, every damage card effectively gains 25% more damage against Vulnerable enemies. Over a multi-turn Vulnerable window (Bash+ = 3 turns), this adds up to massive extra damage.

MATH EXAMPLES (with Paper Phrog):
- Strike (6 base) vs Vulnerable: 6*1.75 = 10 (vs 9 without)
- Immolate+ (28 base) vs Vulnerable: 28*1.75 = 49 (vs 42 without)
- Clothesline (12 base) vs Vulnerable: 12*1.75 = 21 (vs 18 without)
- Twin Strike (5+5 base) vs Vulnerable: (5*1.75)*2 = 17 (vs 14 without)

SYNERGIES: Bash+ (3 turns of amplified Vulnerable), Thunderclap (mass Vulnerable), Dropkick (conditional on Vulnerable already), any high-damage attack card.

EVALUATION: One of the best common relics for Ironclad. Since Bash+ applies Vulnerable in every fight, this relic provides permanent damage amplification. Stronger than most uncommon relics.
