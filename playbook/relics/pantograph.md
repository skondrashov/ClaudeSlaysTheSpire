# Pantograph
EFFECT: Heal to FULL HP at the start of boss fights.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Game-changing. Removes the need to conserve HP before the boss -- take risky elite fights, spend HP at events, and still enter the boss at full. Changes rest site calculus: ALWAYS Smith (upgrade) before boss when you have Pantograph, since HP doesn't matter. One of the best relics in the game.

CONFIRMED VALUE: Pantograph enabled the first Act 2 boss victory. The player entered the Guardian fight at 31% HP and healed to full; entered the Collector fight at 57% HP and healed to full. Without Pantograph, both boss fights would have been entered below the 70% HP threshold and likely lost. Pantograph single-handedly bridges the HP sustain gap that kills most runs in Act 2.

INTERACTION: With Sozu (no potions), Pantograph becomes even MORE critical -- it is the only reliable full heal in the run. Pantograph + Sozu is a viable combination because the energy from Sozu makes fights faster (less HP drain) and Pantograph recovers all HP at the two most dangerous fights.
