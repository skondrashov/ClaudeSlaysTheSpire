# Pantograph
EFFECT: Heal to FULL HP at the start of boss fights.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Game-changing. Removes the need to conserve HP before the boss -- take risky elite fights, spend HP at events, and still enter the boss at full. Changes rest site calculus: ALWAYS Smith (upgrade) before boss when you have Pantograph, since HP doesn't matter. One of the best relics in the game.
