# Pantograph
EFFECT: Heal 25 HP at the start of boss fights. NOT a full heal.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Strong but not game-changing. 25 HP is significant but does not remove the need to manage HP before bosses. Still worth taking risky fights if the 25 HP buffer covers the deficit, but entering a boss at 30% HP with 87 Max HP means healing to ~54% (49+25=74 of 87), not full. Upgrading at rest sites before bosses is still good, but only if the 25 HP heal brings you above a survivable threshold.

CRITICAL CORRECTION: Previous entries stated Pantograph heals to FULL HP. This is WRONG. Pantograph heals exactly 25 HP. In Run 71, the player entered The Champ at 49/87 HP expecting a full heal to 87/87. Instead, Pantograph healed only 25 HP to 74/87. The 38 HP deficit (87-49=38, only 25 recovered) contributed directly to the death. Earlier runs where Pantograph appeared to "heal to full" were likely cases where the player entered with a small enough HP deficit that 25 HP happened to bring them near full.

INTERACTION: With Sozu (no potions), Pantograph is still valuable as a reliable 25 HP heal, but it is NOT a full heal replacement. Plan boss HP thresholds accordingly -- you need to enter bosses at no more than 25 HP below max to benefit fully.
