# Mutagenic Strength (Event Relic)
EFFECT: Gain 3 Strength at the start of each combat. Lose 3 Strength at the end of your first turn.

VALUE: Provides a powerful turn-1 burst window. With +3 Strength, Immolate+ deals 31 instead of 28, Thunderclap+ deals 10 instead of 7, and Hemokinesis deals 18 instead of 15. The combined effect across multiple cards on turn 1 can add 10-20 extra damage. After turn 1, the Strength disappears and has no further effect.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Front-load damage on turn 1. Play as many Attack cards as possible before the Strength expires. Pairs especially well with AOE attacks (Immolate, Thunderclap) where the bonus applies to each enemy hit. The free relic is always worth taking from the event.
