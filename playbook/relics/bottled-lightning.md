# Bottled Lightning (Uncommon Relic)

EFFECT: Choose a Skill card at pickup. That card becomes Innate (always starts in hand at the beginning of combat).

WHY IT'S STRONG: Guarantees a specific Skill card on Turn 1 of every fight. This provides perfect consistency for engine-defining cards. The Skill restriction means it cannot bottle Attack or Power cards, but many of the strongest engine-enabling cards are Skills (Seek, Coolheaded+, Offering, Battle Trance).

BEST TARGETS:
- **Seek/Seek+** (Defect): Guarantees a Turn 1 tutor that can find Echo Form, Barrage+, or any other card needed for the specific fight. This is the strongest known Bottled Lightning target for the Defect.
- **Offering** (Ironclad): Guaranteed Turn 1 energy burst + draw.
- **Coolheaded+** (Defect): Guaranteed Turn 1 Frost orb + 3 draws.

CONFIRMED DOMINANT: In the first Defect victory, Bottled Lightning on Seek+ guaranteed Echo Form+ in hand Turn 1 of every boss fight. This single relic-card combination was the foundation of the entire winning engine.
