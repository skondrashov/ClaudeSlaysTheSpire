# Bag of Marbles (Common Relic)

EFFECT: At the start of each combat, apply 1 Vulnerable to ALL enemies.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Free Vulnerable on Turn 1 without spending energy or a card play. With Paper Phrog (75% Vulnerable), all damage on Turn 1 is amplified by 75% for free. This enables aggressive Turn 1 plays -- every attack card deals significantly more damage before Bash+ is even needed.

SYNERGIES: Paper Phrog (1.75x damage to all enemies Turn 1 for free), Immolate+ (28 * 1.75 = 49 AOE damage Turn 1), Heavy Blade (Str multiplied damage + Vuln), any front-loaded damage strategy.

EVALUATION: One of the best common relics. Free damage amplification on Turn 1 of every combat. Particularly strong with Paper Phrog and high-damage opening plays.
