# Lee's Waffle (Shop Relic, ~157g)
Raise Max HP by 7. Heal all HP.

WHY IT'S GOOD: Immediate full heal plus permanent Max HP increase. At any HP percentage, this restores you to maximum. The +7 Max HP also raises all HP thresholds, giving more buffer for future fights. Observed healing from 31/80 to 87/87 (healed 56 HP and gained 7 Max HP).

WHEN TO BUY: At any HP below 60% when you have the gold. The full heal is worth more than almost any card or card removal at low HP. At 157g it is expensive, but preventing a death from low HP is worth the entire gold reserve.

EVALUATION: One of the strongest shop purchases in the game. Combines the immediate value of a full heal with permanent Max HP scaling. Buy it whenever affordable and HP is below 70%.
