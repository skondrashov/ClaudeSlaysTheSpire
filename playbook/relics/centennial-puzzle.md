# Centennial Puzzle
Draw 3 extra cards the first time you lose HP each combat.

VALUE: Effectively gives an 8-card hand on the turn you first take damage. Extremely strong for setting up big combo turns. The trigger is losing HP (unblocked damage), not taking blocked damage.

STRATEGY: On turn 1, if enemies are attacking, you can intentionally under-block to take a small hit and trigger the draw. In multi-turn fights, the 3 extra cards often let you play a devastating setup turn (Power + burst damage + block all in one turn). Against enemies that buff turn 1 (no damage), the trigger happens on turn 2 instead.

SYNERGIES: Cards that benefit from large hand sizes (Fiend Fire exhausts more cards = more damage). Metallicize (small chip damage still triggers the draw). Fights where you take early chip damage anyway (Louse Curl Up, multi-enemy first attacks).

ANTI-SYNERGY: Full block strategies that prevent all damage delay the trigger. Torii (reduces damage to 1 but still triggers the draw since HP is still lost).
