# Burning Blood (Ironclad Starter Relic)
EFFECT: Heal 6 HP at the end of every combat.

COMBAT RULE: At full HP, up to 6 damage is "free" in hallway fights because Burning Blood refunds it post-combat. This means you can trade HP for tempo (e.g., skip blocking to kill faster) when at max HP. **This buffer does NOT exist if you are below max HP** — taking 6 damage at 70/80 HP means you end the fight at 70/80, not 76/80.

HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Provides baseline healing between fights. At +6/fight, it cannot compensate for fights that drain 30-50 HP (Byrds, Centurion+Mystic). Do not rely on Burning Blood alone for Act 2 sustain — you need Reaper, Feed, or rest sites.
