# Burning Blood
EFFECT: Heal 6 HP at the end of every combat.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Provides baseline healing between fights. At +6/fight, it cannot compensate for fights that drain 30-50 HP (Byrds, Centurion+Mystic). Do not rely on Burning Blood alone for Act 2 sustain -- you need Reaper, Feed, or rest sites.
