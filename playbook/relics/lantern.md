# Lantern
EFFECT: Gain 1 energy on turn 1 of each combat (4 energy turn 1 instead of 3).
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Enables stronger openers. Play a setup Power AND still have energy for damage/block on turn 1. Weaker than Ancient Tea Set (+2E but only after rest sites) but more consistent. Use the extra energy on turn 1 -- don't waste it.
