# Charon's Ashes
EFFECT: Whenever a card is exhausted, deal 3 damage to ALL enemies.
HOW IT CHANGES DECISIONS: Creates an exhaust damage engine. With True Grit (exhaust per turn) and Dark Embrace (draw on exhaust), every exhaust = 3 AOE damage + draw. Over 14 turns: ~30+ total damage. DANGER: encourages over-exhausting, which can thin the deck below survival threshold. Don't exhaust cards just for 3 damage -- consider whether you need the cards for later turns.
