# Strength

Each point of Strength adds 1 damage to every Attack card played. Permanent within combat (unless modified by Disarm or enemy debuffs).

**RESETS BETWEEN COMBATS.** Strength from Inflame, Spot Weakness, Demon Form, and Strength Potion does NOT carry over to the next fight. Only relic Strength (Vajra +1) persists. At the start of each new combat, Strength is 0 (plus relic bonuses). This is a confirmed recurring error.

Negative Strength (from Lagavulin debuffs): reduces all attack damage by that amount. Strike goes from 6 to 5 to 4. Recalculate all damage values after Strength changes.
