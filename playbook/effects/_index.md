# Effects Index

Status effects and stats that modify combat.

## Debuffs
- [Vulnerable](vulnerable.md) — +50% damage taken from attacks
- [Weak](weak.md) — -25% attack damage dealt
- [Frail](frail.md) — -25% block from cards

## Stats
- [Strength](strength.md) — +1 attack damage per point (resets between combats)
- [Dexterity](dexterity.md) — +1 block per point from block cards
