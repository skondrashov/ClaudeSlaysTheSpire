# Ironclad

## Starting Deck

- 5 Strike (Attack, 1E, 6 damage)
- 4 Defend (Skill, 1E, 5 block)
- 1 Bash (Attack, 2E, 8 damage, apply Vulnerable 2)

**Starting Relic: Burning Blood** — heal 6 HP at end of every combat. At full HP, up to 6 damage is "free" in hallway fights. This buffer does NOT exist below max HP.

## Key Mechanics

The Ironclad has no unique combat system (unlike Defect orbs or Watcher stances). Its identity comes from:
- **Strength scaling**: Inflame, Spot Weakness, Demon Form, Limit Break
- **Exhaust synergies**: Corruption + Feel No Pain + Dark Embrace
- **Self-damage trades**: Offering, Hemokinesis, Brutality (risky)
- **HP recovery**: Reaper, Feed, Burning Blood
