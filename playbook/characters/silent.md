# Silent

## Starting Deck

- 5 Strike (Attack, 1E, 6 damage)
- 5 Defend (Skill, 1E, 5 block)
- 1 Survivor (Skill, 1E, 8 block, discard 1 card)
- 1 Neutralize (Attack, 0E, 3 damage, apply Weak 1)

**Starting Relic: Ring of the Snake** — draw 2 additional cards on Turn 1 (7 cards instead of 5).

## Key Mechanics

- **Poison**: Noxious Fumes, Deadly Poison, Crippling Cloud. Scales over time — each turn poison deals its value then decreases by 1.
- **Shivs**: 0-cost attacks that deal 4 damage (6 with Accuracy). Shiv generators: Blade Dance, Cloak and Dagger, Infinite Blades, Storm of Steel.
- **Discard synergies**: Survivor, Acrobatics, Calculated Gamble, Tactician, Reflex.
- **Weak application**: Neutralize, Leg Sweep, Malaise, Sucker Punch.
