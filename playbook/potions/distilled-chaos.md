# Distilled Chaos (Potion)
Plays the top card of your draw pile for free (costs 0 energy).

WHEN TO USE: When draw pile contains high-cost cards (Immolate+, Barricade, Demon Form) that would normally consume your entire turn's energy. The free play is incredible value. Also good when you need an extra action but have 0 energy.

KEY INTERACTION: The card played is random (top of draw pile), so outcomes vary. Best used when draw pile is stacked with good options. After Battle Trance or other draw effects thin the draw pile, remaining cards are more predictable.

OBSERVED (Run 20): Used vs Gremlin Leader at 11 HP. Drew and played Immolate+ for free, dealing 30 AOE that killed both gremlins. This effectively won the elite fight -- without it, the fight was unwinnable at that HP level.

SYNERGIES: Decks with expensive Power cards, Immolate/heavy AOE in draw pile, situations where you need both offense and defense but only have energy for one.
