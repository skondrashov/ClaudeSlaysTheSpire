# Cultist Potion
EFFECT: Gain Ritual 1 (gain +1 Strength at the start of each turn, permanently stacking).
WHEN TO USE: Turn 1 of long fights (bosses, elites). The earlier it is used, the more turns of Strength accumulation. In a 9-turn boss fight, Cultist Potion used Turn 1 provides +1, +2, +3... up to +8 Str by Turn 9. This is comparable to Demon Form (+2/turn) over 4 turns.
SYNERGIES: Rampage (Strength compounds with Rampage's per-play scaling for multiplicative damage growth). Sword Boomerang (multi-hit multiplies Strength bonus). **Shivs + Accuracy (VICTORY-PROVEN)**: Ritual Str stacks with Accuracy bonus on every Shiv. By Turn 10, each 0-cost Shiv deals 23 damage (4 base + 8 Accuracy + 11 Str). This was a key contributor to the first Silent victory. Any long fight where damage scaling matters.
BEST TARGETS: Hexaghost (13-turn fight, massive scaling value), Bronze Automaton (long fight, DPS race), **Time Eater (10-turn fight, Str reached 11 in first Silent victory)**, any boss fight. Do NOT waste on hallway fights -- the fight ends before meaningful scaling occurs.
