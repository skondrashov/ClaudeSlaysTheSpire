# Snecko Oil
EFFECT: Draw 5 cards. Randomize the cost of ALL cards in hand (0-3 energy each), including cards already in hand before the draw.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Desperation plays or burst turns. Very high variance -- can give incredible turns (multiple 0-cost cards) or unplayable hands. Best as a last resort when the standard hand can't win, or when you need a miracle turn.
CONFIRMED INTERACTION: Cards already in hand when Snecko Oil is used also get their costs randomized. This means high-cost cards like Double Tap (1E) can become free, enabling massive burst combos.
