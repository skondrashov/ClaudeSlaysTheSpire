# Snecko Oil
EFFECT: Draw 5 cards. Randomize the cost of ALL cards in hand (0-3 energy each), including cards already in hand before the draw.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Desperation plays or burst turns. Very high variance -- can give incredible turns (multiple 0-cost cards) or unplayable hands. Best as a last resort when the standard hand can't win, or when you need a miracle turn.
CONFIRMED INTERACTION: Cards already in hand when Snecko Oil is used also get their costs randomized. This means high-cost cards like Double Tap (1E) can become free, enabling massive burst combos.
RISK: Randomized costs persist on cards until played. Key cards can become 3E (unplayable at 3E budget). In one observed fight, Strikes were randomized to 3E each, making them dead cards. At low HP, the variance of Snecko Oil can seal a loss by making block cards unplayable on critical survival turns. Do NOT use Snecko Oil when your survival depends on playing specific cards -- use it only when the default hand is already insufficient.
COST RANDOMIZATION AND CARD SELECTION: When multiple copies of a card exist in hand with different randomized costs, the game engine now selects the cheapest copy when a card is played by name. This prevents the issue of accidentally playing a 3E copy when a 0E copy exists.
