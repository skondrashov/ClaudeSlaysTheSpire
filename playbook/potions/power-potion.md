# Power Potion
EFFECT: Choose from 3 random Power cards and add one to your hand. It costs 0 energy this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1 of a boss fight, especially on a free turn. Powers are permanent, so early setup = maximum value. Can find Metallicize (passive block), Inflame (Strength), or other strong Powers. Always choose the permanent scaling option -- avoid Berserk (self-Vulnerable).
