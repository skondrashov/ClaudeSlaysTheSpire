# Fairy in a Bottle
EFFECT: When HP reaches 0, automatically revive with ~30% Max HP. Triggers automatically, no manual use needed.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Keep as safety net for boss fights or dangerous elites. Triggers when you would die, reviving at ~30% Max HP. WARNING: Fairy is often consumed in Centurion+Mystic hallway fights. If possible, save it for elites/bosses -- don't waste it in hallway fights.
NOTE: This is a one-time safety net. Once consumed, it's gone. Plan paths so Fairy is available for the most dangerous fight, not consumed early.
GREMLIN LEADER WARNING: In scaling fights like Gremlin Leader, Fairy can be consumed mid-fight (e.g., turn 6 of an 8-turn fight) when Rally-boosted gremlins spike damage past your remaining HP. Once consumed, there is no safety net for the final turns when Rally scaling peaks (+9 Str to all enemies). Fairy buys one extra turn but does NOT solve the underlying problem of insufficient damage output vs Rally scaling.
