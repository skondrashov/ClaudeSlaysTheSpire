# Smoke Bomb (Potion)

Escape from a non-boss combat. Immediately ends the fight. No rewards are given.

WHEN TO USE: When the fight is unwinnable and continuing will result in death. Confirmed to work mid-combat against hallway fights (used against Snecko at 16 HP). Cannot be used against bosses. Cards can still be played on the same turn as Smoke Bomb (observed: Shockwave + Reaper played after Smoke Bomb use, Reaper healing applied before escape).

DECISION FRAMEWORK: Use Smoke Bomb when estimated incoming damage on the next turn exceeds your current HP + maximum possible block. Do not save it for "a worse situation" -- dying to an avoidable fight is worse than losing rewards from one combat.

VALUE: Extremely high. A single Smoke Bomb can extend a run by multiple floors. Carry one into Act 2 whenever possible as insurance against dangerous hallway fights (3 Cultists, Byrds, Snake Plant) at low HP. Also valuable in Act 1 against Gremlin Gang fights at low HP -- 4 gremlins deal 22+ combined damage on Turn 1, which is lethal at sub-30% HP without strong AOE.

CONFIRMED SAVES: Escaped a lethal Gremlin Gang fight at 19 HP where 38 incoming damage exceeded max block of 16. The escape forfeited card rewards but preserved HP for the boss fight.
