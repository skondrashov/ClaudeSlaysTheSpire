# Strength Potion
EFFECT: Gain +2 Strength for the rest of combat (PERMANENT within the fight).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1, especially on free turns (Gremlin Nob buff turn, Cultist Ritual turn). Every Attack card for the rest of the fight gains +2 damage. The earlier it's used, the more value it provides.
NOTE: This is PERMANENT for the combat. Different from Flex Potion (1 turn only). Strictly better -- use as early as possible.
