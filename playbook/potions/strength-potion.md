# Strength Potion
EFFECT: Gain +2 Strength for the rest of combat (PERMANENT within the fight).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Turn 1, especially on free turns (Gremlin Nob buff turn, Cultist Ritual turn). Every Attack card for the rest of the fight gains +2 damage. The earlier it's used, the more value it provides.
NOTE: This is PERMANENT for the combat. Different from Flex Potion (1 turn only). Strictly better -- use as early as possible.
CRITICAL RULE: If HP is below 35% entering a fight, use this potion IMMEDIATELY on turn 1. Do not save it. A Strength Potion shortens fights by 2-3 turns against high-HP enemies (Shelled Parasite, Centurion+Mystic), which means 2-3 fewer turns of incoming damage. An unused Strength Potion on a death screen means 10-20 damage was left on the table. The potion's value is zero if you die holding it.
POTION USAGE DISCIPLINE: In fights where the enemy is near death from poison or other ticking effects, a single point of direct damage can determine the outcome. Against Giant Head with Metallicize 8, even +2 Str on a Strike turns it from 6 damage (bounces off Metallicize) to 8 damage (exactly breaks through). In any fight approaching lethal, use ALL offensive potions before the window closes. Do not save potions for a fight you might not reach. Potions do not count as card plays (no Slow interaction).
