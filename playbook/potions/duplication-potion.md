# Duplication Potion
EFFECT: This turn, your next card is played twice.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Before a high-value card play. Rampage at 40 damage = 80 total. Carnage+ at 28 = 56 total. Iron Wave+ = double block + double damage (14/14 total). Bash+ = double Vulnerable application (redundant). Best with damage cards for burst or Iron Wave+ for combined offense/defense. Similar to Double Tap but works on any card type (Skills, Powers too).
CONFIRMED USES: Duplicated Iron Wave+ for 14 block + 14 damage against Guardian (correct, defensive value at low HP). Used on Chosen to accelerate kill (correct, preventing HP drain from extended fight). Under Confusion (Snecko fight), duplicated a 0-cost Strike for free 26 damage -- excellent use, targeting a card whose cost was randomly set to 0 for guaranteed value regardless of Confusion RNG. **Best confirmed use: Duplication Potion on 0-cost Immolate+ dealt 60 AOE to both Donu and Deca on Turn 3 of the second victory.** Under Snecko Eye, wait for Immolate+ to roll 0E, then duplicate for double AOE at zero energy cost. This is the highest-value Duplication Potion play observed.
