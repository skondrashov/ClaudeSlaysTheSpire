# Duplication Potion
EFFECT: This turn, your next card is played twice.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Before a high-value card play. Rampage at 40 damage = 80 total. Carnage+ at 28 = 56 total. Iron Wave+ = double block + double damage (14/14 total). Bash+ = double Vulnerable application (redundant). Best with damage cards for burst or Iron Wave+ for combined offense/defense. Similar to Double Tap but works on any card type (Skills, Powers too).
CONFIRMED USES: Duplicated Iron Wave+ for 14 block + 14 damage against Guardian (correct, defensive value at low HP). Used on Chosen to accelerate kill (correct, preventing HP drain from extended fight).
