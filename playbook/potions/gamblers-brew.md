# Gambler's Brew (Potion)

Discard any number of cards from your hand, then draw that many cards.

WHEN TO USE: When the current hand lacks what you need to survive the turn. Discard attack cards to draw block cards (or vice versa). Particularly valuable in boss fights where a specific card type is needed on a critical turn.

DECISION FRAMEWORK: Best used when you can identify which cards in hand are useless for the current turn. Discard all damage cards when you need block to survive, or discard all block when you have a free turn for burst. The more cards you discard, the higher the variance -- but at 2 HP needing block, maximum redraw is correct.

CRITICAL: Never discard block cards (Shrug It Off, Defend, Ghostly Armor) when facing an attack intent, even if the current plan is to burst damage. Block cards are insurance -- discarding them gambles the entire run on the redraw. One observed misplay: discarding Shrug It Off via Gambler's Brew against Chosen resulted in insufficient block, forcing a Smoke Bomb escape and losing both potions.

OBSERVED: Used against The Collector on Turn 4 at 2 HP. Discarded 3 attack cards (Limit Break, Bash+, Immolate), drew Rage+ and Thunderclap -- the Rage+Thunderclap combo provided exactly 15 block vs 14 incoming, surviving with 1 block to spare. Gambler's Brew can convert a dead hand into a survival hand.
