# Speed Potion
EFFECT: Gain +5 Dexterity for the current turn only. Adds 5 block to every block-gaining card played that turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: Before a big incoming damage turn where you play multiple block cards. Defend goes from 5 to 10 block with +5 Dex. Use when you need to survive a massive attack (Inferno, Guardian 32). Best when you can play 2-3 block cards on the same turn (10-15 extra block total). Against multi-enemy fights with high combined damage, Speed Potion on a defensive turn can be fight-saving.
