# Blood Potion
EFFECT: Heal floor(maxHP * 0.2) HP. Confirmed: heals 20% of Max HP (16 HP at 80 Max HP, 16 HP at 83 Max HP).
OPTIMAL TIMING: Use before a dangerous fight to increase survival odds, or mid-fight when HP is critical. Scales with Max HP -- more valuable when Feed has increased the pool. At 80 Max HP, heals 16. At 100 Max HP, heals 20.
