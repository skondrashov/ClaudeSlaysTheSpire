# Liquid Memories
EFFECT: Choose a card from your DISCARD pile, return it to your hand. It costs 0 this turn.
OPTIMAL TIMING: When a key card is in the discard pile and you need it NOW. Retrieve Impervious for a big incoming attack, Bash+ to refresh Vulnerable, or a kill card for a lethal opportunity. The 0 cost makes it free value.

**CRITICAL: Liquid Memories recovers from the DISCARD pile, NOT the EXHAUST pile.** Exhausted cards (Intimidate, Shockwave, Impervious, Reaper) cannot be recovered by Liquid Memories. If a card has been played and says "Exhaust" on it, that card went to the exhaust pile and is gone for the rest of the fight. If a card was played and does NOT exhaust, it went to the discard pile and CAN be recovered. Do not plan around using Liquid Memories to recover exhausted cards -- it will retrieve a different card from discard instead, wasting the potion.
