# 3 Shapes (hallway, Act 3)

Three randomly selected Shapes from the pool: 2x Repulsor, 2x Exploder, 2x Spiker. The encounter picks 3 of these 6.

Possible compositions include any combination of Exploders, Repulsors, and Spikers. See individual entries for each type:
- [Exploder](exploder.md) — 30 HP, explodes Turn 3 for 30 damage
- [Repulsor](repulsor.md) — 29-35 HP, adds Dazed to draw pile
- [Spiker](spiker.md) — 42-56 HP, Thorns that scale each turn

## Kill Priority

1. **Exploders FIRST** — They detonate on Turn 3 for 30 damage each. Must die before detonation. This is non-negotiable.
2. **Repulsors SECOND** — Dazed clogging degrades hand quality. Low HP makes them easy kills.
3. **Spikers LAST** — Thorns are annoying but Spikers' direct damage (7 per turn) is low. Poison bypasses Thorns entirely.

## Strategy

- Check composition at fight start and prioritize Exploders
- AOE is excellent here -- all Shapes have relatively low HP
- If no Exploders are present: kill Repulsors first (stop Dazed generation), then Spikers (use poison if possible)
- If 2 Exploders: split damage to kill both before Turn 3 or accept 30 damage from one while killing the other

## SILENT-SPECIFIC

- Poison is the Silent's best tool here. It kills Spikers without triggering Thorns and provides passive damage on all targets
- Blade Dance / Dagger Spray for fast Exploder kills
- Noxious Fumes hits all 3 targets passively
- Avoid using Shivs on Spikers with high Thorns

## Danger Level

Medium. Exploders are the primary threat. Without them, the fight is low-risk. With 2 Exploders, the Turn 3 timer creates urgency.
