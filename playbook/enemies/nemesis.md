# Nemesis (elite, Act 3, HP: 185)

PATTERN: First turn has 50/50 chance of Scorch (Burns) or Attack. Subsequent turns: ~35% Scorch, ~35% Attack, ~30% Scythe. Cannot use Scorch or Scythe twice in a row. Cannot use Attack three times in a row. Every other turn starting from Turn 2, gains Intangible (all damage reduced to 1 for that turn).

KEY MECHANIC: Intangible. On Intangible turns, ALL damage dealt to Nemesis is reduced to 1 -- including poison ticks, Thorns, everything. This means half your turns deal essentially zero damage. You must maximize damage on non-Intangible turns.

DAMAGE:
- Attack (multi-hit): 6x3 = 18 damage (7x3 = 21 at A3)
- Scythe: 45 damage (single massive hit)
- Scorch: 0 damage, adds 3 Burns to discard pile (5 Burns at A18)

DEBUFFS: Burns added to discard pile (Unplayable, deal 2 damage at end of turn if in hand)

HP: 185 at A0, 200 at A8

WHAT THIS MEANS: The fight has a rhythm -- non-Intangible turns are your damage windows, Intangible turns are for setup/blocking. Scythe (45 damage) is extremely dangerous and requires serious block (Impervious or Weak + multiple block cards). Burns accumulate and clog hand quality over a long fight.

STRATEGY:
- Track the Intangible pattern: Turn 1 (no Intangible), Turn 2 (Intangible), Turn 3 (no), Turn 4 (Intangible), etc.
- On non-Intangible turns: deal maximum damage. Front-load burst here
- On Intangible turns: do NOT waste damage cards. Use the turn for block, setup, Powers, or applying debuffs
- Poison is partially countered -- each tick only deals 1 on Intangible turns. Poison still works but takes twice as long
- Block for Scythe (45 damage). Impervious is ideal. Weak reduces it to 33
- Burns management matters in long fights. Exhaust tools or kill speed to end before Burns overwhelm
- Apply Weak to survive Scythe turns. Without Weak, 45 damage is near-unsurvivable

SILENT-SPECIFIC: Poison is less efficient than usual due to Intangible halving effective poison damage (ticks reduced to 1 every other turn). High-burst poison (Catalyst+) on a non-Intangible turn is still strong. Wraith Form+ provides Intangible for YOU on the dangerous Scythe turns. Piercing Wail reduces the 6x3 multi-hit. Malaise reduces Strength for multiple turns. Well-Laid Plans+ can save burst damage for non-Intangible turns.

DANGER LEVEL: High. Scythe (45 damage) can one-shot at low HP. Intangible doubles effective fight length. Burns clog draws.

APPEARS WITH: Solo encounter.
