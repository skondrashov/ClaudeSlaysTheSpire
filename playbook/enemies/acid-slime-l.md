# Acid Slime L (hallway/boss split, Act 1, HP: 65-69 standalone, or equal to Slime Boss's remaining HP at split)

PATTERN: 30% Corrosive Spit, 40% Tackle, 30% Lick. Cannot use Tackle twice in a row; other moves cannot repeat three times in a row. Splits into 2x Acid Slime (M) when HP drops to 50% or below.

DAMAGE:
- Corrosive Spit: 11 damage + 2 Slimed cards to discard pile (12 at A2)
- Tackle: 16 damage (18 at A2)
- Lick: Applies 2 Weak (no damage)
- Split: At 50% HP or below, spawns 2 Acid Slime (M) with current HP each

KEY MECHANIC: Weak 2 + Split at 50% HP + Slimed card generation. Tackle is the dangerous move at 16 damage.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Killing this enemy creates 2 Acid Slime (M) enemies. In the Slime Boss fight, this means going from 2 enemies to 3 unless you kill both large slimes in quick succession. Weak reduces YOUR damage output, making it harder to finish off remaining enemies.
BRING: Burst to kill before Weak stacks become crippling. AOE for the medium slimes that spawn on split.
AVOID: Killing this while the other large slime is at full HP. Spread damage first.

DANGER LEVEL: Medium. Tackle hits hard (16 damage) but Lick turns are free.
