# Jaw Worm (hallway, Act 1, HP: 40-44)

PATTERN: Always starts with Chomp (11 damage). After Chomp: 59% Bellow, 41% Thrash. After Bellow: 56% Thrash, 44% Chomp. After Thrash: 45% Bellow, 30% Thrash, 25% Chomp (if Thrash used twice in a row: 64% Bellow, 36% Chomp).

KEY MECHANIC: Strength scaling from Bellow. Each Bellow gives +3 Str and 6 Block. Damage increases each turn the fight goes on.

DAMAGE:
- Chomp: 11 damage (12 at A2)
- Bellow: Gains 3 Strength + 6 Block (4 Str at A2, 5 Str + 9 Block at A17)
- Thrash: 7 damage + 5 Block

WHAT THIS MEANS: Turn 1 is always Chomp (11 damage) -- block for it. Turn 2 is likely Bellow (free turn, 0 damage). Kill fast before Strength accumulates. Apply Vulnerable turn 1-2, burst during Bellow turns.
BENCHMARKS: 4-turn kill typical. 10-17 damage per turn with Vulnerable.
BRING: Bash for Vulnerable, Strikes for follow-up.
AVOID: Slow decks that let Strength accumulate.

DANGER LEVEL: Low. Standard Act 1 opener.
