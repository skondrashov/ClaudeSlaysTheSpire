# Spike Slime L (hallway/boss split, Act 1, HP: equal to Slime Boss's remaining HP at split, max 70)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~16 damage. Applies Frail. Adds Slimed cards. Splits into 2x Spike Slime (M) on death.
KEY MECHANIC: Split on death. Creates 2 medium slimes, extending the fight significantly. Frail reduces block gained from cards by 25%.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Prepare for the split -- have enough HP and resources to handle 2 medium slimes after killing the large one. In the Slime Boss fight, killing this while Acid Slime (L) is at full HP creates a 3-enemy nightmare.
BRING: AOE for post-split. Exhaust for Slimed.
AVOID: Long attrition fights (8+ turns drain HP badly). Killing this before softening the other large slime.
