# Spike Slime L (hallway/boss split, Act 1, HP: 64-70 standalone, or equal to Slime Boss's remaining HP at split)

PATTERN: 30% Flame Tackle, 70% Lick. Cannot use same move three times in a row. Splits into 2x Spike Slime (M) when HP drops to 50% or below.

DAMAGE:
- Flame Tackle: 16 damage + 2 Slimed cards to discard pile (18 at A2)
- Lick: Applies 2 Frail (no damage)
- Split: At 50% HP or below, spawns 2 Spike Slime (M) with current HP each

KEY MECHANIC: Split at 50% HP. Creates 2 medium slimes with current HP, extending the fight significantly. Frail 2 reduces block gained from cards by 25%.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Prepare for the split -- have enough HP and resources to handle 2 medium slimes after the split. In the Slime Boss fight, killing this while Acid Slime (L) is at full HP creates a 3-enemy nightmare.
BRING: AOE for post-split. Exhaust for Slimed.
AVOID: Long attrition fights (8+ turns drain HP badly). Killing this before softening the other large slime.

DANGER LEVEL: Medium.
