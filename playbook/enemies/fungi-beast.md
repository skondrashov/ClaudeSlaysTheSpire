# Fungi Beast (hallway, Act 1-2, HP: ~19, appears in pairs or with Shelled Parasite)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~9 damage. Has buff turns (free damage windows). On death, applies Spore Cloud: 2 Vulnerable to the player.
KEY MECHANIC: Spore Cloud on death. Killing one makes you take 50% more damage from the survivor.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill one when the other is NOT attacking. Block on the turn you expect to kill one, because Vulnerable from Spore Cloud amplifies the next hit.
BENCHMARKS: Low HP, but the Spore Cloud makes kill order important.
BRING: Enough block to handle post-kill Vulnerable.
AVOID: Killing one on a turn when the other attacks.
ACT 2 PAIRING: Appears with Shelled Parasite in Act 2. This is a dangerous combination -- Fungi Beast's Spore Cloud applies Vulnerable, which makes Shelled Parasite's 13+ damage attacks hit for 19+. Kill Fungi Beast first to avoid giving Shelled Parasite a damage amplifier, but be prepared to block heavily on the turn it dies (Vulnerable from Spore Cloud + Shelled Parasite attack).
