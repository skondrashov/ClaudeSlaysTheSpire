# Fungi Beast (hallway, Act 1-2, HP: 22-28, appears in pairs or with Shelled Parasite)

PATTERN: Each turn, 60% chance Bite, 40% chance Grow. Cannot use Bite 3 times in a row. Cannot use Grow twice in a row.

KEY MECHANIC: Strength scaling from Grow (+3 Str per use) + Spore Cloud 2 on death (applies 2 Vulnerable to player).

DAMAGE:
- Bite: 6 damage (base, scales with accumulated Strength)
- Grow: Gains 3 Strength
- Spore Cloud 2: On death, applies 2 Vulnerable to player

Strength scaling example: After 1 Grow = 9 Bite, after 2 Grows = 12 Bite, after 3 Grows = 15 Bite. Two Fungi Beasts that both attack on the same turn deal combined 18 (early) to 27+ (late). The fight becomes lethal if it drags past Turn 5-6 due to compounding Strength.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast. Unlike static enemies, Fungi Beasts scale with time. A 6-turn fight against 2 Fungi Beasts faces 27 combined damage on the final turn. Kill one when the other is NOT attacking to avoid Spore Cloud Vulnerability + attack in the same turn.
BENCHMARKS: Must kill within 5-6 turns or Str scaling makes incoming unblockable. HP is higher than documented in some pairings (observed 22-28 HP range in Act 1).
BRING: Enough burst to kill one in 3-4 turns. Block for the escalating later turns. AOE is excellent (both are low-HP, AOE shortens the fight before scaling gets dangerous).
AVOID: Killing one on a turn when the other attacks. Long fights (Str scaling is the real threat, not raw HP). Curse clogging (Parasite or other curses reduce effective hand size, making it harder to both block and deal damage against scaling enemies).
ACT 2 PAIRING: Appears with Shelled Parasite in Act 2. This is a dangerous combination -- Fungi Beast's Spore Cloud applies Vulnerable, which makes Shelled Parasite's 13+ damage attacks hit for 19+. Kill Fungi Beast first to avoid giving Shelled Parasite a damage amplifier, but be prepared to block heavily on the turn it dies (Vulnerable from Spore Cloud + Shelled Parasite attack).
