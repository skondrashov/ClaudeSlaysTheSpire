# The Maw (Act 3 Hallway, HP: 300)

Tanky single-target enemy with Roar on Turn 1 and escalating damage. Long fight (8-9 turns) that rewards defensive setup over burst. HP does NOT change with Ascension.

PATTERN:
- **Turn 1**: Roar (applies 3 Weak + 3 Frail). No damage.
- **Turns 2+**: After Drool -> 50/50 Slam or Nom Nom. After Slam -> 50/50 Nom Nom or Drool. After Nom Nom -> always Drool.

DAMAGE:
- Roar: 0 damage, applies 3 Weak + 3 Frail
- Slam: 25 damage (30 at A2)
- Nom Nom: 5xN damage (N increases -- roughly current turn / 2 rounded up)
- Drool: 0 damage, gains 3 Strength (5 at A5)

Multiple free turns (Drool and Roar intents) allow Power setup without HP loss.

KEY MECHANICS:
- **STRONG_DEBUFF Turn 1**: Applies Weak, Frail, and Vulnerable simultaneously. Frail reduces block by 25%, Weak reduces attack by 25%, Vulnerable increases damage taken by 50%. Lasts 2-3 turns. Plan to block through Frail-reduced values.
- **Escalating damage**: Early attacks are trivial (5-16 damage), but later attacks reach 33-56+. Must have block scaling online before the big hits arrive.
- **Multiple BUFF turns**: The Maw frequently buffs instead of attacking, giving free turns for Power setup. This makes Barricade, Metallicize, Inflame, and Evolve exceptionally strong -- each free turn compounds their value.

STRATEGY:
- **Setup on free turns.** The Maw gives 3-4 free turns across the fight. Use every one for Power cards (Barricade, Metallicize, Inflame, Evolve).
- **Barricade + Metallicize is the solution.** Block from early turns carries over, stacking higher each turn. By the time damage escalates to 33-56, the block wall exceeds incoming. With 2x Metallicize (7 block/turn) + Barricade, zero damage taken is achievable.
- **Mummified Hand + Power-heavy decks excel.** Multiple Power plays on free turns trigger Mummified Hand repeatedly, enabling 3-4 free plays per turn.
- **Headbutt + Immolate+ loop for damage.** Headbutt places Immolate+ on top of draw pile for guaranteed 28-31 damage on the next draw.
- **Feed is safe to exhaust early.** Feed cannot kill the Maw (300 HP), so play it for 10 damage and thin the deck. Save Feed for killable enemies in other fights.

WHAT NOT TO DO:
- Waste Bloodletting+ on Turn 1. The STRONG_DEBUFF turn is free -- Bloodletting's -3 HP matters more here because Vulnerable increases future damage taken.
- Ignore the Frail debuff. Block values are reduced by 25% during Frail turns. Account for this in block math (Defend+ = 6 with Frail, not 8).
- Rush the kill. The Maw has ~300 HP -- this is a 8-9 turn fight minimum. Defensive setup is more important than early burst.
