# Snecko (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~114-120)
PATTERN: Turn 1 applies Confusion debuff (randomizes card costs). Subsequent turns: alternates between attacks (13-22 damage) and debuff turns. Can apply Vulnerable to the player, making subsequent attacks deal 50% more damage. The Confusion + Vulnerable combo is Snecko's kill pattern.
KEY MECHANIC: Confusion debuff. When applied, all cards in hand are randomly assigned costs of 0-3 each turn for the rest of combat. This can make expensive cards free or cheap cards unplayable. Persists until combat ends.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast before cost confusion ruins multiple turns. With good RNG, confused turns can be better than normal (0-cost Bash+, 0-cost Fiend Fire, 2-cost Demon Form). With bad RNG, entire turns are wasted on unplayable 3-cost cards. Apply Vulnerable early to maximize every successful attack turn. Metallicize is excellent here since Powers are played once (before or after Confusion) and provide guaranteed value every turn regardless of cost rolls. Demon Form is particularly strong: if drawn at reduced cost under Confusion, play it immediately for guaranteed scaling regardless of future cost rolls.
HP IS HIGH (~114-120): Despite being a hallway fight, Snecko is tanky. Plan for a 5-7 turn fight even with good damage.
BRING: Burst damage, Vulnerable, Powers (guaranteed value regardless of cost rolls), Metallicize (passive block every turn), Duplication Potion (duplicate a card that Confusion set to 0-cost for guaranteed free value), Gambler's Brew (redraws hand with new randomized costs -- effectively re-rolls Confusion RNG. Confirmed lifesaver at 7 HP when initial Confusion hand was unplayable).
AVOID: Long fights where cost confusion compounds. Relying solely on expensive cards (2-3 cost) since they may be randomly assigned 3 cost anyway.
CONFUSION + X-COST INTERACTION: Whirlwind (X-cost) under Confusion uses ALL remaining energy regardless of randomized cost. Confirmed: Whirlwind+ at 2E remaining dealt 2 hits even under Confusion. X-cost cards are reliable under Confusion because they always consume whatever energy is left.
NOTE: Red Mask relic applies 1 Weak to Snecko at combat start, slightly reducing first attack.
ESCAPE OPTION: Smoke Bomb works against Snecko (confirmed). If Confusion has randomized costs badly and survival is impossible, escaping with Smoke Bomb is correct. Losing rewards is better than dying. Snecko's Confusion + Vulnerable combo can create unwinnable damage math within 3-4 turns.
