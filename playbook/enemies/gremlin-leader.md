# Gremlin Leader (elite, Act 2, HP: ~131)
PATTERN: Summons Sneaky Gremlins (~10-12 HP each). Uses DEFEND_BUFF (Rally): gives ALL enemies +3 Strength permanently and stacking. Gremlins attack for ~9 base + accumulated Str. Leader itself attacks for significant damage when not rallying. Leader re-summons gremlins when they die -- killing gremlins is temporary relief, not permanent removal.
KEY MECHANIC: Rally gives +3 Str to ALL enemies (Leader + all gremlins). Permanent and stacking. After 2 rallies: every enemy has +6 Str. The fight's damage scales exponentially with turn count. With +3 Str after one rally, gremlins deal 9+3=12 each. With two gremlins attacking, that is 24 damage from gremlins alone, plus the Leader's own attacks.
WHAT THIS MEANS: KILL GREMLINS FAST to reduce per-turn damage, but prioritize Leader damage when possible because gremlins get re-summoned. AOE is critical for clearing gremlins efficiently. Apply Shockwave early for mass Weak + Vulnerable. The fight becomes mathematically unwinnable once Strength stacks too high (by turn 8+, incoming damage is 49+).
BENCHMARKS: Must win by turn 6-7 or exhaust cards run out and scaling overwhelms you. Enter with 60%+ HP minimum. At 25% HP (14/56), the fight is unwinnable even with Nilry's Codex generating Impervious and Immolate.
BRING: AOE (Thunderclap, Whirlwind, Immolate+), Shockwave+ (mass debuff), Disarm (reduce Leader Str). Non-exhaust damage cards for sustained fighting. Rage (0E power that generates block per attack -- excellent value in multi-attack turns). Potions -- this fight demands aggressive potion use.
AVOID: Entering below 60% HP. Exhaust-heavy decks (once Impervious/Shockwave/Disarm are gone, remaining deck is too weak). If below 50% HP, take any alternative path. Decks with reduced Max HP (e.g., post-Vampires at 56 Max HP) have no margin for error against this fight's scaling.
