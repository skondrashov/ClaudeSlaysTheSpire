# Gremlin Leader (elite, Act 2, HP: high)
PATTERN: Summons Sneaky Gremlins (~10-12 HP each). Uses DEFEND_BUFF (Rally): gives ALL enemies +3 Strength permanently and stacking. Gremlins attack for ~9 base + accumulated Str.
KEY MECHANIC: Rally gives +3 Str to ALL enemies (Leader + all gremlins). Permanent and stacking. After 2 rallies: every enemy has +6 Str. The fight's damage scales exponentially with turn count.
WHAT THIS MEANS: KILL GREMLINS FAST. Dead gremlins don't benefit from future rallies. AOE is critical. Apply Shockwave early for mass Weak + Vulnerable. The fight becomes mathematically unwinnable once Strength stacks too high (by turn 8+, incoming damage is 49+).
BENCHMARKS: Must win by turn 6-7 or exhaust cards run out and scaling overwhelms you. Enter with 60%+ HP minimum. At 13% HP, the fight is unwinnable regardless of play quality.
BRING: AOE (Thunderclap, Whirlwind), Shockwave+ (mass debuff), Disarm (reduce Leader Str). Non-exhaust damage cards for sustained fighting.
AVOID: Entering below 60% HP. Exhaust-heavy decks (once Impervious/Shockwave/Disarm are gone, remaining deck is too weak). If below 50% HP, take any alternative path.
