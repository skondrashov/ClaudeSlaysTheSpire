# Gremlin Leader (elite, Act 2, HP: 140-148)

PATTERN: Move selection depends on gremlin count:
- 0 gremlins: Rally! 75% / Stab 25%
- 1 gremlin (last was Encourage): Rally! 50% / Stab 50%
- 1 gremlin (last was Stab): Rally! 62.5% / Encourage 37.5%
- 2+ gremlins: Encourage 66% / Stab 34%
Cannot use any intent twice in a row.

DAMAGE:
- Stab: 6x3 = 18 damage
- Encourage: All enemies gain 3 Strength + other gremlins gain 6 Block (Str scales to 4-5, Block to 10-18 at higher Ascensions)
- Rally!: Summons 2 random Gremlins (max 3 small gremlins at a time)

HP: 140-148 at A0, 145-155 at A8

Starts with 2 gremlins already present. Leader re-summons gremlins when they die -- killing gremlins is temporary relief, not permanent removal.
KEY MECHANIC: Rally gives +3 Str to ALL enemies (Leader + all gremlins). Permanent and stacking. After 2 rallies: every enemy has +6 Str. The fight's damage scales exponentially with turn count. With +3 Str after one rally, gremlins deal 9+3=12 each. With two gremlins attacking, that is 24 damage from gremlins alone, plus the Leader's own attacks.
RALLY SCALING MATH: Rally fires roughly every other turn. By turn 3, all enemies have +3 Str. By turn 5, all enemies have +6 Str. By turn 7, all enemies have +9 Str. With 2 gremlins alive at +9 Str, gremlins alone deal (9+9)*2 = 36, plus the Leader's own attacks. Total incoming by turn 7-8 exceeds 50 per turn. Every wasted turn is effectively +3 Str to the entire board -- a single lost turn (e.g., ending turn early after Battle Trance draw without playing drawn cards) can be the difference between winning and losing.
WHAT THIS MEANS: TURN ECONOMY IS EVERYTHING. Every turn must deal maximum damage or apply critical debuffs. Wasting even one turn (misplaying end-of-turn, failing to use drawn cards) allows Rally to stack once more, which compounds across all gremlins. KILL GREMLINS FAST to reduce per-turn damage, but prioritize Leader damage when possible because gremlins get re-summoned. AOE is critical for clearing gremlins efficiently. Apply Shockwave early for mass Weak + Vulnerable. The fight becomes mathematically unwinnable once Strength stacks too high (by turn 3-4 with multiple gremlins alive, incoming damage is 40+).
BENCHMARKS: Must win by turn 5-6 or Rally scaling overwhelms you. Enter with 60%+ HP minimum AND at least 1 potion. At 52% HP (42/80) with Inflame+ and 3 potions, died on turn 9 -- Inflame+ + Impervious on Turn 1 (zero damage) extended the fight past the survivable window. By turn 5, Rally had stacked to +12 Str on all enemies (4 Rallies), producing 34+ incoming/turn at 23 HP. At 55% HP (44/80) with zero potions and no Strength scaling, died on turn 4 to 47 incoming damage (Leader 12x3=36 + Mad Gremlin 11). At 56% (56/98) was insufficient even with Corruption + Feel No Pain engine. At 25% HP (14/56), unwinnable even with Nilry's Codex. At 4 HP (Silent) with +9 Str Rally and 5 Apparitions exhausted, 28 incoming vs 20 max block was lethal -- poison engine had Leader at only ~40% HP with insufficient time remaining. Bloated decks (19+ cards with 4+ Strikes) cannot draw answers fast enough against Rally's exponential scaling.
TURN 1 PRIORITY: Deal damage, NOT setup. Playing Inflame+ (1E setup) + Impervious (2E block) on Turn 1 means zero damage dealt. This wastes the most valuable turn against Rally scaling. The correct Turn 1 against Gremlin Leader is Inflame+ + the best available Attack (Bash+, Strike+, Rampage). Take the unblocked hit -- HP lost on Turn 1 is recoverable, but a wasted damage turn against Rally scaling is not. Impervious should be saved for Turn 4-5 when Rally-scaled damage is 34+ per turn and block is critical for survival, not Turn 1 when incoming is only ~22. Confirmed: Inflame+ + Impervious on Turn 1 at 42 HP led to a 9-turn fight (needed 5-6), death at 10 HP.
BRING: AOE (Thunderclap, Whirlwind, Immolate+) for gremlin clearing, Shockwave+ (mass debuff), Disarm (reduce Leader Str). Non-exhaust damage cards for sustained fighting. Rage (0E power that generates block per attack -- excellent value in multi-attack turns). Potions -- this fight demands aggressive potion use. Fairy in a Bottle as safety net (but expect it to be consumed early if the fight goes long -- a Fairy consumed on turn 6 leaves no safety for turns 7-8 when Rally scaling peaks).
CORRUPTION + FNP ENGINE: Corruption makes Skills free, Feel No Pain generates block per exhaust. This engine is theoretically strong here but ONLY if it comes online by turn 2-3. If the engine activates on turn 7 of 8, Rally has already stacked to +9 Str on all enemies and incoming damage exceeds all available block. The engine needs early activation or it arrives too late.
STRENGTH SCALING IS MANDATORY: Without permanent Strength, you cannot kill the Leader fast enough. In run 98, Mutagenic Strength (+3 Str turn 1 only) enabled 35 damage to Leader on turn 1, but by turn 2 with 0 Str, damage output collapsed. The DPS race against Rally requires at least +2 permanent Strength (Inflame, Spot Weakness, Demon Form) or enough burst cards (Immolate + Heavy Blade + Bash+) to close the fight in 5 turns. Evaluate whether your deck can realistically kill 144 HP in 5-6 turns BEFORE entering this fight.
GREMLIN WIZARD PRIORITY: If Gremlin Wizard is present, it charges for a massive 28-damage nuke. Kill it before it fires. In run 98, Thunderclap+ (7 AOE + Vuln) into Pommel Strike+ (15 Vuln damage) = 22 damage exactly killed a 22 HP Wizard on turn 3, preventing 28 damage that would have been instantly fatal. Always check Wizard charge timer and prioritize killing it on the turn before it fires.
SILENT-SPECIFIC: The Silent's poison engine (Noxious Fumes + Bouncing Flask + Catalyst+) is excellent against the Leader -- poison ignores the 6 Metallicize block all enemies gain per turn. A Thousand Cuts provides passive AOE damage per card played, chipping gremlins down. Apparitions are critical survival tools: 5 Intangible turns buy time for poison to tick while Rally stacks Str harmlessly. Piercing Wail reduces all enemies' Str for 1 turn, buying a critical survival turn at peak Rally scaling. At 4 HP vs +9 Str Rally scaling (28+ incoming), even Piercing Wail + Fear Potion + Noxious Fumes was insufficient -- the fight was unwinnable at that HP. The Silent MUST kill the Leader before Apparitions run out, or have enough block density + Piercing Wail to bridge the gap.
AVOID: Entering below 60% HP. Entering without potions. Entering without permanent Strength scaling (Ironclad) or poison engine (Silent). Exhaust-heavy decks (once Impervious/Shockwave/Disarm are gone, remaining deck is too weak). If below 50% HP, take any alternative path. Decks with reduced Max HP (e.g., post-Apparitions at 29 Max HP or post-Vampires at 56 Max HP) have no margin for error against this fight's scaling. Wasting turns -- do NOT batch `end` with draw effects like Battle Trance; the drawn cards need to be played that same turn.
