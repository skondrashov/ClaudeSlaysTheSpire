# 3 Cultists (hallway, Act 2, 3 Cultists with Ritual 3 each, ~50 HP each)

**THIS IS THE MOST LETHAL HALLWAY FIGHT IN THE GAME. Six deaths across all runs -- more than any other single encounter. A hallway fight that scales like an elite and has killed at 5%, 30%, 37%, 39%, 52%, and 53% HP entry.**

PATTERN: Turn 1: ALL three use Ritual (+3 Str each, 0 damage). Subsequent turns: attacks that escalate. Each Cultist gains +3 Str/turn independently = +9 total Str/turn across the group.
KEY MECHANIC: Independent Ritual +3 Str EACH per turn. Triple independent scaling means combined incoming damage grows by +9 EVERY TURN. No other hallway fight has this escalation rate.

SCALING MATH:
- Turn 2: Each Cultist has 3 Str. Base attack 6 + 3 = 9 each. Combined: 27 damage.
- Turn 3: Each has 6 Str. 6+6=12 each. Combined: 36 damage.
- Turn 4: Each has 9 Str. 6+9=15 each. Combined: 45 damage.
- Turn 5: Each has 12 Str. 6+12=18 each. Combined: 54 damage.
- Turn 7: Each has 18 Str. 6+18=24 each. Combined: 72 damage.
- With Weak applied: multiply by 0.75 (Turn 2 weakened = 20 combined).

WHAT THIS MEANS: Turn 1 is free -- use it for Powers (Metallicize, Inflame) and initial damage on one target. Turn 2-3: play Shockwave+ on the FIRST ATTACK TURN for mass Weak + Vulnerable. Focus fire one Cultist at a time -- each dead Cultist permanently removes one scaling threat. The fight becomes unwinnable if it goes past turn 6-7 with all 3 alive.

KILL PRIORITY: Kill one Cultist ASAP. Reducing from 3 to 2 cuts incoming by 33%. Reducing to 1 makes the fight trivial. Each turn a Cultist stays alive, it gets permanently stronger.

BENCHMARKS: **Enter with 50%+ HP MINIMUM -- this is a hard threshold, not a guideline.** Six encounters confirm this: deaths at 5%, 30%, 37%, 39%, 52%, and 53% HP entry. The 5% entry (Run 112) was caused by Snake Plant draining HP from 49 to 7 on the prior floor -- the classic Act 2 death spiral of consecutive combat rooms. Even at 53% HP with Immolate (AOE), Impervious+ (40 block), and Calipers (block retention), Ritual scaling outpaced blocking. Even strong defensive tools cannot compensate for low HP entry because the fight demands both burst kill speed AND sustained block for 4-6 turns simultaneously. The math is simple: if you cannot survive 4 turns of 18-27 combined damage per turn (after Weak), you die.

WHY DECKS FAIL HERE: Slow scaling engines (Demon Form, Limit Break) need 2-3 free setup turns that do not exist at low HP. Even fast scaling (Rampage) killed one Cultist but could not finish the second before combined damage was lethal. Even strong block tools (Impervious+, Calipers) only delay the inevitable if kill speed is insufficient -- Ritual scaling is +9 combined Str/turn, and block generation cannot keep pace past turn 5-6. The fight demands BOTH burst damage AND sustained block for 4-6 turns simultaneously. Without a healing card, HP attrition from earlier Act 2 fights (Spheric Guardian, Looter/Mugger) frequently pushes entry HP below the 50% threshold.

BRING: Shockwave+ (MUST play on first attack turn), Powers for free turn setup (Inflame, Metallicize), single-target burst for focus fire (Rampage, Fiend Fire, Iron Wave+), immediate block (Defend, Ghostly Armor).
AVOID: Entering below 50% HP (three deaths confirm this). Delaying Shockwave+ past the first attack turn. Spreading damage across all 3 instead of focus-firing. Using numeric card indices (index shifting has caused fatal misplays in this fight). Relying solely on slow scaling (Demon Form) without fast burst to complement it.
