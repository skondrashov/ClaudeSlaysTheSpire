# 3 Cultists (hallway, Act 2, 3 Cultists with Ritual 3 each)
PATTERN: Turn 1: ALL three use Ritual (+3 Str each, 0 damage). Subsequent turns: attacks that escalate +9 total Str/turn. By turn 4: ~15 damage each. By turn 7: ~21 damage each.
KEY MECHANIC: Independent Ritual +3 Str EACH per turn. This is the fastest-scaling fight in the game.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Turn 1 is free -- use it for AOE damage + Vulnerable setup (Thunderclap). Turn 2: play Shockwave+ IMMEDIATELY for mass Weak + Vulnerable. Focus fire one Cultist at a time -- each dead Cultist removes one scaling threat. The fight becomes unwinnable if it goes past turn 7.
BENCHMARKS: Turn 2 combined damage: ~27. Turn 4: ~45. Turn 7: ~63. Enter with 50%+ HP.
BRING: Shockwave+ (MUST play turn 2), AOE (Thunderclap), single-target burst for focus fire.
AVOID: Delaying Shockwave+ past turn 2. Entering below 50% HP. Spreading damage across all 3 instead of focus-firing.
