# Repulsor (hallway, Act 3, HP: 29-35)

PATTERN: Each turn, 80% chance to use Bash (adds 2 Dazed to draw pile), 20% chance to use Repulse (attack). Cannot use Repulse twice in a row.

KEY MECHANIC: Dazed card generation. Dazed cards are Unplayable + Ethereal status cards that clog your draw pile. Each Bash adds 2 Dazed, rapidly degrading hand quality. Over 4 turns, 6-8 Dazed cards can be added, making it impossible to draw meaningful hands.

DAMAGE:
- Repulse: 11 damage (13 at A2)

DEBUFFS: Adds 2 Dazed cards to draw pile per Bash use

WHAT THIS MEANS: Repulsors are not directly dangerous (low attack damage, low HP) but they degrade your deck quality, making OTHER enemies more dangerous. In multi-enemy fights, Repulsors make it harder to draw block and damage cards when you need them.

STRATEGY:
- Kill after Exploders but before Spikers in the 3 Shapes encounter
- Low HP (29-35) means they die quickly to any focused damage
- Evolve converts Dazed into card draws (if available)
- The fight is a minor annoyance for strong decks but can cripple thin decks that rely on specific card draws

SILENT-SPECIFIC: Dazed clogging is extra painful for the Silent since key combo pieces (Catalyst+, Wraith Form) need to be drawn at the right time. Tools of the Trade helps filter Dazed cards. Well-Laid Plans+ retains key cards across turns. Poison ignores deck clogging once applied.

DANGER LEVEL: Low. Direct threat is minimal. Danger is indirect -- Dazed clogging makes companion enemies harder to deal with.

APPEARS WITH: Other Shapes (Spikers, Exploders) in the 3 Shapes encounter.
