# Mugger (hallway, Act 2, HP: 48-52)

Act 2 version of the Looter. Steals gold and deals significant damage.

PATTERN: Turns 1-2: Mug (10 damage + steals gold). Turn 3+: 50% Lunge (16 damage) or 50% Smoke Bomb (11 Block). After Smoke Bomb, uses Escape next turn (flees with stolen gold). Same sequence as Looter but higher damage and block values.

DAMAGE:
- Mug: 10 damage (steals gold)
- Lunge: 16 damage
- Smoke Bomb: 0 damage, gains 11 Block
- Escape: Flees combat with stolen gold

KEY MECHANIC: Gold theft, similar to Looter but appears in Act 2 where enemy damage is higher overall.

APPEARS WITH: Often paired with a Looter in the Looter + Mugger encounter. Combined damage from both enemies is 20+ per turn with no free turns. This is a dangerous multi-enemy fight if you lack block or AOE.

STRATEGY: Kill one enemy quickly to reduce incoming damage. Prioritize the higher-damage enemy first. AOE (Cleave, Thunderclap, Immolate) is efficient here. Do NOT play expensive setup cards (3E Powers like Corruption or Barricade) on turn 1 -- the combined unblocked damage from both enemies will be 20+ and potentially fatal.

BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns. Every extra turn costs gold and HP.

BRING: Burst damage, AOE, sufficient block for 20+ combined incoming.

AVOID: Slow setup turns with no block. Playing 3E powers unprotected.
