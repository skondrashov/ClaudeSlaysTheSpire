# Chosen (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~95)
PATTERN: Applies Hex (adds Dazed to draw pile when you play Skills). Applies Weak to player. Gains +3 Strength over the fight. Attacks escalate from ~13 to 16+ to 36+.
KEY MECHANIC: Hex. Every Skill card played adds a Dazed to draw pile. Blocking is actively costly -- each Defend clogs the deck.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Minimize Skill usage. Front-load damage with Attacks. Bash+ is safe (Attack). Kill before Strength buffs make attacks lethal. With Weak on you, recalculate damage: Strike = floor((6+Str)*0.75).
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 5-6 turns. Damage output drops from Weak -- plan accordingly.
BRING: Attack-heavy deck. Iron Wave (Attack that blocks). Inflame (Strength overcomes Weak penalty). Bash+ (Attack, applies Vulnerable).
AVOID: Playing Defend unless absolutely necessary (each one adds Dazed). Slow kill (Strength scaling is lethal).
