# Blue Slaver (hallway, Act 1, HP: 46-50)

PATTERN: Each turn, 60% chance to use Stab, 40% chance to use Rake. Cannot use the same attack three times in a row.

KEY MECHANIC: Applies Weak via Rake. Moderate sustained damage with no free turns.

DAMAGE:
- Stab: 12 damage (13 at A2)
- Rake: 7 damage + 1 Weak (8 damage + 1 Weak at A2)

DEBUFFS: Weak (from Rake)

HP: 46-50 at A0

WHAT THIS MEANS: Straightforward damage dealer. Slightly less dangerous than Red Slaver (no Entangle, lower Stab damage). Weak from Rake reduces your damage output, extending the fight slightly.

STRATEGY:
- Apply Vulnerable, burst down. Standard 3-4 turn kill
- Block on Stab turns (12 damage), damage on Rake turns (only 7 damage)
- No special mechanics to worry about

SILENT-SPECIFIC: Straightforward fight. Neutralize for Weak on Turn 1, then burst with Strikes. Poison handles this efficiently.

DANGER LEVEL: Low. Below-average threat for Act 1.

APPEARS WITH: Can appear solo in Act 1 Hard Pool. Also appears in the Act 2 Slavers elite (alongside Red Slaver + Taskmaster).
