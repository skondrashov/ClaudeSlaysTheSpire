# Reptomancer (elite, Act 3, HP: 180-190)

PATTERN: Always opens with Summon Dagger. Afterward uses approximately equal distribution of Snake Strike, Big Bite, and Summon Dagger. Cannot use Snake Strike or Big Bite twice in a row. Cannot use Summon Dagger three times in a row. If Summon Dagger is chosen with 4 Daggers already in play, uses Snake Strike instead. Maximum 4 Daggers active at any time.

KEY MECHANIC: Dagger minions. Daggers are summoned with 20-25 HP and follow a fixed pattern: Stab (9 damage + adds Wound to discard) -> Explode (25 damage, Dagger dies). Each Dagger is a 2-turn time bomb. If not killed before Explode, it deals 25 damage. Multiple Daggers exploding on the same turn is lethal.

DAMAGE:
- Big Bite: 30 damage (34 at A3)
- Snake Strike: 13x2 = 26 damage + 1 Weak (16x2 = 32 at A3)
- Dagger Stab: 9 damage + 1 Wound to discard
- Dagger Explode: 25 damage (Dagger dies)

HP: Reptomancer 180-190. Daggers 20-25 each.

DEBUFFS: Weak from Snake Strike. Wound cards from Dagger Stab.

COMBAT SETUP: Fight starts with Reptomancer + 2 Daggers (3 enemies total).

WHAT THIS MEANS: This is one of the most dangerous fights in the game. The starting 2 Daggers will Stab Turn 1 (9 damage each + Wounds) then Explode Turn 2 (25 damage each = 50 total from Daggers alone). Meanwhile Reptomancer herself deals 26-30 damage. Total incoming Turn 2 can exceed 75 damage. You MUST kill Daggers before they Explode.

STRATEGY:
- KILL DAGGERS IMMEDIATELY. Each Dagger that explodes deals 25 damage. Kill them on their Stab turn before Explode
- AOE is critical. Die Die Die, Blade Dance + Accuracy, Dagger Spray all hit multiple targets. Cards that damage all enemies are the highest priority
- After clearing initial Daggers, focus damage on Reptomancer. She resummons, but reducing her HP means fewer total Dagger waves
- Big Bite (30 damage) requires serious block. Track her pattern to anticipate Big Bite turns
- Weak from Snake Strike reduces your damage -- re-apply Vulnerable to compensate

SILENT-SPECIFIC: This is the Silent's best elite matchup in Act 3. Die Die Die+ (17x3 AOE) can kill all Daggers in one play. Blade Dance + Accuracy provides cheap multi-target damage. Piercing Wail reduces all enemy damage (critical for surviving multi-Dagger turns). Corpse Explosion turns killed Daggers into AOE damage against Reptomancer. Poison on Reptomancer ticks while you manage Daggers.

KILL PRIORITY: Daggers about to Explode > Daggers that just Stabbed > Reptomancer

DANGER LEVEL: Extreme. Combined burst from Dagger Explodes + Reptomancer attacks can deal 75+ in a single turn. Without AOE or fast Dagger removal, this fight ends runs.

APPEARS WITH: Solo encounter (Reptomancer + her summoned Daggers).
