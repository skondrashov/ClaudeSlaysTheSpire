# Centurion + Mystic (hallway, Act 2, Centurion HP: ~65, Mystic HP: ~49)
PATTERN: Mystic heals Centurion ~12 HP/turn and gains ~15 block. Centurion attacks for 12-18 damage per turn. Both can have buff/defend turns (free damage windows).
KEY MECHANIC: Mystic heals Centurion. Centurion is unkillable while Mystic lives.
WHAT THIS MEANS: KILL MYSTIC FIRST. Non-negotiable. If you attack Centurion while Mystic is alive, you're in an unwinnable attrition war. Focus all damage on Mystic despite Centurion being the bigger per-turn threat. Apply Weak to Centurion to reduce incoming while you kill Mystic. Expect 6-12 turns.

THE DRAIN PATTERN: This fight reliably drains 20-30+ HP even with correct target priority and debuffs applied. Centurion's sustained damage over the 6-12 turns needed to kill Mystic through its block accumulates to lethal levels at low HP. The fight functions as an HP attrition check -- if you enter below 50%, the cumulative Centurion damage will leave you dead or at critical HP for the next fight. Observed drains: 29 HP to 9 HP (20 HP lost) with Shockwave+ and Reaper available. In run 98, entered at 39 HP (49%), killed Mystic by turn 4, but had only 2 HP remaining by the time Centurion phase started. The Centurion solo phase then required 6 more turns (Centurion at 77 HP with +2 Str), surviving at 2 HP for multiple consecutive turns with Metallicize+ and careful blocking.
MYSTIC HEALS BOTH ENEMIES: Mystic heals Centurion AND itself. In run 98, Mystic healed Centurion back to full (77/77) after turn 2, negating 26 damage dealt on turn 1. The +3 temporary Strength from Mutagenic Strength was wasted because Mystic healed through all the turn-1 burst. This reinforces KILL MYSTIC FIRST -- never split damage, even with Mutagenic Strength or temporary damage buffs, because Mystic healing negates any Centurion damage.

BENCHMARKS: Kill Mystic in 5-6 turns. Expect 25-42 HP lost even with correct play. Enter with 50+ HP (49% entry in run 98 left 2 HP after fight -- barely survived). Fairy in a Bottle is often consumed in this fight -- save it for elites if possible.
BRING: Burst damage for Mystic (need to break through 15 block/turn). Weak for Centurion. Double Metallicize is excellent. Headbutt+ (12+ damage, 13 Vuln through block). Stone Calendar is ideal here -- the fight naturally lasts 7+ turns, and the 52 damage nuke lands automatically on Centurion after Mystic is dead.
LETHAL FINISH PATTERN: Once Mystic is dead, if you are at very low HP (under 10), look for lethal combos that kill Centurion before his attack phase. In run 98, at 2 HP vs Centurion at 23 HP, Thunderclap+ (7 dmg + Vuln) into Immolate (31 Vuln damage) = 38 total killed Centurion during the player turn, preventing a fatal 14-damage attack. Always check if you can burst the remaining Centurion in one turn rather than blocking.
AVOID: Attacking Centurion while Mystic lives. Entering below 50% HP. Wasting Fairy here when elites are ahead. Splitting damage between Centurion and Mystic even with temporary Strength buffs.

OBSERVED FIGHT LENGTHS: This fight can extend to 10 turns with insufficient burst for Mystic. At 10 turns, even Metallicize and Weak on Centurion cannot prevent 30-65 HP drain. Stone Calendar (52 damage on turn 7) helps close out the fight before attrition becomes lethal.
