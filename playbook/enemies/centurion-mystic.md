# Centurion + Mystic (hallway, Act 2, Centurion HP: ~65, Mystic HP: ~49)
PATTERN: Mystic heals Centurion ~12 HP/turn and gains ~15 block. Centurion attacks for 12-18 damage per turn. Both can have buff/defend turns (free damage windows).
KEY MECHANIC: Mystic heals Centurion. Centurion is unkillable while Mystic lives.
WHAT THIS MEANS: KILL MYSTIC FIRST. Non-negotiable. If you attack Centurion while Mystic is alive, you're in an unwinnable attrition war. Focus all damage on Mystic despite Centurion being the bigger per-turn threat. Apply Weak to Centurion to reduce incoming while you kill Mystic. Expect 6-12 turns.

THE DRAIN PATTERN: This fight reliably drains 20-30+ HP even with correct target priority and debuffs applied. Centurion's sustained damage over the 6-12 turns needed to kill Mystic through its block accumulates to lethal levels at low HP. The fight functions as an HP attrition check -- if you enter below 50%, the cumulative Centurion damage will leave you dead or at critical HP for the next fight. Observed drains: 29 HP to 9 HP (20 HP lost) with Shockwave+ and Reaper available.

BENCHMARKS: Kill Mystic in 5-6 turns. Expect 25-42 HP lost even with correct play. Enter with 50+ HP. Fairy in a Bottle is often consumed in this fight -- save it for elites if possible.
BRING: Burst damage for Mystic (need to break through 15 block/turn). Weak for Centurion. Double Metallicize is excellent. Headbutt+ (12+ damage, 13 Vuln through block). Stone Calendar is ideal here -- the fight naturally lasts 7+ turns, and the 52 damage nuke lands automatically on Centurion after Mystic is dead.
AVOID: Attacking Centurion while Mystic lives. Entering below 50% HP. Wasting Fairy here when elites are ahead.

OBSERVED FIGHT LENGTHS: This fight can extend to 10 turns with insufficient burst for Mystic. At 10 turns, even Metallicize and Weak on Centurion cannot prevent 30-65 HP drain. Stone Calendar (52 damage on turn 7) helps close out the fight before attrition becomes lethal.
