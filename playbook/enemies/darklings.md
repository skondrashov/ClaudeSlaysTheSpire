# Darklings (Act 3 Hallway)

Three enemies. Each has moderate HP. Life Link mechanic: if any Darkling is killed while others survive, the killed Darkling revives at half HP at the start of the next turn.

## Mechanics

- **Life Link**: Must kill all 3 Darklings simultaneously (same turn) or they revive at half HP. This is the defining mechanic -- partial kills are wasted damage.
- Each Darkling attacks independently. Combined incoming can reach 25-35 damage/turn.
- Darklings can buff and debuff on non-attack turns.

## Strategy

1. **AOE is mandatory.** Immolate+ is the ideal card -- deals enough damage to kill all 3 simultaneously. Without AOE, the Life Link mechanic makes this fight near-unwinnable.
2. **Soften all 3 to kill range first.** Use single-target attacks to bring each Darkling to low HP, then use a single AOE play to finish all 3 at once.
3. **Save AOE for the kill turn.** Do not waste AOE early when Darklings are at full HP. Build Strength, whittle them down, then deliver the killing AOE blow.
4. **Simultaneous AOE kills bypass Life Link.** If all 3 die in the same turn, none revive. This was confirmed in the second victory where Darklings were killed with simultaneous AOE.

## What You Need

- AOE damage (Immolate+, Whirlwind+, Cleave)
- Strength scaling to ensure the AOE kill hits hard enough
- Block density for the 4-6 turns of softening before the kill turn
- 50%+ HP entry recommended
