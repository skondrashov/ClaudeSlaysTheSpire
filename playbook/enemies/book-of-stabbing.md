# Book of Stabbing (elite, Act 2, HP: 161)
PATTERN: Multi-hit attacks that gain +1 hit every turn. Each hit deals ~7 damage. Turn 1: 7x2 (~14). Turn 2: 7x3 (~21). Turn 3: 7x4 (~28). Turn 4: 7x5 (~35). Turn 5: 7x6 (~42). Each attack adds Wound status cards (Unplayable, persist in deck).
KEY MECHANIC: Scaling multi-hit + Wound generation. The hit count increases by 1 every turn AND Wounds clog draws, preventing you from finding block or offense. This creates a death spiral.

THE WOUND CLOG PROBLEM: By turn 6-7, Wounds can comprise 60%+ of the deck. A 21-card deck accumulating 3+ Wounds per turn means that by mid-fight, you draw 3 Wounds and 2 playable cards per turn. This makes it impossible to find block AND offense in the same hand. Strong cards like Double Tap, Reaper, Inflame become buried under Wounds. The fight becomes unwinnable not because of raw damage scaling but because the deck stops functioning.

WHAT THIS MEANS: Front-load damage -- every turn the fight goes on, attacks get worse AND Wounds clog draws. Apply Vulnerable immediately. Use exhaust tools for Wound removal. Enter with HIGH HP (60%+ minimum). Deck size matters: a 15-card deck survives Wound clog better than a 21-card deck because each Wound is a smaller percentage of draws.

BENCHMARKS: Even with strong burst, expect 5+ turns. 140 damage dealt in 5 turns still left Book at 21 HP in one fight. At 6 HP entry, the fight is unwinnable. At 23 HP entry, the fight is unwinnable even with Demon Form scaling -- the escalating multi-hit outpaces block before Strength ramp matters. At 80 HP entry with a 21-card deck (no exhaust tools), HP went from 80 to 3 over 7 turns -- barely survivable even at full HP. Minimum entry: 60% HP with exhaust tools, or avoid entirely.

BRING: Exhaust tools for Wounds (True Grit+, Burning Pact, Second Wind, Fiend Fire) -- THIS IS THE #1 PRIORITY. Burst damage front-loaded in turns 1-3. Vulnerable application (Bash+). Dropkick+ (free cycling when Vulnerable). Weak (reduces each individual hit).

AVOID: Entering below 60% HP. Entering with a large deck (20+ cards) and no exhaust. Slow play (each turn is worse than the last). Flex and other low-impact cards that dilute draws.

DECK REQUIREMENTS: If your deck has no exhaust tools (True Grit+, Burning Pact, Second Wind, Fiend Fire), DO NOT fight Book of Stabbing voluntarily. The Wound clog WILL overwhelm any deck that cannot remove status cards from the draw pile. This is not a "nice to have" -- it is mandatory for decks above 15 cards.
