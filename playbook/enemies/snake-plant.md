# Snake Plant (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~67-75)
PATTERN: Attacks for 9x3=27 damage per turn (multi-attack). Has turns where it applies Frail and Weak as debuffs instead of attacking. Has Malleable mechanic. Can appear in Unknown rooms.
KEY MECHANIC: **Malleable** -- gains increasing block each time it is attacked in a single turn. First hit in a turn: gains ~3 block. Second hit: more block. This punishes multi-hit strategies and rewards single large hits per turn. Also applies Frail (block reduced 25%) and Weak (damage reduced 25%), making the fight harder over time. The Frail + Weak combination with 9x3 multi-attacks is devastating: Frail cripples your block while 27 incoming overwhelms weakened defenses. This can drain 40+ HP in a single fight even from high HP.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Use single large attacks rather than many small hits per turn. Apply Vulnerable early. Clothesline/Uppercut for Weak reduces 9x3=27 to 6x3=18. Kill in 4-5 turns before debuffs stack. The debuff turn is a free turn for offense.
BRING: Burst damage (Immolate+, Carnage+, Rampage), Clothesline/Uppercut for Weak, single large hits. Bloodletting for extra energy on burst turns. **Strength scaling is critical** -- without Inflame or similar, even correct play (single large hits, Vulnerable applied) results in a 7-turn fight draining 40+ HP. With Strength scaling, the fight shortens to 4-5 turns and HP loss drops to 15-25.
AVOID: Multi-hit attacks which trigger Malleable block repeatedly. Whirlwind, Twin Strike, Sword Boomerang, and especially Pummel (4-5 hits at 2 base damage each) are the worst offenders. Pummel against Snake Plant generates 3+4+5+6 = 18 block from Malleable alone, making its 8-10 damage effectively zero. Two Snake Plant encounters with Whirlwind and Pummel in the deck can drain 40+ HP total if multi-hit cards are played carelessly. Use single large hits (Bludgeon, Carnage, Hemokinesis) instead. Entering at low HP -- 27 damage/turn with Frail making block weaker is a death spiral.
DANGER: Run 112 played the Snake Plant fight correctly (single large hits, Bash+ for Vulnerable, avoided Whirlwind) but the fight still lasted 7 turns and drained HP from 49 to 7 (42 HP lost) because the deck had ZERO Strength scaling. Without Strength, Weak+Malleable reduce each hit to 3-6 effective damage, making kills take 2-3 extra turns of 21-27 incoming. The Snake Plant fight into 3 Cultists at 7 HP was lethal. **This fight is a Strength scaling check** -- decks without it will lose 40+ HP even with correct tactics.
NOTE: Appears in Unknown rooms (not just hallway combat). Cannot be avoided if map is routed through Unknown rooms at low HP.
