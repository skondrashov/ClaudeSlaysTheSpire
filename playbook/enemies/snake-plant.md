# Snake Plant (hallway, Act 2, HP: ~67-75)
PATTERN: Attacks for 7x3=21 damage per turn (multi-attack). Has turns where it applies Frail and Weak as debuffs instead of attacking. Has Malleable mechanic. Can appear in Unknown rooms.
KEY MECHANIC: **Malleable** -- gains increasing block each time it is attacked in a single turn. First hit in a turn: gains ~3 block. Second hit: more block. This punishes multi-hit strategies and rewards single large hits per turn. Also applies Frail (block reduced 25%) and Weak (damage reduced 25%), making the fight harder over time.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Use single large attacks rather than many small hits per turn. Apply Vulnerable early. Clothesline/Uppercut for Weak reduces 7x3=21 to 5x3=15. Kill in 4-5 turns before debuffs stack. The debuff turn is a free turn for offense.
BRING: Burst damage (Immolate+, Carnage+, Rampage), Clothesline/Uppercut for Weak, single large hits. Bloodletting for extra energy on burst turns.
AVOID: Multi-hit attacks (Whirlwind, Twin Strike) which trigger Malleable block repeatedly. Entering at low HP -- 21 damage/turn with Frail making block weaker is a death spiral. At 38 HP entry (Run 20), fight drained to 17 HP even with Immolate+ burst.
NOTE: Appears in Unknown rooms (not just hallway combat). Cannot be avoided if map is routed through Unknown rooms at low HP.
