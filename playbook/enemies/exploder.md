# Exploder (hallway, Act 3, HP: 30)

PATTERN: Fixed sequence: Slam -> Slam -> Explode. Uses Slam twice, then detonates on Turn 3. The Explosive timer starts at 3 and decreases by 1 at end of each turn. When it reaches 0, Exploder uses Explode and dies.

KEY MECHANIC: Explode deals 30 damage and kills the Exploder. This is a hard timer -- if you cannot kill the Exploder in 2 turns, you take 30 damage on turn 3. Explosion damage does NOT ignore block.

DAMAGE:
- Slam: 9 damage (11 at A2)
- Explode: 30 damage (kills self)

BUFFS: Explosive 3 (countdown timer, decreases by 1 each turn)

WHAT THIS MEANS: You have exactly 2 turns to kill the Exploder or eat 30 damage. At 30 HP, this is extremely dangerous. Kill priority is highest among the Shapes -- Spikers and Repulsors are annoying but not immediately lethal.

STRATEGY:
- KILL FIRST in any multi-enemy fight. 30 HP is very low -- most decks can kill in 1-2 turns
- Front-load all damage on Exploders. Even at the cost of taking Slam damage (9), preventing the 30 explosion is correct
- Poison can handle Exploders if applied heavily on Turn 1 (need 30+ total poison ticks in 2 turns)
- AOE can efficiently damage Exploders alongside other enemies
- Block the Explosion if you cannot kill in time -- it does NOT bypass block

SILENT-SPECIFIC: Deadly Poison+ (12 poison) on Turn 1 kills the Exploder before detonation (12 + 11 = 23 by end of Turn 2... need supplemental damage). Noxious Fumes alone is too slow (2 + 3 = 5 poison by Turn 2). Best approach: direct attacks + Backstab for burst.

DANGER LEVEL: High if not killed quickly. The 30-damage explosion can end runs at low HP. Low if you have burst damage or AOE.

APPEARS WITH: Other Shapes (Spikers, Repulsors) in the 3 Shapes encounter.
