# Orb Walker (Act 3 Hallway, HP: ~93)

Fast-scaling single enemy. Gains +3 Strength per turn. Must burst down before damage becomes unsurvivable.

PATTERN:
- Attacks every turn with increasing damage as Strength accumulates.
- Turn 1: ~15 damage.
- Turn 2+: Damage increases by 3 per turn (+3 Str per buff cycle).
- No free turns -- blocks or attacks every turn.

KEY MECHANICS:
- **Strength scaling (+3/turn)**: By Turn 3, attacks deal 21+. By Turn 5, attacks deal 27+. This makes the fight a DPS race -- every extra turn costs significantly more HP.
- **Moderate HP (~93)**: Killable in 2-3 turns with Strength scaling and Immolate+ burst.

STRATEGY:
- **Kill fast.** This is a DPS race, not a block fight. Inflame+ + Immolate+ + Strike+ can kill in 2 turns.
- **Bloodletting+ enables Turn 1 burst.** Extra energy on Turn 1 allows massive front-loaded damage (Evolve+ + Immolate+ + multiple attacks).
- **Headbutt + Immolate+ guarantees a Turn 2 kill.** Headbutt places Immolate+ on top of draw pile for 28-31 guaranteed damage on Turn 2.
- **Feed kills are valuable.** At ~93 HP, Feed can land the killing blow for +3 max HP. Prioritize Feed as the last hit.

WHAT NOT TO DO:
- Set up Powers on Turn 1. Barricade, Metallicize, and Evolve waste energy that should go to damage. The fight is too short for setup to pay off.
- Let the fight go past Turn 4. By then, incoming damage exceeds 24+ and HP drain accelerates.
