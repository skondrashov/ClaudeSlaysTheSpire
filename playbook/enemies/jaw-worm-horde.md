# Jaw Worm Horde (hallway, Act 3, HP: 40-44 each, 3 enemies)

Three Jaw Worms that start combat with the effects of Bellow already applied (+3 Strength and 6 Block each). This is an Act 3 Hard Pool encounter.

PATTERN: Unlike Act 1 Jaw Worm (which always opens with Chomp), Act 3 Jaw Worms start with Bellow effects pre-applied and their first action is randomized: 45% Bellow, 30% Thrash, 25% Chomp.

MOVES (same as regular Jaw Worm):
- Chomp: 11 damage (12 at A2)
- Bellow: Gains 3 Strength + 6 Block (4 Str + 6 Block at A2)
- Thrash: 7 damage + 5 Block

Starting state: Each Jaw Worm begins with +3 Strength (from pre-applied Bellow). So Turn 1 Chomp deals 14, Thrash deals 10.

AI after first action:
- After Chomp: 59% Bellow, 41% Thrash
- After Bellow: 56% Thrash, 44% Chomp
- After Thrash: 45% Bellow, 30% Thrash, 25% Chomp (cannot Thrash 3x in a row: 64% Bellow, 36% Chomp)

KEY MECHANIC: Triple independent Strength scaling. Each Jaw Worm gains Strength independently. Starting at +3 each, a single Bellow round pushes them to +6 each. Combined damage escalates rapidly: Turn 1 with all attacking = 30-42 combined, Turn 3 = 40-60+ depending on Bellow count.

WHAT THIS MEANS: This is a DPS race against triple scaling. AOE is critical for managing three 40-44 HP targets. Focus fire kills one quickly to reduce incoming by 33%.

STRATEGY:
- AOE is mandatory. Die Die Die, Blade Dance + Accuracy, Dagger Spray, Corpse Explosion
- Focus fire one Jaw Worm while AOE chips the others
- Apply Weak or Piercing Wail to reduce combined incoming on big attack turns
- Kill order: whichever has lowest HP first, then next lowest
- The fight becomes unmanageable after 4-5 turns if all 3 are still alive and scaling

SILENT-SPECIFIC: Noxious Fumes applies poison to all 3 simultaneously. Piercing Wail reduces all 3 enemies' Strength. Die Die Die+ for burst AOE. Blade Dance provides multi-target damage. Wraith Form for emergency Intangible if scaling gets out of control.

DANGER LEVEL: High. Triple Strength scaling makes this one of the most dangerous Act 3 hallway fights. Combined Turn 1 damage can be 30+ with no free setup turn.

APPEARS WITH: Solo encounter (3 Jaw Worms only).
