# Looter (hallway, Act 1, HP: 44-48)

PATTERN: Turns 1-2: Mug (10 damage + steals gold). Turn 3+: 50% Lunge (12 damage) or 50% Smoke Bomb (6 Block). After Smoke Bomb, uses Escape next turn (flees with stolen gold, combat ends). If Lunge on Turn 3, Smoke Bomb follows on Turn 4, then Escape Turn 5.

KEY MECHANIC: Thievery -- steals gold whenever it attacks. Stolen gold is returned if the Looter is killed. If it escapes, gold is lost permanently.

DAMAGE:
- Mug: 10 damage (steals gold)
- Lunge: 12 damage
- Smoke Bomb: 0 damage, gains 6 Block
- Escape: Flees combat with stolen gold

WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill by Turn 3-4 to prevent escape and recover stolen gold. If Smoke Bomb intent appears, you have one turn left before it flees.
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns to limit theft and prevent escape.
BRING: Burst damage.
AVOID: Slow play (hemorrhages gold and risks escape).

ACT 2 PAIRING: Looter appears alongside Mugger in an Act 2 hallway fight (Looter + Mugger). Combined damage is 20+ per turn with no free setup turns. This pairing punishes expensive setup cards (Corruption at 3E, Barricade at 3E) because you cannot afford a turn without block against two enemies attacking simultaneously. AOE and fast kills are critical.
