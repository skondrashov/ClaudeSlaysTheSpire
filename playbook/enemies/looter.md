# Looter (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~44-48)
PATTERN: Steals 15 gold per turn. Attacks for 10+ damage. May try to escape after stealing enough.
KEY MECHANIC: Gold theft. Each turn it lives costs 15 gold.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast to minimize gold loss. Prioritize over non-stealing enemies in multi-enemy fights.
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns to limit theft.
BRING: Burst damage.
AVOID: Slow play (hemorrhages gold).

ACT 2 PAIRING: Looter appears alongside Mugger in an Act 2 hallway fight (Looter + Mugger). Combined damage is 20+ per turn with no free setup turns. This pairing punishes expensive setup cards (Corruption at 3E, Barricade at 3E) because you cannot afford a turn without block against two enemies attacking simultaneously. AOE and fast kills are critical.
