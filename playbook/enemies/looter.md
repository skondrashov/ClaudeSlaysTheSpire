# Looter (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~44-48)
PATTERN: Steals 15 gold per turn. Attacks for 10+ damage. May try to escape after stealing enough.
KEY MECHANIC: Gold theft. Each turn it lives costs 15 gold.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Kill fast to minimize gold loss. Prioritize over non-stealing enemies in multi-enemy fights.
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns to limit theft.
BRING: Burst damage.
AVOID: Slow play (hemorrhages gold).
