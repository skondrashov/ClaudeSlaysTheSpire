# Small Slimes (hallway, Act 1)

Encounter consists of an Acid Slime (S) + Spike Slime (S), or variants with medium slimes. This is an Easy Pool encounter.

## Acid Slime (S) — HP: 8-12

MOVES:
- Lick: Applies 1 Weak (no damage)
- Tackle: 3 damage

PATTERN: 50/50 between Lick and Tackle. Cannot use same move three times in a row.

## Spike Slime (S) — HP: 10-14

MOVES:
- Tackle: 5 damage

PATTERN: Uses Tackle every turn. No alternate moves.

## Acid Slime (M) — HP: 28-32

MOVES:
- Corrosive Spit: 7 damage + 1 Slimed to discard
- Lick: Applies 1 Weak
- Tackle: 10 damage

PATTERN: 30% Corrosive Spit, 40% Tackle, 30% Lick. Cannot use Tackle twice in a row; other moves cannot repeat three times in a row.

## Spike Slime (M) — HP: 28-32

MOVES:
- Flame Tackle: 8 damage + 1 Slimed to discard
- Lick: Applies 1 Frail

PATTERN: 30% Flame Tackle, 70% Lick. Cannot use same move three times in a row.

STRATEGY:
- Trivial encounter. Small slimes have very low HP and damage
- Kill the Acid Slime first to avoid Weak stacking
- No special preparation needed
- Good fight for preserving HP early in Act 1

DANGER LEVEL: Low. One of the easiest encounters in the game.
