# Cultist (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~50-53)
PATTERN: Turn 1: Ritual/Incantation (gains +3 Strength, does NOT attack). Subsequent turns: attacks with escalating damage (base 6 + 3 per turn of Ritual).
KEY MECHANIC: Ritual +3 Strength per turn. Turn 1 is completely free.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Go all-in turn 1 (Bash + Strikes for 17+ damage). Kill by turn 3-4 before attacks become dangerous. By turn 4, Cultist attacks for 15+.
BENCHMARKS: Kill in 3-4 turns. Turn 1 free damage: 17-21.
BRING: Bash for turn 1 Vulnerable setup, burst attacks.
AVOID: Slow play. Every turn increases incoming damage.
ACT 2 APPEARANCES: Cultist appears paired with Chosen in Act 2 hallway fights. In this pairing, Cultist still buffs turn 1 (free). Kill Cultist first since it has lower HP and its Strength scaling becomes dangerous fast. The Chosen will apply Hex/Vulnerable but attacking it while Cultist scales is worse.
