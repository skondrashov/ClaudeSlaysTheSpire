# Spike Slime M (hallway, Act 1, HP: 28-32)

PATTERN: 30% Flame Tackle, 70% Lick. Cannot use same move three times in a row.

DAMAGE:
- Flame Tackle: 8 damage + 1 Slimed card to discard pile (10 at A2)
- Lick: Applies 1 Frail (no damage)

KEY MECHANIC: Frail (reduces block by 25%) + Slimed card generation (clogs deck).
WHAT THIS MEANS: Mostly uses Lick (70% of turns), so blocking needs are moderate. Exhaust Slimed cards when possible. Frail reduces block effectiveness.
BENCHMARKS: Standard fight. Kill in 3-4 turns.
BRING: Exhaust tools for Slimed cards.
AVOID: Long fights where Slimed cards accumulate.

DANGER LEVEL: Low.
