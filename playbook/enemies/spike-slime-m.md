# Spike Slime M (hallway, Act 1, HP: ~28)
PATTERN: Attacks for ~8 damage. Has debuff turns (applies Frail instead of attacking). Adds Slimed status cards to deck.
KEY MECHANIC: Frail + Slimed card generation.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Exhaust Slimed cards when possible. Block is reduced by Frail.
BENCHMARKS: Standard fight. Kill in 3-4 turns.
BRING: Exhaust tools for Slimed cards.
AVOID: Long fights where Slimed cards accumulate.
