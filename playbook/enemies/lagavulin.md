# Lagavulin (elite, Act 1, HP: 110)
PATTERN: Starts asleep with 8 block/turn. Wakes when attacked or after 3 turns. After waking: alternates between attack (18 damage) and debuff (-1 Strength, -1 Dexterity permanently).
KEY MECHANIC: Permanent -1 Str/-1 Dex debuffs. These stack and never go away. Each debuff turn makes all your attacks and block cards permanently worse.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Wake it immediately with Bash (Vulnerable > chipping through 8 block). Front-load damage on debuff turns (free turns). Kill before Strength debuffs accumulate. After 2+ debuff turns, recalculate all damage values: Strike goes from 6 to 5 to 4.
BENCHMARKS: 7-turn fight typical. Expect 20-36 HP damage. Use Weak Potion to reduce 18 to 13.
BRING: Bash+ for Vulnerable, Headbutt+ (top-deck Bash+ for Vulnerable refresh), burst damage, Weak source.
AVOID: Slow play. Every debuff turn compounds the problem.
