# Lagavulin (elite, Act 1, HP: 109-111)

PATTERN: Starts asleep with Metallicize 8 (gains 8 Block every turn). Wakes when it takes any damage (stunned for 1 turn, then attacks) or after 3 turns of no damage (immediately attacks). After waking, Metallicize is removed. Repeating cycle: Attack -> Attack -> Siphon Soul.

DAMAGE:
- Attack: 18 damage (20 at A3)
- Siphon Soul: Applies -1 Strength and -1 Dexterity permanently (-2 each at A18)

HP: 109-111 at A0, 112-115 at A8

KEY MECHANIC: Permanent -1 Str/-1 Dex debuffs from Siphon Soul. These stack and never go away. Each Siphon Soul makes all your attacks and block cards permanently worse.
WHAT THIS MEANS: Wake it immediately with Bash (Vulnerable > chipping through 8 block). Front-load damage on debuff turns (free turns). Kill before Strength debuffs accumulate. After 2+ debuff turns, recalculate all damage values: Strike goes from 6 to 5 to 4.
BENCHMARKS: 7-turn fight typical. Expect 20-36 HP damage. Use Weak Potion to reduce 18 to 13.
BRING: Bash+ for Vulnerable, Headbutt+ (top-deck Bash+ for Vulnerable refresh), burst damage, Weak source.
AVOID: Slow play. Every debuff turn compounds the problem.
