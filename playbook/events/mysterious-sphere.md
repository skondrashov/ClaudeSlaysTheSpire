# Mysterious Sphere (Act 3 Event)

Two options:

1. **[Open Sphere]** -- Fight a combat encounter. Reward: Rare Relic.
2. **[Leave]** -- Walk away. No reward, no risk.

## The Fight

The combat encounter spawns 2 Orb Walkers. See orb-walker.md for mechanics. Key threat: combined Str scaling of +6/turn, plus Burns clogging draws and dealing end-of-turn damage. Even with a strong deck, this fight can drain 40-70 HP.

## Decision Framework

**Open Sphere when:**
- HP is above 70% AND healing is available (rest sites ahead, no Mark of the Bloom)
- The deck can handle 2 Orb Walkers without using irreplaceable boss resources (Catalyst+)
- A Rare Relic could meaningfully improve the boss fight

**Leave when:**
- Mark of the Bloom is active (any damage taken is permanent -- confirmed 66 HP drain from this fight under Mark of the Bloom, leaving 3 HP with no recovery)
- HP is below 50%
- The boss fight is imminent and Catalyst+ would need to be spent here
- The deck already has strong relics and the marginal value of another is low

The Rare Relic is not worth the risk if any damage taken here cannot be recovered before the boss. A dead run gets zero value from a Rare Relic.
