# Masked Bandits (Act 2 Event)

OPTIONS:
1. **Pay gold (toll)** — Lose all gold, avoid combat. The safe option when at low HP.
2. **Fight** — Fight a group of bandits. Standard combat encounter.

WHEN TO PAY: At low HP where combat risks death. Gold is recoverable; HP is not. If below 60% HP and the alternative is a fight that could cascade into a death spiral, pay the toll without hesitation. Gold has no value if you die.

WHEN TO FIGHT: At high HP with a strong deck. The fight is a standard Act 2 hallway encounter. If the deck handles hallway fights cleanly, fight to preserve gold for shops.

STRATEGIC NOTE: This event appeared in Run 72 at critically low HP (4 HP after the Chosen + Byrd fight). Paying the toll was correct -- any combat at 4/80 HP is near-certain death. The toll preserved the run long enough to reach the next rest site.
