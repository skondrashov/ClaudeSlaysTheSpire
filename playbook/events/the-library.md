# The Library

Act 2 event. Choose 1 card from a selection of 20 to add to your deck.

OPTIONS:
- [Read] Browse 20 cards and choose 1 to add to your deck.
- [Leave] Leave without taking a card.

DECISION FRAMEWORK: Always choose [Read]. A pool of 20 cards is by far the largest selection in the game -- dramatically increases the odds of finding a specific card you need. This is the best opportunity to find Reaper, Feed, or other critical cards that may not have appeared in normal rewards.

CRITICAL INTERACTION: At any point in the run where a healing card (Reaper, Feed) has not yet been found, The Library is potentially game-saving. The probability of at least one healing card appearing in 20 random cards is much higher than in the standard 3-card reward.

PRIORITY PICKS from The Library follow the same card evaluation rules as normal rewards, but the larger pool means you are more likely to find the exact card your deck needs rather than settling for the best of 3.
