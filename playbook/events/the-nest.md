# The Nest (Act 2 Event)

OPTIONS:
1. **Smash** (99 Gold): Take 99 gold. Safe, no HP cost.
2. **Rest** (Ritual Dagger): Lose 6 HP, gain Ritual Dagger card (starts at 15 damage, gains +3 per kill, exhausts on use).

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- At low HP (<30), always take gold. 6 HP can be lethal when consecutive fights follow.
- Ritual Dagger is a long-term investment card. Only worth it if many fights remain AND current HP can absorb the 6 HP loss without entering the next fight below threshold.
- 99 Gold is reliable value (enables shop purchases, curse removal, potions).
- If next room is combat and HP is marginal, gold is correct. The 6 HP loss cannot be recovered before the fight.
