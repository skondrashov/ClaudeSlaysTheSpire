# Sssserpent (Act 1 Event)

OPTIONS:
- **[Agree]**: Gain 175 gold. Receive Doubt curse (gain Weak each turn in combat).
- **[Disagree]**: Nothing happens.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- **Check the map for upcoming shops before deciding.** If a shop is within 2-3 floors, TAKE THE GOLD. Curse removal costs 75g at a shop. Net profit: 175 - 75 = 100 gold for free.
- **Refuse only if:** (a) no shop is reachable before a dangerous fight, or (b) the curse cannot be removed before a boss/elite.
- Doubt is a manageable curse -- Weak on yourself reduces your attack damage by 25%, but does not affect block. If the curse gets stuck in hand for 1-2 fights before removal, the cost is reduced damage output, not death.

KEY INSIGHT:
Many events that offer gold + curse are designed with the expectation that you can route to a shop to remove the curse. The correct play is often: take the gold, route to a shop, remove the curse, net profit. This applies to Sssserpent and similar curse-giving events. The exception is Parasite, which cannot be removed at shops.

WHAT NOT TO DO:
- Refuse the gold reflexively because "curses are bad." Evaluate the map first.
- Take the gold when no shop is reachable and the next fight is an elite or boss.
