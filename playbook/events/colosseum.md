# Colosseum (Act 2 Event)

CHOICES:
1. **[Continue]** -> Forced into a 2-wave combat. First wave: 2 Slavers. Second wave: another fight (or option to escape with [COWARDICE]).
2. No alternative -- the event forces the [Continue] choice.

MECHANICS:
- Wave 1: Fight 2 Slavers (Red Slaver + Blue Slaver). Combined ~20+ damage/turn. Entangle debuff from Blue Slaver prevents attacks for 1 turn.
- After Wave 1 victory: Choose [Fight] for Wave 2 rewards or [COWARDICE] to escape with no additional reward.
- This is a FORCED COMBAT event. Juzu Bracelet does NOT prevent it (Juzu prevents monster encounters in Unknown rooms, but Colosseum is an event with forced combat, not a monster room).

DECISION POINTS:
- **[COWARDICE] is correct at low HP.** If HP is below 40% after Wave 1, escaping is the right call. Wave 2 rewards are not worth dying before the boss.
- **[Fight] only if HP is above 50% after Wave 1** and the deck can handle another multi-enemy fight.
- **Wave 2 enemies are unpredictable and can be extremely dangerous.** Wave 2 can include Gremlin Nob, Taskmaster, or other elites. A Skill-heavy deck (especially Defect) facing Gremlin Nob in Wave 2 is near-unwinnable at low HP because Nob's Enrage punishes every Skill played. At 21 HP, a Wave 2 Gremlin Nob + Taskmaster killed a Defect deck that had Snecko Eye + Necronomicon -- raw power cannot compensate for Nob's anti-Skill scaling at critical HP.

ESCAPE DECISION FRAMEWORK:
1. After Wave 1, check HP. Below 30 HP: ALWAYS escape. The Wave 2 enemy pool includes Gremlin Nob, which can kill any deck at low HP.
2. Check deck composition. Skill-heavy decks (Defect, early Silent) face worst-case matchups in Wave 2. Escape unless HP is above 60%.
3. Check potions. If all defensive potions were used in Wave 1, escape -- Wave 2 requires fresh resources.
4. The rewards from Wave 2 are NEVER worth a run-ending death. Colosseum appears mid-Act 2 where the remaining path has plenty of card/relic opportunities.

WARNINGS:
- **Pre-boss HP drain**: This event appears in Act 2 Unknown rooms. If encountered 1-2 floors before the boss, the forced combat can drain 15-25 HP, dropping the player below boss entry thresholds. When pathing before the Act 2 boss, Unknown rooms carry Colosseum risk even with Juzu Bracelet.
- **Entangle**: Blue Slaver's Entangle prevents all attacks for 1 turn. Plan for 1 turn of pure blocking.
- **Gremlin Nob in Wave 2**: Nob + Taskmaster is a confirmed Wave 2 combination. Nob's Enrage (+2 Str per Skill) is devastating for Skill-heavy decks. The Defect's starting composition (6 Skills vs 4 Attacks) and common pickups (Coolheaded, Aggregate, Fission) make this matchup extremely punishing. Even Necronomicon-doubled Attacks cannot compensate if most of the hand is Skills that feed Nob's Strength.
