# Colosseum (Act 2 Event)

CHOICES:
1. **[Continue]** -> Forced into a 2-wave combat. First wave: 2 Slavers. Second wave: another fight (or option to escape with [COWARDICE]).
2. No alternative -- the event forces the [Continue] choice.

MECHANICS:
- Wave 1: Fight 2 Slavers (Red Slaver + Blue Slaver). Combined ~20+ damage/turn. Entangle debuff from Blue Slaver prevents attacks for 1 turn.
- After Wave 1 victory: Choose [Fight] for Wave 2 rewards or [COWARDICE] to escape with no additional reward.
- This is a FORCED COMBAT event. Juzu Bracelet does NOT prevent it (Juzu prevents monster encounters in Unknown rooms, but Colosseum is an event with forced combat, not a monster room).

DECISION POINTS:
- **[COWARDICE] is correct at low HP.** If HP is below 40% after Wave 1, escaping is the right call. Wave 2 rewards are not worth dying before the boss. Run 110: escaped at 27/83 (32%) after Wave 1 -- correct decision, but the HP drain from Wave 1 (37->27) contributed to entering the Act 2 boss at only 57% HP, which proved fatal.
- **[Fight] only if HP is above 50% after Wave 1** and the deck can handle another multi-enemy fight.

WARNINGS:
- **Pre-boss HP drain**: This event appears in Act 2 Unknown rooms. If encountered 1-2 floors before the boss, the forced combat can drain 15-25 HP, dropping the player below boss entry thresholds. When pathing before the Act 2 boss, Unknown rooms carry Colosseum risk even with Juzu Bracelet.
- **Entangle**: Blue Slaver's Entangle prevents all attacks for 1 turn. Plan for 1 turn of pure blocking.
- **Run 110 case study**: Colosseum on Floor 30 drained HP from 37 to 27. Healed to 49/86 at rest site. Entered Collector at 57% -- below 70% threshold. Died Floor 33. The Colosseum HP drain was a direct contributing factor to the boss death.
