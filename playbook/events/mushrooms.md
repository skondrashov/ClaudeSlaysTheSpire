# Mushrooms (Act 1)
OPTIONS:
- [Stomp] Fight 3 Fungi Beasts. Reward: Odd Mushroom relic (Vulnerable 1.5x -> 1.25x for player AND enemies).
- [Eat] Heal 21 HP. Adds Parasite curse to deck.
- [Leave] No effect.

FIGHT DETAILS: The 3x Fungi Beast fight is significantly harder than most Act 1 hallway fights. The Fungi Beasts apply Vulnerable via Spore Cloud on death, and with 3 enemies dealing independent damage, expect 30-44 HP loss even with a decent deck. This is an event fight, NOT a normal hallway -- it can drain more HP than most Act 1 elites.

**PARASITE CURSE WARNING:** The Eat option heals 21 HP but adds a Parasite curse to the deck. Parasite is an Unplayable card that clogs draws permanently until removed at a shop or via exhaust. In a 12-card deck, Parasite reduces effective hand size by ~1 card every other turn, costing block and damage across every subsequent fight. Parasite contributed to a death where 2 Fungi Beasts dealt lethal damage because curse clogging reduced available energy and cards for blocking. Only eat if the 21 HP heal is immediately needed for survival AND a shop or curse-removal method is available soon.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- **Eat (Heal 21 HP + Parasite)** when: HP is below 30% AND a shop is reachable within 2-3 floors for curse removal, OR HP is so critically low that dying before reaching a shop is more likely than the curse killing you. The heal is NOT free -- weigh the Parasite cost.
- **Stomp (Fight)** when: HP is above 70%, the deck does NOT rely on Vulnerable for damage, AND the boss is Guardian or Slime Boss (Odd Mushroom is less harmful against these bosses).
- **Leave** when: HP is above 40% or no shop is available to remove Parasite within 3 floors. The Parasite curse is worse than 21 HP in most situations.

**CRITICAL WARNING -- HEXAGHOST ANTI-SYNERGY:** Odd Mushroom reduces Vulnerable from 1.5x to 1.25x on enemies. Against Hexaghost, where kill speed determines whether Burns overwhelm you, this ~17% damage reduction extends the fight by 1-2 turns and results in 1-2 additional Burns added to the deck. Confirmed as a contributing factor in a death: the 3x Fungi Beast fight drained 40 HP (44 to 4 HP), and the Odd Mushroom relic then made the Hexaghost fight longer, leading to lethal Burns accumulation with Hexaghost at only 24% HP remaining. Take the Eat option or Leave instead.

PRIORITY: Leave if HP above 40% or no shop nearby (Parasite cost outweighs heal). Eat only at critical HP with shop access. Stomp only at 70%+ HP with non-Hexaghost boss.
