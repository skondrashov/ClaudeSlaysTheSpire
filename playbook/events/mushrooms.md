# Mushrooms (Act 1)
OPTIONS:
- [Stomp] Fight 3 Fungi Beasts. Reward: Odd Mushroom relic (Vulnerable 1.5x -> 1.25x for player AND enemies).
- [Eat] Heal 22 HP.
- [Leave] No effect.

FIGHT DETAILS: The 3x Fungi Beast fight is significantly harder than most Act 1 hallway fights. The Fungi Beasts apply Vulnerable via Spore Cloud on death, and with 3 enemies dealing independent damage, expect 30-44 HP loss even with a decent deck. This is an event fight, NOT a normal hallway -- it can drain more HP than most Act 1 elites.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- **Eat (Heal 22 HP)** when: HP is below 50%, OR Hexaghost is the Act 1 boss (Odd Mushroom anti-synergy with Hexaghost is confirmed lethal -- see below), OR the deck relies heavily on Bash+/Vulnerable for damage output.
- **Stomp (Fight)** when: HP is above 70%, the deck does NOT rely on Vulnerable for damage, AND the boss is Guardian or Slime Boss (Odd Mushroom is less harmful against these bosses).
- **Leave** when: HP is above 80% and you want to avoid both the fight and the relic.

**CRITICAL WARNING -- HEXAGHOST ANTI-SYNERGY:** Odd Mushroom reduces Vulnerable from 1.5x to 1.25x on enemies. Against Hexaghost, where kill speed determines whether Burns overwhelm you, this ~17% damage reduction extends the fight by 1-2 turns and results in 1-2 additional Burns added to the deck. Confirmed as a contributing factor in a death: the 3x Fungi Beast fight drained 40 HP (44 to 4 HP), and the Odd Mushroom relic then made the Hexaghost fight longer, leading to lethal Burns accumulation with Hexaghost at only 24% HP remaining. Take the Heal option instead.

PRIORITY: Eat (heal) if Hexaghost boss or HP below 50%. Stomp only if HP above 70% and boss is not Hexaghost. Leave as last resort.
