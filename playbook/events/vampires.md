# Vampires (Act 2)

Also known as "Falling" or the Vampires event. Encounter with a group of vampires.

OPTIONS:
- [Accept] Lose ALL Strikes from your deck. Gain 5 Bite cards. Lose ~30% of your Max HP (observed: 80 -> 56 = -24 Max HP).
- [Refuse] Walk away. Nothing happens.

WHAT BITE DOES: Bite is a 1E Attack card that deals 7 damage and heals 2 HP. It is a colorless card (not Ironclad-specific). Five Bites replace your Strikes, giving the deck built-in healing on every attack.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- The Max HP loss is SEVERE. Losing 24 Max HP (from 80 to 56) means every HP threshold shifts drastically. Fights you could survive at 40 HP before now require entering at nearly full health. Boss HP thresholds (70% = 39 HP instead of 56 HP) become razor-thin.
- The healing from Bites (2 HP per Bite played) is meaningful over long fights but cannot compensate for the reduced HP pool against burst damage. In a 5-turn fight playing 1 Bite/turn, you heal 10 HP -- but you lost 24 Max HP permanently.
- Removing all Strikes is good for deck quality (Bites are strictly better than Strikes: same cost, similar damage, plus healing).
- AVOID if: you are below 70% HP, approaching a boss or elite, or your deck already has healing (Reaper, Feed). The Max HP loss compounds with Act 2's HP drain fights.
- CONSIDER if: you have no other healing sources and are at high HP with a safe path ahead. The sustained healing from 5 Bites across many fights can partially offset the Max HP loss over a long run.
- CRITICAL WARNING: At 56 Max HP, the Gremlin Leader elite (27+ damage/turn with Str scaling) and 3 Cultists become nearly impossible to survive. The reduced HP pool leaves zero margin for error.

PRIORITY: Refuse (most situations) > Accept (only when at high HP with no healing sources and a safe upcoming path).
