# Forgotten Altar (Act 2)
OPTIONS:
- [Sacrifice] Lose 20 HP, gain +5 Max HP.
- [Desecrate] Gain Decay curse (2 damage/turn when in hand, Unplayable, Ethereal).
- [Leave] Walk away with nothing.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- If HP > 40 and a rest site is upcoming: Sacrifice. 20 HP loss is recoverable; +5 Max HP is permanent value.
- If HP <= 20: Desecrate (Sacrifice would kill you). Decay curse can be exhausted by Fiend Fire or removed at shops.
- If HP 20-40 with no rest coming: Leave. Neither option is safe.
- If you have exhaust tools (Fiend Fire, True Grit+, Second Wind): Desecrate is less punishing since you can exhaust the curse in combat.

PRIORITY (high HP): Sacrifice > Leave > Desecrate.
PRIORITY (low HP, forced): Desecrate > Leave. Never Sacrifice below 21 HP.

TRAP: At 15 HP (Run 20), Sacrifice = death, Desecrate adds a curse that deals 2 damage/turn. Neither option is good. The real mistake was arriving at this event at 15 HP -- map pathing upstream is the fix.
