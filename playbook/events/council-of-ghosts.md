# Council of Ghosts (Act 2)
OPTIONS:
- [Accept] Lose ~29-40 Max HP (scales with current Max HP), gain 5 Apparition cards (prevent ALL damage for 1 turn each, Ethereal).
- [Refuse] Leave.

DECISION FRAMEWORK: 5 turns of invincibility is powerful, but the Max HP loss is devastating. At 100+ Max HP with a strong deck that ends fights quickly, Accept can work. At low Max HP (<80), Refuse -- being at 39 Max HP means one bad fight ends the run.

**SILENT POISON SYNERGY:** Apparitions are exceptionally powerful with a poison deck. Intangible buys time for poison to tick -- Noxious Fumes applies damage every turn regardless of Intangible status. With 5 Apparitions, you get 5 turns of guaranteed poison scaling while taking near-zero damage. Catalyst+ tripling a poison stack during Intangible turns is the optimal sequencing: build poison safely, then survive with block after Intangible expires. Tools of the Trade filters Apparitions to when they are needed; Hovering Kite generates energy from discarding unneeded ones.

**Apparition management under Hex:** Apparitions are Skills, so they trigger Hex (add Dazed). Playing 3-4 Apparitions against Chosen adds 3-4 Dazed to the draw pile. This is still worth it -- Intangible is more valuable than the Dazed penalty.

PRIORITY: Accept (poison deck with Noxious Fumes/Catalyst+, or 100+ Max HP) > Refuse (default, especially <80 Max HP without poison engine).
