# Wheel of Change

Act 2 event. Spin a wheel for a random outcome.

OPTIONS:
- [Play] Spin the wheel -- random outcome, includes both positive and negative possibilities.
- Outcomes observed: Lose 8 HP (negative).

DECISION FRAMEWORK: This is a gamble with uncontrolled variance. At low HP, losing 8 HP can be the difference between surviving the next fight and dying. At high HP (70%+), the risk is acceptable since even the worst outcome leaves enough buffer.

CAUTION: At below 50% HP, the downside of losing 8 HP is disproportionately dangerous. In Act 2 where every point of HP matters, an unnecessary 8 HP loss can push you below survival thresholds for the next hallway fight. Consider whether the potential upside justifies the risk at your current HP.
