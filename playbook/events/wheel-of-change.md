# Wheel of Change

Act 2 event. Spin a wheel for a random outcome.

OPTIONS:
- [Play] Spin the wheel -- random outcome, includes both positive and negative possibilities.
- Outcomes observed: Lose 8 HP (negative). Other outcomes unknown.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- Below 60% HP: SKIP. The 8 HP loss outcome is potentially lethal in Act 2. One observed HP loss from this event directly contributed to a death spiral (Run 59).
- Above 60% HP: Acceptable to spin. Even the worst known outcome (-8 HP) leaves a buffer.

This event offers an Unknown room node, so the player has already committed to entering it. If the wheel is mandatory (no leave option), spinning is forced. But if a "Leave" option exists, take it below 60% HP.
