# Wheel of Change

Act 2/3 event. Spin a wheel for a random outcome.

OPTIONS:
- [Play] Spin the wheel -- random outcome, includes both positive and negative possibilities.
- Outcomes observed: Lose 8 HP (negative), Heal to full health (positive). Other outcomes unknown.

DECISION FRAMEWORK:
- Below 60% HP: Risky. The 8 HP loss outcome is potentially lethal in Act 2. One observed HP loss from this event directly contributed to a death spiral.
- Above 60% HP: Acceptable to spin. Even the worst known outcome (-8 HP) leaves a buffer, and the best known outcome (full heal) is extremely valuable.

This event appears in both Act 2 and Act 3. The full outcome table is still partially unknown.
