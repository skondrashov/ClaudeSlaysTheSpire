# Mind Bloom (Act 3 Event)

Three options:

1. **[I am War]** -- Fight a random Act 1 boss. Obtain a Rare Relic on victory.
2. **[I am Awake]** -- Upgrade ALL cards in your deck. Obtain **Mark of the Bloom** relic (you can no longer heal for the rest of the run).
3. **[I am Rich]** -- Gain 999 gold. Obtain 2 Normality curses (limit hand to 3 cards per turn).

## Recommendations

**I am War is the safest choice for a strong deck.** An Act 1 boss (Guardian, Slime Boss, Hexaghost) is trivial with an Act 3 deck. The Rare Relic reward is guaranteed and can be game-changing. Champion Belt was obtained this way and provided free Weak on every Vulnerable application for the rest of the run.

**I am Awake is a TRAP -- do not choose this option.** Upgrading all cards is tempting, but **Mark of the Bloom prevents ALL healing for the rest of the run** -- rest sites cannot heal, potions cannot heal, Burning Blood does nothing, Feed does nothing. Every point of damage taken from this moment forward is permanent. Any remaining fights before the boss (hallway fights, events with combat, Mysterious Sphere) become potentially lethal because there is zero recovery. Even entering a fight at near-full HP, a single hard fight (2 Orb Walkers dealt 66 damage in one fight) can leave you at single-digit HP with no way to recover. The upgrade value rarely compensates for the loss of all healing when any fights remain on the path to the boss.

**I am Rich is a trap with Snecko Eye.** Normality limits the hand to 3 cards per turn. With Snecko Eye drawing 7 cards, 4 are immediately wasted. The 999 gold is useless in late Act 3 (few shops remaining). Avoid.

## Decision Framework

- Strong deck that can beat Act 1 boss trivially: **War** (free Rare Relic, no downside)
- **Awake: Almost never.** Only consider if ALL of the following are true: (1) the deck has multiple unupgraded cards where upgrades are transformative, (2) the path from here to the boss contains ZERO remaining combat encounters (no hallway fights, no events that could resolve as combat, no Mysterious Sphere), AND (3) current HP is near max. If any fights remain on the path, choose War instead.
- Never: **Rich** (Normality curses are devastating)
