# Donu and Deca

- **Type:** Boss
- **Act:** 3
- **Donu HP:** 250
- **Deca HP:** 250
- **Combined:** 500 HP

Each starts with [[buffs/Artifact]] 2.

## Pattern

- **Donu:** Alternates Circle of Power and Beam (Circle -> Beam -> Circle -> Beam...)
- **Deca:** Alternates Beam and Square of Protection (Beam -> Square -> Beam -> Square...)

## Attacks

| Enemy | Move | Damage | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|--------|
| Donu | Circle of Power | -- | All enemies gain 3 [[buffs/Strength]] |
| Donu | Beam | 10x2 = 20 | -- |
| Deca | Beam | 10x2 = 20 | Adds 2 Dazed to discard |
| Deca | Square of Protection | -- | All enemies gain 16 Block |

## Mechanics

**Donu [[buffs/Strength]] Scaling:** Buffs +3 [[buffs/Strength]] for BOTH enemies every other turn. Kill Donu first to stop scaling.

**Deca Block:** Gives both enemies 16 Block every other turn.

**[[buffs/Artifact]] 2:** First 2 debuffs per enemy are negated.

**Combined Incoming:** Starts around 20/turn and scales as Donu buffs [[buffs/Strength]].

**Fight Length:** 6-10 turns depending on deck power.
