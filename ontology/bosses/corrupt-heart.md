# The Corrupt Heart

- **Type:** Boss
- **Act:** 4
- **HP:** 750 (A0), 800 (A1+)

## Pattern

Turn 1: Debilitate. Turn 2: Blood Shots. Turn 3+: cycles through Debilitate → Blood Shots → Echo → repeat.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Debilitate | -- | Apply 2 [[debuffs/Vulnerable]], 2 [[debuffs/Weak]], 2 [[debuffs/Frail]] |
| Blood Shots | 2x15 (A0) | Multi-hit |
| Echo | 40 | -- |
| Buff | -- | Gains 2 [[buffs/Strength]], gains [[debuffs/Painful Stabs]], Beat of Death |

## Mechanics

- **Beat of Death:** Player takes 1 (2 at A19) damage for each card played
- **Invincible:** Can only take 200 (300 A0) damage per turn; excess is negated. Resets each turn.
- **Requires 3 Keys:** Can only fight the Heart if you collected the Emerald, Sapphire, and Ruby keys during the run
