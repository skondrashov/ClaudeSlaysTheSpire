# Awakened One

- **Type:** Boss
- **Act:** 3
- **Phase 1 HP:** 300 (320 at A9)
- **Phase 2 HP:** 300

Two-phase fight. Regenerate 10 HP/turn in Phase 2.

## Pattern

**Phase 1:** Opens with Slash. Then 75% Slash / 25% Soul Strike. Cannot use Slash 3x in a row or Soul Strike 2x in a row.

**Phase 2:** Opens with Dark Echo (40 damage). Then 50% Tackle / 50% Sludge. Cannot use either 3x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Slash | 20 | -- |

| Dark Echo | 40 | Phase 2 opener |
| Tackle | 10x3 = 30 | Phase 2 |
| Sludge | 18 | Adds Void card to deck (Phase 2) |
| Rebirth | -- | Full heal to 300 HP, removes debuffs, loses Curiosity |

## Mechanics

**Curiosity:** Every time the player plays a [[types/Power]] card, the Awakened One gains +2 [[buffs/Strength]] permanently. This is the defining mechanic of the fight.

**Phase Transition:** At Phase 1 HP = 0, enters Phase 2 with full HP (300). All player Powers are STRIPPED but their EFFECTS (Dex from Footwork, After Image block-per-card) appear to persist. [[debuffs/Poison]] on boss is REMOVED. Curiosity [[buffs/Strength]] gained in Phase 1 RESETS.

**Regenerate 10 (Phase 2):** Heals 10 HP per turn. Must out-damage the regeneration or lose to attrition.

**Phase 1 Minions:** Starts with two [[enemies/Cultist]] minions (~48 HP each, [[buffs/Ritual]] scaling).

**Curiosity Math:**
- 3 Powers = +6 Str. 10x3 multi-attack becomes 16x3 = 48.
- 6 Powers = +12 Str. 10x3 becomes 22x3 = 66.
