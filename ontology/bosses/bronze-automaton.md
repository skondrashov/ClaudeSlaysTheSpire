# Bronze Automaton

- **Type:** Boss
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 300 (A0), 320 (A9)

## Starting State

[[buffs/Artifact]] 3. Accompanied by 2 Bronze Orbs (52-58 HP each).

## Pattern

- **Turn 1:** Always Spawn Orbs -- summons 2 Bronze Orbs.
- **Subsequent turns:** Fixed repeating cycle: Flail -> Boost -> Flail -> Boost -> HYPER BEAM -> Stunned.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Flail | 7x2 = 14 (8x2 = 16 at A4) | -- |
| Boost | -- | Gains 3 [[buffs/Strength]] + 9 Block |
| HYPER BEAM | 45 (50 at A4) | Automaton is Stunned next turn |
| Stunned | -- | Does nothing |

## Bronze Orbs

- HP: 52-58
- Has [[debuffs/Stasis]] (75% chance each turn until used, once per combat).
- After [[debuffs/Stasis]]: 70% Support Beam (Automaton gains 12 [[rules/Block]]), 30% Beam (8 damage).

## Mechanics

Automaton is stunned the turn after Hyper Beam.
