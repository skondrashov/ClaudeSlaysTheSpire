# Time Eater

- **Type:** Boss
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 456 (A0), 480 (A9)

Single target. The card-count boss.

## Pattern

45% Reverberate, 35% Head Slam, 20% Ripple. Cannot use Reverberate 3x in a row. Cannot use Head Slam or Ripple 2x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Reverberate | 7x3 = 21 (8x3 = 24 at A4) | -- |
| Head Slam | 26 (32 at A4) | Applies 1 [[debuffs/Draw Reduction]] (adds 2 Slimed at A19) |
| Ripple | -- | Gains 20 Block + applies 1 [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] + 1 [[debuffs/Weak]] (+ 1 [[debuffs/Frail]] at A19) |

## Mechanics

**[[debuffs/Time Warp]]:** Every 12 cards played, Time Eater heals 32 HP, gains +2 [[buffs/Strength]], and ends your turn immediately. Every card play counts, including 0-cost cards, Shivs, Powers, and cards generated mid-turn.

**Haste (at 50% HP, once):** Removes all debuffs, heals to 50% HP (228 at A0), gains 32 Block. Effectively means ~684 total damage needed to kill (456 * 1.5). Must re-apply debuffs after.

**Effective HP:** ~684 total damage needed (456 HP + 228 heal from Haste + 32 per Time Warp trigger).

**Echo Form Interaction (Defect):** Each card doubled by Echo Form counts as 2 plays toward Time Warp, not 1. Effective budget per cycle is ~6 real card plays with Echo Form active.
