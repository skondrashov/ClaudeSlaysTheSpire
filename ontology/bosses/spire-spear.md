# Spire Spear

- **Type:** Boss (Act 4, pre-Heart fight)
- **Act:** 4
- **HP:** 160-170

## Pattern

Alternates between Burn Strike and Piercer. Can also Skewer.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Burn Strike | 5x2 | Shuffle 2 Burns into discard pile |
| Piercer | 10 | Apply 2 [[debuffs/Frail]] |
| Skewer | 8x3 | -- |

## Mechanics

- Paired with [[bosses/Spire Shield]] in the Act 4 elite fight before the Heart
- Burns clog deck; [[debuffs/Frail]] reduces your blocking capability
