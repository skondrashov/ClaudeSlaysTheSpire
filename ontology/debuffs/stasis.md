# Stasis

- **Effect:** An Orb slot is occupied by a stolen card. The card is returned to your hand when the enemy holding it dies.
- **Duration:** Until the enemy dies
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] (steals a card from your hand and places it in Stasis)
- **Interaction:** Reduces available Orb slots for the [[characters/Defect]]. The stolen card is unavailable until the Automaton is defeated. If fighting [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] as [[characters/Defect]], losing an Orb slot reduces [[buffs/Focus]] value and Orb cycling efficiency.
