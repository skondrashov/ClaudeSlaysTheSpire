# Painful Stabs

- **Effect:** Whenever you lose HP from an attack, add a Wound ([[types/Status]]) to your discard pile.
- **Duration:** Permanent (lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]]
- **Interaction:** Wounds are unplayable [[types/Status]] cards that clog your hand. Punishes taking unblocked damage. [[buffs/Block]] fully negating an attack prevents the trigger. Multi-hit attacks that partially connect can add multiple Wounds.
