# Slow

- **Effect:** The afflicted enemy takes 10% more damage for each card you play this turn. Resets at start of each turn.
- **Duration:** Permanent (does not decrement)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/The Guardian]], [[enemies/Lagavulin]], [[bosses/Time Eater]] — these enemies have Slow as an innate mechanic applied to themselves (it's actually a debuff the player benefits from)
- **Interaction:** Stacks multiplicatively with [[debuffs/Vulnerable]]. Playing many cheap cards maximizes the damage bonus. After 5 cards played, the enemy takes 50% more damage. Strongly rewards low-cost card-heavy decks.
