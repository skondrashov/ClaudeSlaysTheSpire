# Constricted

- **Effect:** Take N damage at the end of each turn (unblockable — directly reduces HP past [[buffs/Block]]).
- **Duration:** Permanent unless removed
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple applications increase the damage per turn.
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] (Constrict attack applies it), [[enemies/Giant Head]]
- **Interaction:** Cannot be blocked — it bypasses [[buffs/Block]] entirely. Can be prevented by [[buffs/Buffer]]. [[buffs/Artifact]] does NOT prevent it (it's applied via an attack, not as a standalone debuff application in all cases — but the debuff itself is negatable by [[buffs/Artifact]] when applied).
