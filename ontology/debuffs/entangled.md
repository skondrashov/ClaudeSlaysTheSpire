# Entangled

- **Effect:** Cannot play [[types/Attack]] cards for the rest of this turn.
- **Duration:** Until end of current turn (removed at start of next turn)
- **Sources (enemy):** Slavers ([[enemies/Blue Slaver]]'s net attack), [[enemies/Book of Stabbing]]
- **Interaction:** Only restricts [[types/Attack]] cards. [[types/Skill]]s, [[types/Power]]s, and other card types can still be played. [[buffs/Artifact]] negates the application of Entangled.
