# Frail

- **Effect:** [[buffs/Block]] gained from cards is reduced by 25% (x0.75 multiplier)
- **Duration:** N turns, decrements at start of player's turn
- **Formula:** `floor(block * 0.75)`. [[cards/Defend]] 5 → 3. [[cards/Shrug It Off]] 8 → 6.
- **Scope:** Only affects block from card plays. Does not affect block from relics ([[buffs/Metallicize]]), orbs (Frost passive), or other non-card sources.
