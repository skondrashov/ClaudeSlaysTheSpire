# Hex

- **Effect:** Whenever you play a non-[[types/Attack]] card, add a Dazed ([[types/Status]]) to your draw pile.
- **Duration:** Permanent (lasts rest of combat unless cleansed)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/Hexaghost]] (applies Hex via Inferno)
- **Interaction:** Punishes playing [[types/Skill]]s and [[types/Power]]s. Dazed is an unplayable [[types/Status]] that clogs hand slots. Particularly dangerous in Power-heavy or Skill-heavy decks. [[types/Attack]]-focused builds are less affected.
- **Removal:** Can be removed by [[buffs/Artifact]] (prevents application) but once applied, cannot be cleansed mid-combat.
