# Corpse Explosion

- **Effect:** When the afflicted enemy dies, deal damage equal to its Max HP to ALL other enemies.
- **Duration:** Permanent (persists until the enemy dies)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Corpse Explosion]] card ([[characters/Silent]] [[types/Skill]], 2E), applies [[debuffs/Poison]] and this debuff
- **Stacking:** Multiple applications do NOT stack — only one instance is meaningful per enemy.
- **Strategy implication:** Extremely powerful in multi-enemy fights. Kill the weakest enemy first to chain AoE damage. Particularly strong against enemies with high Max HP in groups.
