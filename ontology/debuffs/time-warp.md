# Time Warp

- **Effect:** Tracks cards played by the player. After 12 cards are played in a single turn, the enemy gains 2 [[buffs/Strength]] and the player's turn ends immediately.
- **Duration:** Permanent (resets counter each turn)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/Time Eater]]
- **Interaction:** Hard-counters decks that play many cards per turn (Shiv decks, 0-cost spam, infinite combos). Forces the player to be strategic about card count per turn. The 12-card limit includes ALL card plays ([[types/Attack]]s, [[types/Skill]]s, [[types/Power]]s). The [[buffs/Strength]] gain is permanent and cumulative across multiple triggers.
