# Time Warp

- **Effect:** Tracks cards played by the player. After 12 cards are played in a single turn, the enemy gains 2 [[buffs/Strength]] and the player's turn ends immediately.
- **Stacking:** Non-stacking
- **Interactions:** The 12-card limit includes ALL card plays ([[types/Attack]]s, [[types/Skill]]s, [[types/Power]]s). The [[buffs/Strength]] gain is permanent and cumulative across multiple triggers.
