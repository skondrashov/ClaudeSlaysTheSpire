# Poison

- **Effect:** At the start of the poisoned entity's turn, lose HP equal to Poison stacks. Then Poison decreases by 1.
- **Duration:** Decreases by 1 each turn, persists until 0
- **Stacking:** Additive. Applying 3 Poison to an enemy with 5 Poison = 8 Poison.
- **Damage sequence:** Poison 7 deals 7+6+5+4+3+2+1 = 28 total damage over 7 turns.
- **Sources:** [[cards/Deadly Poison]], [[cards/Noxious Fumes]] ([[types/Power]], applies each turn), [[cards/Poisoned Stab]], [[cards/Bouncing Flask]], [[cards/Crippling Cloud]], [[cards/Bane]] (bonus damage if target is poisoned), [[cards/Corpse Explosion]], [[cards/Envenom]]
- **Scaling:** [[cards/Catalyst]] doubles (or triples upgraded) current Poison stacks. [[debuffs/Corpse Explosion]] makes enemies deal AoE damage on death equal to their HP.
- **Key property:** Poison ignores [[buffs/Block]]. It deals damage directly to HP.
