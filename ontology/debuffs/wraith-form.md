# Wraith Form

- **Effect:** Gain N stacks of [[buffs/Intangible]]. At the start of each subsequent turn, lose 1 [[buffs/Dexterity]].
- **Duration:** The [[buffs/Dexterity]] loss is permanent (stacks each turn for rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Wraith Form]] card ([[characters/Silent]] [[types/Power]], 3E), grants 2/3 [[buffs/Intangible]]
- **Interaction:** Extremely powerful but with severe downside. [[buffs/Intangible]] reduces ALL damage to 1 for N turns, but the cumulative [[buffs/Dexterity]] loss makes [[buffs/Block]] generation increasingly difficult after Intangible expires. Often a "win within N turns or lose" card. [[buffs/Artifact]] does NOT prevent the Dexterity loss (it's a self-applied effect from a [[types/Power]] you control).
