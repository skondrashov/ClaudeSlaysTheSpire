# Split

- **Effect:** When the enemy's HP drops to half or below, it dies and spawns two smaller versions of itself.
- **Duration:** One-time trigger
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/Slime Boss]] (splits into 2 Large Slimes at 50% HP), [[enemies/Acid Slime L]] and [[enemies/Spike Slime L]] (split into 2 medium slimes)
- **Strategy implication:** Overkill damage is wasted. Ideally deal exactly enough damage to trigger the split, then handle the spawns. AoE damage becomes very valuable after the split occurs. The spawned slimes inherit [[debuffs/Poison]] stacks proportionally.
