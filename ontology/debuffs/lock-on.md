# Lock-On

- **Effect:** The afflicted enemy takes 50% more damage from Orb attacks. [[characters/Defect]]-specific.
- **Stacking:** Duration stacks, but the 50% bonus does not increase beyond 50%
- **Interactions:** Only affects Orb damage (Lightning evoke/passive, Dark evoke). Does NOT affect card damage. Frost passive does not deal damage and is unaffected.
