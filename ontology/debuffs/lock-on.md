# Lock-On

- **Effect:** The afflicted enemy takes 50% more damage from Orb attacks (Lightning, Dark, Frost passive does not deal damage so not affected).
- **Duration:** Decreases by 1 each turn
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Bullseye]] card ([[characters/Defect]] [[types/Attack]]), [[cards/All For One]] interactions
- **Stacking:** Duration stacks (lasts more turns), but the 50% bonus does not increase beyond 50%.
- **Interaction:** Only affects Orb damage (Lightning evoke/passive, Dark evoke). Does NOT affect card damage. Core debuff for Orb-focused [[characters/Defect]] builds.
