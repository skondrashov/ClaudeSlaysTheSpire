# Vulnerable

- **Effect:** Target takes 50% more damage from [[types/Attack]]s (x1.5 multiplier)
- **Duration:** N turns, decrements at start of affected entity's turn
- **Direction:** Vulnerable on enemy = player's attacks deal 50% more to that enemy. Vulnerable on player = enemy attacks deal 50% more to player.
- **Removed by:** [[buffs/Artifact]] (negates the application)
