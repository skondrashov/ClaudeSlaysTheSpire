# Retain

- **Effect:** Card stays in hand at end of turn instead of being discarded
- **Types:**
  - Innate Retain: card always retains (keyword on the card itself, e.g., Miracle)
  - Granted Retain: given by other effects (Well-Laid Plans, Establishment)
- **Interaction:** Retained cards still count toward hand size. They are not discarded and not drawn again.
- **Watcher:** Heavy use of Retain (Miracle, Perseverance, Establishment Power)
