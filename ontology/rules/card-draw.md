# Card Draw

- Standard draw: 5 cards per turn
- Draw happens at start of turn from the draw pile
- When the draw pile is empty and a draw triggers, the discard pile shuffles into the draw pile
- Cards that draw (Pommel Strike, Shrug It Off, Battle Trance) add cards to hand mid-turn
- Hand size limit: 10 cards. Draws past 10 are discarded.
- At end of turn, all cards in hand (except [[Retain]]ed cards) go to the discard pile

## Draw modifiers
- Relics: Ring of the Snake (+2 Turn 1), Bag of Preparation (+2 Turn 1)
- Powers: Machine Learning (+1/turn), Brutality (+1/turn)
- Cards: Offering (draw 3), Battle Trance (draw 3), Backflip (draw 2)
