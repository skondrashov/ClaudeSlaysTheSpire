# Energy

- Base energy: 3 per turn (at Ascension 0, no modifiers)
- Energy resets each turn — unspent energy is lost
- Some relics modify base energy: Lantern (+1 Turn 1 only), Coffee Dripper (+1 permanent, can't rest), Fusion Hammer (+1 permanent, can't upgrade), Ectoplasm (+1 permanent, can't gain gold), Sozu (+1 permanent, can't gain potions)
- Some cards grant energy: Offering (+2), Bloodletting (+2), Seeing Red (+2), Aggregate (variable), [[Miracle]] (+1)
- X-cost cards consume ALL remaining energy when played

## Energy from Stances (Watcher)

- Leaving [[Calm]] grants +2 Energy
- Entering [[Divinity]] grants +3 Energy
