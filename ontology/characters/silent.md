# Silent

## Starting Deck

- 5 Strike (Attack, 1E, 6 damage)
- 5 Defend (Skill, 1E, 5 block)
- 1 Survivor (Skill, 1E, 8 block, discard 1 card)
- 1 Neutralize (Attack, 0E, 3 damage, apply Weak 1)

## Starting Relic

**Ring of the Snake** — draw 2 additional cards on Turn 1 (7 cards instead of 5).

## Key Mechanics

- **Poison:** Applied to enemies, deals its value as damage at start of their turn, then decreases by 1. Stacks additively.
- **Shivs:** 0-cost Attack cards that deal 4 damage (6 with Accuracy). Generated by Blade Dance, Cloak and Dagger, Infinite Blades, Storm of Steel. Exhaust after playing.
- **Discard synergies:** Some cards trigger effects when discarded (Tactician: gain Energy, Reflex: draw cards).
