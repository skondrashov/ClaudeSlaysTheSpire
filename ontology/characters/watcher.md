# Watcher

## Stances

The Watcher has four stances. Only one can be active at a time. Entering a new stance replaces the current stance.

| Stance | Effect | Entry Cards | Exit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wrath** | ALL attack damage dealt is DOUBLED. ALL damage received is DOUBLED. | Eruption, Crescendo, Fear No Evil (conditional) | Enter Calm, enter Divinity, or Empty Body (No Stance) |
| **Calm** | No combat bonuses. When leaving Calm, gain +2 Energy. | Vigilance, Inner Peace, Meditate, Fear No Evil (conditional) | Enter Wrath, enter Divinity, or Empty Body |
| **Divinity** | ALL attack damage dealt is TRIPLED. Gain +3 Energy on entry. Exits automatically at end of turn. | Accumulate 10+ Mantra (Worship, Prostrate, Devotion, Damaru) | Automatic at end of turn (returns to No Stance) |
| **No Stance** | Neutral. No bonuses or penalties. Starting stance. | Empty Body, or after Divinity ends, or at combat start | Any stance-entry card |

### Stance Change Triggers

When the Watcher changes stance (enters a new stance from a different one), certain cards trigger:
- **Flurry of Blows:** Returns from discard pile to hand on any stance change
- **Rushdown:** Draw 2 (or 3 upgraded) cards on entering Wrath
- **Mental Fortress:** Gain 4 (or 6 upgraded) block on any stance change

### Energy Loop Mechanics

Leaving Calm grants +2 Energy. Eruption costs 2E (1E upgraded). Therefore:
- Eruption from Calm: spend 2E, gain 2E = net 0E cost + enter Wrath
- Eruption+ from Calm: spend 1E, gain 2E = net +1E + enter Wrath

## Starting Deck

- 4 Strike (Attack, 1E, 6 damage)
- 4 Defend (Skill, 1E, 5 block)
- 1 Eruption (Attack, 2E, 9 damage, enter Wrath)
- 1 Vigilance (Skill, 2E, 8 block, enter Calm)

## Starting Relic

**Pure Water** — adds 1 Miracle to hand at the start of each combat. Miracle: 0E Skill, Retain, gain 1 Energy, Exhaust.
