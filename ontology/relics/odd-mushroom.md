# Odd Mushroom

- **Rarity:** Event relic
- **Effect:** Reduces [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] damage multiplier from 1.5x to 1.25x when applied to YOU. Also reduces YOUR [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] application on enemies -- enemies take only 1.25x instead of 1.5x from your attacks while [[debuffs/Vulnerable]].
- **Obtained:** Mushroom event reward
