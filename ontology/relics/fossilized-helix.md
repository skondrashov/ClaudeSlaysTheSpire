# Fossilized Helix

- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Effect:** Prevent the first instance of HP loss you would take each combat
- **Interactions:** [[rules/Block]] is consumed before Fossilized Helix checks. If an attack deals 6 damage and you have 5 Block, Block absorbs 5 and Helix prevents the remaining 1 HP loss. Block is still consumed. Against multi-hit attacks, Block is depleted by hit 1 and Helix prevents any overflow HP loss from hit 1 only — hits 2+ land against remaining Block (usually 0).
