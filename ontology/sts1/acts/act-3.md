# Act 3

16 floors + 1 boss floor (Floor 50).

## Map Structure

- Floors 34-48: Mix of Monster, Elite, Rest Site, Shop, Unknown
- Floor 49: Always a Rest Site
- Floor 50: Boss fight
- HP fully restored at start of Act 3
