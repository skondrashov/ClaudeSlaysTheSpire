# Cursed Tome

- **Act:** 2
- **Options:**
  - Read: Start reading the book. At each stage choose Continue or Stop:
    - Stage 1: Lose 1 HP
    - Stage 2: Lose 2 HP
    - Stage 3: Lose 3 HP
    - Stage 4 (final): Lose 10 HP, obtain a random book relic ([[relics/Enchiridion]], [[relics/Nilry's Codex]], or [[relics/Necronomicon]])
  - Leave: Nothing happens
