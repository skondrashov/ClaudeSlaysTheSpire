# Mind Bloom

- **Act:** 3
- **Options:**
  - I am War: Fight a random Act 1 boss. Obtain a Rare Relic on victory.
  - I am Awake: Upgrade ALL cards in your deck. Obtain [[relics/Mark of the Bloom]].
  - I am Rich: Gain 999 Gold. Obtain 2 Normality [[types/Curse]]s.
  - I am Healthy: Heal to full HP. Obtain Doubt [[types/Curse]].
