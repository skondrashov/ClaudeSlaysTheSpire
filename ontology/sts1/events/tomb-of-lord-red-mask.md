# Tomb of Lord Red Mask

- **Type:** Event
- **Act:** 3

## Choices

| Choice | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| Offer Gold | Lose ALL your gold. Obtain the [[relics/Red Mask]] relic. (Works even at 0 gold — the relic is free.) |
| Don the Red Mask | Only available if you already have the Red Mask (e.g. from Face Trader). Gain 222 Gold. |
| Leave | Nothing happens. |

## Notes

[[relics/Red Mask]]: at the start of each combat, apply 1 [[debuffs/Weak]] to ALL enemies.
