# Masked Bandits

- **Act:** 2
- **Options:**
  - Pay gold (toll): Lose all gold, avoid combat
  - Fight: Fight the three bandits ([[enemies/Romeo]], [[enemies/Bear]], [[enemies/Pointy]]) — a standard Act 2 combat encounter. Reward on win: Red Mask relic.

## The Trio

| Bandit | HP | Role |
|--------|-----|------|
| [[enemies/Bear]] | 38-42 | Debuffer: -2 [[buffs/Dexterity]] (Bear Hug), then Maul 18/20 |
| [[enemies/Romeo]] | 35-39 | 2 [[debuffs/Weak]] + plain hits; opens on a no-damage Mock |
| [[enemies/Pointy]] | 30 (A7+: 34) | Pure damage: Stab 5x2 (A2+: 6x2) every turn |
