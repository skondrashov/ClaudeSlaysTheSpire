# Exhaust

- **Effect:** An exhausted card leaves play for the rest of combat — it goes to the exhaust pile, not the discard, and is not reshuffled into the draw pile.
- **Sources:**
  - Cards that exhaust themselves on play (most one-shot Powers, [[cards/Apotheosis]], many Skills).
  - [[types/Status]] / Curse cards removed via exhaust effects ([[cards/Fiend Fire]], [[cards/Feel No Pain]] generators).
  - Ethereal cards exhaust automatically if still in hand at end of turn.
  - [[cards/Corruption]] makes all Skills cost 0 and exhaust after play.
- **Retrieval:** [[cards/Exhume]] returns a card from the exhaust pile to hand. Otherwise exhausted cards are gone until next combat.
- **Payoffs:** Several cards/relics trigger *on exhaust* — [[buffs/Feel No Pain]] (gain Block), [[buffs/Dark Embrace]] (draw a card), [[relics/Charon's Ashes]] (deal damage to all enemies). Exhausting is a resource these engines consume.
- **Scope:** Exhaust is per-combat. The deck is restored in full at the start of the next combat.
