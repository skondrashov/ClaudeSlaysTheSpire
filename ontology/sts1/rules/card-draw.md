# Card Draw

- Standard draw: 5 cards per turn
- Draw happens at start of turn from the draw pile
- When the draw pile is empty and a draw triggers, the discard pile shuffles into the draw pile
- Hand size limit: 10 cards. Draws past 10 are discarded.
- At end of turn, all cards in hand (except [[rules/Retain]]ed cards) go to the discard pile

## Draw Modifiers

- Relics: [[relics/Ring of the Snake]] (+2 Turn 1), [[relics/Bag of Preparation]] (+2 Turn 1)
- Powers: [[cards/Machine Learning]] (+1/turn), [[cards/Brutality]] (+1/turn)
- Cards: [[cards/Offering]] (draw 3), [[cards/Battle Trance]] (draw 3), [[cards/Backflip]] (draw 2)
