# Energy

- Base energy: 3 per turn (Ascension 0, no modifiers)
- Energy resets each turn — unspent energy is lost
- Some relics modify base energy: [[relics/Lantern]] (+1 Turn 1 only), [[relics/Coffee Dripper]] (+1, can't rest), [[relics/Fusion Hammer]] (+1, can't upgrade), [[relics/Ectoplasm]] (+1, can't gain gold), [[relics/Sozu]] (+1, can't gain potions)
- Some cards grant energy: [[cards/Offering]] (+2), [[cards/Bloodletting]] (+2), [[cards/Seeing Red]] (+2), [[cards/Aggregate]] (variable), [[cards/Miracle]] (+1)
- X-cost cards consume ALL remaining energy when played

## Stances ([[characters/Watcher]])

- Leaving [[buffs/Calm]] grants +2 Energy
- Entering [[buffs/Divinity]] grants +3 Energy
