# Burning Elite

When the final act is unlocked, one elite on each act's map burns (a flame marks its node). Defeating it grants the Emerald Key, one of the three keys needed to enter Act 4.

- **Buff:** every monster in a burning elite fight gains ONE of four buffs, chosen at random for that node:

| Buff | Act 1 | Act 2 | Act 3 |
|------|-------|-------|-------|
| [[buffs/Strength]] (act + 1) | +2 | +3 | +4 |
| Max HP +25% | +25% | +25% | +25% |
| [[buffs/Metallicize]] (act x 2 + 2) | 4 | 6 | 8 |
| Regenerate (1 + act x 2) | 3 | 5 | 7 |

- **Reward:** the normal elite rewards plus the Emerald Key.
- The buff applies to all enemies in the fight, including any the elite summons at spawn.
