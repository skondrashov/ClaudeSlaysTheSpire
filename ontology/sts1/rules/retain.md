# Retain

- **Effect:** Card stays in hand at end of turn instead of being discarded
- **Types:**
  - Innate Retain: keyword on the card itself (e.g., [[cards/Miracle]])
  - Granted Retain: given by other effects ([[cards/Well-Laid Plans]], [[cards/Establishment]])
- **Interaction:** Retained cards still count toward hand size limit (10). They are not discarded and not drawn again.
