# Heart of Iron

- **Rarity:** Boss
- **Class:** [[characters/Ironclad]]
- **Effect:** At the start of each combat, gain 6 [[buffs/Metallicize]] (6 [[rules/Block]] at the end of every turn, for the whole fight).
- **Identity note:** This is a RELIC, not a potion. The Metallicize 6 it grants is passive and combat-long — there is no "use" action. Observed value in play: Metallicize 6.
