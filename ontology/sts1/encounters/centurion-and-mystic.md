# Centurion + Mystic

- **Act:** 2
- **Type:** Hallway
- **Composition:** 1x [[enemies/Centurion]], 1x [[enemies/Mystic]]

## Kill-order structure (mechanical)

The fight's shape is determined by which enemy is left alone — the two endgames are not symmetric:

- **Mystic dies first → lone Centurion:** Fury (6x3, A2+ 7x3) replaces Protect in its AI, so every former shield turn becomes a 21-damage multi-hit, and Strength applies per hit — any +Str the Mystic banked earlier is tripled (+3 Str → 30). Combined with the Frail the Mystic has been applying, this is the hardest-to-block sustained pattern in the fight, with no free turns.
- **Centurion dies first → lone Mystic:** 8/9-damage Frail attacks, slow self-buffing, self-heal only when its own missing HP crosses the threshold. The smallest per-turn damage in the fight, but it ramps and can stall low-damage decks.

The Mystic's heal arms on the GROUP's summed missing HP (> 15; A17+ > 20) — chip spread across both enemies arms it just as fast as chip on one.
