# Centurion + Mystic

- **Act:** 2
- **Type:** Hallway
- **Composition:** 1x [[enemies/Centurion]], 1x [[enemies/Mystic]]

## Last-enemy behavior

- **Lone [[enemies/Centurion]]:** Fury (6x3, A2+ 7x3) replaces Protect in its AI — every former shield turn is an attack, and Strength applies per Fury hit (+3 Str → 10x3 = 30).
- **Lone [[enemies/Mystic]]:** 8/9-damage Frail attacks; buff and heal target only itself.

The Mystic's heal arms on the GROUP's summed missing HP (> 15; A17+ > 20) — chip spread across both enemies counts the same as chip on one.
