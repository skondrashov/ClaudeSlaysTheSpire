# Plated Armor

- **Effect:** Gain N [[rules/Block]] at end of turn. Decreases by 1 each time the entity takes unblocked HP damage.
- **Interactions:** Multi-hit attacks that break through Block remove 1 stack per hit that reaches HP. NOT modified by [[buffs/Dexterity]] or [[debuffs/Frail]] (passive effect, not a card play).
