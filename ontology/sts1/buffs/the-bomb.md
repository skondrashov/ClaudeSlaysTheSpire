# The Bomb

- **Effect:** After 3 turns, deal 40/50 damage to ALL enemies. Counter decreases by 1 each turn.
- **Duration:** 3 turns (triggers when counter reaches 0)
- **Stacking:** Multiple Bombs track independently, each with its own 3-turn timer
- **Interactions:** Damage is fixed and unaffected by [[buffs/Strength]].
