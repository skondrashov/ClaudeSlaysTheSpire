# Calm

- **Effect:** Calm has no effect while active. Upon exiting Calm, gain 2 [[rules/Energy]].
- **Category:** Stance ([[characters/Watcher]]). Only one stance is active at a time; entering a stance replaces the current one.
- **Entered by:** [[cards/Fear No Evil]], [[cards/Inner Peace]], [[cards/Meditate]], [[cards/Tranquility]], [[cards/Vigilance]].
