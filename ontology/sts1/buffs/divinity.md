# Divinity

- **Effect:** Upon entering Divinity, gain 3 [[rules/Energy]]. Attacks deal triple damage. Exit Divinity at the start of your next turn.
- **Category:** Stance ([[characters/Watcher]]). Only one stance is active at a time; entering a stance replaces the current one.
- **Entered by:** [[cards/Blasphemy]], or by reaching 10 [[buffs/Mantra]].
