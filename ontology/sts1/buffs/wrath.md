# Wrath

- **Effect:** While in Wrath, your Attacks deal double damage, and you take double damage from attacks.
- **Category:** Stance ([[characters/Watcher]]). Only one stance is active at a time; entering a stance replaces the current one.
- **Entered by:** [[cards/Crescendo]], [[cards/Eruption]], [[cards/Indignation]], [[cards/Simmering Fury]], [[cards/Tantrum]].
