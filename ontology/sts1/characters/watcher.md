# Watcher

## Starting Deck

- 4 [[cards/Strike]] ([[types/Attack]], 1E, 6 damage)
- 4 [[cards/Defend]] ([[types/Skill]], 1E, 5 block)
- 1 [[cards/Eruption]] ([[types/Attack]], 2E, 9 damage, enter [[buffs/Wrath]])
- 1 [[cards/Vigilance]] ([[types/Skill]], 2E, 8 block, enter [[buffs/Calm]])

## Starting Relic

[[relics/Pure Water]] — add 1 [[cards/Miracle]] to hand at start of combat

## Unique Mechanics: Stances

The Watcher has four stances. Only one active at a time. Entering a new stance replaces the current one.

| Stance | Effect | Exit Effect |
|---|---|---|
| [[buffs/Wrath]] | Deal and receive double damage | -- |
| [[buffs/Calm]] | No combat effect | Gain 2 Energy on exit |
| [[buffs/Divinity]] | Deal triple damage, gain 3 Energy on entry | Exits automatically at end of turn |
| No Stance | Neutral. Starting state. | -- |

### Stance Change Triggers

Cards that trigger on stance change:
- [[cards/Flurry of Blows]]: Returns from discard to hand on any stance change
- [[cards/Rushdown]]: Draw 2 (3 upgraded) on entering [[buffs/Wrath]]
- [[cards/Mental Fortress]]: Gain 4 (6 upgraded) [[rules/Block]] on any stance change
