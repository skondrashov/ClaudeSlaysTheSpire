# Power

A card type. Powers are played once and remain in effect for the rest of combat. They do not go to the discard pile.

## Properties
- Cannot be retrieved once played (no discard/draw pile interaction)
- Multiple copies of the same Power stack additively

## Modifiers
- Affected by [[cards/Amplify]], [[relics/Mummified Hand]], [[relics/Bird-Faced Urn]]
