# Shelled Parasite

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 68-72

## Pattern

Cannot use Fell on first turn. 40% Double Strike, 40% Life Suck, 20% Fell. Cannot use Double Strike or Life Suck 3x in a row. Cannot use Fell 2x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Double Strike | 6x2 = 12 | -- |
| Life Suck | 10 | Heals equal to unblocked damage |
| Fell | 18 | Applies 2 [[debuffs/Frail]] |

## Mechanics

Has [[buffs/Plated Armor]] 14. Stunned for one turn when all [[buffs/Plated Armor]] is removed (once per combat).
