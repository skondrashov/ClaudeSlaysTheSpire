# Gremlin Leader

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 140-148 (A8+: 145-155)

Starts with 2 gremlins already present. Maximum 3 small gremlins active at a time.

## Pattern

Move selection depends on the count of LIVING gremlins — **Rally exists only below 2; with 2+ gremlins alive the Leader never summons:**

- **0 gremlins:** Rally 75% / Stab 25%
- **1 gremlin:** Rally 50% / Encourage 30% / Stab 20%
- **2+ gremlins:** Encourage 66% / Stab 34% (no Rally)

No move twice in a row; a blocked repeat falls through to the bracket's other moves (e.g. at 1 gremlin after a Rally: Encourage 60% / Stab 40%).

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Stab | 6x3 = 18 | same at all ascensions |
| Encourage | -- | ALL enemies (Leader included) gain 3 [[buffs/Strength]] (A3+: 4, A18+: 5); the gremlins (not the Leader) also gain 6 Block (A18+: 10) |
| Rally! | -- | Summons 2 random gremlins (including [[enemies/Gremlin Wizard]]) |

## Mechanics

- Rally is a no-damage, no-buff turn; Encourage's Strength is permanent and stacks.
- Strength applies per Stab hit: each +1 Strength is +3 Stab damage.
