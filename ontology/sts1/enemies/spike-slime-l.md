# Spike Slime L

- **Type:** Hallway / Boss split
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 64-70 standalone, or equal to Slime Boss's remaining HP at split

## Pattern

30% Flame Tackle, 70% Lick. Cannot use same move three times in a row. Splits into 2x Spike Slime (M) when HP drops to 50% or below.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Flame Tackle | 16 | Adds 2 [[cards/Slimed]] to discard pile |
| Lick | -- | Applies 2 [[debuffs/Frail]] |
| Split | -- | At 50% HP or below, spawns 2 Spike Slime (M) with current HP each |
