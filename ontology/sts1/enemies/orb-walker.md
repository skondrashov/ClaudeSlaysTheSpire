# Orb Walker

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 90-96

Can appear as a single enemy or as a pair (2 Orb Walkers).

## Pattern

Each turn: 60% Laser, 40% Claw. Cannot use either move 3x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Laser | 10 | Adds 1 [[cards/Burn]] to draw pile + 1 [[cards/Burn]] to discard pile |
| Claw | 15 | -- |

## Mechanics

Gains 3 [[buffs/Strength]] at end of each turn.
