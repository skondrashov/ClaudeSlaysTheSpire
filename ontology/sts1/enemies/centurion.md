# Centurion

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 76-80 (A7+: 78-83)

## Pattern

Roll each turn: 35% Protect, else Slash; no move 3x in a row (a blocked repeat falls through to the other move). **Fury exists only while Centurion is the last enemy standing — it occupies exactly Protect's slot in the AI.** While any ally lives, every Fury-roll is a Protect instead; once alone, every would-be Protect turn is a Fury. There is no "free" (no-damage) turn from a lone Centurion.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Slash | 12 (A2+: 14) | single hit |
| Protect | -- | 15 Block (A17+: 20) to a random living ally; never used alone |
| Fury | 6x3 (A2+: 7x3 = 21) | only while alone |

## Mechanics

- **Strength applies per Fury hit:** each +1 Strength is +3 Fury damage. The Mystic's buffs (+2/+3/+4 by ascension, stacking) make a lone Centurion's Fury the fight's largest number — +3 Str turns A2+ Fury into 10x3 = 30.
- Fury is three separate hits: per-hit block/thorns/Blur interactions apply three times.
