# Sentry

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 38-42 each (appears as group of 3)

## Pattern

Outer two Sentries start with Bolt, middle Sentry starts with Beam. They alternate: Bolt -> Beam -> Bolt -> Beam.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Bolt | -- | Adds 2 [[cards/Dazed]] to discard pile |
| Beam | 9 (10 at A2) | -- |

## Mechanics

Each Sentry starts with [[buffs/Artifact]] 1.
