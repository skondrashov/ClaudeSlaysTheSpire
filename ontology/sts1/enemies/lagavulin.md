# Lagavulin

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 109-111

## Pattern

Starts asleep with [[buffs/Metallicize]] 8. Wakes when it LOSES HP (stunned 1 turn, then attacks) or after 3 turns idle (attacks immediately, no stun). Hits fully absorbed by its block/Metallicize do not reduce HP and therefore do not wake it -- chip damage that never breaks through leaves it asleep until the natural turn-3 wake, which attacks at once. [[buffs/Metallicize]] removed on wake. Awake cycle: Attack, Attack, Siphon Soul (repeating).

| Siphon Soul | -- | Apply -1 [[buffs/Strength]] and -1 [[buffs/Dexterity]] permanently |
