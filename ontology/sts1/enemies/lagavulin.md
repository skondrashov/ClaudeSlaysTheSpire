# Lagavulin

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 109-111

## Pattern

Starts asleep with 8 Block + [[buffs/Metallicize]] 8. Two wake paths, with identical-looking timelines:

- **Damage-wake** (requires actual HP loss -- hits fully absorbed by its block/Metallicize do NOT wake it): it is Stunned on that same monster turn, then attacks from the next turn. The stun replaces a turn it would have slept through anyway, so no attack is visibly skipped -- waking it early does NOT grant an extra free turn, it only starts its attack clock sooner.
- **Natural wake** (after 3 idle turns): it stirs on turn 3 (no action) and attacks from turn 4. Left alone, you get three free setup turns; an early damage-wake trades those away.

[[buffs/Metallicize]] removed on wake. Awake cycle: Attack, Attack, Siphon Soul (repeating).
