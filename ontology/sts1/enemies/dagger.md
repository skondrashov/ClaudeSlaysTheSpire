# Dagger

- **Type:** Elite (summon)
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 20-25

## Pattern

Fixed: Stab -> Explode. Each Dagger is a 2-turn time bomb.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Stab | 9 | Adds 1 [[cards/Wound]] to discard |
| Explode | 25 | Dagger dies |

## Mechanics

- Summoned by [[enemies/Reptomancer]]
