# Shield Gremlin

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 12-15

## Pattern

While allies alive: Protect (give random ally 7 Block) every turn.
When alone: Shield Bash every turn.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Protect | -- | Give random ally 7 Block |
| Shield Bash | 6 | Only when alone |
