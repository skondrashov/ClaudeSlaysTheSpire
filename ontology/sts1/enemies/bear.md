# Bear

- **Type:** Hallway (member of the [[events/Masked Bandits]] trio: [[enemies/Romeo]], Bear, [[enemies/Pointy]])
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 38-42

## Pattern

The debuffer of the trio. Opens by stripping your Dexterity (Bear Hug), then alternates a heavy single hit (Maul) with a smaller block-and-hit (Lunge). The Dexterity-down is what makes this fight expensive: it cuts the value of every Block card you play for the rest of combat, so the longer Bear lives the more each enemy hit leaks through.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |

| Bear Hug | -- | Applies -2 [[buffs/Dexterity]] |

## Notes

- Kill Bear FIRST. Its Dexterity-down debuff compounds across the whole fight, so every turn it survives degrades your block; the trio's total damage drops sharply once the debuff source is gone.
