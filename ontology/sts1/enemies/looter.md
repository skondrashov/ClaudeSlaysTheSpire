# Looter

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 44-48

## Pattern

Turns 1-2: Mug (10 damage + steals gold). Turn 3+: 50% Lunge (12 damage) or 50% Smoke Bomb (6 Block). After Smoke Bomb, uses Escape next turn (flees with stolen gold, combat ends). If Lunge on Turn 3, Smoke Bomb follows on Turn 4, then Escape Turn 5.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Mug | 10 | Steals gold (theft happens even if the attack is fully blocked) |
| Lunge | 12 | -- |
| Smoke Bomb | -- | Gains 6 Block |
| Escape | -- | Flees combat with stolen gold |

## Mechanics

Stolen gold is returned if the Looter is killed. If it escapes, gold is lost permanently.
