# The Transient

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 999
- **[[buffs/Fading]]:** 5

## Pattern

Attacks every turn with escalating damage. The ladder shifts +10 at A2+ (Normal enemies deadlier), so at A9 all play uses the A2+ column.

| Turn | Base (A0-A1) | A2+ (incl. A9) |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | 30 | 40 |
| 2 | 40 | 50 |
| 3 | 50 | 60 |
| 4 | 60 | 70 |
| 5 | 70 | 80 |

Damage increases by 10 per turn. A9 turn-5 hit is 80.

## Mechanics

Has [[buffs/Fading]] 5. The Transient attacks BEFORE fading.

Has [[buffs/Shifting]].
