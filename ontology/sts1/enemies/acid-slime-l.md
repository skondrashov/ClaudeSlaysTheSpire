# Acid Slime L

- **Type:** Hallway / Boss split
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 65-69 standalone, or equal to [[bosses/Slime Boss]] remaining HP at split

## Pattern

30% Corrosive Spit, 40% Tackle, 30% Lick. Cannot use Tackle twice in a row; other moves cannot repeat three times in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Corrosive Spit | 11 | Add 2 [[cards/Slimed]] to discard pile |
| Tackle | 16 | -- |
| Lick | -- | Apply 2 [[debuffs/Weak]] |

## Mechanics

Splits into 2 [[enemies/Acid Slime M]] when HP drops to 50% or below. Each copy has this enemy's current HP at time of split.
