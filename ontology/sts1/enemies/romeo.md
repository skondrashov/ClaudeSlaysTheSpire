# Romeo

- **Type:** Hallway (member of the [[events/Masked Bandits]] trio: Romeo, [[enemies/Bear]], [[enemies/Pointy]])
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 35-39

## Pattern

Opens with Mock (a no-damage taunt turn), then alternates a [[debuffs/Weak]]-applying strike (Agonizing Slash) with a larger plain hit (Cross Slash). Lower priority than Bear — its Weak is a softer debuff than Bear's permanent Dexterity-down.
