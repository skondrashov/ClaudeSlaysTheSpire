# Mystic

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 48-56 (A7+: 50-58)

## Pattern

Priority: **Heal whenever the TOTAL missing HP across all living enemies exceeds 15 (A17+: 20)** — the sum, not any single enemy, so small chip spread across the group arms it. Heal can be used twice in a row but not three times. When the heal is not armed: 60% Attack (+Frail), else Buff; Attack and Buff cannot repeat 3x (A17+: Attack cannot even repeat 2x).

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Attack | 8 (A2+: 9) | Apply 2 [[debuffs/Frail]] |
| Buff | -- | ALL living enemies +2 [[buffs/Strength]] (A2+: +3, A17+: +4), itself included |
| Heal | -- | ALL living enemies heal 16 (A17+: 20), itself included |

## Mechanics

- Heal threshold is the group's summed missing HP (> 15; A17+ > 20) — in a two-enemy fight, 8 chip on each is enough to arm it.
- **Alone**, the Mystic keeps the same kit pointed at itself: self-heal when its own missing HP crosses the threshold, self-buff +Str, and the 8/9-damage Frail attack.
