# Nemesis

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 185 (A0), 200 (A8)

## Pattern

First turn: 50/50 chance of Scorch (Burns) or Attack. Subsequent turns: ~35% Scorch, ~35% Attack, ~30% Scythe. Cannot use Scorch or Scythe twice in a row. Cannot use Attack three times in a row. Every other turn starting from Turn 2, gains Intangible.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Attack (multi-hit) | 6x3 = 18 (7x3 = 21 at A3) | -- |
| Scythe | 45 | Single massive hit |
| Scorch | -- | Adds 3 Burns to discard pile (5 Burns at A18) |

## Mechanics

Gains [[buffs/Intangible]] every other turn starting from Turn 2 (Turn 2, Turn 4, Turn 6, etc.).
