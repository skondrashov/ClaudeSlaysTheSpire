# Ascension

A difficulty system of 20 cumulative levels (Ascension 1-20). Selecting Ascension N
applies the modifiers of every level from 1 through N; Ascension 0 is the base game.

Each level adds one modifier, recorded as its own entry (Ascension 1 .. Ascension 20).
The modifier text here is the qualitative rule. The *quantitative* effect at a given
ascension (exact HP/damage increases for a specific enemy) is a derived composition of
the base entity with these modifiers -- the same base+delta relationship that upgraded
cards have with their phenomena -- and is not yet materialized. Base entity entries
(enemies, bosses, cards) therefore record A0 (base) numbers only.
