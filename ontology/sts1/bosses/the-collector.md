# The Collector

- **Type:** Boss
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 282

## Pattern

- **Turn 1:** Always Spawn -- summons 2 Torch Head minions (HP: 38-40, attack for 7 damage each).
- **Turn 2-3:** Fireball (18 damage) or Buff (all enemies gain 3 [[buffs/Strength]], Collector gains 15 Block).
- **Turn 4:** Always Mega Debuff -- Applies 3 [[debuffs/Weak]] + 3 [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] + 3 [[debuffs/Frail]] simultaneously. No damage.
- **Turn 5+:** With 0-1 minions: Spawn 25% / Fireball 45% / Buff 30%. With 2 minions: Fireball 70% / Buff 30%. Cannot use Buff 2x in a row. Cannot use Fireball 3x in a row.
