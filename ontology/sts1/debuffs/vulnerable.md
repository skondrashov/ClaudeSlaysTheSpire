# Vulnerable

- **Effect:** Target takes 50% more damage from [[types/Attack]]s (x1.5 multiplier)
- **Direction:** On enemy = player's Attacks deal 50% more. On player = enemy Attacks deal 50% more.
- **Scope:** [[types/Attack]] damage only. [[buffs/Thorns]], [[buffs/Juggernaut]], and potion damage are NOT multiplied.
