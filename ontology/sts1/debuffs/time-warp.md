# Time Warp

- **Effect:** Tracks cards played by the player across all turns. After 12 total cards are played (cumulative, not per turn), the enemy gains 2 [[buffs/Strength]] and the player's turn ends immediately. The counter then resets to 0.
- **Stacking:** Non-stacking
- **Interactions:** The 12-card counter includes ALL card plays ([[types/Attack]]s, [[types/Skill]]s, [[types/Power]]s) and persists across turns. The [[buffs/Strength]] gain is permanent and cumulative across multiple triggers. [[cards/Echo Form]] copies count as separate plays toward the counter.
