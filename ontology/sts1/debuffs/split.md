# Split

- **Effect:** When the enemy's HP drops to half or below, it dies and spawns two smaller versions of itself.
- **Duration:** One-time trigger
- **Stacking:** Non-stacking
- **Interactions:** The spawned slimes inherit [[debuffs/Poison]] stacks proportionally. Overkill damage beyond the split threshold is not dealt to the spawns.
- **Timing:** The split is performed as the enemy's action on its turn, replacing whatever move it had declared — a declared attack does not land if the enemy's HP is at or below the threshold when its turn comes.
