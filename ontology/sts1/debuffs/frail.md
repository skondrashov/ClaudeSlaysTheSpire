# Frail

- **Effect:** [[rules/Block]] gained from cards is reduced by 25% (x0.75 multiplier)
- **Formula:** `floor(block * 0.75)` (e.g., [[cards/Defend]] 5 becomes 3, [[cards/Shrug It Off]] 8 becomes 6)
- **Scope:** Only affects Block from card plays. Does NOT affect Block from [[buffs/Metallicize]], Frost Orbs, or other non-card sources.
