# Mushrooms

- **Act:** 1
- **Options:**
  - Stomp: Fight 3 [[enemies/Fungi Beast]]s. Reward: [[relics/Odd Mushroom]] relic ([[debuffs/Vulnerable]] multiplier reduced from 1.5x to 1.25x for player AND enemies)
  - Eat: Heal 21 HP, gain Parasite [[types/Curse]] (Unplayable card that clogs draws permanently until removed)
  - Leave: Nothing happens
- **Fight details:** 3 [[enemies/Fungi Beast]]s apply [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] via Spore Cloud on death. Significantly harder than most Act 1 hallway fights. Expect 30-44 HP loss.
