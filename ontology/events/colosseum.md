# Colosseum

- **Act:** 2
- **Options:**
  - Continue (forced): Enter a 2-wave combat. No alternative to avoid the first wave.
    - Wave 1: Fight 2 Slavers ([[enemies/Red Slaver]] + [[enemies/Blue Slaver]]). Combined ~20+ damage/turn. [[enemies/Blue Slaver]]'s [[debuffs/Entangled]] prevents attacks for 1 turn.
    - After Wave 1: Choose Fight (Wave 2 for rewards) or COWARDICE (escape with no additional reward).
    - Wave 2: Fight unpredictable enemies (can include [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]], [[enemies/Taskmaster]], or other elites).
- **Note:** [[relics/Juzu Bracelet]] does NOT prevent this event (it is an event with forced combat, not a monster room).
