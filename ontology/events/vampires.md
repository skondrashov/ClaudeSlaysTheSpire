# Vampires

- **Act:** 2
- **Options:**
  - Accept: Lose ALL Strikes from your deck. Gain 5 Bite cards (1E Attack, 7 damage, heals 2 HP). Lose approximately 30% of Max HP (observed: 80 -> 56 = -24 Max HP).
  - Refuse: Nothing happens
- **Bite card:** 1 Energy, deal 7 damage, heal 2 HP. Colorless card (not class-specific).
