# Mind Bloom

- **Act:** 3
- **Options:**
  - I am War: Fight a random Act 1 boss. Obtain a Rare Relic on victory.
  - I am Awake: Upgrade ALL cards in your deck. Obtain [[relics/Mark of the Bloom]] relic (you can no longer heal for the rest of the run).
  - I am Rich: Gain 999 gold. Obtain 2 Normality [[types/Curse]]s (limit hand to 3 cards per turn).
  - I am Healthy: Heal to full HP. Obtain Doubt [[types/Curse]] (1 [[debuffs/Weak]] at end of each turn).
