# Cursed Tome

- **Act:** 2
- **Options:**
  - Read: Start reading the book. At each stage choose Continue or Stop:
    - Stage 1: Lose 1 HP
    - Stage 2: Lose 2 HP
    - Stage 3: Lose 3 HP
    - Stage 4 (final): Lose 10 HP, obtain a book relic
  - Leave: Nothing happens
- **Total HP cost to complete:** 16 HP (1+2+3+10). Can leave at any stage.
- **Known book relics:**
  - [[relics/Enchiridion]]: Free random 0-cost [[types/Power]] card at start of each combat
  - [[relics/Nilry's Codex]]: At end of each turn, choose 1 of 3 randomly generated cards to add to hand
  - [[relics/Necronomicon]]: First [[types/Attack]] costing 2+ energy each turn plays twice. Adds Necronomicurse (unplayable, unremovable) to the deck.
