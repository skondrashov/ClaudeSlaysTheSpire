# Mysterious Sphere

- **Act:** 3
- **Options:**
  - Open Sphere: Fight a combat encounter (2 [[enemies/Orb Walker]]s). Reward: Rare Relic.
  - Leave: Nothing happens
- **Fight details:** 2 [[enemies/Orb Walker]]s with combined [[buffs/Strength]] scaling of +6/turn, plus Burns clogging draws and dealing end-of-turn damage. Can drain 40-70 HP even with a strong deck.
