# Snecko Oil

- **Effect:** Draw 5 cards. Randomize the cost of ALL cards in hand (0-3 energy each), including cards already in hand before the draw.
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Interaction:** Cards already in hand also get costs randomized. Similar to [[relics/Snecko Eye]]. When multiple copies exist with different randomized costs, the game selects the cheapest copy when played by name.
