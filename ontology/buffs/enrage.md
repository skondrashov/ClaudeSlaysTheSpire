# Enrage

- **Effect:** Whenever the player plays a [[types/Skill]] card, the entity with Enrage gains N [[buffs/Strength]]
- **Duration:** Permanent (does not decrement)
- **Trigger:** Player playing a [[types/Skill]] card. [[types/Power]] cards and [[types/Attack]] cards do NOT trigger it.
- **Sources (enemy):** [[enemies/Gremlin Nob]] (gains Enrage 2 via Bellow on Turn 1)
- **Timing:** Skills played before Enrage is active (before Bellow) do not trigger it.
