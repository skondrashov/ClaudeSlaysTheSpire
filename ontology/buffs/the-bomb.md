# The Bomb

- **Effect:** After 3 turns, deal 40 damage to ALL enemies. Counter decreases by 1 each turn.
- **Duration:** 3 turns (triggers when counter reaches 0)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/The Bomb]] card (Colorless [[types/Skill]], 2E), available from shops/events
- **Stacking:** Multiple Bombs track independently. Each has its own 3-turn timer.
- **Interaction:** Damage is fixed at 40 (50 upgraded) and is dealt at end of the 3rd turn after playing it. Good for long fights where you can survive 3 turns. Unaffected by [[buffs/Strength]].
