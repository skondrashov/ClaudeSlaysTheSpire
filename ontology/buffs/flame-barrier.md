# Flame Barrier

- **Effect:** Whenever you are attacked this turn, deal N damage back to the attacker.
- **Duration:** Until end of turn (expires at start of next turn)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Flame Barrier]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Skill]], 2E), deals 4/7 damage per attack received, also grants [[buffs/Block]]
- **Interaction:** Triggers per attack instance, not per hit in a multi-hit attack. If an enemy attacks 5x2, Flame Barrier triggers once (the entire action is one attack). Similar to [[buffs/Thorns]] but temporary and typically higher value.
- **Note:** Also grants [[buffs/Block]] when played (12/16), making it both offensive and defensive.
