# Fire Breathing

- **Effect:** Whenever you draw a [[types/Status]] or [[types/Curse]] card, deal N damage to ALL enemies.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Fire Breathing]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Power]], 1E), deals 6/10 damage
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple instances stack the damage dealt.
- **Interaction:** Triggers on draw, not on play. Synergizes with cards that shuffle Statuses into your deck ([[cards/Wild Strike]], [[cards/Reckless Charge]], [[cards/Power Through]]) and with [[buffs/Evolve]] (which draws more when Statuses are drawn, potentially chaining).
