# Pen Nib

- **Effect:** Every 10th [[types/Attack]] card played deals double damage
- **Duration:** Counter persists across all combats (does not reset between fights)
- **Stacking:** Non-stacking (single counter)
- **Interactions:** Only counts [[types/Attack]] card plays. [[types/Skill]]s and [[types/Power]]s do not increment the counter. Counter resets after the 10th Attack triggers.
