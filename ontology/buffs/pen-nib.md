# Pen Nib

- **Effect:** Every 10th [[types/Attack]] card played deals double damage
- **Source:** [[relics/Pen Nib]]
- **Persistence:** Counter tracks across ALL combats (does not reset between fights)
- **Interaction:** Only counts [[types/Attack]] card plays. [[types/Skill]]s and [[types/Power]]s do not increment the counter.
- **Activation:** On the 10th Attack, that Attack deals double damage. Counter then resets.
