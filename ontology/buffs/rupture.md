# Rupture

- **Effect:** Whenever you lose HP from a card YOU play, gain N [[buffs/Strength]].
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Rupture]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Power]], 1E), grants 1/2 [[buffs/Strength]] per trigger
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple Ruptures increase Strength gained per trigger.
- **Interaction:** Triggers on self-damage from cards like [[cards/Hemokinesis]], [[cards/Offering]], [[cards/Bloodletting]], [[cards/Brutality]], [[cards/Combust]]. Does NOT trigger on enemy damage or [[debuffs/Poison]] damage. Key component of self-damage [[characters/Ironclad]] builds.
