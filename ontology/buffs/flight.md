# Flight

- **Effect:** ALL damage taken is halved (rounded down). Each time the entity takes damage (each hit), Flight decreases by 1. When Flight reaches 0, damage is no longer halved.
- **Duration:** Reapplied each turn by the enemy ([[enemies/Byrd]]s get Flight 3 each turn at A0)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[enemies/Byrd]]s (Flight 3/4)
- **Interaction with multi-hit:** Each hit decrements Flight AND is halved individually. Multi-hit attacks strip Flight faster. A 5x2 attack removes 5 Flight charges while each hit deals 1 damage.
- **Strategy implication:** Multi-hit attacks ([[cards/Sword Boomerang]], [[cards/Pummel]], [[cards/Dagger Spray]]) strip Flight efficiently. Single large hits are halved wastefully.
