# Omega

- **Effect:** At the end of each turn, deal 50 damage to ALL enemies.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Omega]] card ([[characters/Watcher]] [[types/Power]], 3E) — obtained only through the [[cards/Alpha]] > Beta > Omega card chain
- **Interaction:** Extremely powerful but requires setup. [[cards/Alpha]] (1E) shuffles Beta into draw pile; Beta (2E) shuffles Omega into draw pile. The 50 damage is fixed and hits all enemies every turn indefinitely.
