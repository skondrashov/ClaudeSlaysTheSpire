# Thorns

- **Effect:** Whenever the entity is attacked, deal N damage back to the attacker
- **Duration:** Permanent (does not decrement)
- **Sources (player):** [[relics/Bronze Scales]] (3 Thorns), [[buffs/Flame Barrier]] (temporary, 4/6 per turn)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[enemies/Spiker]] (starts with Thorns 3, gains more each turn)
- **Interaction with multi-hit:** Triggers once per hit. A 3x4 attack against Thorns 3 = 9 counter damage.
- **Ignores block:** Thorns damage bypasses the attacker's [[buffs/Block]].
