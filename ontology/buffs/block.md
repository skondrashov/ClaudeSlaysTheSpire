# Block

- **Effect:** Absorbs incoming damage before HP. Damage reduces Block first; excess hits HP.
- **Duration:** Expires at the start of the player's turn (resets to 0)
- **Exceptions:**
  - [[buffs/Barricade]] ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Power]]): Block does NOT expire between turns. Accumulates.
  - [[relics/Calipers]]: Lose only 15 Block at start of turn (instead of all).
  - [[cards/Blur]] ([[characters/Silent]]): Block doesn't expire this turn.
- **Modified by:**
  - [[buffs/Dexterity]]: +1 Block per point to all block-gaining cards
  - [[debuffs/Frail]]: x0.75 Block from cards
- **Sources:** [[cards/Defend]] cards, [[types/Skill]]s that grant Block, Frost orbs ([[characters/Defect]]), [[buffs/Metallicize]] (end of turn), [[buffs/Plated Armor]] (end of turn)
- **Enemy block:** Enemies also gain Block. It does NOT expire for enemies unless specified. Enemy Block absorbs your attacks.
