# Loop

- **Effect:** At the start of each turn, trigger the passive ability of the first Orb N additional times.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Loop]] card ([[characters/Defect]] [[types/Power]], 1E), triggers 1/2 additional times
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple Loops increase the number of additional passive triggers.
- **Interaction:** Applies to whichever Orb is in the first (leftmost) slot. Lightning passive deals damage, Frost passive gains [[buffs/Block]], Dark passive gains Dark damage. Extremely strong with Frost + [[buffs/Focus]] for repeatable Block generation.
