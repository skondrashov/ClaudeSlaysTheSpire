# Evolve

- **Effect:** Whenever you draw a [[types/Status]] card, draw N additional cards.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Evolve]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Power]], 1E), draw 1/2 cards per Status drawn
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple Evolves stack the draw count.
- **Interaction:** Triggers on [[types/Status]] cards (Wounds, Burns, Dazed, Slimed, Void). Does NOT trigger on [[types/Curse]]s. Synergizes with [[cards/Wild Strike]], [[cards/Reckless Charge]], [[cards/Power Through]] (which add Statuses to your deck).
