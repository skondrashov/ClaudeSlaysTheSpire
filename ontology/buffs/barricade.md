# Barricade

- **Effect:** [[buffs/Block]] is not removed at the start of turn. All Block persists indefinitely until used to absorb damage.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Barricade]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Power]], 3E / 2E upgraded), [[relics/Calipers]] (keeps up to 15 Block between turns — similar but not identical)
- **Interaction:** Stacks with all Block-generating effects ([[buffs/Metallicize]], [[buffs/Flame Barrier]], [[buffs/Feel No Pain]]). Block accumulates without limit across turns.
- **Strategy implication:** Extremely powerful with repeatable Block generation. Enables "infinite Block" strategies.
