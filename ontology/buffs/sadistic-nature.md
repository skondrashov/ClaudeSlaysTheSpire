# Sadistic Nature

- **Effect:** Whenever you apply a debuff to an enemy, deal N damage to that enemy.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Sadistic Nature]] card ([[characters/Silent]] [[types/Power]], 0E), deals 5/7 damage per debuff applied
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple instances increase damage per debuff application.
- **Interaction:** Triggers on each debuff application — [[debuffs/Vulnerable]], [[debuffs/Weak]], [[debuffs/Poison]] (each stack counts as one application), etc. Particularly strong with multi-target debuffs ([[cards/Crippling Cloud]]) or frequent debuff application ([[buffs/Noxious Fumes]]).
