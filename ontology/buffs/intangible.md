# Intangible

- **Effect:** Reduce ALL damage taken to 1 (both attacks and non-attack damage)
- **Duration:** N turns, decrements at start of entity's turn
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Wraith Form]] (3 turns, [[characters/Silent]]), Apparition cards (1 turn each), [[relics/Incense Burner]] (every 6th turn)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[enemies/Nemesis]] (2 turns, alternating pattern)
- **Interaction with multi-hit:** Each hit deals 1 damage separately. A 5x6 attack deals 5 damage total (1 per hit).
