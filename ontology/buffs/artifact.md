# Artifact

- **Effect:** Negates the next N debuffs applied to the entity. Each debuff negated consumes 1 stack.
- **Duration:** Permanent until all stacks consumed
- **Sources (player):** [[relics/Clockwork Souvenir]] (1 at combat start), [[cards/Panacea]] (1/2), [[cards/Core Surge]] (1)
- **Sources (enemy):** [[bosses/Bronze Automaton]] (3 at combat start), various bosses/elites
- **What counts as a debuff:** [[debuffs/Vulnerable]], [[debuffs/Weak]], [[debuffs/Frail]], Strength Down, etc.
- **Strategy interaction:** Must strip Artifact before debuffs will apply. Cheap debuffs ([[cards/Thunderclap]] 1E for [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] to all) are efficient Artifact strippers.
