# Plated Armor

- **Effect:** Gain N [[buffs/Block]] at end of turn. Decreases by 1 each time the entity takes unblocked HP damage.
- **Duration:** Permanent until reduced to 0 by taking unblocked damage
- **Sources:** [[cards/Plated Armor]] card (4/6), some enemies
- **Key mechanic:** Each instance of unblocked damage removes 1 stack. Multi-hit attacks that break through Block remove 1 stack per hit that reaches HP.
- **NOT modified by [[buffs/Dexterity]] or [[debuffs/Frail]]:** Passive effect, not a card play.
- **Difference from [[buffs/Metallicize]]:** Metallicize is permanent. Plated Armor degrades when you take HP damage.
