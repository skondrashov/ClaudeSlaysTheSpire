# Strength

- **Effect:** Each point adds 1 damage to every [[types/Attack]] card played
- **Duration:** Permanent within combat, resets to 0 between combats
- **Exception:** Relic Strength ([[relics/Vajra]] +1) persists across combats
- **Sources (within combat):** [[cards/Inflame]] (+2), [[cards/Spot Weakness]] (+3/+4), [[cards/Demon Form]] (+2/turn), [[potions/Strength Potion]] (+2), [[cards/Limit Break]] (double current Str)
- **Negative Strength:** Reduces attack damage by that amount. Applied by [[enemies/Lagavulin]] debuff, [[cards/Disarm]].
