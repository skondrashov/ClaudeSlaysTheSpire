# Dark Embrace

- **Effect:** Whenever a card is Exhausted, draw 1 card.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Dark Embrace]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Power]], 2E / 1E upgraded)
- **Stacking:** Multiple instances stack. Two Dark Embraces = draw 2 cards per Exhaust.
- **Interaction:** Synergizes with Exhaust-heavy builds ([[cards/Corruption]], [[cards/Sentinel]], [[buffs/Feel No Pain]]). With Corruption, every [[types/Skill]] exhausts on play, drawing a replacement card.
