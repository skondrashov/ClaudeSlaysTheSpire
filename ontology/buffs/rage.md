# Rage

- **Effect:** Whenever you play an [[types/Attack]] card, gain N [[buffs/Block]].
- **Duration:** Until end of turn (expires at start of next turn)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Rage]] card ([[characters/Ironclad]] [[types/Skill]], 0E), grants 3/5 Block per Attack played
- **Interaction:** Turns every Attack card into a hybrid attack/defense card. Particularly strong in turns where many cheap Attacks are played. Synergizes with [[buffs/Juggernaut]] (each Block gain triggers damage).
