# Burst

- **Effect:** The next N [[types/Skill]] cards played are played twice (the card is played an additional time for free).
- **Duration:** Expires at end of turn if unused, or after N Skills are doubled
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Burst]] card ([[characters/Silent]] [[types/Skill]], 1E), duplicates Skill effects
- **Stacking:** Additive. Burst 2 means the next 2 Skills are doubled.
- **Interaction:** Similar to [[cards/Double Tap]] (which doubles [[types/Attack]]s) and [[cards/Amplify]] (which doubles [[types/Power]]s). Does not duplicate card draw costs or exhaust — the copy plays automatically.
