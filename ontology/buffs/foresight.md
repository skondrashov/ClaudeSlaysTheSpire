# Foresight

- **Effect:** At the start of each turn, Scry N (look at the top N cards of draw pile and optionally discard any of them).
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Foresight]] card ([[characters/Watcher]] [[types/Power]], 1E), Scry 3/4
- **Stacking:** Additive. Multiple instances increase the Scry amount.
- **Interaction:** Improves draw quality each turn. Synergizes with cards that benefit from discard or that trigger on Scry.
