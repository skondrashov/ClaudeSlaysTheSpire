# Mantra

- **Effect:** A resource that accumulates. When reaching 10+, the player enters Divinity stance and Mantra resets to 0 (keeping overflow). [[characters/Watcher]]-specific.
- **Interactions:** 10+ Mantra triggers Divinity (triple damage, +3 Energy). If 12 Mantra accumulated, enters Divinity with 2 Mantra remaining.
