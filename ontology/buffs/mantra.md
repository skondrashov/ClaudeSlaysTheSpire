# Mantra

- **Effect:** A resource that accumulates. When reaching 10+, the player enters Divinity stance ([[characters/Watcher]]) and Mantra resets to 0 (keeping overflow).
- **Sources:** [[cards/Worship]] (+5 Mantra), [[cards/Prostrate]] (+2 Mantra + 4 [[buffs/Block]]), [[cards/Devotion]] ([[types/Power]], +2/3 Mantra per turn), [[relics/Damaru]] (+1 Mantra per turn)
- **Persistence:** Mantra persists between turns within combat. Does not persist between combats.
- **Divinity entry:** 10+ Mantra → enter Divinity (triple damage, +3 Energy), Mantra resets. If you had 12 Mantra, you enter Divinity with 2 Mantra remaining.
