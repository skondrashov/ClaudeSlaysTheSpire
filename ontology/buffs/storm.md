# Storm

- **Effect:** Whenever you play a [[types/Power]] card, channel 1 Lightning Orb.
- **Duration:** Permanent ([[types/Power]] effect, lasts rest of combat)
- **Sources (player):** [[cards/Storm]] card ([[characters/Defect]] [[types/Power]], 1E / 0E upgraded)
- **Stacking:** Multiple instances stack. Two Storms = channel 2 Lightning per Power played.
- **Interaction:** Since Storm itself is a [[types/Power]], playing it with an existing Storm triggers the effect. Synergizes with Power-heavy [[characters/Defect]] builds. Each Lightning channeled benefits from [[buffs/Focus]] for passive damage and evoke damage.
