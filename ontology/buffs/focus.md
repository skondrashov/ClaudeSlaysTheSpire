# Focus

- **Effect:** Increases ALL Orb passive and evoke values by N. [[characters/Defect]]-specific.
- **Formula:**
  - Lightning passive: 3 + Focus damage. Evoke: 8 + Focus damage.
  - Frost passive: 2 + Focus block. Evoke: 5 + Focus block.
  - Dark passive: gains 6 + Focus damage per turn. Evoke: accumulated.
  - Plasma: unaffected by Focus.
- **Negative Focus:** Reduces Orb values.
