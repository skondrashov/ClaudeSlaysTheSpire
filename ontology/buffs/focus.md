# Focus

- **Effect:** Increases ALL orb passive AND evoke values by N ([[characters/Defect]]-specific)
- **Duration:** Permanent within combat (unless modified by [[cards/Biased Cognition]] decay or enemy debuffs)
- **Formula:**
  - Lightning passive: 3 + Focus damage. Evoke: 8 + Focus damage.
  - Frost passive: 2 + Focus block. Evoke: 5 + Focus block.
  - Dark passive: gains 6 + Focus damage per turn. Evoke: accumulated.
  - Plasma: unaffected by Focus.
- **Sources:** [[cards/Defragment]] (+1/+2 permanent), [[cards/Consume]] (+2 permanent, lose 1 orb slot), [[relics/Data Disk]] (+1 innate), [[cards/Biased Cognition]] (+4 then -1/turn)
- **Negative Focus:** Reduces orb values. Applied by [[cards/Biased Cognition]] decay over time.
