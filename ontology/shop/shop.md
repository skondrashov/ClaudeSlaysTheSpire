# Shop

Available on shop ($) map nodes. Offers cards, relics, potions, and services.

## Inventory

### Cards (5 offered)
- 2 Attack cards (colored, from your character's pool)
- 2 Skill cards (colored, from your character's pool)
- 1 Colorless card

### Pricing
- Common cards: 45-55 gold
- Uncommon cards: 68-82 gold
- Rare cards: 135-165 gold
- Colorless uncommon: 81-99 gold
- Colorless rare: 162-198 gold

Cards on sale (marked): 50% off.

### Relics (3 offered)
- Pricing varies by rarity:
  - Common: 143-157 gold
  - Uncommon: 238-262 gold
  - Rare: 285-315 gold
  - Shop-exclusive: 143-157 gold

### Potions (3 offered)
- Common: 48-52 gold
- Uncommon: 72-78 gold
- Rare: 95-105 gold

### Services
- **Card removal:** Remove one card from your deck permanently. Cost starts at 75 gold, increases by 25 each time used (75, 100, 125...).
- Curses can be removed (except Parasite)
- Strikes and Defends can be removed

## Membership Card (relic)
All shop prices reduced by 50%.

## Courier (relic)
Shop always has a third relic slot.

## The Courier + Membership Card
Stacks. Third relic at 50% off.
