# Chosen

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 95-99

## Pattern

Always starts with Poke, then Hex. After that, repeats: (Debilitate 50% / Drain 50%) followed by (Poke 60% / Zap 40%).

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Poke | 5x2 = 10 (6x2 = 12 at A2) | -- |
| Zap | 18 (21 at A2) | -- |
| Debilitate | 10 (12 at A2) | Applies 2 Vulnerable |
| Drain | -- | Applies 3 Weak + gains 3 Strength |
| Hex | -- | Applies Hex debuff |

## Mechanics

**Hex:** Whenever the player plays a non-Attack card, shuffle 1 Dazed into draw pile. Lasts rest of combat. Applied on Turn 2. Powers do NOT trigger Hex. Attacks are safe.

**Drain Strength Scaling:** Each Drain gives +3 Str. With Vulnerable on the player, even base Poke (5x2) becomes dangerous over time.

## Appearances

Paired with Cultist in Act 2 hallway fights. Can appear with Byrds (Chosen+Byrd fight).
