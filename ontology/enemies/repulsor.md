# Repulsor

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 29-35

## Pattern

Each turn, 80% chance to use Bash (adds 2 Dazed to draw pile), 20% chance to use Repulse (attack). Cannot use Repulse twice in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Repulse | 11 (13 at A2) | -- |
| Bash | -- | Adds 2 Dazed cards to draw pile |

## Mechanics

**Dazed Generation:** Dazed cards are Unplayable + Ethereal status cards that clog the draw pile. Each Bash adds 2 Dazed, rapidly degrading hand quality. Over 4 turns, 6-8 Dazed cards can be added.

## Appearances

Appears with other Shapes (Spikers, Exploders) in the 3 Shapes encounter.
