# Spiker

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 42-56

## Pattern

Each turn, 50/50 chance of Cut (attack) or Spike (buff Thorns). Cannot use Cut twice in a row. Cannot use Spike after it has been used 6 times (forces Cut instead).

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Cut | 7 (9 at A2) | -- |
| Spike | -- | Gains +2 Thorns |

## Mechanics

**Thorns 3 (innate):** Starts with Thorns 3. Each Spike adds +2 Thorns. Every time the player deals attack damage to a Spiker, the player takes damage equal to current Thorns.

**Thorns Scaling:** 3 -> 5 -> 7 -> 9 -> 11 -> 13 -> 15 (after 6 Spikes, maximum reached). Multi-hit attacks trigger Thorns per hit (4-hit Blade Dance against Thorns 9 costs 36 HP).

**Maximum Spikes:** After 6 Spike uses, Spiker can only use Cut.

## Appearances

Appears with other Shapes (Exploders, Repulsors) in the 3 Shapes encounter. Can appear in mixed Act 3 hallway fights.
