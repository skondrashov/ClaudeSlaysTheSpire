# The Transient

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 999
- **[[buffs/Fading]]:** 5

## Pattern

Attacks every turn with escalating damage:
- Turn 1: 30 (40 at A2)
- Turn 2: 40 (50 at A2)
- Turn 3: 50 (60 at A2)
- Turn 4: 60 (70 at A2)
- Turn 5: 70 (80 at A2)

Damage increases by 10 per turn.

## Mechanics

Has [[buffs/Fading]] 5 ([[buffs/Fading]] 6 at A17). The Transient attacks BEFORE fading.

Has [[buffs/Shifting]].
