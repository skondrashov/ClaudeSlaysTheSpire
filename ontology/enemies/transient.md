# The Transient

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 999
- **Fading:** 5 (dies after 5 turns)

## Pattern

Attacks every turn with escalating damage:
- Turn 1: 30 (40 at A2)
- Turn 2: 40 (50 at A2)
- Turn 3: 50 (60 at A2)
- Turn 4: 60 (70 at A2)
- Turn 5: 70 (80 at A2)

Total damage over 5 turns: 250 at A0. Damage increases by 10 per turn.

## Mechanics

**Fading 5:** Loses 1 per turn, dies at 0 (Fading 6 at A17). The Transient attacks BEFORE fading -- you must survive every hit including the last.

**Shifting:** When the Transient takes damage, it LOSES that much Strength for the rest of the turn. If the Transient would deal 50 and you deal 20 damage to it, it only hits for 30. This means attacking REDUCES its damage this turn.

**Damage Efficiency:** 1 energy on a Strike (6 damage) = 6 less incoming via Shifting = effectively 6 block. 1 energy on a Defend (5 block) = 5 block. Attacking is strictly better than blocking because damage dealt via Shifting reduces the attack AND you still play block cards with remaining energy.
