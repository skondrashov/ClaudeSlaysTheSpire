# The Maw

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 300 (does NOT change with Ascension)

## Pattern

- **Turn 1:** Roar (applies 3 Weak + 3 Frail). No damage.
- **Turns 2+:** After Drool -> 50/50 Slam or Nom Nom. After Slam -> 50/50 Nom Nom or Drool. After Nom Nom -> always Drool.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Roar | -- | Applies 3 Weak + 3 Frail |
| Slam | 25 (30 at A2) | -- |
| Nom Nom | 5xN (N increases -- roughly current turn / 2 rounded up) | -- |
| Drool | -- | Gains 3 Strength (5 at A5) |

## Mechanics

**Turn 1 Debuff:** Applies Weak, Frail, and Vulnerable simultaneously. Lasts 2-3 turns.

**Escalating Damage:** Early attacks are trivial (5-16 damage), but later attacks reach 33-56+.

**Multiple Free Turns:** The Maw frequently buffs (Drool) instead of attacking, giving free turns for Power setup.

**Fight Length:** 8-9 turns minimum due to 300 HP.
