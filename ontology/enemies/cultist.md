# Cultist

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 48-54

## Pattern

Always starts with Incantation (gains 3 Ritual, does NOT attack). Subsequent turns: uses Dark Strike every turn.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Incantation | -- | Gains Ritual 3 |
| Dark Strike | 6 (base, +3 per turn from Ritual) | -- |

Damage by turn: Turn 2: 9. Turn 3: 12. Turn 4: 15. Turn 5: 18.

## Mechanics

**Ritual 3:** Gains +3 Strength per turn permanently. Turn 1 is completely free (no attack).

## Act 2 Appearances

Cultist appears paired with Chosen in Act 2 hallway fights. In this pairing, Cultist still buffs Turn 1 (free).
