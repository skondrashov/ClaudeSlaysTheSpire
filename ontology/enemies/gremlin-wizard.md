# Gremlin Wizard

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 21-25

## Pattern

Charges for 2 turns (no damage), then Ultimate Blast. After first blast, charges for 3 turns before each subsequent blast.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Charging | -- | No effect (telegraph) |
| Ultimate Blast | 25 | -- |

## Mechanics

- Extremely dangerous if left alive — 25 damage is nearly lethal in Act 1
- Kill priority: before first Ultimate Blast fires (2 turns to kill)
