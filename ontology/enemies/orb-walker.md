# Orb Walker

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 90-96 (A0), 92-102 (A7)

Can appear as a single enemy or as a pair (2 Orb Walkers).

## Pattern

Each turn: 60% Laser, 40% Claw. Cannot use either move 3x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Laser | 10 (11 at A2) | Adds 1 [[cards/Burn]] to draw pile + 1 [[cards/Burn]] to discard pile |
| Claw | 15 (16 at A2) | -- |

## Mechanics

Gains 3 [[buffs/Strength]] at end of each turn (5 at A17).
