# Orb Walker

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 90-96 (A0), 92-102 (A7)

Can appear as a single enemy or as a pair (2 Orb Walkers).

## Pattern

Each turn: 60% Laser, 40% Claw. Cannot use either move 3x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Laser | 10 (11 at A2) | Adds 1 Burn to draw pile + 1 Burn to discard pile |
| Claw | 15 (16 at A2) | -- |

## Mechanics

**Strength Up 3:** Gains 3 Strength at end of each turn (5 at A17). Attacks every turn with increasing damage.

- Turn 1: 10-15 damage per OW
- Turn 2: 13-18 per OW
- Turn 3: 16-21 per OW

**Burns:** Added to draw pile. Clog the hand and deal 2 damage each at end of turn (4 for Burns+). In long fights, 4+ Burns deal 8-16 end-of-turn damage on top of attacks.

**Two Orb Walker Variant:** Combined Str scaling is +6/turn. Combined incoming grows from 20 (Turn 1) to 44+ (Turn 4).
