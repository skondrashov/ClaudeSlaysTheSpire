# Centurion

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 76-80

## Pattern

**While [[enemies/Mystic]] alive:** 65% Slash, 35% Protect. Cannot use same move 3x in a row.
**After [[enemies/Mystic]] dies:** 65% Slash, 35% Fury. Cannot use same move 3x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Slash | 12 | -- |
| Protect | -- | Gives [[enemies/Mystic]] 15 [[buffs/Block]] |
| Fury | 6x3 | Only used after [[enemies/Mystic]] dies |

## Mechanics

- Mode shift when [[enemies/Mystic]] dies: replaces Protect with Fury (multi-hit)
