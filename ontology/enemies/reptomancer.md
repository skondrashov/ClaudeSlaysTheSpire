# Reptomancer

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 3
- **Reptomancer HP:** 180-190
- **Dagger HP:** 20-25 each

## Pattern

Always opens with Summon Dagger. Afterward: approximately equal distribution of Snake Strike, Big Bite, and Summon Dagger. Cannot use Snake Strike or Big Bite twice in a row. Cannot use Summon Dagger three times in a row. If Summon Dagger is chosen with 4 Daggers already in play, uses Snake Strike instead. Maximum 4 Daggers active at any time.

## Combat Setup

Fight starts with Reptomancer + 2 Daggers (3 enemies total).

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Big Bite | 30 (34 at A3) | -- |
| Snake Strike | 13x2 = 26 (16x2 = 32 at A3) | Applies 1 [[debuffs/Weak]] |
| Summon Dagger | -- | Summons 2 Daggers |

## Mechanics

Starts with 2 [[enemies/Dagger]] minions. Maximum 4 [[enemies/Dagger]] active at any time.
