# Acid Slime L

- **Type:** Hallway / Boss split
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 65-69 standalone, or equal to Slime Boss's remaining HP at split

## Pattern

30% Corrosive Spit, 40% Tackle, 30% Lick. Cannot use Tackle twice in a row; other moves cannot repeat three times in a row. Splits into 2x Acid Slime (M) when HP drops to 50% or below.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Corrosive Spit | 11 (12 at A2) | Adds 2 Slimed cards to discard pile |
| Tackle | 16 (18 at A2) | -- |
| Lick | -- | Applies 2 Weak |
| Split | -- | At 50% HP or below, spawns 2 Acid Slime (M) with current HP each |

## Mechanics

**Split at 50% HP:** Creates 2 Acid Slime (M) enemies with current HP each.

**Weak 2:** Reduces player damage output by 25% for 2 turns.
