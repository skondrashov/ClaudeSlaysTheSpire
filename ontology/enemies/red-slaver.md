# Red Slaver

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 46-50

## Pattern

Always starts with Stab. Then repeats Scrape -> Scrape -> Stab until Entangle is used. Has 25% chance per turn to use Entangle instead of the normal pattern. After Entangle (used only once per combat): 55% Stab, 45% Scrape (cannot use same move 3x in a row).

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Stab | 13 (14 at A2) | -- |
| Scrape | 8 (9 at A2) | Applies 1 Vulnerable (2 Vuln at A17) |
| Entangle | -- | Applies Entangled (cannot play Attack cards this turn). Once per combat. |

## Mechanics

**Entangle:** Prevents playing ALL Attack cards for one turn. The only source of Entangled in the game. Can only be used once per combat. 25% chance per turn means it usually fires by turn 3-4.

## Appearances

Solo in Act 1. With Blue Slaver + Taskmaster in Act 2 elite Slavers fight.
