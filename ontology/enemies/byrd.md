# Byrd

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 25-31 each (appears in groups of 3)

## Pattern (while airborne)

50% Peck (1x5 damage), 30% Caw (+1 Strength), 20% Swoop (12 damage). Cannot use Caw or Swoop twice in a row. Cannot use Peck three times in a row.

## When Grounded (Flight removed)

Stunned -> Headbutt (3 damage) -> Go Airborne (gains 3 Flying) -> returns to normal pattern.
