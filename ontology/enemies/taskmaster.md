# Taskmaster

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 54-60

## Pattern

Uses Scouring Whip every turn. Attacks from Turn 1.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Scouring Whip | 7 | Shuffle 1 Wound into discard pile |

## Mechanics

- Generates Wounds every turn, clogging deck over time
- Part of the [[encounters/Slavers]] elite fight with [[enemies/Blue Slaver]] and [[enemies/Red Slaver]]
