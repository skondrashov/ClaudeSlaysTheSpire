# Exploder

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 30

## Pattern

Fixed sequence: Slam -> Slam -> Explode. Uses Slam twice, then detonates on Turn 3. The Explosive timer starts at 3 and decreases by 1 at end of each turn. When it reaches 0, Exploder uses Explode and dies.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Slam | 9 (11 at A2) | -- |
| Explode | 30 | Kills self |

## Mechanics

**Explosive 3 (countdown timer):** Decreases by 1 each turn. At 0, Exploder detonates for 30 damage and dies. Hard timer -- 2 turns to kill or take 30 damage.

**Explosion does NOT ignore block.** Can be blocked if you cannot kill in time.

## Appearances

Appears with other Shapes (Spikers, Repulsors) in the 3 Shapes encounter.
