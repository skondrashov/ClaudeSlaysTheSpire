# Fungi Beast

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1-2
- **HP:** 22-28

## Pattern

Each turn, 60% chance Bite, 40% chance Grow. Cannot use Bite 3 times in a row. Cannot use Grow twice in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Bite | 6 (base, scales with Strength) | -- |
| Grow | -- | Gains 3 [[buffs/Strength]] |

## Mechanics

**Spore Cloud 2:** On death, applies 2 [[debuffs/Vulnerable]] to player.
