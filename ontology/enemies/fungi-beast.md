# Fungi Beast

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1-2
- **HP:** 22-28 (appears in pairs or with Shelled Parasite)

## Pattern

Each turn, 60% chance Bite, 40% chance Grow. Cannot use Bite 3 times in a row. Cannot use Grow twice in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Bite | 6 (base, scales with Strength) | -- |
| Grow | -- | Gains 3 Strength |

## Mechanics

**Strength Scaling (+3 per Grow):** After 1 Grow = 9 Bite, after 2 Grows = 12 Bite, after 3 Grows = 15 Bite. Two Fungi Beasts attacking same turn deal combined 18 (early) to 27+ (late).

**Spore Cloud 2:** On death, applies 2 Vulnerable to player.

## Act 2 Pairing

Appears with Shelled Parasite in Act 2. Fungi Beast's Spore Cloud applies Vulnerable, which makes Shelled Parasite's 13+ damage attacks hit for 19+.
