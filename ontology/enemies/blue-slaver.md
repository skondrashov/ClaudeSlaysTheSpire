# Blue Slaver

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 1
- **HP:** 46-50

## Pattern

Each turn, 60% chance to use Stab, 40% chance to use Rake. Cannot use the same attack three times in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Stab | 12 (13 at A2) | -- |
| Rake | 7 (8 at A2) | Applies 1 Weak |

## Mechanics

**Weak Application:** Rake applies 1 Weak, reducing player damage output by 25% for 1 turn. No free turns -- attacks every turn.

## Appearances

Solo in Act 1 Hard Pool. Also appears in the Act 2 Slavers elite (alongside Red Slaver + Taskmaster).
