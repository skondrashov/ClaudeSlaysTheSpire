# Giant Head

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 3
- **HP:** 500

## Pattern

For the first 4 turns, uses Count (13 damage) or Glare (1 Weak) with 50/50 chance (cannot repeat either 3x in a row). After 4 turns, exclusively uses "It Is Time" for the rest of combat.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Count | 13 | -- |
| Glare | -- | Applies 1 Weak |
| It Is Time | 30 (base, increases by 5 per use: 30 -> 35 -> 40 -> 45 -> 50 -> 55 -> 60 max) | -- |

## Mechanics

**Slow:** Whenever the player plays a card, the Giant Head receives 10% more damage from Attacks this turn. This REWARDS playing many cards per turn -- more plays = more damage dealt to it.

**It Is Time Escalation:** After 4 turns, every subsequent turn deals 30+ and growing (+5 per use). The fight is a DPS race -- kill before it reaches 50+.

**Metallicize 8:** Gains 8 Block per turn passively.

**Fight Length:** 10+ turns at 500 HP.
