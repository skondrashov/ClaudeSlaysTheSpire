# Snecko

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 114-120

## Pattern

Always starts with Perplexing Glare (Confused debuff). Then each turn: 60% Bite, 40% Tail Whip. Cannot use Bite 3x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Perplexing Glare | -- | Applies Confused |
| Bite | 15 (18 at A2) | -- |
| Tail Whip | 8 (10 at A2) | Applies 2 Vulnerable (+ 2 Weak at A17) |

## Mechanics

**Confusion:** When applied, all cards in hand are randomly assigned costs of 0-3 each turn for the rest of combat. Persists until combat ends.

**X-Cost Interaction:** Whirlwind (X-cost) under Confusion uses ALL remaining energy regardless of randomized cost. X-cost cards are reliable under Confusion because they always consume whatever energy is left.

**Tail Whip + Bite Combo:** Vulnerable from Tail Whip makes subsequent Bite attacks hit for 22+ damage.

**Red Mask Relic:** Applies 1 Weak to Snecko at combat start, slightly reducing first attack.
