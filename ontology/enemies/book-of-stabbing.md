# Book of Stabbing

- **Type:** Elite
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 160-164

## Pattern

85% Multi Stab, 15% Single Stab. Cannot use Multi Stab 3x in a row or Single Stab 2x in a row.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Multi Stab | 6xN (N = times used + 2). Turn 1: 6x2=12, Turn 2: 6x3=18, Turn 3: 6x4=24, Turn 4: 6x5=30, Turn 5: 6x6=36 | (7xN at A3) |
| Single Stab | 21 (24 at A3) | -- |

## Mechanics

**Escalating Multi Stab:** Multi Stab gains +1 hit every time it is used. The scaling is from hit count increasing. Each Multi Stab adds more hits: 2 -> 3 -> 4 -> 5 -> 6.

**Painful Stabs:** Whenever the player receives attack damage, adds Wounds to discard pile. Wounds are Unplayable status cards that clog the deck.

**Wound Clog:** By turn 6-7, Wounds can comprise 60%+ of the deck. A 21-card deck accumulating 3+ Wounds per turn means by mid-fight, draws are 3 Wounds and 2 playable cards per turn.
