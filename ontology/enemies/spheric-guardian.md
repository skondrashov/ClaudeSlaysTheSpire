# Spheric Guardian

- **Type:** Hallway
- **Act:** 2
- **HP:** 20 (effectively 60+ with starting block)

## Starting State

- Artifact 3
- Block 40
- Barricade (block never expires)

## Pattern

Fixed sequence: Activate (25 Block) -> Slam (10 damage + 5 Frail) -> then alternates Slam (10x2 = 20 damage) and Harden (10 damage + 15 Block) for rest of combat.

## Attacks

| Move | Damage | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| Activate | -- | Gains 25 Block |
| Slam (first) | 10 | Applies 5 Frail |
| Slam (subsequent) | 10x2 = 20 | -- |
| Harden | 10 | Gains 15 Block |

## Mechanics

**Barricade:** Block never expires. The Spheric Guardian gains block on defend turns (15-20 per action) and since block persists, it stacks indefinitely.

**Artifact 3:** First 3 debuff applications are negated.

**Frail 5:** Reduces block from ALL sources by 25% for 5 turns. Defend gives 3 instead of 5. Shrug It Off gives 6 instead of 8.

**Malleable Block Growth:** Combined with alternating attack/defend, block grows faster than chip damage can remove it at low Strength.

## Appearances

Paired with Sentry in Act 2 hallway fights. Paired with Spikers in Act 3.
