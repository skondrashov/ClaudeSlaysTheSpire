# Attack

A card type. Attacks deal damage to enemies.

## Mechanical interactions

- Damage is modified by Strength (→ effects/strength.md): +1 damage per point
- Damage is modified by Weak (→ effects/weak.md) on the attacker: x0.75
- Damage is boosted by Vulnerable (→ effects/vulnerable.md) on the target: x1.5
- Does NOT trigger Gremlin Nob's Enrage
- Does NOT trigger Hex (Chosen)
- Counts toward Pen Nib (every 10th Attack deals double)
- Counts toward Shuriken/Kunai/Ornamental Fan triggers (every 3 Attacks)
- Affected by Double Tap (play the next Attack twice)
- Affected by Necronomicon (first Attack costing 2+ each turn is played twice)
