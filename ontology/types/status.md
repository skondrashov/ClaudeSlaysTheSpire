# Status

A card type. Status cards are added to the player's deck/hand by enemies or events. They are generally harmful.

## Properties

- Cannot be permanently removed from the deck (they're added during combat and leave after combat)
- Some are Unplayable (Burn, Dazed, Wound) — they clog the hand
- Some cost energy to play (Slimed costs 1E, does nothing except Exhaust itself)
- Some have Ethereal (Dazed) — self-exhaust at end of turn if not played
- Some deal damage (Burn: 2 damage at end of turn if in hand)

## Common Status cards

| Card | Playable | Effect | Source |
|------|----------|--------|--------|
| Slimed | Yes (1E) | Exhaust | Slime enemies |
| Burn | No | 2 damage at end of turn if in hand | Hexaghost |
| Dazed | No | Ethereal (self-exhausts end of turn) | Chosen (Hex), Sentries |
| Wound | No | Nothing (pure hand clog) | Book of Stabbing |
| Void | No | Ethereal, lose 1 Energy when drawn | Nemesis, some events |
