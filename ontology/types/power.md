# Power

A card type. Powers are played once and remain in effect for the rest of combat. They do not go to the discard pile — they leave the hand and activate permanently.

## Mechanical interactions

- Cannot be retrieved once played (no discard/draw pile interaction)
- Triggers Awakened One's Curiosity: +2 Strength per Power played (Phase 2 only)
- Does NOT trigger Gremlin Nob's Enrage
- Does NOT trigger Hex (Chosen)
- Mummified Hand: playing a Power makes a random card in hand cost 0
- Bird-Faced Urn: heal 2 HP per Power played
- Multiple copies of the same Power stack (e.g., two Metallicize = double the block)
